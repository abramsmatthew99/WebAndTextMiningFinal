Responsibilities include teaching a broad range of computer science courses. A willingness to support the mission of the University, familiarity with current trends in computer sciences and computer sciences education, as well as the ability to use instructional technology to enhance students learning is expected of the successful candidate. An ideal candidate would be someone that could bring a new vision and direction to the department in terms of the curriculum and areas to pursue within the program. Ideally the candidate is able to support our Data Science major as well. Other duties include active involvement in activities to stay current in their field, advising students, and University, professional and public service. There is also opportunity to teach service courses and contribute to the general education core.
