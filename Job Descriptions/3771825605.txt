technical and business environment, familiarity with security standards, experience with business continuity, disaster recovery, auditing, risk management, vulnerability assessments, and cyber-security and incident management.</li><li>Ability to work closely with people at all levels of the organization and facilitate the implementation of corrective action as needed.</li><li>Ability
