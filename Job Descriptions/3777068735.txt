Montgomery, AL and requires in-person availability starting day 1.</p><p><strong>Job Overview:</strong></p><ul><li>The successful candidate will be an experienced Quality Assurance Engineer resource assessment of each vendor’s test methodology and practices in alignment with the client, clientInformation Security Office (ISO) and client Data Governance Office (DGO) non-functional requirements, elements</li><li>Hands-on experience with the creation and execution of automated tests</li><li>5 years in performing, monitoring, or validating performance testing including load, stress, and spike testing</li><li>Experience with multiple various, concurrent development/test methodologies</li><li>Analytical thinking</li><li>Attention to detail</li><li>Experience performing gap methods</li><li>Experience developing tests for functional, non-functional, regression, and end-to-end testing that include automated, ad-hoc, and manual testing elements</li><li>Good understanding of various QA/Testing tools and technologies – e.g., Azure DevOps or TFS, SOAP UI, HP ALM, JIRA, iServer</li><li>In-depth knowledge of testing Web-based applications from unit to system integration and through user acceptance, staging, and smoke testing production</li><li>Strong test planning and test case development skills</li><li>Ability to work closely with other developers or analysts within TEAMS</li><li>Strong understanding of generating and managing test data</li><li>Strong understanding of validating data across multiple interfacing/integrated solutions</li></ul><p><strong>Preferred Qualifications:</strong></p><ul><li>Experience with State Government, Medicaid, CHIP, and DHR business area or health care industry.</li><li>Experience collaborating across multi-vendor, solution,
