<p>Our client is seeking a highly skilled and experienced Network Engineer to join our growing team in Kokomo, IN.</p><p>In your day-to-day you will have the opportunity to participate in all enterprise network aspects, including solutions design, technical support, project management, ticket resolutions, and more. Our goal is to provide excellent customer service and deliver high network availability.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p><ul><li>Perform network lifecycle management.</li><li>Design, implement, manage, troubleshoot and support hundreds of critical network devices (Cisco switches, routers, PAN firewalls, Cisco wireless controller and access points, etc.)</li><li>Process and assist with system requests/tickets, which include defining solutions for internal customers, planning, preparation, deployment, implementation, monitoring, documentation.</li><li>Responsible for planning and execute multiple projects related to infrastructure expansion, replacement, upgrades (HW/SW).</li><li>Proactively monitor network performance, report/escalate as appropriate, and follow up with internal SLA.</li><li>Perform network audits ensuring implementation and configurations meeting company standards, plan, and implement corrections.</li><li>Provide 2nd and 3rd Level of Support in the resolution of network incidents and/or complex network troubleshooting, providing guidance to entry level Engineers.</li><li>Review, provide feedback, and approve other team members’ network procedures (Release Plans/MOPs) to validate technical accuracy for network changes.</li><li>Provide onsite and after-hours support as needed, participating in an on-call rotation.</li><li>Execute lab tests to validate solutions, bug fixes, impact analysis.</li><li>Work with vendors and ISPs for issue escalation, follow-up, maintenance windows, RMA processing.</li><li>Help create, and adhere to standard operating procedures, checklists, and conform to corporate change-control and security policies.</li><li>Support the network team with multiple internal tasks (inventory management, HW staging/preparation, peer review).</li><li>Create reports per management’s request, and on a weekly basis.</li><li>Maintain documentation for all network systems (LAN/Data center/Wireless/firewalls/load balancers).</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Requirements</strong></p><ul><li>Associate's degree in Computer Science or a related field.</li><li>4+ years of experience supporting/designing large enterprise network.</li><li>Hands-on experience with EIGRP, OSPF, BGP, VPC, STP, MLag, VRF, IPv4 subnetting, VSS, IGMP, PIM, Anycast-RP, MSDP, 802.1x, TACACS+, SYSLOG, Cisco IOS, Cisco NX-OS, Cisco EVPN, IPSec tunnels.</li><li>Ability to lift equipment up to 40lbs.</li><li>Occasional use of cleanroom gear and PPE in a manufacturing environment.</li><li>Experience with Unix/Linux.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>This job is being filled by Teamanics, Metabyte's rapidly growing peer network. Employment through Metabyte, Inc.</strong></p><p></p>
