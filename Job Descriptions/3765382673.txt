        POWER Engineers, Inc. - Information Technology Infrastructure Department has an immediate opening for a Network Admin reporting to the IT Infrastructure Back Office Support Area Lead. The Network Administrator position is responsible for the day-to-day support, maintenance, installation of all network services. This position requires technical knowledge, and hands-on experience, with routers, switches, network caching devices, firewalls, SDWAN, VPNs, WIFI and telephony systems. This position is also responsible for having the ability to advise and cross-train fellow administrators and individuals within the Tier 1 support organization.
