<p>Bring your heart to CVS Health.</p><p><br/></p><p>Every one of us at CVS Health shares a single, clear purpose: Bringing our heart to every moment of your health. This purpose guides our commitment to deliver enhanced human-centric health care for a rapidly changing world. Anchored in our brand — with heart at its center — our purpose sends a personal message that how we deliver our services is just as important as what we deliver.</p><p><br/></p><p>Our Heart At Work Behaviors™ support this purpose. We want everyone who works at CVS Health to feel empowered by the role they play in transforming our culture and accelerating our ability to innovate and deliver solutions to make health care more personal, convenient and affordable.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Position Summary</strong></p><p><br/></p><p>We are seeking a Data Engineering Delivery lead to work closely with engineering, product managers and tenant teams. This role requires a strong leader with a blend of operations, technical project management, delivery and agile experience to help guide the growth and hardening of our Enterprise Data Platform.</p><p><br/></p><p>Reporting into the Lead Director, Cloud Data Engineering in the Enterprise Data Governance, Platform and Products Department, this role is responsible for managing stakeholder engagement / communications and coordinating agile delivery with support from the Release Train Engineer. This role requires a deep understanding of agile and demonstrated comfort in agile delivery. This role will be required to understand and track key issues, risks, dependencies and blockers across the train and help escalate and resolve them. Initially, this candidate will work as the Program Delivery Manager on the Hadoop to Google Cloud Source Data Migration Train with the different Teams, prioritizing source data migration activities. The candidate needs to have a a very strong handle of Microsoft Suite – Excel and Powerpoint to create status reports and metrics for executive reporting. The successful candidate will also need to have enough technical knowledge to help guide the Agile teams towards a solution as well as significant experience working and communicating clearly to senior leadership groups.</p><p>Responsibilities of the Data Engineering Delivery lead</p><p><br/></p><ul><li>Stakeholder Management: Work closely with Engineering Teams and Business Stakeholders</li><li>Reporting: Support regular, quantifiable and measurable perspective on product health.</li><li>Agile Coaching: Support agile coaching activities across the team to aid in our agile transformation. Support RTE during PI Planning and coaching scrum masters on agile ceremonies.</li><li>Technical Project Management: Track key dependencies, blockers and issues across the train and escalate and resolve them. Responsible for managing high-level integrated timelines to promote transparency to stakeholders.</li><li>Communication: Own program communications to senior leadership and external stakeholders including status, issues, progress, etc.</li><li>Coordination and problem solving: Responsible for constant coordination across engineering, product and platform experience teams and stakeholders. Using creative problem solving to resolve key issues quickly and effectively across teams</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Required Qualifications</strong></p><p><br/></p><ul><li>10+ years of Information Technology or Analytics Experience.</li><li>5+ years of Experience with big data / data warehouse like Teradata, Oracle, Hadoop , DB2 etc</li><li>2+ years managing a large program / initiative related to Large Data and Application Migration or building a new enterprise data warehouse / data lake environment within a large Fortune 50 Company.</li><li>1+ years of Cloud Data or Analytics Experience on AWS / Azure / GCP platforms</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Preferred Qualifications</strong></p><p><br/></p><ul><li>Excellent understanding of product management and project management principles</li><li>Excellent understanding of agile frameworks and agile delivery practices</li><li>Experience with Large Healthcare data and systems having Petabytes of data</li><li>Experience implementing complex, enterprise-wide data transformation and processing solutions</li><li>Worked in a large multi-tenancy environment</li><li>Technical understanding and ability to communicate with both technical and non-technical audiences</li><li>Structured approach and comfortable working at speed to tight deadlines</li><li>Collaborative and happy working cross-functionally</li><li>Driven personality with strong ownership, accountability traits and excellent problem solver</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Education</strong></p><p><br/></p><ul><li>Bachelor's degree or equivalent work experience in Computer Science, Engineering, Machine Learning, or related discipline. Master’s degree preferred.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Pay Range</strong></p><p><br/></p><p>The typical pay range for this role is:</p><p><br/></p><p>$115,000.00 – $230,000.00</p><p><br/></p><p>This pay range represents the base hourly rate or base annual full-time salary for all positions in the job grade within which this position falls. The actual base salary offer will depend on a variety of factors including experience, education, geography and other relevant factors. This position is eligible for a CVS Health bonus, commission or short-term incentive program in addition to the base pay range listed above. This position also includes an award target in the company’s equity award program.</p><p><br/></p><p>In addition to your compensation, enjoy the rewards of an organization that puts our heart into caring for our colleagues and our communities. The Company offers a full range of medical, dental, and vision benefits. Eligible employees may enroll in the Company’s 401(k) retirement savings plan, and an Employee Stock Purchase Plan is also available for eligible employees. The Company provides a fully-paid term life insurance plan to eligible employees, and short-term and long term disability benefits. CVS Health also offers numerous well-being programs, education assistance, free development courses, a CVS store discount, and discount programs with participating partners. As for time off, Company employees enjoy Paid Time Off (“PTO”) or vacation pay, as well as paid holidays throughout the calendar year. Number of paid holidays, sick time and other time off are provided consistent with relevant state law and Company policies.</p><p><br/></p><p>For more detailed information on available benefits, please visit jobs.CVSHealth.com/benefits</p><p><br/></p><p>CVS Health requires certain colleagues to be fully vaccinated against COVID-19 (including any booster shots if required), where allowable under the law, unless they are approved for a reasonable accommodation based on disability, medical condition, religious belief, or other legally recognized reasons that prevents them from being vaccinated. </p><p><br/></p><p>You are required to have received at least one COVID-19 shot prior to your first day of employment and to provide proof of your vaccination status or apply for a reasonable accommodation within the first 10 days of your employment.
