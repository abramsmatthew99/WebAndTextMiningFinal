        A Junior Systems Engineer is an entry-level position in the field of systems engineering. Systems engineering is a discipline that focuses on designing, integrating, and managing complex systems throughout their entire lifecycle. These systems can range from computer systems and software applications to large-scale infrastructure projects.<br/><br/>Key Responsibilities:<br/><br/><ul><li>Supporting Senior Engineers: Junior Systems Engineers often work closely with more experienced engineers to learn and assist in various tasks related to system design, development, and maintenance.</li><li>Documentation: They may be responsible for creating and maintaining technical documentation, including system design specifications, process documentation, and configuration management records.</li><li>Troubleshooting: Identifying and resolving issues with systems, applications, or infrastructure components. This may involve troubleshooting hardware or software problems.</li><li>Testing: Conducting tests and quality assurance activities to ensure that systems meet performance and reliability standards.</li><li>System Maintenance: Assisting in routine system maintenance tasks, such as updates, patches, and backups.</li><li>Collaboration: Collaborating with cross-functional teams, including software developers, network engineers, and project managers, to ensure systems meet project requirements.</li><li>Requirements Analysis: Participating in requirements gathering and analysis to understand the needs of the system users and stakeholders.</li><li>Research: Staying updated with industry trends and emerging technologies to contribute to the improvement of systems and processes.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Desired Skills<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Experience with cloud computing platforms (AWS, Azure, GCP, Nutanix)</li><li>Experience with virtualization technologies (VMware, Hyper-V)</li><li>Experience with networking protocols and technologies (TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP)</li><li>Experience with security technologies (firewalls, intrusion detection systems, encryption)</li><li>Experience with programming languages (Python, Bash, PowerShell)<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in computer science, information technology, or a related field</li><li>2-3 years of experience in system administration or a related field</li><li>Strong knowledge of operating systems, software, and hardware</li><li>Experience with system troubleshooting and problem solving</li><li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team</li><li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills<br/><br/><br/></li></ul>Flexible work from home options available.
