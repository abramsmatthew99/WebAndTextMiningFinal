        Position Summary: The IT/Data Analyst is responsible for the day-to-day IT technical support of the organization as well as the management and manipulation of card data required to produce gift cards. This role involves working with large volumes of data, ensuring data integrity, and supporting the overall IT infrastructure.<br/><br/>Key Responsibilities:<br/><br/>IT Application and Infrastructure Support:<br/><br/><ul><li> Install, configure, and maintain hardware, software, and networking infrastructure.</li><li> Manage user accounts, access controls, and permissions for data and IT systems through active directory and Office 365.</li><li> Provide technical support and troubleshooting for data and IT-related issues.</li><li> Implement and enforce security measures, data privacy, and compliance standards.</li><li> Review server and antivirus and take action to remediate as needed.</li><li> Create and monitor email phishing campaigns using supplied tools as needed.</li><li> Maintain and create new reports using Cognos.</li><li> Provide basic support for SharePoint.</li><li> Stay updated with emerging data and IT trends, technologies, and best practices.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul>Data Management:<br/><br/><ul><li> Utilize tools available to create data files necessary to imprint/encode gift cards and carriers.</li><li> Utilize Bartender Labelling software to maintain label templates and create label proofs.</li><li> Generate test cards as needed.</li><li> Run scripts as necessary to maintain the performance of the data automation tools.</li><li> Recommend improvements to the data generation process and outputs.</li><li> Research any data inconsistencies identified by the clients.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul>Qualifications and Skills:<br/><br/><ul><li> Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, or a related field.</li><li> Understanding of networking, hardware, and software systems.</li><li> Excellent problem-solving skills and ability to troubleshoot data and IT issues.</li><li> Strong Excel and text editor skills.</li><li> Ability to adapt and learn new technologies as needed.</li><li> Knowledge of programming languages such as C# or Visual Basic would be a plus.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul>Competitive pay, including sign on bonus. Optimum Card Solutions offers Health/Dental/Life insurance and 401K with matching contributions.
