The analyst must have a high attention to detail, be very adaptable, and have intellectual curiosity to learn and sometimes discover and/or create new technical tools and procedures. The analyst must also have experience working closely with internal resources, vendors, outside counsel, and in-house counsel. Finally, the analyst must have experience working in or with large and complex enterprise environments.<br/><br/><strong>Key Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Work with others to support the processes to improve efficiency and uphold accuracy.</li><li>Recommend and update documentation, training materials, and SOPs, with approval from Toyota Legal One</li><li>Troubleshoot and resolve One</li><li>Possess proven ability to learn new technology quickly and share knowledge with others<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>What You Bring<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree (or higher) in technologies</li><li>2 or more years of hands-on technical work experience in Microsoft O365, Google Workspace, and Slack eDiscovery</li><li>2 or more years of hands-on experience with EnCase, Cellebrite, Nuix, Magnet Forensics, ZL, Relativity, and other eDiscovery technology solutions</li><li>Strong written and verbal communication skills with the ability to create and present concepts, influence, and persuade the business, and make recommendations to management</li><li>Ability to handle multiple priorities, work independently, and meet stringent deadlines</li><li>Experience working directly with a variety of technical and non-technical end users</li><li>Understanding of the ESI lifecycle and related industry standards<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Added bonus if you have <br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Experience with PowerShell, Python, and other scripting languages</li><li>One or more certifications related to eDiscovery or digital forensics (e.g., EnCE, GCFE, GASF, CEDS, and others)</li><li>Automotive manufacturing/industry experience<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>What We’ll Bring<br/><br/></strong>During your interview process, our team can fill you in on all the
