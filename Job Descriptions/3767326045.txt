Bespoke transformed leadership at portfolio companies achieving buyout transactions totaling more than $25 billion in 2021 alone. Bespoke's unique, data-driven services complete searches in typically half the industry average time with a 99% placement success rate.<br/><br/>The firm partners with more than 70 top tier private equity firms, providing human capital services that accelerate execution on value creation plans, cut the risk of mis-hiring, and empower leadership to achieve the investment thesis.<br/><br/>Bespoke Partners has 85+ employees across four offices in San Diego, Austin, Philadelphia, and San Francisco.<br/><br/><strong>Who Are you?<br/><br/></strong>Drive. Instinct. Grit. Smarts.<br/><br/>Just like our search process you are collaborative, thorough, accelerated and therefore effective. Passionate about Client services and growing and developing the best teams and talent. You are determined, solution-oriented and energized by exceeding your own potential. No matter your title, you lead and mentor with passion and purpose being memorable in every client and employee interaction you experience. Motivated by transparency, you thrive in a feed forward, team-first environment and continue to be laser focused on data driven and effective results. If this sounds like you, please read on!<br/><br/><strong>About The Role<br/><br/></strong>The Research Analyst is the primary driver of the research strategy and execution for talent identification throughout the search process. The Research Analyst is responsible for conducting market analysis, developing research and candidate sourcing strategies, while securing strong, effective potential candidate pipelines for C-suite level functions. This role is the first impression of Bespoke and builds relationships with candidates from outreach and sourcing candidates, Boolean search and diverse profiling. They align the sourcing activities and work with Search managers to understand the recruitment needs. This role works closely with Bespoke Principals and Partners during search assignments to achieve and contribute to the overall success of the search.<br/><br/><strong>Duties &amp; Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Define and executes research strategy (through collaboration with the rest of the search team) for search engagements</li><li>Continual analysis and monitoring of industry, trade publications for trends in software, including mergers and acquisitions (M&amp;A), private equity, and growth equity investments to gather market insights and perspectives on talent pools</li><li>Ensures research projects associated with search assignments are effective and completed, including ongoing monitoring of candidate progress, implementing communication plans, and harvesting relevant data; including the preparations of search update reports and drafting meeting notes</li><li>Builds talent pools of specific candidate profiles that fit search functions and client requirements through the identification of appropriate target organizations and channels using existing internal and external resources</li><li>Measures and monitors the effectiveness of the strategic sourcing framework against predefined outcomes</li><li>Performs queries of the Bespoke Partners (BP) database to discover candidate information such as compensation data, location data, and proprietary assessment data</li><li>Supporting business development efforts by conducting market research and compiling key information on target company and competitors</li><li>Develop skills to serve as an industry or field subject matter in software, private equity and human capital</li><li>And other tasks, projects, or duties as required of the position<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Strong project management skills, ability to employ critical thinking and analysis, and proven success in creative problem solving </li><li>Metrics-driven working style with a knack for compiling, interpreting, and articulating data </li><li>Able to present and communicate information clearly and concisely to diverse audiences </li><li>Must be comfortable with using reference materials, databases, online services, and internet searching tools </li><li>Excellent organizational and time management skills </li><li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills and the ability to interact professionally with senior-level leaders </li><li>Results-oriented mindset with ability to multi-task in a fast-paced consulting environment </li><li>Previous internship experience is preferred</li><li>Boolean search logic experience preferred <br/><br/></li></ul><em>The wage range for this role takes into
