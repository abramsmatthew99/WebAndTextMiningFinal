Your role will require excellent customer service skills and quick decision-making abilities. Responsibilities: - Provide onsite customer support to a diverse workforce, including employees from different backgrounds, abilities, and identities - Troubleshoot and resolve technical issues related to laptops, software, and hardware - Work with a mix of Windows PCs, Macs, and Mobile Devices - Ensure excellent customer service and timely issue resolution - Utilize ticketing and traffic reporting tools such as Zendesk, HPSM, and ServiceNow Basic Qualifications: - High School Diploma, GED, or equivalent certification - At least 1 year of experience in remote administration and end-user support - Experience with ticketing and traffic reporting tools Preferred Qualifications (not required, but a plus): - Associate's Degree in Business, Information Systems, Computer Science, or Military experience - Experience with Active Directory services integration - Experience with process management functions (change, incident, problem management) - Apple certifications - Experience with virtualization technologies (VMWare) - macOS Server administration experience - Experience with platform management and integration tools (JAMF Casper Suite or SCCM) - Experience with productivity tools on Mac (MS Office, Lync or Skype) - Experience supporting Mac users - Experience with image deployment, software testing, system testing, and encryption and security tools (McAfee) Note: Capital One will not sponsor new applicants for employment authorization for this position. Salary Range: New York City (Hybrid On-Site): $71,400 - $81,500 for Principal Coordinator, Support Operations Other locations salary range will be specific to that location.
