anomalies and biases to further refine data quality.</li></ul><p>Strong candidates might also have:</p><ul><li>Familiarity with tools used for data labeling (e.g., LabelStudio), data collection (e.g., VPN, Selenium), and data processing (e.g., Hadoop, Datasketch).</li><li>Experience in constructing extensive datasets.</li><li>Hands-on experience with cloud platforms such as AWS, Azure, or GCP.</li><li>Practical experience in machine learning, such as language, vision, or audio-related projects.</li><li>An active presence on GitHub, showcasing contributions to projects.</li><li>Multilingual proficiency contributing to the enrichment of language diversity, a crucial aspect of robust model training.</li><li>Familiarity with fairness considerations, toxicity, data privacy regulations, and compliance aspects.</li><li>If you're passionate about data engineering and machine learning and are motivated to make a difference, we encourage you to explore this opportunity. Our team is dedicated to cultivating a diverse environment where learning and innovation thrive. Whether you are an experienced engineer or a junior looking to grow, we welcome you to apply. Your contributions could shape the development of foundational models and contribute to our mission.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Required Education: Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.</p>
