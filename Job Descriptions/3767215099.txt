STIGS, CIS Benchmarks, and vendor best practices. </li><li> Facilitate the Accreditation and Authorization (A&amp;A) process (formerly C&amp;A) to include package preparation for the Authorizing Security Plans (SSPs), as well as all system-related documentation such as Account Management policies, hardware/software lists, network diagrams, etc. </li><li> This is a “hands-on” position that requires someone who is capable of multi-tasking.It requires an individual with a service-oriented mentality that can focus and take ownership of assignments/issues and manage/resolve those issues. </li><li> Work well in a team-oriented environment and be able to self-manage their tasks to ensure success. </li><li> Communicate up and down within the organization; prepare detailed descriptions of issues, status updates, recommendations, and reports.Applicants must be organized with an eye for detail. </li><li>The job is located in Fairfax, VA, with frequent local trips to our customer sites in Springfield, VA.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong> Required Skills: <br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Must be US Citizen </li><li> BA/BS degree in Computer Science/Engineering or a related Information Technology </li><li> Extensive Experience with KASM management, GITLAB management and vulnerability scanning tools (ie: Fortify, twistlock, etc) </li><li> Experience developing Cyber or Physical Security CONOPS. </li><li> Demonstrated experience with McAfee HBSS (ePO, HIPS, Anti-Virus, etc…). </li><li> Thorough understanding of DoD Information Technology best practices </li><li> Thorough understanding of DoD Cybersecurity best practices </li><li> Thorough knowledge of DODD 8500.1, DODI 8500.2, and other IA guidance. </li><li> Demonstrated experience with security / validation testing tools to include vulnerability scanners (Retina, Nessus), DISA STIGs, SRR, and DISA checklists. </li><li> Must be proficient with desktop programs such as Adobe Acrobat, MS Excel, MS Word, MS Visio </li><li> Must <br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Bachelor’s Degree in a computer science related field </li><li> Vendor Certification with one or more of the following: VMWare, MS Windows, and Red Hat Linux. </li><li>
