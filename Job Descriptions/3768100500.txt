<p><strong>IT Business Analyst</strong></p><p><br/></p><p>Veritas Partners is seeking an IT Business Analyst to join an established firm focused on harnessing modern technologies to accelerate growth. This position reports directly to the company’s Chief Operating Officer and will be responsible for a broad portfolio of IT project management initiatives. The IT Business Analyst will seamlessly work across departments, interacting with diverse teams possessing a wide variety of opportunities for technical advancement. The ideal candidate will also have experience coding in JavaScript or Ruby, as the IT Business Analyst will need to regularly interface with the company’s third-party software development team.</p><p><br/></p><p>This position is hybrid after the initial 60 days. Following that initial period, the office/remote schedule is based on a two-week (10 day) period with seven days in the office and three days remote – effectively 1.5 days each week remote. </p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Responsibilities:</strong></p><ul><li>Develop short- and long-term IT strategy for the company</li><li>Document and prioritize business requirements from multiple stakeholders </li><li>Build and maintain analytics dashboards </li><li>Serve as company lead POC to the third-party IT Managed Service Provider</li><li>Liaise with the third-party MSP to create a comprehensive IT security plan</li><li>Liaise with the third-party MSP to create a comprehensive incident response plan</li><li>Lead equipment purchasing and leasing strategy </li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><em>Software Development:</em></strong></p><ul><li>Serve as company lead POC to third-party software development team</li><li>Capture business requirements and translate those into technical requests</li></ul><p>· Test software enhancements and features in a dev/sandbox environment</p><ul><li>Triage and prioritize software bug reports</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Qualifications:</strong></p><p><strong>· </strong>5+ years in an IT Analytics, Developer, or Program Manager role</p><ul><li>Strong project management and leadership skills</li><li>Strong problem-solving ability, including metrics-driven thinking</li><li>Experience with Agile methodology and sprint-based development cycles</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><em>Preferred Qualifications:</em></strong></p><ul><li><strong>Computer science degree</strong></li><li>Coding languages: JavaScript, Ruby, SQL</li><li>Experience with Zendesk or similar ticketing systems</li><li>Experience with HubSpot, WordPress, and Google Analytics</li></ul>
