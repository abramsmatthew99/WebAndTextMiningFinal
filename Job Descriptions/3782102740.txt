This internship provides a unique chance to apply your academic knowledge in a real-world setting, gain hands-on experience, and contribute to the development of state-of-the-art wearable devices.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Collaborate with the Health Lab team to design and implement machine learning algorithms for PPG data analysis</li><li></li><li> Conduct research to explore novel signal processing techniques and machine learning models to improve the accuracy and efficiency of health-related insights from wearable devices</li><li></li><li> Work closely with cross-functional teams, including hardware and software engineers, to integrate machine learning solutions into OPPO's wearable devices</li><li></li><li> Analyze and interpret experimental results, providing insights and recommendations for further improvements<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong><strong>Basic Qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> processing, and data analysis</li><li> Proficiency in programming languages such as Python and experience with machine learning frameworks (e.g., TensorFlow, PyTorch)</li><li> Excellent problem-solving skills and a passion for pushing the boundaries of technology</li><li> Strong communication and collaboration skills</li><li> Prior experience with wearable devices or health-related projects is a plus<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Preferred Qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Demonstrated research experience in machine learning, signal processing, or related fields through
