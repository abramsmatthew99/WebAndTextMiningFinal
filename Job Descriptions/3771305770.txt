PACCAR Inc is recognized as a global leader in the commercial vehicle, financial, and customer service fields with internationally recognized brands such as Kenworth, Peterbilt, and DAF trucks. PACCAR is a global technology leader in the design, manufacture and customer support of high-quality light-, medium- and heavy-duty trucks under the Kenworth, Peterbilt and DAF nameplates. PACCAR designs and manufactures advanced diesel engines and also provides customized financial services, information technology and truck parts related to its principal business.<br/><br/>Whether you want to design the transportation technology of tomorrow, support the staff functions of a dynamic, international leader, or build our excellent products and services — you can develop the career you desire with PACCAR. Get started!<br/><br/>Division Information<br/><br/>PACCAR's Information Division (ITD), located in Renton, WA utilizes cutting-edge technology to provide systems development, consulting, voice and data communications services to the entire Corporation, which has high visibility in the technology sector.<br/><br/>Requisition Summary<br/><br/>PACCAR is a global truck technology leader with operations around the world. The Digital Solutions team is helping PACCAR make better decisions faster by moving to the cloud, adopting DevOps/CICD best practices, and unlocking a new level of productivity &amp; value. We are looking for technology leaders to drive this evolution.<br/><br/>We are looking for an experienced Database Administrator of SQL and PostgreSQL databases to manage thousands of enterprise-level databases in a scalable database solution. The ideal candidate is enthusiastic about learning new technologies and implementing them to empower internal customers and scale the existing platform. The ideal candidate demonstrates reliable business and communication skills and works with business and technical teams. In this role, you would serve as the expert at designing, implementing, and operating stable, scalable, and low-cost database solutions. The Database Administrator would design, develop, implement, test, document, and operate large-scale, high-volume, high-performance database structures. Implement data structures using best practices in data modeling. Will provide operational database services to the organization, and own, track, and resolve database-related incidents and requests. On a day-to-day level, there will be database backups, maintenance, monitoring, and responding to database-related alerts and escalations.<br/><br/>The DBA would be tuning performance on queries running over millions of rows of data. The person in this position should be analytical, have a high customer focus, and have a passion for process improvement. Familiar with DevOps methodology to automate the database deployment.</li><li>Provide database support estimates for new projects.</li><li>Support multiple RDBMS engines and work in the AWS Cloud or Azure environment.</li><li>Support for Agile projects related to databases.</li><li>Contributes to and maintains database standards.</li><li>Ensures security is integrated into all data solutions to meet compliance standards.</li><li>Perform database backup and restore procedures.
