<strong>Your Job<br/><br/></strong>Koch Tax is building something new and valuable, a tax data platform that enables efficient and repeatable process transformation across our enterprise and makes many types of data available for automation that was not previously possible. Developing this new platform in support of our Tax Innovation Vision will be critical to our success as a Tax team at Koch Industries. The Software Engineer role is a key part of the Platform Delivery team. This role is responsible to provide reusable components which enable the acceleration of transformation processes. This role takes guidance from other software architects and engineers to build python-based object-oriented programs that ensure the environment is highly configurable, supports automated testing, continuous deployment at the highest levels of code compliance. This role also participates in the research and design for applications of natural language processing, symbolic mathematics, data mining and task automation.<br/><br/><strong>Our Team<br/><br/></strong>As a member of the Data Management, Enablement &amp; Transformation (DMET) team, you will have the opportunity to drive innovation and leverage your tax knowledge in an exciting data driven environment. This key role will create value by partnering across capabilities to drive sustainable transformation.
