This DIT is a key contributor to DMF Lighting’s vision as a forward-thinking industrial design and engineering company that is very responsive and service oriented. The DIT is a thought leader for DMF’s business systems and IT infrastructure.<br/><br/>If you are a fast-thinking individual who seeks challenges with enthusiasm, has a strong sense of urgency and is able to multitask, DMF is for you. This should include having a process to prioritize a list of strategic projects (aka, IT governance). </li><li>IT Team Leadership: Be a thought leader with a great attention to detail that is not afraid to roll up your sleeves to problem solve. Use data analytics to make data-driven decisions and recommendations where efficiently possible. </li><li>Information Security and Compliance: Implement and maintain robust IT security measures. Safeguard sensitive data and ensure compliance with industry-specific regulations and standards. Also, maintain/implement the appropriate networks and user procedures to avoid network hacking or ransomware scenarios. </li><li>Budget Management: Develop and manage the Business Systems department's budget, ensuring efficient resource allocation and cost control. Ensure cost-effective technology solutions that meet business requirements. </li><li>Team Development: Recruit, train, and mentor IT staff, promoting their professional growth within the context of business systems and broader technology. Also, leverage the specialized skills of 3rd party service providers for level 2 help vs trying to staff internally. </li><li>Knowledge Sharing and Communication: Promote IT transparency though an environment of sharing knowledge among DMF staff.
