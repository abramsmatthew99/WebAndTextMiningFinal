<p>Alongside Global is a collective of creative businesses which are changing the world of production. Unreal Engine and Autodesk products are a large component as well as rendering and networking setup and troubleshooting. As an integral part of the team the role will work across UK and USA time zones (within reason) and support multiple business entities within our collective.</p><p><br/></p><p>The ideal candidate has experience in a VFX, Post Production Facility or similar environment.</p><p><br/></p><p>Our studio is 75% standard creative studio office management with macbook laptop users, using cloud service on assigned tasks with minimal supervision </li><li>Have strong debugging, development testing, and problem-solving abilities, and you validate your work with methodical and rigorous testing.</li></ul><p> </p><p><strong>Responsibilities:</strong></p><p>Production Teams support</p><ul><li>Provide technical support to artists and troubleshoot software-related applications failing to open or connect to servers and render platforms.</li><li>machines not mounting or losing mount points.</li><li>remote access failures for PCIOP systems.</li><li>SAN and NAS storage issues, including drive rebuilds and coordination with providers.</li><li>Resolve Deadline rendering issues and implement fixes.</li><li>Understand and troubleshoot Thinkbox and AWS</li><li>Knowledge of computer assembly and the ability to troubleshoot hardware failures such as overheating and random reboots.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>IT Operations</p><ul><li>Oversee the management of software licenses assignment and renewals, collaborating with various team members across the organization and liaising with providers.</li><li>Perform employees and freelancers onboarding and handle user account creation/de-activation across the organization</li><li>Thoroughly monitor and report on AWS usage and cost management and billing</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>Internal Processes</p><ul><li>Assist in the development and maintenance of the VFX pipeline, ensuring the efficient flow of assets and data. </li><li>Actively Document, maintain and educate teams on tools and processes, communicate solutions to common technical challenges. </li><li>Maintain regular communication with Leadership in order to address active production and operational requirements</li><li>Identify opportunities to improve operational efficiency and stability.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Qualifications:</strong></p><p>Proficiency</p><ul><li>Possess a thorough understanding of Linux, Mac, and Windows operating systems, including Linux installation and troubleshooting.</li><li>Proficiency in Fiber Channel and 1-10 gigabyte internetwork file
