This position is open to candidates who reside in and have the legal right to work in the country where the job is located.<br/><br/>Company Name: FM Global<br/><br/><strong>Job Description<br/><br/></strong>Job Opportunity: Data Analyst - Remote Position Company Background:<br/><br/>FM Global is a leading property insurer that provides risk management and property insurance solutions to the world's largest businesses. We help our clients maintain continuity in their business operations by utilizing advanced loss-prevention engineering, research, risk management skills, and support services. With a culturally diverse team of employees working in over 100 countries, we strive to be inclusive and offer challenging roles.<br/><br/>This remote position may require periodic travel to our headquarters in Johnston, RI, and the candidate must be willing to work EST hours.<br/><br/>Responsibilities:<br/><br/>The Data Analyst will be responsible for managing FM Global's metadata assets through the development and refinement of enterprise and product-specific data models. They will analyze existing applications and develop data business rules, as well as gather data business rules through project-related work. The Data Analyst will collaborate with product teams, peers, business partners, and cross-functional stakeholders to ensure the integration of data business rules across subject areas and business domains. They will also provide consultation on data quality issues, data management, and data business rule development.<br/><br/>Qualifications:<br/><br/><ul><li>A bachelor's degree is required, preferably in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related discipline. </li><li>At least 3 years of experience in data analysis, developing logical and enterprise data models using industry-leading tools. </li><li>Technical knowledge in transactional data modeling, data repositories, metadata management, and Agile methodologies. </li><li>Hands-on experience with data governance, data quality, and master data management tools is a plus. </li><li>Excellent analytical, problem-solving, and communication skills. </li><li>Ability to work effectively as part of a team and across teams. <br/><br/></li></ul>Salary and Benefits:<br/><br/>The salary for this position ranges from $97,500 to $159,400, depending on geographic location, education, skills, and experience. As an FM Global employee, you will have access to a comprehensive Total Rewards program, which includes an incentive plan, generous health and well-being programs, a 401(k) and pension plan, career development opportunities, tuition reimbursement, flexible work arrangements, time off allowances, and more.<br/><br/>Application and Inclusivity:<br/><br/>To apply for this position or learn more about FM Global, please visit our website. We are an equal opportunity employer committed to attracting, developing, and retaining a diverse workforce.<br/><br/>#FMG
