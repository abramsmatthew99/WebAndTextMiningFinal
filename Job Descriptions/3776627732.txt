Working with cross-functional teams, gather and document reporting requirements to meet business needs. Using skills and experience as a BI Developer, use those requirements to design, develop, test, and implement tools that utilize the underlying data stores/data warehouses/data marts.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Responsibilities include the following:</strong></p><p>· Participates in business analysis activities to gather requirements</p><p>· Translates business requirements configuration management, security, and change controls are implemented and tested</p><p>· Provides technical assistance and cross training to other team members</p><p>· Designs and implements technology best practices, guidelines, and repeatable processes</p><p>· Perform duties capably with moderate to low supervision</p><p>· Trains other team members/staff on relevant topics at which you implementing, and maintaining applications, including the following:</p><p>o Working with users in a requirements analysis role</p><p>o Data warehouse implementations, operation, and maintenance, dimensional)</p><p>· Performance tuning experience (MS SQL Server configuration, query optimization, hardware configuration)</p><p>· Excellent interpersonal (verbal and written) communication skills Ability to manage multiple priorities, and assess and adjust quickly to changing priorities</p><p>· Adapts easily to different environments such as team projects or working independently.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>What We'd Like to See in You-The Nice-to-Haves</strong></p><p>· Programming/scripting experience related to ETL and analytical processes such as PowerShell and
