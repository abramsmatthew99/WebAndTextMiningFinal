<p><strong>JOB TITLE: Director of Information Technology</strong></p><p><strong>REPORTS TO: Chief Financial Officer</strong></p><p><strong>STATUS: Exempt</strong></p><p><strong>DIVISION: CRS Information Technology</strong></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>This position will be onsite in our Chicago, IL or Orland Park, IL office with the opportunity to be hybrid on occasion. </strong></p><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>GENERAL DESCRIPTION</u></strong> – <em>Summary</em></p><p>The Director of Information Technology is responsible for developing a strategic plan that is aligned with Agency objectives. This position oversees all technical aspects of the company and technology strategies for the delivery of IT services. This includes the overall planning, organizing, and execution of all IT functions, security and compliance policies across all locations. </p><p><strong><u> </u></strong></p><p><strong><u>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</u></strong> <em>include the following: </em></p><ul><li>Oversee the IT team ensuring they are productive and cohesive</li><li>Manage the IT budgets and responsible for allocating resources to ensures ROI on technology investments</li><li>Access and manage implementation of new technologies to meet the Agencies needs</li><li>Identify and mitigate risk including cybersecurity threats, software/hardware failure points</li><li>Create and update IT policies, standards and procedures;</li><li>Select and Manage relationships with technology vendors and service providers</li><li>Oversee IT projects, ensuring they are completed on time and within budget</li><li>Continuously monitor IT performance, identifying areas for improvement, and implementing solutions</li><li>Responsible for overall network performance and stability to assure that all systems are operational during all hours of business operations.</li><li>Support Merger and Acquisition initiatives.</li><li>Supports and engages with data initiatives to advance overall data strategy across the agency</li><li>Ensure new acquisitions are integrated in accordance with onboarding plans and timelines</li><li>Responsible for developing and implementing IT services Business Continuity plan and procedures.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES</u></strong></p><ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Engineering, or Business Administration with concentration around IT is required </li><li>The employee is responsible for adherence to the stated expectations of the E&amp;O Plus Quality Management Program</li><li>Ability to approach and solve problems in a variety of scenarios and situations.</li><li>Ability to read, analyze and interpret technical procedures and compliance requirements</li><li>Ability to create reports, presentations, business correspondence, policies and procedures</li><li>Minimum of eight (8) years direct experience with Information Technology, including network planning, operations, and management</li><li>Experience leading complex, major change initiatives; demonstrated skills in change management</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>WORKING CONDITIONS</u></strong></p><p>Fast-paced, multi-tasking environment requiring tolerance for schedule change and disruption with potential for travel.</p><p> </p><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>JOB PERFORMANCE STANDARDS</u></strong></p><p>The job description is intended to describe the essential responsibilities The Horton Group requires. Essential functions are outlined; other duties may be assigned as needs arise or as required to support the essential functions.</p><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS</u></strong></p><p>Ability to: </p><p>· Perform work on a computer for extended periods of time.</p><p>· Sit/stand for extended periods of time in work area.</p><p>· Grasp objects using your fingers (fine motor skills) </p><p>· Communicate and speak in a clear, concise and professional manner both in person and on the telephone. </p><p>· Travel by air or ground transportation as required in performing job duties.</p><p>· Be able to lift and carry objects that weigh a minimum of 20 pounds.</p><p> </p><p> </p><p><em>Nothing in this job description or in any other written documents or forms maintained by The Horton Group creates, or may be deemed to create, an employment contract between The Horton Group and any of its employees.</em></p><p><em> </em></p><p><em>The Horton Group reserves the right to revise the contents of this job description, at any time, without prior notice to its employees.</em></p><p><em> </em></p><p><em>This job description supersedes any previous oral or written job descriptions.</em></p><p></p>
