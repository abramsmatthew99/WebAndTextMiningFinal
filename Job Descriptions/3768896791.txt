<strong>Minimum qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, a related technical field, or equivalent practical experience.</li><li>5 years of experience with data migration strategies and moving production systems in on-premise/data center environments to the cloud.</li><li>5 years of experience with database technologies and database administration techniques. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Preferred qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Master's degree in Computer Science or a related technical field.</li><li>5 years of experience with optimizing database performance with respect to transactional or analytic workloads.</li><li>4 years of experience in technical sales or professional consulting in the fields of systems integration, data transfer/management, and enterprise database performance.</li><li>Knowledge of Disaster Recovery (DR) and data backup strategies.</li><li>Ability to learn, understand, and work with new emerging technologies, methodologies, and solutions in the Cloud/IT Technology space.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>About The Job<br/><br/></strong>When leading companies choose Google Cloud it's a huge win for spreading the power of cloud computing globally. Once educational institutions, government agencies, and other businesses sign on to use Google Cloud products, you come in to facilitate making their work more productive, mobile, and collaborative. You liaise with the product marketing management and engineering teams to stay on top of industry trends and devise enhancements to Google Cloud products.<br/><br/>As an Enterprise Customer Engineer, you will work directly with the Technical Sales teams as an enterprise database subject matter expert to differentiate and portray the goal of Google Cloud to our customers.
