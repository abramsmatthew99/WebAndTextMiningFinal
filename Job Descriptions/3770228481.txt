        Chief Technology Officer, MassDOT - ( 23000BFP )<br/><br/><strong>Description<br/><br/></strong>An agency on the move, the <strong>Massachusetts Department of Transportation (MassDOT)</strong> takes great pride in connecting the Commonwealth’s residents and communities. Powered by a growing data-analytics discipline – and working in conjunction with municipalities, public agencies, and the private sector – we’re finding new ways to improve the Bay State’s transportation infrastructure.<br/><br/><strong> MassDOT </strong> is responsible for developing, implementing, and coordinating transportation policies and projects for the Commonwealth of Massachusetts and to efficiently plan, design, construct, and maintain a safe statewide transportation system which effectively meets the transportation needs of the Commonwealth. MassDOT’ s divisions include Highway, Aeronautics, Registry of Motor Vehicles, Rail &amp; Transit, and Planning &amp; Enterprise Services. There are approximately 3,600 employees across the organization.<br/><br/>MassDOT is seeking to hire a<strong> Chief Technology Officer (CTO) to </strong>directly support the Chief Information Officer (CIO) in designing and implementing the Commonwealth’s technology and architecture standards. The CTO is responsible for leading the Commonwealth’s IT modernization projects and standards related to infrastructure and operations. The CTO manages architecture, infrastructure, and operations teams, including cloud, servers, storage, telecom, network, desktop, and service desk. The CTOwill lead the management, development standardization, deployment, and support of IT infrastructure solutions that support the delivery of services across the organization.<br/><br/>The primary work location for this role will be <strong>10 Park Plaza Boston, Massachusetts 02116.</strong> The work schedule for this position is <strong>Monday through Friday, 9AM to 5PM EST</strong>. This position would be expected to follow a <strong>hybrid model of reporting</strong> to work that combines in-office workdays and work from home days as needed.<br/><br/>All offers of employment into this position are conditional and subject to passing: a Massachusetts Criminal Background Check (CORI); a security clearance (fingerprinting) consistent with IRS and/or public safety requirements; and security training.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><strong> Responsibilities and duties include, but are not limited to: <br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Provide the technical vision to complement the business vision of MassDOT. departments and agencies. </li><li> Lead the development of an architecture roadmap that facilitates the success of IT strategic initiatives. </li><li> Lead the preparation and testing of a disaster recovery program for MassDOT. </li><li> Play key role in the consolidation of data center/server and implementation of One Network with EOTSS </li><li> Design and manage an outreach strategy/education plan in order to ensure agencies understand and comply with enterprise standards/guidelines. </li><li> Provide technology consulting and expertise with all significant IT Projects from procurement to implementation. </li><li> Forecast and manage expenses for operating and capital budgets. </li><li> Lead the planning, assessment, development, and support of the technology infrastructure. </li><li> Develop the strategic plan and roadmap for the IT infrastructure for MassDOT. </li><li> Develop and annually review plan for improvements to the MassDOT infrastructure. </li><li> Ensure alignment with EOTSS standard desktop configuration and successful rollout of standardized image creation, deployment, and maintenance. </li><li> Develop a cohesive implementation and integration plan across all MassDOT IT groups. </li><li> Research emerging technologies and recommend adoption strategies for the MassDOT. </li><li> Develop and champion a culture of customer service, quality, and continuous process improvement. </li><li> Provide technology leadership to IT organizations across MassDOT. </li><li> Apply advanced business and technical knowledge and skills in assessing technical requirements and developing solutions to problems of unusual complexity. </li><li> Conduct architecture reviews for IT Projects to validate project technology alignment with established standards. </li><li> Provide the technology leadership to engineering teams necessary in order to provide effective IT solutions for the multiple agencies that they service. </li><li> Participate in MassDOT project approval and oversight processes to ensure adherence to enterprise standards and architecture requirements. </li><li> Point person for managing the on-going relationship with EOTSS and other key IT vendors and partners. </li><li> Provide representation of MassDOT IT on key internal and external committees <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Required Knowledge, Skills &amp;
