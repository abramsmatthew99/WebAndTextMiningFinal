<p><strong><u>Internal Audit:</u></strong></p><p> Internal Audit is an independent assurance &amp; risk management function, reporting directly to our client's Audit Committee of the Board, with the mission of protecting their firm &amp; enhancing its control environment. to achieve this objective, our client will work with all businesses to assess internal control processes, identify areas of improvement, and ensure appropriate risk remediation.</p><p> Specific to the IT Auditor role, the Technology Audit Team assesses controls for technology &amp; cybersecurity processes &amp; systems, located both on-premises and in the cloud. Items in scope include business applications, technology software, and infrastructure components, such as networks, operating systems, and databases.</p><p><strong><u>Qualifications:</u></strong></p><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in computer science, information systems</li><li>3-5 years of experience with information technology in Internal Audit, IT operations, IT Risk &amp; compliance, or Information Security in the Financial Services industry</li><li>Technology auditing certification (e.g. CISA) or equivalent</li><li>Working knowledge of general computer &amp; application controls, information security principles, software development lifecycle, business continuity &amp; disaster recovery, an other technology areas including information technology governance standards &amp; industry best practices (e.g. NIST, CIS, MITRE, OWASP, etc.)</li></ul><p><strong><u>Role Description:</u></strong></p><ul><li>Planning - Identity &amp; evaluate key processes, risks &amp; controls; contribute to preparation of audit programs &amp; other procedures to test controls</li><li>Fieldwork - Timely execution, including stakeholder interviews, documentation (e.g. process narratives, walkthroughs, testing, etc.). Assess the design &amp; operating effectiveness of IT controls</li><li>Findings &amp; Issues - Identify &amp; document potential findings &amp; issues</li><li>Reporting - Participate in drafting audit report, including overall risk evaluation of control environment, detailed audit findings &amp; issues</li><li>Coordination &amp; Collaboration - Coordinate &amp; communicate effectively with audit &amp; client contacts to deliver work in a professional &amp; timely manner</li><li>Technology Risk &amp; Control Framework - Leverage technology &amp; financial industry regulations &amp; guidance to assess risks &amp; control design &amp; operating effectiveness</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><em>** Please note: US Citizenship or a Green Card is required</em></strong></p>
