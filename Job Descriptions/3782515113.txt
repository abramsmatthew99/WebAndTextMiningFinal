<strong>About Signos<br/><br/></strong>Signos is on a mission to tackle the obesity epidemic via healthy habits, metabolic signals, and AI. Using a continuous glucose monitor, Signos powers data-driven weight loss by translating an individual’s metabolic signals into timely recommendations for eating, exercise, and better health. With over 75% of the American population overweight or obese, Signos provides a new way for people to act on their body’s unique response to what they eat. By making micro changes to people’s everyday routines, we can improve health and kickstart weight loss. The executive team is led by two successful repeat entrepreneurs, an excellent technical leader, a data scientist who wrote an NYT bestseller on consumer behavior, and a Stanford Medical School professor, and they’re just getting started.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong>As a member of the Mobile team, you will be responsible for designing, building, and deploying the features and underlying systems that power our core answer engine.
