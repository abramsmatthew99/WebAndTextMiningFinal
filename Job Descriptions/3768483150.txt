This is an end-to-end business intelligence solution that involves highly complex data transformations flowing into the LD Whitebox-powered data layer, and the LD Unify reporting application.<br/><br/>You will interface with the client to understand requirements and translate the requirements into technical design. You will then manage and co-develop complex, fully automated data ingestion processes that meet the client's needs. Databricks experience a plus</li><li>1+ years Amazon S3 experience</li><li>Experience working with third-party job schedulers (Control-M, Airflow a plus)</li><li>Eagerness to learn and adapt to evolving Hadoop tooling advancements</li><li>Strong track record in kick-starting, executing, and advancing client-focused projects</li><li>Experience leading offshore development teams</li><li>Strong communication and leadership skills required</li><li>25% Travel required after COVID restrictions are lifted<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Circana Behaviors<br/><br/></strong>As well as the technical skills, experience and attributes that are required for the role, our shared behaviors sit at the core of our organization.
