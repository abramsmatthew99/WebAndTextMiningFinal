Additionally, the candidate should be well-versed in standard IT governance models and have hands-on experience in implementing and managing these processes using tools such as ServiceNow, Jira and related third-party software. General Control framework</li><li>Microsoft Office Suite (Outlook, Word, Excel, Power Point, Visio).</li><li>Demonstrate excellent verbal/presentation and written communication skills</li><li>Possess
