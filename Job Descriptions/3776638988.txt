<p><strong>Role : Network Administrator</strong></p><p><strong>Location : Hybrid</strong></p><p><br/></p><ul><li>Responsible for the installation, customization, security compliance, maintenance and monitoring of the local, wide area and Cloud Based network equipment, support, and monitoring. </li><li>This position performs at a moderate level of complexity with a high level of proficiency under general supervision. </li><li>This position is primarily focused on Cisco routers and switches, Check Point Firewalls, however other vendor network equipment and technologies are used to support and enhance the network environment. </li><li>This role requires a strong foundation and knowledge in the Cisco and Check Point portfolio is required. This position performs at a moderate level of complexity with a high level of proficiency under general supervision.</li><li>Assist in the planning, installation, customization and upgrades of the local and wide area network hardware and software to ensure optimal deployment of resources.</li><li>Help to secure networks by developing network access, monitoring and control while maintaining documentation.</li><li>Assist in the correction of network problems as reported or discovered in the local and wide area networks.</li><li>Assist in the installation, customization, and software upgrades of the F5 Load Balancers.</li><li>Assist in the administration of Check Point Firewall configurations and troubleshooting.</li><li>Maintain the latest security patches and remediate vulnerabilities according to ESO guidelines.</li><li>Adhere to the ESO ATO program guidelines and maintain network uptime.</li><li>Consistently evaluate the infrastructure and applications to determine upgrade pathways and implementation.</li><li>Conduct research on the latest software/technology and make necessary upgrade recommendations to management.</li><li>Maintain Data Center Network and Security Infrastructure.</li><li>Manage, configure, and monitor highly complex network configurations/changes, upgrades, and security tasks as they relate to the network infrastructure. </li><li>Collaboration with other (IT) personnel within the department units, functional users and vendors during project assigned tasks and upgrades.</li><li>Help to scope, plan and prioritizes multiple projects.</li><li>Participate in the annual planning, installation, and customization of the Disaster Recovery Network during testing or actual recovery.</li><li>Must be able to have a regular and predictable on-site attendance for a highly interactive role.</li><li>Remain current with industry standards and best practices as they apply to the infrastructure.</li><li> May sit on/or participate in industry specific user groups/or committees. </li><li> Attend vendor specific conferences and forums to be current and career social with peers across the industry.</li><li>Comply with the Agency’s enterprise security and privacy policies and departmental procedures.</li><li>Other duties as assigned.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Bachelor’s degree in computer science or equivalent experience and four plus years of experience in a network environment on an enterprise scale </strong>or any equivalent combination of experience, training, and/or certification(s).</p><p><br/></p><ul><li>Pass Federal Security Clearance Requirement 5C or 6C access.</li><li>Hold and maintain an Industry Standard, Agency applicable certification in the Networking Field.</li><li>Advanced knowledge of all levels of Cisco platforms and protocols such as ACI, ISE, SD WAN and DNS concepts.</li><li>Experience working in a multi-platform environment.</li><li>Working knowledge of Infoblox architecture and configurations.</li><li>Working Knowledge of Check Point architecture administration and configuration.</li><li>Knowledge of the Cisco Product Portfolio (ACI, ISE, Stealthwatch, SD-WAN, Umbrella and Cisco Duo).</li><li>Ability to troubleshoot network issues and provide solution(s).</li><li>Proven skills in utilizing common functions such as: Cloud Services and connection technologies, Office 365</li><li>Demonstrated ability to learn and support new network hardware, software and monitoring tools and apply innovative business solutions.</li><li>Proficient ability to read computer vendor specifications and technical product manuals for implementation, customization and/or administrative purposes.</li><li>Demonstrated ability to plan, organize and work on multiple tasks simultaneously.</li><li>Excellent written, communication and collaboration skills.</li><li>This position requires being available 24x7x365 via phone and responding to calls within the agreed upon timeframe set within standards and having internet connectivity from home using a supplied laptop and VPN connection.</li><li>Flexibility for occasional off-site travel required.</li><li>Performs work required for this position in an office environment.</li><li>Remain sedentary for significant periods of time.</li><li>Ability to manage multiple assignments/tasks while meeting tight timelines.</li><li>Highly developed problem-solving skills and the ability to focus attention on details.</li><li>Ability to work accurately, efficiently and concentrate for long periods of time in a detailed environment.</li><li>Ability to promote and support a consistent, professional, customer focus.</li><li>Demonstrated ability to work effectively within a team.</li><li>Demonstrated analytical, critical thinking, and organizational skills.</li></ul><p></p>
