You will facilitate functional, qualitative, and performance testing efforts while using a mix of manual and technical testing skills to execute a variety of testing techniques across your product to analyze and report on quality. You are expected to work in a highly autonomous environment embedded on a specific team or product while working closely with designers, engineers, and production staff, using a combination of quality defects</li><li>Previous experience working with data and/or analytics preferred but not required</li><li>Strong communication skills and ability to confidently collaborate with senior members on
