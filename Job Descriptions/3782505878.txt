Analyst</strong>.<br/><br/>This Senior Analyst role requires a mix of analytical thinking, reporting, technical depth, project management, user-centric design, cross-team collaboration, internal, and external customer engagement.<br/><br/><strong>Reporting &amp; Analytics<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>The Senior Analyst will own the quarterly COGS (Cost of Goods Sold) audit process (planning, consolidation, agreements review, data validation &amp; reporting, execution, support, closure &amp; SOX compliance deliverables).</li><li>Analyze business processes &amp; reports to identify gaps that can improve productivity.</li><li>Create &amp; support existing reports that enable management teams to make strategic decisions to meet business goals.</li><li>Support existing tools &amp; develop automated reporting tools for more efficient use of data.</li><li>Analyze current customers (internal or external) performance and identify trends.</li><li>Use reporting tools (Power of BI, Tableau, Smartsheet, etc.) to bring together key performance indicators, charts, and trends.</li><li>Support existing reporting tools for our Team.</li><li>Perform other related reporting duties as assigned.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Business Process &amp; Project Management<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>The Senior Analyst will own their assigned projects in all aspects (planning, scope, scheduling, execution, deliverables, support, closure).</li><li>Establish project plans by determining project objectives, technology, systems, and information specifications, schedules, and staffing.</li><li>Develop and implement process solutions to improve operational efficiency, quality, and simplification.</li><li>Coordinate with different departments and organize cross-functional teams to know the requirements for the improvement of the process.</li><li>Support operations by resolving technology, systems, and information issues.</li><li>Track and communicate status of business initiative projects to management.</li><li>Design, develop and maintain business process documentations that will be used as reference for preparing test cases, training manuals, process outlines, flowcharts, and implementation procedures.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Minimum Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Degree or equivalent, ideally with a BS/MS in Business, Computer Science, Engineering, or a related subject.</li><li>Typically requires 4+ years of relevant experience.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Critical Skills<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>4+ years of experience leading, working &amp;/or supporting process improvements &amp;/or development teams.</li><li>4+ years of experience leading projects is highly preferred.</li><li>Power BI, Tableau, Snowflake, SQL and Smartsheet experience are a significant plus.</li><li>Experience with tools such as Alteryx, Informatica, Python, or Power Query are also a huge plus.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Additional Skills<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Strong project management, time management, and organization skills are a must.</li><li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li><li>Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail.</li><li>Self-starter, motivated with an entrepreneurial mindset.</li><li>A positive and collaborative attitude.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Working Conditions<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Hybrid work environment – Irving, TX.</li><li>Must be authorized to work in the US unrestricted – This position is not eligible for
