<strong>Job Summary<br/><br/></strong>As a member of the Service Operations Center team, the Data Center Analyst position plays a critical role in ensuring efficient functioning of all aspects of data center operations, including installation and management of supporting data center infrastructure such as server racks, power distribution, units, and power busway taps. Your role will involve installation of servers, storage, and cabling infrastructure to support network communication. The data center analyst role works collaboratively with Network Engineering, and Systems-VMWare and Storage teams to install, monitor, analyze, and troubleshoot data center systems to ensure optimal performance, availability and security. Additionally, the data center analyst role works with Facilities Management department to ensure data center power and cooling systems are optimized for operations.<br/><br/><strong>Duties &amp; Work Performed<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Install, configure, and maintain computer systems and network equipment in the data center facility.</li><li>Monitor and troubleshoot data center infrastructure, including servers, storage devices, network switches, and power distribution units.</li><li>Conduct routine inspections to ensure proper functioning of cooling, power, and environmental systems.</li><li>Implement and enforce data center security measures, including access controls, surveillance systems, and monitoring tools.</li><li>Maintain accurate inventory records of all hardware, software, and network components.</li><li>Develop and maintain data center floor layout diagrams.</li><li>Installation, operation, and maintenance of Data Center Infrastructure Management (DCIM) tools (e.g. OpenDCIM, Environet) </li><li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams, such as network engineers and system administrators, and Facilities Management Department to resolve technical issues, electrical/mechanical infrastructure issues and ensure high availability of services.</li><li>Assist in the development and implementation of data center policies, procedures, and best practices.</li><li>Stay updated with the latest advancements in data center technologies and industry standards.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Soft Skills<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Team orientation: demonstrated ability to collaborate with others to solve problems.</li><li>Able to effectively process, prioritize and respond to multiple data points (e.g., alerts, incidents, issues) simultaneously and to maintain focus on overarching issue status and outcomes in a high-pressured environment.</li><li>Exceptional multi-tasking capabilities</li><li>Exceptional judgment: ability to make appropriate decisions in a time-pressured situation.</li><li>Excellent analytical skills and solutions-oriented approach</li><li>Demonstrated strong verbal and written communication skills.</li><li>Self-motivated and goal-oriented</li><li>Ability to work under minimal supervision.</li><li>Ability to interact professionally under pressure with a wide range of customers ranging from end-users to IT staff to management to external service providers.</li><li>Proven ability to dynamically prioritize work efforts to meet deadlines and work effectively with minimal supervision in a team environment. </li><li>Flexibility to work on a rotating shift schedule, including nights, weekends, and holidays.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><strong>EDUCATION:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in computer science, information technology, or a related field, or equivalent work experience.</li><li>Relevant certifications, such as CompTIA Server+, CCNA Data Center, Certified Data Center Professional (CCDP), ITIL Foundation are highly desirable.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Experience &amp; Skills<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>In-depth knowledge of data center operations, infrastructure components, and networking principles.</li><li>Proficiency in server and networking hardware installation, configuration, and troubleshooting.</li><li>Familiarity with industry-standard data center tools and equipment.</li><li>Strong understanding of cooling, power, and environmental systems in a data center environment.</li><li>Must be able to lift at least 40 pounds and perform work requiring extended walking and bending. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Working Conditions<br/><br/></strong>Includes office, data center and public work environment. <strong>Work hours include second, third and weekend
