<strong>Job Summary<br/><br/></strong>The Vice President of Information Technology will oversee the planning, development, implementation, maintenance, and security of the company’s information systems and processes including computer, communication, and office systems.<br/><br/><strong>Supervisory Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Recruits, interviews, hires, and trains staff in the department.</li><li> Oversees the schedules and responsibilities of staff in the department.</li><li> Provides constructive and timely performance evaluations.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Duties/Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Establishes the strategic direction of the company’s information systems (IS); executes this strategy in collaboration with company leadership.</li><li> Directs and coordinates professional services such as systems analysis and design, feasibility studies, and programming of information, communication, and office automation systems.</li><li> Identifies and recommends new technologies and services that will improve efficiency, processes, and operations; reviews requests for additional services and upgrades, assessing the costs and benefits to the organization.</li><li> Approves the purchase, rent, lease, license, or other acquisition of hardware, software, or services needed to meet company needs.</li><li> Monitors and reports progress on development projects, resource utilization, and production performance to leadership team.</li><li> Ensures the integrity and reliability of computerized information systems, communication systems, and office systems including the security of data acquired and stored.</li><li> Facilitates preparation of project plans and proposals, outlining project objectives, IT requirements, and analytical and programming staffing requirements for proposed systems.</li><li> Maintains pace with technological progress, economic change, and company needs by directing continual improvements of IT staff, equipment, and procedures.</li><li> Prepares, implements, and executes IT budget.</li><li> Performs other duties as assigned.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Required Skills/Abilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Excellent verbal and written communication skills.</li><li> Excellent organizational skills and attention to detail.</li><li> Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.</li><li> Strong supervisory and leadership skills.</li><li> Thorough understanding of IT policies and procedures.</li><li> Thorough understanding of or the ability to quickly master technology used in the organization.</li><li> Proficient with Microsoft Office Suite or related software.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Education And Experience<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Bachelor’s degree in computer science required; Master’s degree or MBA preferred, Operations Research, or Computer Science, or equivalent training and experience required.</li><li> At least ten years of experience in a technical role required, with significant leadership experience preferred.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Physical Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Prolonged periods of sitting at a desk and working on a computer.</li><li> Must be able to lift up to 15 pounds at times.</li></ul>
