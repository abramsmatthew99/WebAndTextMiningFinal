<p><strong>About Geocomp</strong></p><p><br/></p><p>Geocomp provides products and services to help clients manage risk associated with the subsurface elements of infrastructure. We specialize in the engineering and control of risk for construction below the ground surface for all types of structures. We excel on tough projects with challenging subsurface conditions and requirements.</p><p><br/></p><p>Geocomp’s Products Division manufactures highly versatile Automated Geotechnical Testing Systems and data acquisition systems that have been successfully used for more than forty years for production, teaching, and research around the world. Geocomp’s strong reputation for solving our clients' complex engineering problems and providing world-class testing products gives employees many opportunities for professional development and individual growth in real-world applications.</p><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Position Overview:</strong></p><p><br/></p><p>We are seeking a full-time Embedded Software Engineer in for Geocomp’s Products Division to join our team of highly skilled engineers in our Acton, MA headquarters. You will help our team support and enhance our existing product line of automated products that test soil and rock by upgrading and expanding on the system software components that interact with the user interfaces and the embedded controllers. This is a fast-paced product and project development environment. The ideal candidate will jump in with hands-on testing and debugging experience to enhance our existing products and develop the next generation. The successful person will be results-oriented, customer-focused, and a team player helping us meet challenging project goals.</p><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Job Expectations:</strong></p><p><br/></p><ul><li>Design, program, and document software for the functional components of our Lab Systems.</li><li>Ensure designs implement proper technologies and techniques</li><li>Assist with technical documentation</li><li>Work with software development teams, interdisciplinary engineers, IT, and other groups.</li><li>Perform bench and lab testing and qualifications of software.</li><li>Coordinate testing of software systems with the end-user and other project team members.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Required Qualifications:</strong></p><p><br/></p><ul><li>BS degree in electrical engineering, computer engineering, or computer science.</li><li>Proficient skills in C and C++ in an embedded ARM 32-bit environment</li><li>Experience with Real Time Operating Systems.</li><li>Strong system control theory experience.</li><li>Embedded network programming.</li><li>Sensor interfacing.</li><li>Working knowledge interfacing with embedded systems with desktop systems.</li><li>MATLAB experience a plus</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong><em>Geocompis an Equal Employment Opportunity Employer</em></strong><em>.
