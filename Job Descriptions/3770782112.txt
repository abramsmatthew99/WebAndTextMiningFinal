<strong><strong>Company Description<br/><br/></strong></strong><strong>ITW (Illinois Tool Works Inc.)</strong> is a Fortune 200 company and is one of the world’s leading diversified manufacturers of specialized industrial equipment, automotive components, consumables, and related service businesses. This role delivers technical assistance, problem solving, proactive monitoring, and quick responses to network infrastructure services, including the installation, setup, scripting, troubleshooting, and maintenance of network hardware and software. Candidates need to be able to break down challenges, think critically, and have strong attention to detail. In addition, qualified candidates will be able to easily communicate with technical and non-technical team members and have excellent organizational and time management skills. Disaster recovery system periodic testing for multiple location.</li><li>Operational knowledge in wireless / mobile communications management Experience with MDM is plus.</li><li>3+ years of Active Directory and GPO experience.</li><li>Off hours’ work may be required</li><li>Able to travel locally between division facilities (Bartlett, Elgin, Glenview, Lake Forest, and Libertyville, IL).</li><li>Up to 10% travel to Arkansas manufacturing facility. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong><strong>Additional Information<br/><br/></strong></strong><strong>Work Environment:<br/><br/></strong><em>The work environment characteristics described here are representative of those an employee encounters while performing the essential functions of this job.
