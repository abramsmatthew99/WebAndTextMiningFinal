<p><strong>Mission Systems Mission Analyst</strong></p><p><strong>Longmont, CO (Hybrid)</strong></p><p><strong>$80K - $130K + Benefits</strong></p><p><br/></p><p>You will have the chance to work with a company that is a leader in unlocking the promise of space intelligence and collaborates with governments and businesses to address challenges on Earth and beyond. With a rich history supporting space missions and partnerships, you will be working for a company that is a frontrunner in monitoring global change, broadband communications, and advancing space operations. They are currently seeking a Mission Systems Mission Analyst to join their data services team.</p><p><br/></p><p>You will be working as a Mission Analyst as an individual contributor within their mission planning and performance analysis team. As a Mission Analyst, you will play a pivotal role in developing analytics and models to provide insight into the outcomes of mission planning. The position involves significant collaboration with groups across Our Client, focusing on the development of capacity models, analysis of image scheduling functionality, tasking load efficiency, and the development of algorithms for mission planning workflows. </p><p><br/></p><p>Key Responsibilities:</p><p><br/></p><ul><li>Develop capacity models in support of current and predicted loads.</li><li>Analyze and tune image scheduling functionality.</li><li>Analyze tasking load and efficiency, including cloud cover and yield.</li><li>Develop algorithms for mission planning workflow.</li><li>Analyze and tune short-term and long-term strategic collection feasibility.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>Minimum Qualifications:</p><p><br/></p><ul><li>Must be a U.S. Citizen and willing and able to apply for and hold a Secret National Security Clearance.</li><li>BS in Aerospace Engineering, Mathematics, Physics, Computer Science, or related discipline.</li><li>Experience with a technology or scientific company.</li><li>Experience in scientific or engineering algorithm development.</li><li>Experience in Unix/Linux and Windows OS environments.</li><li>Strong verbal, written, and organizational skills.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>Preferred Qualifications:</p><p><br/></p><ul><li>Experience with AI/ML data science techniques.</li><li>Demonstrated ability to effectively analyze and model complex processes.</li><li>Experience with statistical analysis and data visualization (e.g., MATLAB).</li><li>Knowledge of GIS tools (e.g., ArcGIS).</li><li>Knowledge of satellite operations and planning systems.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>If you want to join a business that is pushing the boundaries of space intelligence and you want to make a significant impact on their missions, please apply now!</p>
