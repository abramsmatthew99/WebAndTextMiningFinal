Understand and verify data generated in IT business tools</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Qualifications:</strong></p><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in information technology, computer science, or a related field</li><li>5+ years of experience in IT project management; PMP certification preferred</li><li>Experience with a variety of IT project management methodologies</li><li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills</li><li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills</li><li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team</li><li>Ability to work under pressure and meet deadlines</li><li>SAP experience (preferred)</li><li>Experience with Microsoft Power Platform</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>About 3A Composites:</u></strong></p><p>3A Composites is a leading player in segments such as composite panels for high-quality facades and display applications.
