<p>Lawrence Harvey have partnered with a company who specialize in robotics solutions for warehouse automation. We are seeking a Senior Robotics Software Engineer who has good experience with rust. </p><p><br/></p><p>In this role, you will play a key part in enhancing our robot control stack, collaborating closely with cutting-edge perception and adaptive decision-making algorithms. Our AI software empowers industrial robots with the autonomy required for a diverse range of tasks, seamlessly integrating with our clients' intricate software environments.</p><p><br/></p><p>You will take the lead in motion planning and robot control initiatives. Your responsibilities include optimizing our software architecture and code to ensure optimal performance for algorithms and systems. Collaboration is essential, as you'll work with various engineering teams, spanning Mechatronics, Machine Learning, and Infrastructure.</p><p><br/></p><p>Responsibilities:</p><ul><li>Lead the development of path planning and/or robot control systems for industrial robotic manipulators.</li><li>Architect, design, and implement software interfaces with industrial robots for optimal real-time performance.</li><li>Lead discussions on software design and conduct code reviews.</li><li>Collaborate with robotics and software engineers to design, implement, and test robotic algorithms, covering hardware integrations, kinematics, dynamics, and trajectory planning.</li><li>Develop robust, production-ready code for imminent deployment.</li><li>Innovate advanced controllers and control algorithms for robotic arms to enhance system performance, robustness, and versatility.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>Requirements:</p><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Robotics, or a related field.</li><li>5+ years of experience in one or more robot control/motion planning areas, such as path planning, trajectory optimization, inverse kinematics, or robotic software integration.</li><li>Proficiency in Rust, C++, and Python development, including API design and architecture.</li><li>Proven track record of designing, building, and maintaining complex, real-time, multi-process software systems.</li><li>In-depth knowledge of robotic system design, integration, debugging, verification, and testing, especially for industrial robotic manipulators.</li><li>Experience in interfacing with low-level robotic hardware controllers.</li></ul><p></p>
