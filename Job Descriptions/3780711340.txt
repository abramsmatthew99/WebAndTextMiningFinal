<p>Our client, a higher education institution, is seeking a Senior Network Engineer to join their team!</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Position Duties</strong></p><ul><li>Configure, test, install, and maintain LAN/WAN equipment and related services.</li><li>Identify, diagnose, and resolve data network issues and anomalies in a timely manner.</li><li>Create and maintain comprehensive documentation for all implemented networks and associated hardware.</li><li>Timely review and response to network issues reported to the University Helpdesk and Network Manager.</li><li>Collaborate with the Manager to develop a personal education and skills plan designed to improve and expand technical skills continually.</li><li>Daily monitoring of network devices/appliances and log review to ensure optimum network performance, network stability, and security.</li><li>Researching and implementing necessary upgrades and firmware updates.</li><li>Configure, maintain, and regularly review firewall access lists, rules, and campus network security.</li><li>Participation in on-call rotation to cover 24×7×365 network support coverage, as well as occasional weekend and off-hours work when projects and maintenance work demand.</li><li>Work on major project initiatives.</li><li>Research and evaluate new technology hardware and software.</li><li>Performs other such duties as may be assigned from time to time.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Position Qualifications</strong></p><ul><li>B.S. degree in Computer Science or Information Systems required; Master’s degree in IT discipline preferred.</li><li>Cisco networking experience is required. At least one of the following certifications is preferred: Certified Wireless Network Administrator (CWNA), Cisco Certified Design Associate (CCDA), Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA), Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA).</li><li>Cisco ACI data center fabric experience.</li><li>Cisco Firepower NW Firewall experience.</li><li>Knowledge of Cisco Call Center and telephony / VOIP.</li><li>At least three years of recent experience in day-to-day configuration, operation, and troubleshooting of a complex Cisco enterprise data network.</li><li>Solid experience in network and infrastructure design.</li><li>Proven operations experience with complex routed IP networks, layer two/three switches, routers, firewalls, LAN/WAN networking hardware, and protocols.</li><li>Current hands-on experience in the configuration, management, and operation of Cisco appliances, including Firepower, ACI, Catalyst and Nexus switches, router, and core devices.</li><li>Knowledge of TLS, Wireless, and other LAN/WAN networks.</li><li>Working experience with networking technologies and protocols including but not limited to TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, OSPF, BGP, SNMP, RIP, 802.1x, 802.1q.</li><li>Team player with proven experience providing exceptional customer service.</li><li>Effective communication skills.</li><li>Ability to prioritize in a sometimes rapidly changing and time-critical environment.</li><li>Experience in IT within Higher Ed is highly preferred.</li><li>**car highly preferred**</li></ul>
