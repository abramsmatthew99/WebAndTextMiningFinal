<strong>Job Title:</strong> E.H.R. System Support Specialist (<em>Electronic Health Record System Support Specialist</em>)<br/><br/><strong>Department:</strong> Informatics<br/><br/><strong>Nature of Position:</strong> The <strong>E.H.R. System Support Specialist</strong> is responsible for Tier 1 support of the electronic health record platform utilized by “The Clinic” end users. This position will work as a co-liaison with the Sr. E.H.R. System Administrator in performing platform orientation, troubleshooting errors and issues as needed, direct staff workstation support, manage and resolve support inquiries with the E.H.R. vendor, as well as communicating and reporting needs, issues and progress to the Director of Informatics.<br/><br/><strong>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS: <br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Experience in a clinical field for a minimum of 2 years. </li><li>Experience with electronic health records or database records system (eClinicalWorks or similar E.H.R. preferred). </li><li>Experience with general desktop support and troubleshooting techniques in windows workstation environment. </li><li>Knowledge of public health principles and practice; primarily health data privacy and security standards.</li><li>Excellent interpersonal skills and written communication skills</li><li>Must be able to sit, stand, walk, bend and/or lift for extended periods of time</li><li>Customer Service experience<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>CLASSIFICATION:</strong> Exempt<br/><br/><strong>Supervision Received:<br/><br/></strong><strong>Instruction Received</strong>: Director of Him, E.H.R. System Administrator<br/><br/><strong>Supervision Exercised: N/A<br/><br/></strong><strong>Responsibilities: <br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Tier 1 level support for the Electronic Health Record Platform for ALL sites of The Clinic (COFMC)</li><li>Travel as needed to meet the needs of all COFMC sites. </li><li>Remote Desktop Support and platform management.</li><li>Support Information technology as it relates to the E.H.R. platform; which includes desktop support and trouble- shooting issues with workstation functionality of the E.H.R. on staff laptops, desktops and tablets, as well as peripherals devices attached to the E.H.R. Platform (e.g. EKG, Scanners). </li><li>Facility ticketing directly with E.H.R. vendor to obtain issue fixes as the need arises. </li><li>Basic orientation to new staff in individual job needs as it relates to the E.H.R. </li><li>Work with all Informatics/I.T. staff to gain and maintain an in-depth understanding of the E.H.R., both current and subsequent versions. </li><li>Work with the E.H.R. Systems Administrator in configuring and maintaining the E.H.R. platform under the supervision of the Director of Informatics. </li><li>Serve on Committees as arise.</li><li>Participate in short- and long-term program planning for the Informatics Department, including development of goals and objectives. </li><li>Assumes co-responsibility/accountability with the E.H.R. System Administrator for managing use of the COFMC E.H.R. platform under the supervision of the E.H.R. Systems Administrator and the Chief Information Officer. </li><li>Serve as a resource and positive, proactive member of the Informatics department. </li><li>All other duties as assigned by the Director of Informatics. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>ORGANIZATION</strong>:<br/><br/><ul><li>Abstain from gossip in the workplace.</li><li>Exhibits enthusiasm, a positive attitude and professionalism in the workplace.</li><li>Handles personal conflict and works through it at the lowest possible level (first with party/parties involved, then in necessary, with 3rd party mediation.)</li><li>Is friendly and easy to approach</li><li>Is neat, clean, and well-groomed and dresses in accordance to the dress code policy.</li><li>Leaves personal problems out of workplace.</li><li>Treats ALL co-workers with dignity and respect.</li><li>Arrives on time and works through designated hours.</li><li>Completes tasks effectively, accurately and on time.</li><li>Demonstrates little to no absenteeism/follows leave guidelines.</li><li>Follows designated chain of authority.</li><li>Is flexible, able and willing to respond to unusual job-related demands.</li><li>Manages times well.</li><li>Recognizes and assists others in the work of the department, even though the work may be outside of regular assigned duties.</li><li>Shares equally in the workload of the department.</li><li>Works cooperatively with all team members and departments.</li><li>Works independently requesting assistance from supervisor only as needed.</li><li>Abstains from criticizing COFMC, colleagues and supervisors.</li><li>Demonstrates loyalty to COFMC, colleagues and supervisors.</li><li>Demonstrates support of the mission and vision of COFMC.</li><li>Accepts and participates in cross training.</li><li>Takes initiative to identify skill development.<br/><br/></li></ul>Powered by JazzHR<br/><br/>yVg4nYFjpm
