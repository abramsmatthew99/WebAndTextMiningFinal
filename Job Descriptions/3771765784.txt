<strong>Job Description<br/><br/></strong>You are an experienced data professional, who is able to work with minimal oversight, manage multiple tasks and projects simultaneously, and take ownership of deliverables end-to-end. Critical to the success of this role is your ability to work with large volumes of data, bring simplicity to complex situations, develop insightful analyses that tell a story, work effectively as part of a cross-functional team, think strategically, and communicate findings in a clear and compelling way .<br/><br/>This role can be based out of our New York, NY or Inglewood, CA offices and the NFL has fully returned to office, onsite 100%.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Lead a team of BI Developers.</li><li>Design, build and maintain data models used for reporting and analytics.</li><li>Create dashboards and KPIs to show business performance.</li><li>Build data visualizations that tell the story of trends, patterns, and outliers.</li><li>Monitor and tune BI tools to ensure optimum level of performance.</li><li>Drive business change through foundational data insights across the League and Clubs</li><li>Invent new ways to analyze data to identify trends and gaps to drive improvements in a variety of business areas.</li><li>Interface with cross-functional teams to build centralized tools that will allow for rapid anomaly detection.</li><li>Partner with data engineers and data analysts to deliver foundational insights at scale that meet data visualization best practices.</li><li>Analyze and synthesize large data sets across multiple inputs.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Required Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science, statistics, or related discipline. </li><li>7+ years’ experience as a data/business analyst or business intelligence developer/analytics engineer.</li><li>3+ years’ experience leading a team of data/business analysts or business intelligence developer/analytics engineers.</li><li>Proficiency with data querying and data modeling using advanced SQL and/or Python.</li><li>Advanced proficiency in modern data visualization tools such as Tableau, PowerBI, Qlik Sense and/or Looker.</li><li>Proficiency in analytic data pipeline tools such as Python, Airflow, DBT, Talend, etc. </li><li>Experience working in an Agile development environment.</li><li>Experience and ability to effectively gather information from multiple sources.</li><li>Experience in automated self-service reporting/dashboarding tools.</li><li>Understands and can deliver upon data visualization best practices.</li><li>Confidence and proven track record in effectively dealing with technical and non-technical stakeholders.</li><li>Ability to operate independently and successfully in a fast-paced environment.</li><li>Be comfortable with ambiguity and curious to learn new skills.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Preferred Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Master’s degree in computer science, statistics, or related discipline.</li><li>10+ years of overall technical experience focusing on data/business analyst or business intelligence developer/analytics engineer roles.</li><li>Advanced proficiency and/or certification in PowerBI.</li><li>Comfortable making decisions and leading.</li><li>Familiar with version control and relational databases.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Other Key Attributes / Characteristics<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Superior communication skills both oral and written.</li><li>Positive contributor, strong team member, loves to work with and empower others.</li><li>Collaborates with a team.</li><li>Time management skills.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Physical
