<strong><strong><strong><strong>Job Description Summary</strong></strong></strong> <br/><br/></strong>As a Software Engineer, you’ll work on the cutting edge of cloud technology as you scope and lead complex technical projects, laying the groundwork for early-stage products to iterate and scale. At GE Healthcare, we are committed to bringing cloud-based solutions for our customers: all aspects of computing services across the cloud and edge – including servers, databases, storage, networking, analytics, software, intelligence are delivered over the Internet. Our Science &amp; Technology organization is harnessing the power of technology to make healthcare more precise, more personalized, and more accessible for everyone. From driving the overall clinical research and patient-centric innovation strategy to delivering new digital and machine learning capabilities - we’re committed to leading digital transformation, improving outcomes for patients and providers, and creating a world where healthcare has no limits.
