<p>Seeking a dynamic and experienced Vice President of Product Management and Development to lead our product management and development teams in the fast-paced and innovative world of B2B software development. This leadership role requires a proven track record of successfully managing large teams, a deep understanding of both product management and product development, and specific expertise in enterprise mainframe technologies. If you are a visionary leader who thrives in a dynamic environment, we invite you to join our team and drive our products to new heights.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Responsibilities:</strong></p><ul><li>Develop and communicate a clear product strategy that aligns with the company's overall business goals and objectives. Drive innovation and set a compelling product vision to lead the organization into the future.</li><li>Lead and mentor a team of product managers and developers, fostering a culture of collaboration, innovation, and excellence. Provide guidance, coaching, and professional development to team members.</li><li>Oversee the end-to-end product management process, from defining market needs, conducting competitive analysis, and defining product roadmaps, to launching and optimizing products. Ensure that products meet customer needs and expectations.</li><li>Manage the product development process, from concept to release, ensuring high-quality software solutions that meet industry standards. Coordinate cross-functional teams, including engineering, design, and quality assurance.</li><li>Leverage your expertise in enterprise mainframe technologies to guide the development of products for this specialized market. Stay updated on industry trends, best practices, and emerging technologies.</li><li>Conduct market research to identify trends, customer needs, and opportunities in the B2B software development space. Use data-driven insights to make informed decisions about product development.</li><li>Develop and manage budgets for product management and development, optimizing resource allocation and ensuring cost-effective product delivery.</li><li>Collaborate closely with cross-functional teams, including sales, marketing, and customer support, to ensure a coordinated and customer-centric approach to product development and delivery.</li><li>Identify potential risks and challenges in product development and take proactive steps to mitigate them. Ensure that products are compliant with industry regulations and security standards.</li><li>Implement and maintain best practices for quality assurance and testing processes, ensuring that products are reliable and free from defects.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Qualifications:</strong></p><p><br/></p><ul><li>15+ years directly related work experience.</li><li>Proven experience as a leader in product management and development, with a track record of successfully managing large teams.</li><li>Deep expertise in enterprise technologies and software development within the B2B space.</li><li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills, with the ability to make data-driven decisions.</li><li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to influence and inspire cross-functional teams.</li><li>Strategic thinking and the ability to translate business goals into actionable product plans.</li><li>Strong project management skills and a results-driven approach to achieving product goals.</li><li>Knowledge of agile development methodologies and best practices.</li><li>Experience with compliance and security standards in the enterprise software industry.</li><li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business, or a related field; MBA is a plus.</li></ul><p></p>
