<strong><strong>Position Overview:<br/><br/></strong></strong>The primary responsibility of the Analyst I – Cyber Security role is to monitor cyber security appliances to identify events that require escalated analysis. This entry-level analyst is a member of the cyber security operations team and collaborates with the other team members of the cyber security team in support of a comprehensive cyber security program.<br/><br/>All duties are to be performed in accordance with departmental and The Venetian Resort’s policies, practices, and procedures.<br/><br/><strong><strong>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities:<br/><br/></strong></strong><ul><li> Collaborate closely with SOC team members. </li><li> Monitor the SOC mailbox to identify and analyze incoming emails for potential security threats. </li><li> Escalate alerts needing deeper analysis to Level 2 SOC analysts. </li><li> Gather and report metrics for all property security systems. </li><li> Evaluate, and assist with the maintenance of assigned security systems following industry best practices. </li><li> Participate in the investigation of security incidents and prepare reports, as necessary. </li><li> Stay updated on current vulnerabilities, response strategies, and mitigation techniques essential for supporting cyber security operations. </li><li> Generate regular reports for security solutions on both network and host-based systems. </li><li> Maintain a clean and secure workspace. </li><li> Consistent and punctual attendance is fundamental to this role. </li><li> Execute any additional relevant tasks as delegated. </li><li> Participate in daily status calls with the IT department. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong><strong>Additional Duties &amp; Responsibilities:<br/><br/></strong></strong><strong>Company Standards Of Conduct<br/><br/></strong>Venetian Resort Team Members are expected to conduct themselves in a professional manner at all times, adhere to standard operating procedures, company policies, and regulatory requirements.<br/><br/><strong><strong>Additional Duties &amp; Responsibilities:<br/><br/></strong></strong><strong><strong>Additional Duties &amp; Responsibilities:<br/><br/></strong></strong><strong><strong>Minimum Qualifications:<br/><br/></strong></strong><ul><li>21 years of age.</li><li>Proof of authorization to work in the United States</li><li>Must be able to obtain and maintain Nevada Gaming Control Board registration and any other certification or license, as required by law or policy.</li><li>A degree in cybersecurity, computer science, information technology, or industry recognized certifications are preferred.</li><li>1-2 years of practical experience in working with Information Technology Systems, or equivalent educational background.</li><li>Excellent problem-solving skills, ability to triage and resolve critical technical issues.</li><li>Must be able to collaborate with other teams, vendors, and contractors.</li><li>Experience configuring and installing information security applications.</li><li>Hands-on experience with SOC tools including but not limited to SIEM, IPS, NAC, DLP, and EDR is preferred.</li><li>Fundamental understanding of encryption technologies.</li><li>Understanding of common network traffic, i.e., Transmission Control Protocol/Internet Protocol (TCP/IP), internet traffic, and mail.</li><li>A good understanding of Microsoft operating systems is required, and experience with Linux, MacOS, or mainframe is a bonus.</li><li>Ability to assess network activity and system configuration for anomalous activity to determine system security status.</li><li>Knowledge and understanding of network protocols, network devices, multiple operating systems.</li><li>The ability to assess, prioritize, and differentiate between potential intrusion attempts and false alarms.</li><li>Experience with virtual machine software and how to install and configure a guest operating system.</li><li>Maintain a professional, neat, and well-groomed appearance adhering to Company appearance standards.</li><li>Maintain consistent adherence to The Venetian Resort’s unmatched guest service standards.</li><li>Provide off-hours support on an infrequent, but as needed basis. (Potential shifts may run 24/7 due to the need of the business.)</li><li>Ability to communicate clearly and effectively in English, both in spoken and written form.</li><li>Strong interpersonal skills with the ability to communicate effectively with guests and other Team Members of different backgrounds and levels of experience.</li><li>Must be able to work varied shifts, including nights, weekends, and holidays.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong><strong>Physical Requirements:<br/><br/></strong></strong>Must be able to:<br/><br/><ul><li>Physically access all areas of the property and drive areas with or without a reasonable accommodation.</li><li>Maintain composure under pressure and consistently meet deadlines with internal and external customers and contacts.</li><li>Ability to interact appropriately and effectively with guests, management, other team members, and outside contacts.</li><li>Ability for prolonged periods of time to walk, stand, stretch, bend, and kneel.</li><li>Work in a fast-paced and busy environment.</li><li>Work indoors and be exposed to various environmental factors such as, but not limited to, CRT, noise, dust, and cigarette smoke.</li></ul>
