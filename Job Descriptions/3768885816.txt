<strong>Overview<br/><br/></strong>At Orange County Government, we are proud to serve the public with integrity, honesty, fairness, and professionalism. We develop innovative policies and services that impact Central Florida and shape the future of our community. We have been consistently recognized as a "Top 100 Family Friendly Company," facilitating a healthy work-life balance.<br/><br/>Orange County Utilities provides water resources and solid waste recovery services to protect and enrich the lives of the citizens and guests of Orange County Florida. The Water Division ensures a safe and reliable water supply for all of Orange County Utilities’ customers. The Water Division operates three regional water supply facilities and nine satellite water supply facilities that collectively produce approximately 60 million gallons per day.<br/><br/>Water Division is seeking a highly qualified individual to fill the position of SCADA Technician. This position is responsible for programming, installing, and maintenance of all equipment used for the SCADA (Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition) system. Work requires independent judgment and is performed under the general supervision of the SCADA Administrator. This position requires long periods of computer screen time. Work is reviewed through observations, conferences, reports, and results achieved.<br/><br/>Representative Duties<br/><br/><ul><li>Programs PLC’s (Programmable Logic Controllers).</li><li>Troubleshoots software problems with the SCADA system.</li><li>Programs and troubleshoots HMI (Human-Machine-Interface) software.</li><li>Troubleshoots hardware on the SCADA system.</li><li>Troubleshoots computer software and hardware servers and workstations.</li><li>Configures Network equipment such as Industrial Fiber Network Switches</li><li>Builds and analyses trends for troubleshooting.</li><li>Configures automated reports and other software related to SCADA.</li><li>Assists with purchase orders and work orders.</li><li>Performs other related duties as required.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Minimum Qualifications<br/><br/></strong>Requires an Associate’s Degree from an accredited institution in Computer Science or Electronics Technology or equivalent electronics technical school and four years combination of experience in the following areas: PLC programming, Radio systems, computer usage, instrumentation, electrical or HMI SCADA systems. An equivalent combination of education, training, and experience may be substituted for the education.<br/><br/>Must possess a State of Florida Class (E) Driver’s License<br/><br/>All applicants selected for hire with Orange County must successfully complete a criminal history and a background check.<br/><br/>Preferences<br/><br/><ul><li>Knowledge of SCADA systems including Fiber optics; PLC’s; instrumentation loops, VBA, C+ or similar computer programming language, TCP/IP, MODBUS; and a computerized maintenance management system/MAXIMO<br/><br/><br/></li></ul>Notes<br/><br/>Working hours are 8 hour shifts Monday – Friday.<br/><br/>May include additional hours during emergencies.<br/><br/><ul><li> Please complete each section of the application in detail as only information provided by the applicant regarding their work experience and education on the application will be used to determine the applicant's qualifications for the position.</li><li> Applications that show no employment history, no employment dates, leave number of hours worked per week blank, show duties left blank, or state "see attached resume" will be disqualified, considered incomplete, and will not be reviewed.</li><li> Applicants selected for hire in Orange County must successfully complete a background check, physical and drug screening prior to start of employment.</li><li> Please read the entire posting and print a copy for your future reference, as you may wish to refer back to it during the recruitment process.</li><li> Please ensure that the phone number and email address on your application is accurate, and that it is an email account that you check on a daily basis as we will be using this to contact you for various steps in the hiring process. Should we need to communicate with you, we will only use the email listed on the application. Please check your spam/junk email folders as well your inbox.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul>Application Deadline<br/><br/>Open: 11-17-2023 / Close: 12-8-2023 *Note: Subject to close prior to deadline without notice.<br/><br/>Salary<br/><br/>$27.37/hr to $42.11/hr. Placement based on qualifications.<br/><br/>Please note that internal County employees are not eligible for the sign-on incentive.<br/><br/>For more information see: http://www.ocfl.net/careers/#incentives<br/><br/>Address<br/><br/>Orange County Utilities Laboratory<br/><br/>9124 Curry Ford Road<br/><br/>Orlando, FL 32825<br/><br/>Veterans Preference<br/><br/>If you are claiming Veterans' Preference, you are responsible for providing the required eligibility documentation by the close date of the posting. Please submit a cover letter and copies of documents, not originals, to Orange County Human Resources Division, PO Box 1393, Orlando, FL 32801-1393 or fax to (407) 836-0098 (only Veterans' Preference documentation will be accepted by fax). Your cover letter should include the position posting number you are applying for, the title of the position and the last 4 digits of your social security number.<br/><br/>Educational Requirements<br/><br/>If your education was obtained in the United States, the County recognizes degrees or diplomas which are accredited by an agency recognized by the United States Department of Education (USDE) or the Council for Higher Education Accreditation (CHEA).
