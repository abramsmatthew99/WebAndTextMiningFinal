<p><strong>Augustana College - Full-time Visiting Faculty in Computer Science (February 2024 Start):</strong></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>About Augustana College: </strong></p><p>Augustana College is a selective liberal arts and sciences college of 2,400 students, including 18 percent first generation college students, 22 percent Pell Grant students, 23 percent students of color, and 13 percent international students. Our beautifully wooded and largely residential campus is located in Rock Island, IL, a diverse Illinois-Iowa Quad Cities metropolitan area along the Mississippi River with 400,000 residents about three hours west of Chicago. Strengthening our diverse and inclusive community is central to the college's mission and strategic plan. We seek applicants with an interest in fostering rigorous, inclusive, and high-impact learning environments for a diverse student body. Details about Augustana College, our expectations of the faculty, the selection process, and the Quad Cities all are available at Teaching at Augustana. </p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Job Description:</strong></p><p>Augustana College invites applications for a 1.5 year Visiting Faculty position in Computer Science beginning in the Spring semester (February - May) of the 2023-2024 academic year and continuing through the full 2024-2025 academic year. This position carries a 24-26 credits teaching load in a 10-month academic year (standard courses are 4 credits), with 12 credits of teaching in the spring semester of the 2023-24 academic year. Applicants should be interested in teaching a variety of lower and upper level undergraduate computer science courses across the department's curriculum. <strong>Preference will be given to applicants with Machine Learning experience.</strong> The successful candidate will join a close-knit community of teacher-scholars who work collaboratively to educate the next generation of computer scientists.</p><p><br/></p><p>We require no scholarly research in this position. Visiting professors are welcome to be involved in the life of the department and college beyond their teaching and service. All faculty at Augustana are invited to engage in professional development and mentoring opportunities consistent with their needs throughout their appointment at the college.</p><p><br/></p><p>Review of applications will begin December 15 and continue until the position is filled.</p><p>  </p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Requirements:</strong></p><p>A Master's or Ph.D in Computer Science (ABD considered) or a related discipline is required at or near the time of appointment.</p><p> </p><p><br/></p><p><strong> Additional Information:</strong></p><p>Augustana College is an Equal Opportunity Employer. Augustana College provides equal opportunity to all qualified employees and applicants without regard to race, national origin, religion, sex, sexual orientation, gender identity, age, disability, veteran status, and any other category protected by federal, state, or local law.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong> Application Instructions:</strong></p><p>A complete application will include: a cover letter indicating an interest in and understanding of teaching in a liberal arts setting, curriculum vitae, copy of graduate transcript, and a teaching statement demonstrating commitment to excellence in undergraduate education. Application materials should be submitted through Hirezon. Questions should be directed to Dr. Andrew Sward in the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science andrewsward@augustana.edu.</p><p>Augustana College - Full-time Visiting Faculty in Computer Science (February 2024 Start) </p><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Thank you for considering Augustana College in your search.</p>
