        Programmable DNA, the software of Life, is finally here! Twist Bioscience is developing a disruptive Synthetic DNA technology that will change the world, enabling widespread health and sustainability. While you have a broad interest in biotech and related scientific technologies, you also understand that computer science resources must be utilized to reach the scale required for our most complex solutions. The ideal candidate has computational/software/bioinformatics experience, and a basic knowledge of biology, DNA, antibody development, or protein sciences.
