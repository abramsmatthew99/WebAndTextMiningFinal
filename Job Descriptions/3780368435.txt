You will work closely with cross-functional teams to analyze and visualize data, transforming it into actionable insights and recommendations. If you are a self-starter with outstanding analytical skills who can use sophisticated data sets to deliver concise analytics and build influential presentations, then this role is the perfect opportunity to make an impact at the Walt Disney Company.<br/><br/>The Bookable Experiences Revenue Management Analyst currently reports to the Manager, Bookable Experiences Revenue Management Operations, key stakeholders and executive leadership.</li><li>Develop and maintain SQL queries and scripts for data analysis and reporting.</li><li>Contribute to the development and optimization of SQL</li><li>Ability to extract and assimilate data from multiple sources</li><li>Strong sense of responsibility, ownership, and pride in delivering quality results and understanding the business impact</li><li>Relationship and communication skills to effectively build partnerships, including the ability to ask questions to gain understanding and influence others with differing points of view<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS:<br/><br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Two (2)+ years in a role with data analysis experience</li><li>Demonstrated data analytics skills with
