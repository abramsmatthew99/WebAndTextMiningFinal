Job Title: IT Intern - REACT Website Management Company: Dual Drinks Location: Orlando FL - Remote Type: Part-Time, Unpaid Internship About Dual Drinks: Dual Drinks is a dynamic and innovative startup company in the beverage industry. We specialize in creating unique dual-flavored drinks that delight our customers. As we continue to grow, we are seeking a motivated and talented IT Intern to join our team and help manage our React website. This internship provides an excellent opportunity to gain hands-on experience in web development and technology, with the potential for a full-time role in the future. Responsibilities: - Collaborate with our IT team to maintain and update our React-based website. - Assist in troubleshooting and resolving technical issues related to the website's functionality, performance, and user experience. - Implement web design updates and improvements in coordination with our marketing and design teams. - Monitor website analytics to identify areas for optimization and enhancement. - Ensure website security and assist in implementing cybersecurity best practices. - Stay up-to-date with industry trends and emerging web technologies. - Contribute ideas and solutions to enhance the overall user experience of our website. - Make changes, updates, or new sites as needed Requirements: - Currently enrolled in or recent graduate of a Bachelor's or Master's degree program in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field. - Familiarity with React.js and front-end web development. - Basic knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. - Strong problem-solving skills and attention to detail. - Ability to work independently and collaboratively in a team. - Excellent communication skills. - Enthusiasm for learning and staying updated on the latest web development trends. Schedule: - Part-time, flexible hours (approximately 10 hours per week). - This is an unpaid internship with the potential for future paid opportunities based on performance and company growth. Benefits: - Valuable hands-on experience in web development and IT. - Opportunity to work in a dynamic startup environment. - Collaborative and supportive team culture. - Potential for a full-time role with compensation in the future. - Recommendation letter written by the CEO How to Apply: If you are a motivated individual with a passion for web development and technology, we encourage you to apply for this internship opportunity. Please apply with your resume and a brief cover letter outlining your qualifications and interest in the position on Handshake or email to info@dualdrinks.com Dual Drinks is an equal opportunity employer.
