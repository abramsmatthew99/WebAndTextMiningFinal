<p><strong>Description</strong></p><p>Who is Defined.ai? Well, from a technical point of view, we leverage the power of a global crowd to provide some of the world’s biggest companies with the high-quality data they need to power their artificial intelligence. We’re instrumental to the progression and development of artificial intelligence and we couldn’t be prouder or more inspired to be involved in an industry that is changing the world.</p><p>From a personal point of view, we’re a group of big thinkers, high achievers and creative problem solvers. We bond over our shared love of software engineering, data science, and strong coffee. We like online gaming, running marathons, and team drinks. We celebrate authenticity and diversity and we’re invested in what we do. Our mission? World domination, obviously!</p><p></p><p><strong>Note:</strong> this is an on-site position.</p><p></p><p><strong>What will you do?</strong></p><p>The project manager combines execution skills with strategic expertise to lead cross-functional teams through the entire project lifecycle. We expect you to continuously evolve both personally and professionally through trainings, self-development and, most importantly, by successfully delivering complex projects.</p><p>Our customers are demanding in terms of quality and timing given their need for high-quality data to train and power their AI systems. As a Project Manager (PM) you will play a pivotal role partnering with internal Defined.ai´s teams to deliver on a wide array of precise audio, text, and image data to meet our client’s training needs within the desired timeframe.</p><p></p><p><strong>Your tasks will be to:</strong></p><ul><li>Take ownership for the management of our standard and customized projects, ensuring they are delivered within scope, timelines, quality, and budget as agreed with our customers;</li><li>Plan, execute and coordinate autonomously the structure, governance and activities of the project and manage multi-disciplinary teams;</li><li>Strategize with internal teams to deliver on customer requirements in the most efficient and cost-effective way;</li><li>Track and communicate project timelines and updates to ensure relevant stakeholders are aligned throughout project delivery;</li><li>Monitor and report project status, identifying and communicating effectively risks, problems and critical success factors;</li><li>Develop and leverage understanding of solution costs to inform recommendations related to execution and pricing;</li><li>Develop knowledge on Defined.ai’s service offerings in order to see the challenges and feasibilities of each project you will oversee;</li><li>Facilitate a collaborative environment aligned with company objectives;</li></ul><p></p><p><strong>Who are we looking for?</strong></p><p>Do you have the drive to work in an innovative and ambitious environment?</p><p>We’re looking for someone with a determined and proactive mindset, someone inspired and passionate to help us achieve our goals. Our successful candidate is a strong critical thinker, reliable and transparent, with an ability to learn and communicate. We’re looking for someone special to contribute to our unique culture.</p><p>Our Project Manager would have:</p><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience;</li><li>5+ years' experience honing your Project Management skills in a similar and dynamic role;</li><li>Solid track record on successful project management and delivery;</li><li>Experience with Project Management tools such as MS Project, Jira, Trello,etc.;</li><li>Knowledge of industry standards such as Agile, PMBOK, ITIL, Prince2, etc;</li><li>Clear communication and organizational skills that define your decision-making across teams.</li><li>A firm ability to make decisions with conviction under pressure while understanding the need for flexibility and risk-management;</li><li>Great presentation skills using data and data analytics skills to build your reports;</li><li>Excellent speaking and writing skills in English;</li><li>Demonstrated passion for data driven decision-making;</li><li>Leadership and team management skills;</li><li>Positive and problem-solving attitude;</li><li>Client and result oriented mindset;</li><li>Not required but good to have: Practical knowledge of AI, ML, speech/text, natural language processing (NLP), linguistics or image processing;</li><li>General understanding of machine-learning, data collection and data labeling processes is a plus.</li></ul><p></p><p>You spend a lot of your time at work, so it should be challenging, fun and interesting. At Defined.ai it will be all of those things and more. Here’s what we offer:</p><ul><li><strong>Flexible working schedule and hybrid model.</strong> We know comfort can boost creativity and performance, so you can manage your schedule and work both from one of our modern office spaces or home.</li><li><strong>Excellent career development opportunities in a high growth company.</strong> With us, you can accomplish your career goals and follow a well-described career path with the support of your supervisor.</li><li><strong>Culture of feedback and continuous improvement.</strong> AI is a fast-paced area, so we keep track of tech trends, and we always ask for feedback.</li><li><strong>An international and diverse team.</strong> We have more than 30 nationalities at our 3 locations, and we provide language classes.</li><li><strong>Continuous training opportunities.</strong> You can choose from many options: leveraging hand-on workshops, unlimited access to Udemy and formal development opportunities.</li><li><strong>We love to have fun together.</strong> We joke a lot, and we can't imagine work without fun activities – we already surfed, raced carts and played soccer together.</li></ul><p></p><p><strong>About Us</strong></p><p>Defined.ai offers a platform with multiple data delivery options that leverages machine learning technology and human intelligence to deliver quality-guaranteed training data for AI systems. The platform offers self-service and fully customizable solutions that deliver high-quality project-specific training data, enabling AI products reach market quicker. It is this business model that has allowed Defined.ai to raise a total of $63.6M in funding over 4 rounds. Our value proposition is quality, privacy, speed and scale, covering more than 50 different languages. With strong expertise in speech and natural language processing technologies, we have been serving AI companies and Fortune 500 companies since day one. Defined.ai was founded in Seattle and has offices in Lisbon and Porto.</p><p></p><p><strong>Privacy Notice:</strong>https://defined.ai/dataset/privacy-notice-careers</p>
