<strong>Position Summary:<br/><br/></strong>LAC Federal is seeking an experienced and detail-oriented Cataloger to join our team. As a Cataloger, you will be responsible for cataloging our collection of books for a major federal institution. You will create and update cataloging records in an Integrated Library System (ILS), ensuring that they are accurate and complete.<br/><br/>If you are interested in making a key impact in an innovative environment, we encourage you to apply for this exciting opportunity.<br/><br/><strong>Work Hours: Full Time, ONSITE REQUIRED<br/><br/></strong><strong>Office Location:</strong> Washington, D.C. - Orange/Gray/Blue Line<br/><br/><strong>Job Status:</strong> Long-term Contract - full-time schedule available. Full-time position is eligible for benefits.<br/><br/><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong><strong>Your Impact:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Create Original Cataloging records for collections material in various subject areas in the Japanese and English languages</li><li>Efficiently catalog titles by supplying required metadata in MARC21 compliant bibliographic records</li><li>Create and update records in an ILS cataloging system, ensuring accuracy and completeness</li><li>Assist with recommendations regarding new subject headings and/or classification numbers</li><li>Stay up-to-date with developments in cataloging standards and best practices<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Your Qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Master's degree in Library and Information Science or equivalent degree from an ALA-accredited institution. </li><li>At least 3 years of experience in cataloging. </li><li>Strong knowledge of cataloging standards, including but not limited to RDA, AACR2, LCSH, LCC and MARC21, BIBCO and NACO. </li><li>Familiarity with various library management systems such as Voyager (preferred), ILS, OCLC Connexion, Classification Web, Cataloger's Desktop, and the LC Authorities list. </li><li>Hands-on experience with metadata and taxonomies for catalog collections. </li><li>Excellent written and oral communication, collaboration, and interpersonal skills. </li><li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team in a fast paced environment. </li><li>Strong analytical, detailed orientation, and prioritization skills. </li><li>Japanese language reading and writing skills preferred, but not required<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Physical Requirements:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Must be able to move from place to place within the location; lift, push/pull, hold/carry of items weighing up to (25) pounds and occasional up to (35) pounds such as files, books, stacks of paper, and other materials</li><li>Ability to sit for long periods of time and to maintain focus on projects such as computer screens or detailed paperwork<br/><br/></li></ul>The above position description is not intended as, nor should it be construed as, exhaustive of all duties, responsibilities, skills, efforts, or working conditions associated with this job. The position description may change or be supplemented at any time in accordance with business needs and conditions.<br/><br/><strong>Benefits<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Health Care Plan (Medical, Dental &amp; Vision)</li><li>Retirement Plan (401k, IRA)</li><li>Life Insurance (Basic, Voluntary &amp; AD&amp;D)</li><li>Paid Time Off (Vacation, Sick &amp; Public Holidays)</li><li>Family Leave (Maternity, Paternity)</li><li>Short Term &amp; Long Term Disability</li><li>Training &amp; Development</li><li>Wellness Resources</li></ul>
