<strong>Description<br/><br/></strong><strong>Job Overview<br/><br/></strong>This position is responsible for overseeing the technical infrastructure, eliminating security risks, and determining business requirements while leading a team of IT professionals.<br/><br/>The IT Director will report directly to the Executive Director.<br/><br/><strong>Essential Functions<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Leads development and implementation processes for the organization’s IT systems and department. </li><li>Identifies and eliminates security vulnerabilities with strategic solutions that increase data security. </li><li>Analyzes business requirements of IT needs and systems. </li><li>Manages configurations between offices such as VPN connections, firewall troubleshooting, WAN traffic, etc. </li><li>Coordinates the support of new network equipment including hardware replacement and hardware failures.</li><li>Coordinate SharePoint cloud migration. </li><li>Oversee and manages new office setup and new employee setup </li><li>Performs and oversees software installation and upgrades; maintains licenses for department and organization.</li><li>Evaluate and recommends purchases of all Technology for the entire organization.</li><li>Leads contracting, and negotiations related to IT</li><li>Creates processes and standards for selection, implementation, and support systems</li><li>Leads contracting, and negotiations related to IT</li><li>Ensure smooth delivery and operation of IT services by monitoring systems performance </li><li>Oversees project management and development of applications</li><li>Perform other duties assigned. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong><strong>Qualifications/Experience<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in information technology, Computer Science, Information Systems or a related Field. </li><li>Minimum six years of experience working in IT operations and leading IT teams. </li><li>Strong background in cybersecurity </li><li>Experience in regulations and compliance</li><li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills </li><li>Proficient in the latest technology for IT systems and management (VOIP, Mobile, SaaS, Cloud, etc).</li><li>Experience with network protocols, security, and management. </li><li> Experienced with: </li><li> ISP configuration or change or public IP</li><li> How to overcome DNS issues</li><li> DHCP troubleshooting</li><li> Configuration, upgrades, and support of switches, and router upgrades to new operating systems or user interface</li><li> Migration of current user profiles to Intune (Azure-Cloud)</li><li> Travel is required.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Working conditions<br/><br/></strong>This position operates in a professional work environment.<br/><br/><strong>Physical Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>This is a largely sedentary role, however, some lifting of office supplies of up to 50 pounds may be required.</li><li>Must be able to travel throughout Florida, as needed.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Reports<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>All IT staff.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>EEOC Statement<br/><br/></strong>Florida Rural Legal Services, Inc., does not Director of Florida Rural Legal Services, Inc. is not subject to the FRLS collective bargaining agreement.
