<strong>Tailored Brands, Inc.</strong> is a leading omni-channel specialty retailer of menswear, including suits and a broad selection of business casual offerings as well as the largest provider of tuxedo rental products in the U.S. and Canada. We operate retail stores in all 50 states and Canada. Our U.S. retail stores are operated under the brand names of <strong>Men’s Wearhouse, Jos. A. Bank, Men’s Wearhouse and Tux, and K&amp;G Fashion. </strong>Our Canadian stores are operated under the brand name of <strong>Moores </strong>in ten provinces. Our purpose is we help people love the way they look and feel for their most important moments. We accomplish this by putting customers at the center of every decision, rallying together to achieve common goals, and striving for excellence and continuous improvement. We help fulfill this purpose by valuing differences and knowing they make us better and showing up with courage to always do the right thing. If you want to make a difference, be part of a great team and grow, you might be a perfect fit.<br/><br/><strong>About The Job<br/><br/></strong>We currently have an exciting opportunity for a <strong>Data Analytics Analyst </strong>to join the Tailored Brands Enterprise Analytics team. The Enterprise Analytics team is central to our data-forward strategy, driving insights into every aspect of our business, supporting stakeholders across the organization, and creating a legendary customer experience. As Data Analytics Analyst you will will dive deep into analytical tasks to aid our Store and Real Estate ventures. This position requires a meticulous understanding of brick-and-mortar store data, in-store efficiency metrics, insights for our rental business, and more. Your expertise in data analysis and communication will be vital in delivering analytical outcomes that positively influence the business. This role will also be an opportunity to learn and grow in the Retail Analytics space.<br/><br/><strong>What You’ll Do | Key Accountabilities <br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Work closely under the guidance of the Director, Field Analytics to bring about accurate and timely insights. </li><li>Dive into data to create consolidated stores reporting, staffing optimizations, and understand the impact of KPIs on stores. Employ your knowledge to uncover the factors behind successful store performance. </li><li>Utilize diverse data sources like store, Point Of Sale, customer, and real estate to derive meaningful insights and create visualizations that spur business growth. </li><li>Emphasize customer-centric analysis: Utilize TBI customer data and national consumer behavior data to understand and reach target customers. </li><li>Adopt a systematic test &amp; learn approach to consistently refine store performance metrics and strategies. </li><li>Design, streamline, and maintain reports and dashboards. Determine significant metrics, craft comprehensive reports, and stay proactive in monitoring. Improve data resources and automate tasks to spend more time on valuable projects. </li><li>Clearly communicate and present your analysis to both technical and non-technical audiences. </li><li>Collaborate with Data Engineering teams to improve data quality, establish standardized definitions, and better the tools and methodologies in use. </li><li>Handle and prioritize diverse projects based on their impact on the business and customers. </li><li>Work closely with the wider Analytics team to share insights, align on strategies, and adopt best practices. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>What You’ll Bring | Skills &amp; Experience<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in a technical field like Computer Science, Economics, Finance, Statistics, Analytics, Operations Research, or Industrial Engineering, or relevant experience. Master’s degree preferred</li><li>3+ years of experience in Analytics, with progressive responsibility initiating, executing, and presenting insights from analytics for cross-functional teams. </li><li>Fluent with complex SQL queries and dataset transformations on large datasets. </li><li>Deep experience in advanced data visualization tools such as Google Data Studio, Tableau, or Power BI which allow you to create rich dashboards and derive insights. </li><li>Exposure to a variety of advanced analytics projects and use cases that apply methods like decision science, optimization, supervised and unsupervised learning, and forecasting with the intent of recognizing when they may add value for stakeholders. </li><li>Experience to A/B testing process, outcomes and tools (APT, Qubit, Optimizely, etc) </li><li>Strong communication and collaboration skills; act as a bridge among business, analytics and Data engineering teams. </li><li>Proven ability of delivering highly complex analytics projects involving methodology development, analysis, strategic recommendations, and presentation of the findings. </li><li>Proficient in crafting complex SQL queries and working with large datasets. </li><li>Familiarity with data visualization tools such as Google Data Studio, Tableau, or Power BI. </li><li>Awareness of A/B testing tools (APT, Qubit, Optimizely, etc) is beneficial. </li><li>Excellent communication skills and the ability to work cohesively with business, analytics, and data engineering teams. </li><li>Proven track record of handling intricate analytics projects, from initiation to strategic recommendations and presenting findings. </li><li>Ability to critically assess analyses, especially when data might present inconsistencies.</li><li>Knowledge of the retail industry is a plus. <br/><br/></li></ul>Please note that you do not need to qualify for all requirements to be considered.
