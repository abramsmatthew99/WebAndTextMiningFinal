<strong>Summary<br/><br/></strong>Responsible for modeling complex healthcare inquiries, discovering insights, and identifying opportunities using statistical, algorithmic, data-mining, and visualization techniques. Integrate and analyze large, varied datasets and communicate the results of large-scale analytics.<br/><br/><strong>Description<br/><br/></strong><strong>Logistics:</strong> This position is onsite at 4101 Percival Road, Columbia, SC 29229.<br/><br/><strong>What You'll Do<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Support decision-making by building, maintaining, and improving multiple analytic systems collaboratively. Design experiments, test hypotheses, and build analytical models to explore, explain, and predict health-related outcomes. Model and frame meaningful healthcare scenarios that impact strategic direction throughout the healthcare business.</li><li>Work closely with clients, data stewards, project managers, and Information Systems teams to turn healthcare data into critical information and knowledge that can be used to make sound organizational decisions. Collaborate with subject matter experts to select the relevant sources of information. Educate the organization from technical and business perspectives on advanced analytic approaches. Help the organization understand the principles and the computational analysis behind processes to ensure organizational support. Work with data stewards to ensure compliance with existing governance, regulatory, architecture, and security policies.</li><li>Apply statistical (descriptive, analytical, inferential) analysis and data mining techniques to generate real-world evidence and prediction systems for stand-alone projects or to be assimilated into production systems. Provide evidence that is congruent and reliable. Work iteratively to validate findings using experimental design, scenario modeling, and sensitivity analyses. Conduct data analyses that range from simple descriptive statistics to highly complex algorithms.</li><li>Develop innovative and effective approaches to solving analytics problems. Communicate results and methodologies with transparency. Identify, generate, and apply the appropriate algorithm to discover patterns. Come up with solutions to broadly defined business needs by leveraging pattern detection over complex datasets.</li><li>Present findings to the business leaders through presentations, dashboards, or other analytical output. Provide method validation with assumptions, strengths, and limitations in a way that stakeholders can easily understand.
