        Western Dental Services, Inc., California’s largest dental services provider and one of the nation’s largest dental HMO’s, is looking for a bright, motivated, seasoned data analyst. This position will be based at our Corporate Headquarters in Orange, CA. As a Data Analyst you will be responsible for collecting, analyzing, and interpreting complex data sets to uncover insights and drive data-informed decision-making. You will work closely with cross-functional teams, including business stakeholders, data scientists, and IT professionals, to ensure accurate data analysis and reporting. The ideal candidate has a strong analytical mindset, extensive experience in data analysis, and a proven ability to derive actionable recommendations from data.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong>Data Analysis and Interpretation:<br/><br/><ul><li> Collect, cleanse, and transform large and complex data sets to ensure accuracy and reliability. </li><li> Conduct advanced statistical analysis, data mining, and modeling to identify patterns, trends, and insights. </li><li> Interpret data and communicate findings to stakeholders in a clear and concise manner, including data visualizations, reports, and presentations. <br/><br/></li></ul>Data-Driven Insights:<br/><br/><ul><li> Collaborate with business stakeholders to understand their requirements and provide data-driven insights and recommendations. </li><li> Identify key performance indicators (KPIs) and develop metrics to track and measure business performance. </li><li> Proactively identify opportunities for process improvement, cost reduction, and revenue enhancement based on data analysis. <br/><br/></li></ul>Data Quality and Integrity:<br/><br/><ul><li> Ensure data integrity and accuracy through data validation, quality checks, and adherence to data governance policies. </li><li> Work closely with IT teams to optimize data extraction, transformation, and loading processes. </li><li> Perform data profiling and data cleansing activities to improve data quality. <br/><br/></li></ul>Tools and Technology:<br/><br/><ul><li> Utilize a variety of tools and technologies for data analysis, such as SQL, Python, R, Excel, Tableau, or other data visualization and analytics platforms. </li><li> Stay updated with the latest advancements in data analysis techniques and tools and identify opportunities to leverage new technologies. <br/><br/></li></ul>Cross-Functional Collaboration:<br/><br/><ul><li> Collaborate with cross-functional teams, including business stakeholders, data scientists, and IT professionals, to align data analysis initiatives with organizational goals. </li><li> Provide guidance and mentorship to junior data analysts, sharing best practices and fostering a data-driven culture. <br/><br/></li></ul>Data Standards and Policies:<br/><br/><ul><li> Enforce data standards, naming conventions, and data management best practices. </li><li> Maintain data policies and procedures to ensure compliance with regulatory requirements. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Proven experience as a Data Analyst, with a track record of delivering impactful data-driven insights and recommendations. </li><li> Strong proficiency in data analysis techniques, statistical methods, and data visualization tools. </li><li> Expertise in SQL for data extraction and manipulation, and programming languages such as Python or R for advanced analysis. </li><li> Experience with data visualization tools, such as Power BI, Tableau, or similar platforms, to create interactive dashboards and reports. </li><li> Strong analytical thinking and problem-solving skills, with the ability to analyze complex data sets and derive actionable recommendations. </li><li> Excellent communication and presentation skills, with the ability to effectively convey complex findings to both technical and non-technical stakeholders. </li><li> Ability to work in a fast-paced, dynamic environment with shifting priorities and multiple concurrent projects. </li><li> Strong attention to detail and a commitment to data accuracy and quality. </li><li> Bachelor's degree in a quantitative field, such as Mathematics, Statistics, Economics, Computer Science, or a related field. </li><li> At least 3 years of experience in IT, Software Engineering or Statistical Data Analysis </li><li> Preference will be given to candidates with healthcare industry experience. </li><li> Join our team as a Seasoned Data Analyst and contribute to data-driven decision-making that shapes our organization's success. Apply now and help us unlock the power of data for strategic insights.</li></ul>
