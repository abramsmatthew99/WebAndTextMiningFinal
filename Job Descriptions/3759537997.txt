Managing a small, dedicated team, your hands-on approach to IT support will be the driving force behind our day-to-day operations. If you are ready to channel your passion for technology into a meaningful and transformative role, and contribute to a cause that truly matters, join us in making a difference every day.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><strong><em>Strategy and Leadership:<br/><br/></em></strong><ul><li> Partners with senior management to develop and identify business and technology needs to optimize the use of information technology</li><li> Understand long-term IT needs and lead the development of an enterprise-wide strategy</li><li> Oversee the deployment of technology initiatives throughout the organization</li><li> Adhere to IT standards, policies, and processes</li><li> Assess, select, and manage 3rd party IT vendors to support technology projects and operations</li><li> Assess technology and cybersecurity risk and communicate risk and mitigation strategies to senior management<br/><br/></li></ul><strong><em>IT Systems Administration<br/><br/></em></strong><ul><li> Ensure systems and services are configured and administered for optimal use throughout the organization. These systems include but are not limited to, computers, networks, servers, backups, phones, printers, mobile devices, I-pads, security, email, audio/visual conference equipment, and all related hardware and software, including license management.</li><li> Oversee data security, management, and governance to ensure a robust cybersecurity framework is in place</li><li> Develop and maintain organizational standards, policies and best practices for systems, hardware, and software in line with evolving technology.</li><li> Research, procure, and track inventory for hardware, software, users, license information, and passwords</li><li> Ensure compliance with federal technology requirements for security<br/><br/></li></ul><strong><em>Service Provision and Team Management<br/><br/></em></strong><ul><li> Manage internal helpdesk resources and serve as an escalation point in resolving issues. Manage escalation of help desk issues to outsourced managed services vendor as well as product/project specific vendors. Manage queue and resolution of support tickets to deliver a high level of customer satisfaction</li><li> Oversee and develop the Technical Support Specialist</li><li> Manage vendor performance and contracts to effectively and efficiently meet business objectives</li><li> Manage the technology department budget and ensure alignment of resources with strategy</li><li> Other duties as assigned<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Bachelor’s degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, or related field of study</li><li> 7+ years of experience</li><li> Experience with the following technologies current in use:</li><li>Microsoft Dynamics NAV/Business Central experience preferred</li><li>Jira Service Desk experience</li><li>Jet Professional, Jet Analytics</li><li>Office 365 and Azure administration</li><li>Microsoft Active Directory and Windows infrastructure</li><li>Lansweeper Asset Management</li><li>Mitel Connect Administration</li><li>High Availability HyperV configurations</li><li>Dell server/workstations/SAN</li><li>SDWAN</li><li>Cisco networking</li><li>Aruba wireless</li><li> Experience designing and managing an effective IT governance framework</li><li> Ability to install, configure, and troubleshoot desktop systems including installed software</li><li> Thorough understanding of WAN and LAN design and maintenance</li><li> Positive and proactive communication style</li><li> High level of customer service</li><li> Minimal travel within the state of Arizona as needed, estimate less than 5%<br/><br/></li></ul>St. Mary’s offers a generous compensation package which includes medical, dental, vision, 401K, PTO, employer paid life insurance, short-term and long-term disability and more!<br/><br/>Qualified candidates are invited to submit their resume and salary requirements to: https://jobs.firstfoodbank.org/
