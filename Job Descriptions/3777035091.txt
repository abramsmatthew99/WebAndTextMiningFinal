<strong>TELECOMMUNICATIONS TECHNICIAN- $50,000+/YEAR<br/><br/></strong>Guest Services is now hiring a Telecommunications Technician to join our growing IT team!<br/><br/>We are seeking a service oriented individual who is comfortable with answering a high volume of emails, calls, etc.; maintaining client base, including networking.<br/><br/>Our ideal candidate will have experience multitasking different administrative duties, tech experience, PC Support, and sales experience.<br/><br/>This position pays a competitive base salary from $50,000- $53,000. Base salary will be set commensurate with experience.<br/><br/>Partial remote work options are available! Some travel (up to 30-40%) may be required.<br/><br/>Guest Services, Inc. offers amazing benefits such as medical, dental, vision, a 401K matching program, paid time off, employee discounts at recreational and lodging facilities nationwide, and more!<br/><br/><strong>Job Summary<br/><br/></strong>The Telecommunications Technician is responsible for maintaining company networks and telephony systems to meet company needs. The Telecommunications Technician requires an in-depth knowledge and understanding of telecommunications, network protocols, hardware, and software to fulfill position requirements.<br/><br/><strong>Essential Functions<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Assist Network team with configuration and deployment of company WANs, LANs, Voice, and wireless networks, including routers, firewalls, switches, access points, telephony systems and other telecom equipment.</li><li>Monitor and diagnoses problems with network equipment and performs equipment repair or upgrades when appropriate.</li><li>Source and procure internet and voice services for new and existing locations as needed.</li><li>Perform network and telephone system upgrades to meet company needs.</li><li>Liaison with providers to resolve outages and issues with internet and telephony services in a timely manner.</li><li>Maintain documentation related to network configuration, network mapping, processes, and service records.</li><li>Maintain inventory and perform audits on ISP/telecom accounts to ensure accuracy to prevent loss.</li><li>Assist with administering and maintaining various telephone systems. Manages the creation of new hire telephone and voicemail extensions, routing extensions to appropriate locations and updating information.</li><li>Triage and troubleshoot general computer &amp; peripheral related issues as needed via telephone, email, remote and/or onsite support.</li><li>Possess familiarity with main national cellular carriers and products to include iOS &amp; Android. Activate new services, troubleshoot, and maintain services and devices as needed.</li><li>Responsible for organizing and completing the technical day-to-day operations assigned through work orders and servicing trouble calls.</li><li>Perform other related duties as assigned.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>SKILL AND KNOWLEDGE REQUIREMENTS INCLUDE<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Associates or Bachelors degree in Technology or closely related field preferred plus 3+ years equivalent work experience required.</li><li>Up to 40% travel required.</li><li>Certifications: CompTIA+, CCNA, CWNA a plus</li><li>Must be a U.S. Citizen and willing to obtain a public trust.</li><li>Knowledge of the theories and techniques involved in the implementation and maintenance of internet, networks, and telephony: LAN, WAN, WLAN, VPN, TCP/IP, DNS, DHCP, MPLS, DIA, DSL, Cable, VoIP, SIP, POTs.</li><li>Must be familiar with twisted pair, fiber, and coax installation methods and color-coding conventions.</li><li>The ability to use and operate basic cable tester, crimp and other tools and equipment used in the installation, maintenance, and operation of telecommunications is required.</li><li>Working knowledge of computers, hardware, and software in a Microsoft environment.</li><li>Very strong organizational skills and high attention to detail.</li><li>Proactive problem solving, strong technical and analytical abilities are essential.</li><li>Demonstrated talent for interacting with a wide variety of people; ability to effectively organize and coordinate multiple priorities; ability to learn and develop new skills.</li><li>The ability to handle multiple tasks concurrently, prioritizing and accurately completing them on time in a potentially stressful environment is required.</li><li>The ability to demonstrate initiative and work and solve technical problems independently and as part of a team is required.</li><li>After-hours support on a monthly rotational schedule is required.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>PHYSICAL AND MENTAL REQUIREMENTS<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Ability to travel locally and nationally to units on a frequent basis.</li><li>Ability to sit at a desk in front of a computer for the entire workday.</li><li>Reading and writing work-related documents in English.</li><li>Speech recognition and clarity, including the ability to understand the speech of customers and co-workers and the ability to speak clearly so that you can be understood by customers and co-workers in English.</li><li>Constantly communicates and receives verbal communication with other employees.</li><li>Lifting, carrying, and pushing up to 15 lbs. occasionally, up to 30 lbs. seldom.</li><li>Occasionally stoop, kneel, or crouch. Use hands and arms to reach for, grasp, and manipulate objects.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>EQUIPMENT USED<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Typical office equipment (computers, multi-extension phone system, fax, copiers, scanners, among others).<br/><br/></li></ul>Guest Services, Inc. is an Equal Opportunity / Affirmative Action employer.
