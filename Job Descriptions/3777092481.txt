<p><strong>Title: Business Analyst 2</strong></p><p><strong>Location: Vancouver, WA (Hybrid)</strong></p><p><strong>Duration: 12-month Contract (with possible extensions up to 5 years)</strong></p><p><strong>Pay Range: $45.16 - $48.39 per hour</strong></p><p><br/></p><p>The Business Analyst will provide research and analysis of historical data on equipment database in addition to the design of processes and forms to track a variety of requirements and compliance efforts. This can entail identifying the extent of the missing data in the system, as well as developing and implementing new processes or procedures to track compliance. Because there are limited existing databases, the Business Analyst in this position will need to utilize creative approaches to compliance documentation and verification. In addition, the Business Analyst must provide recommendations for solutions to problems or gaps in data.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Position Responsibilities:</strong></p><ul><li>Gather and organize information on problems or procedures.</li><li>Analyze data gathered and develop solutions or alternative methods of proceeding.</li><li>Confer with personnel concerned to verify successful functioning of newly implemented systems or procedures.</li><li>Develop and implement management approved records management program for filing, protection, and retrieval of records, and assure compliance with program.</li><li>Review forms and reports and confer with management and users about format, distribution, and purpose, and to identify problems and improvements.</li><li>Interview personnel and conduct on-site observation to ascertain unit functions, work performed, and methods, equipment, and personnel used.</li><li>Document findings of study and prepare recommendations for implementation of new systems, procedures, or organizational changes.</li><li>Design, evaluate and recommend changes of forms and reports.</li><li>Plan study of work problems and procedures, such as organizational change, communications, information flow, integrated production methods, inventory control, or cost analysis.</li><li>Develop detailed work plans to enable project success.</li><li>Organize and facilitate large cross functional meetings.</li><li>Promote consensus on a high-level view of the project and drill down into the details to obtain the requirements.</li><li>Document project issues, risks, assumptions, and requirements.</li><li>Analyze and develop business scenarios and use cases.</li><li>Follow policies and procedures to verify audit and compliance.</li><li>Deliver project status and time tracking.</li><li>Create process flows and diagrams necessary for the requirements gathering process.</li><li>Work with management to facilitate resolution of conflicting priorities, requirements, and/or team positions.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Position Requirements:</strong></p><p><strong><em>Education &amp; Corresponding Experience</em> </strong></p><ul><li>A degree in Business Management, Business Administration, Computer Science, Cyber Security, Engineering, Organizational Development or a closely related field is preferred.</li><li>5 years of experience is required with an applicable bachelor's degree.</li><li>7 years of experience is required with an applicable associate degree.</li><li>9 years of experience is required without a degree or applicable degree.</li><li>Experience should be consistent with the specific requirements of business analysis and progressively more technical in nature.</li></ul><p><strong> <em> Required Technical Skills &amp; Experience</em> </strong></p><ul><li>5 years of work experience in Information Technology business analysis or Cyber-Security business analysis.</li><li>Advanced computer skills and knowledge of automated data systems.</li></ul><p><strong> <em> Preferred Skills &amp; Experience </em> </strong></p><ul><li>Experience in preparing for or participating in external audits.</li><li>Experience organizing cross functional meetings.</li><li>SharePoint experience.</li><li>Visio experience.</li><li>Familiarity or experience with NERC CIP standards.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>Everest Consultants offers the following benefits for this position: medical, dental, &amp; vision insurance, short-term disability, life and AD&amp;D insurance, a 401(k)-retirement plan, and a referral bonus program, paid sick/vacation/holidays, and a health and welfare fringe benefit.</p><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Everest Consultants is an equal opportunity employer and does not discriminate on the basis of race, color, religion, sex, national origin, age, disability, or any other characteristic protected by applicable local, state, or federal civil
