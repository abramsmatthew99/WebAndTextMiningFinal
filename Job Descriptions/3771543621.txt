<strong>Job Description<br/><br/></strong>MAINSAIL Group has an exciting opportunity for a Test and Evaluation Engineer located at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base in Dayton, Ohio to support our EPASS GB contract. Within the AFLCMC/GB Business and Enterprise Systems Directorate (BES) located at Wright-Patterson Air Force Base in Dayton, OH. The Depot Maintenance Legacy Systems (DMLS) is a family of independent depot maintenance software applications that support operations at WR-ALC (Warner Robins AFB), OC-ALC (Tinker AFB), and OO-ALC (Hill AFB). DMLS manufacture and overhaul operational weapon systems parts and aircraft to ensure maximum Mission Capability (MICAP) for the warfighter.<br/><br/><strong>Aircraft Maintenance Production/Compression Report (AMREP) </strong>– Inventory and schedule control; maintain depot maintenance status of aircraft<br/><br/><strong>Depot Maintenance Workload Planning and Control System (DMWPCS) </strong>– Tracks results of workload and manpower planning activities<br/><br/><strong>Information Systems Management Tool (ISMT) </strong>– Tracks info system requirements and trouble reports<br/><br/><strong>Reparability Forecast Model (RFM) </strong>– Labor standards for production costing; Bill of Material (BOM) management and end item asset availability<br/><br/><strong>ESSENTIAL DUTIES/POSITION DESCRIPTION:<br/><br/></strong>The successful candidate will be able to provide Test and Evaluation (T&amp;E) support for product lines, capability delivery teams, and development and delivery of BES Test Services. These shall be accomplished in accordance with DoD, AF, AFMC, AFLCMC, BES, product line, and/or capability delivery team, Acquisition and T&amp;E policy, guidance processes and procedures, and standard tools and services. Key policy and guidance includes, but is not limited to, AFI 99-103, Capabilities Based Test and Evaluation and related policy and guidance. Duties include, but are not limited to:<br/><br/><ul><li>Prepare, coordinate review and approval, and manage execution of test strategy, concepts, methodology, plans, processes and procedures;</li><li>Provide T&amp;E-related expertise in various DoD Adaptive Acquisition Framework (AAF) and System/Software Development Life Cycles (SDLCs); advise, advocate, and/or incorporate; optimal combinations within test strategies, plans, policy &amp; guidance, and/or procedures</li><li>Apply Scientific Test and Analysis Techniques (STAT) and Design of Experiments (DoE) methods;</li><li>Provide support to plan, implement, and execute test automation leveraging DevSecOps;</li><li>Manage and support test execution, data gathering. data reduction and analysis, and reporting;</li><li>Review, analyze, report, and recommend updates to Key Performance Indicators (KPis), Critical Technical Parameters (CTPs), and/or measures (of effectiveness, suitability, performance, and survivability);</li><li>Support implementation and management of system/software quality reporting, e.g., cumulative tracking and management of software problem reports and/or defects;</li><li>Support capability delivery team Integrated Test Team (ITT) formation, organization, and operations;</li><li>Independently prepare (or advise others in the preparation of) test criteria, computation/analyses, specifications, cost estimates, and technical reports;</li><li>Select and apply T&amp;E techniques and procedures to analyze and evaluate specific problems, data, or other features of the work which are broad in scope and complexity;</li><li>Review and evaluate the quantity, quality, and overall adequacy of results and conclusions drawn from accomplishment of T&amp;E tasks;</li><li>Devise new and innovative approaches to problems encountered, and accomplish assigned tasks with efficiency, timeliness, and thoroughness;</li><li>Evaluate the configuration, capability, and operational relevance of test environments and ensure they are properly available for the thorough testing of software releases, e.g., fault insertion/fault identification;</li><li>Prepare T&amp;E segments of Acquisition documentation;</li><li>Support T&amp;E-related certification test activities, e.g., Joint Interoperability Test Center (JITC);</li><li>Support T&amp;E-related audit and/or inspection activities;</li><li>Provide and/or support T&amp;E-related training required to execute test activities;</li><li>Support planning, execution, and reporting of Cybersecurity Test and Evaluation (CSTE)</li><li>Support FCAs/PCAs;</li><li>Support review and recommendations for higher-level DoD, AF, AFMC, AFLCMC, and BES T&amp;E related policy, guidance, processes, and procedures;</li><li>Participate in DoD and/or AF T&amp;E-related summits, conferences, and/or meetings to deliver or receive presentations on T&amp;E project/program status, accomplishments, challenges encountered and/or lessons learned, support requirements, technical developments, program changes, and the interpretation and advocacy of program and project objectives relative to T and/or,</li><li>Provide test report support services by way of the documentation of test events and shall conduct technical reviews of engineering documentation (CORL A003) associated with T&amp;E that includes but is not limited to:</li><ul><li>Engineering drawings</li><li>Specifications</li><li>Technical reports</li><li>Design discipline. Possesses a comprehensive understanding and ability to apply associated standards, procedures and practices in the software test field.<br/><br/><ul><li>Extensive knowledge and proficiency with
