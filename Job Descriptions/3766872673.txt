fast-paced environment<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science (or related field) or equivalent experience</li><li>3+ years’ experience analyzing &amp; written communication skills</li><li>Demonstrated collaborative skills and ability to work well within a team</li><li>Enthusiastic to learn new technologies required for project requirements</li><li>Ability to work in a fast-paced &amp;
