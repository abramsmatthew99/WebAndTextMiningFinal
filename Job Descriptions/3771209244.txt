<strong>Description<br/><br/></strong><strong>Quality Assurance Engineer Role: <br/><br/></strong>As a member of the company’s Information Technology team, the QA Engineer is responsible for testing all code changes, ensuring the customer’s requirements are met and validating the overall quality of the production systems.<br/><br/><strong>Duties<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Develop, publish and implement test plans for complex commercial off-the-shelf (COTS) applications such as Salesforce and/or SAP</li><li>Conduct functional, integration, system and regression testing for all software releases</li><li>Analyze functional specifications and design documents and translate them into test plans</li><li>Work directly with business analysts, developers, and product owners/end-user groups to ensure software functionality and technical design requirements are met </li><li>Develop and execute automated test cases/scripts using Apex, Provar, Selenium and C#</li><li>Create and maintain all QA documentation including; test cases and test results for all phases of testing </li><li>Define, track and report metrics such as defect densities and open defect count </li><li>Participate in company’s SOX/Audit compliance activities </li><li>Support User Acceptance Testing with business personnel </li><li>Promote and demonstrate the behaviors consistent with CoreSite’s culture and Guiding Principles</li><li>Special projects as assigned<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong><strong>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Ability to occasionally work evenings and weekends in response to needs of the systems being supported</li><li>Ability to thrive in a hybrid work environment and work in the downtown Denver office Tuesdays and Wednesdays</li><li>Ability to focus on deadlines and deliverables</li><li>Self-starter, self-motivated, driven individual</li><li>Ability to work well with shifting priorities</li><li>Experience with JAVA or C# based testing framework/tools</li><li>Understanding of database schemas and querying tools (SOQL or SQL)</li><li>Experience with Systemic and Functional Testing in a Salesforce environment</li><li>Experience in regression test management is required</li><li>Proficiency in the following tools is a plus: Agile Accelerator, Postman, MuleSoft, Provar, Selenium, SQL, Workbench, JIRA</li><li>Solid understanding of QA methodologies in an Agile environment is required</li><li>Experience with manual white and black box testing for backend and customer applications</li><li>Proven track record that demonstrates continual growth in leadership, technical and business analysis, along with quality assurance skills</li><li>Excellent interpersonal, written and oral communication skills</li><li>Strong analytical and trouble shooting skills</li><li>Ability to write technical instructions in the use of Apex test scripts and/or test script modifications</li><li>Knowledge of Continuous Integration (CI) and use of CI tools like Jenkins.</li><li>Knowledge of Test Driven Development (TDD) and/or Behavior Driven Development (BDD)<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Education/Experience<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>BA/BS in Information Technology, Computer Science, or related field or equivalent work experience</li><li>At least 3-5 years of testing experience</li><li>At least 1-2 years automation experience </li><li>Experience in an Agile SDLC methodology environment<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Physical Demands<br/><br/></strong>The physical demands described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of this job. The employee must occasionally lift and/or move up to 25 pounds.<br/><br/><strong>Compensation<br/><br/></strong>Compensation for this role includes a base salary between $80,000 and $95,000 annually.
