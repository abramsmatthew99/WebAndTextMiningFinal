Momentus provides industry-leading event and venue management software to customers in over 50 countries around the world, serving thousands of customers that power millions of events. In the age of digital transformation, our comprehensive platform offers event professionals leading-edge SaaS technology that provides a 360 view of their business, allowing them to cut costs, save time, and increase revenue. Momentus is used for top shows from across the world, famous museums, global convention centers, performing arts venues, professional sports arenas, and other unique events. Our client list includes The Apollo Theatre, Mercedes-Benz Stadium, Harvard University, Portland'5 Centers for the Arts, the Javitz Center, and St. Louis Art Museum. successful delivery of IT services to support our business operations worldwide.<br/><br/>Responsibilities:<br/><br/><ul><li> IT Strategy and Planning:</li><li>Develop and implement the company's IT of the IT department, ensuring high availability and reliability of technology infrastructure and systems. </li><li>Manage a team of IT professionals, providing guidance, mentorship, and performance feedback. </li><li>Establish and enforce IT policies, standards, and procedures to ensure security, compliance, and data integrity. </li><li>Monitor IT service levels, identify areas for improvement, intellectual property. </li><li>Conduct regular security assessments, vulnerability scanning, and penetration testing to identify and address security risks. </li><li>Stay updated on the latest security threats and industry best practices, and implement appropriate measures to mitigate risks. </li><li> Vendor Management:</li><li>Evaluate and select technology vendors and manage vendor relationships, ensuring service level agreements are met. </li><li>Negotiate contracts and pricing, and oversee vendor performance to ensure quality service delivery. </li><li>Collaborate with procurement and legal teams to review and approve IT-related contracts and agreements. </li><li> Budgeting and Resource Management:</li><li>Develop and manage the IT department's budget, ensuring cost-effective utilization of resources. </li><li>Identify and procure necessary hardware, software, and services to meet business requirements. </li><li>Optimize resource allocation, including staffing, to maximize efficiency and productivity. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field (Master's degree preferred). </li><li>Proven experience (8+ years) in IT management roles, preferably in a global software company. </li><li>Strong knowledge of IT infrastructure, network design, and cybersecurity principles. </li><li>Experience in strategic planning, budgeting, and resource management. </li><li>Strong familiarity with the Microsoft technology stack </li><li>Familiarity with cloud technologies and enterprise applications. </li><li>Excellent leadership and team management skills. </li><li>Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities. </li><li>Excellent verbal and written communication skills. </li><li>Relevant certifications (e.g., CISSP, PMP, ITIL) are a plus. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Additional
