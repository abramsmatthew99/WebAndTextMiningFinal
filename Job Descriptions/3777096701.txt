<p><strong><u>About Us:</u></strong></p><p><br/></p><p>Botrista is a VC-backed, growth stage, SF based beverage technology company providing beverage solutions to foodservice operators to deliver a variety of craft beverages, including infused teas &amp; lattes, flavored lemonades, iced coffees and more! Botrista enables consumers to gain access to healthier freshers everywhere they go. Food operators can now offer customizable and personalized refreshers without the investment in a full bar. Botrista has ~150 employees globally and is awarded as BEST CALIFORNIA B2B STARTUP in 2023. Botrista has the most beverage robots deployed among the space since its commercial launch in 2021.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Position Summary:</u></strong></p><p><br/></p><p>As a <strong>Manager of Corporate Strategy</strong>, you will lead critical projects related to data analysis, report generation, and generation of actionable insights to guide strategic decisions. You will closely partner with Botrista’s executive team to play a vital role in supporting our organization's data-driven decision-making processes by gathering, analyzing, and interpreting complex business data. The ideal candidate will have a strong analytical mindset, excellent problem-solving skills, and a passion for turning data into actionable insights. This role will report directly to Botrista’s co-founder and Chief Business Officer, Momo Wu.</p><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Responsibilities:</u></strong></p><p><br/></p><ul><li>Support Chief Business Officer on M&amp;A, fundraising, partnerships and corporate development activities(market trend and reports, internal data sets, competitor analysis, content creation).</li><li>Develop overall growth strategies by generating insights and test new business models and initiatives as the innovation center.</li><li>Define, track and report company key performance metrics and KPI and communicate those across business units.</li><li>Play a pivotal role in supporting strategic decision-making processes by providing data-backed recommendations, reports, and analytics.</li><li>Create compelling presentations and narratives with a strong focus on visual communication and storytelling. Availability to make presentations efficiently, logically and visually appealing is a must.</li><li>Perform market research and gather relevant data to stay up-to-date with industry trends and best practices in business intelligence and data analytics.</li><li>Build and maintain trusted relationships with new and existing investors.</li><li>Conduct in-depth data analysis to identify trends, patterns, and actionable insights. Collaborate with partners, investors, and cross-functional internal teams to understand their needs and provide problem solving solutions.</li><li>Develop and maintain data models, reports, and dashboards to effectively communicate learnings and insights. You will serve as the internal champion of Botrista’s data!</li><li>Implement and maintain data quality standards to ensure data accuracy and consistency in partnership with the Product and Engineering teams.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Minimum Qualifications:</u></strong></p><p><br/></p><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Business, Economics, Mathematics, Computer Science, or a related field.</li><li>2-4 years of experience as a Business Analyst in relevant fields such as business development, market research, or business intelligence.</li><li>Strong analytical skills with the ability to work with large datasets and extract meaningful insights.Previous experience with PowerBI is a plus!</li><li>Excellent communication and presentation skills, with the ability to quickly translate complex data into clearly communicated reports and visuals in Powerpoint and Google Slides.</li><li>Exceptional attention to detail to ensure accuracy in data analysis and reporting.</li><li>Previous experience in management consulting or the consumer packaged goods (CPG) industry is a plus.</li><li>Familiarity with market research methodology and databases.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Salary range:</u></strong></p><p><br/></p><ul><li>The base salary range is $120,000 to $180,000. Your base salary will be determined based on your experience. This position will be eligible for bonuses and stock options.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Benefits: </u></strong></p><p><br/></p><ul><li>401(k), Dental and Health Insurance, Paid Time Off, Vision Insurance</li></ul>
