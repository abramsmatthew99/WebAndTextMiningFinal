So join our team and help us build a brighter future.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>What's In It for You</u></strong></p><p>Come be a part of our IT team where you will have the opportunity, as a Geographic Information Systems Analyst, to learn and grow in a thriving industry! We all share a responsibility to advance the company’s mission by excelling at our three corporate priorities – the safety of our people and the public, operational excellence in all that we do, and ensuring the best possible customer experience.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Core Responsibilities</u></strong></p><ul><li>Under general supervision, develops, maintains, and supports IT application portfolio in the GIS domain for Electric or Gas operations in the IT BSD GIS Department. This individual's responsibilities include designing, coding, configuring, testing, documenting, problem solving, training, and administering and maintaining geographical information system (GIS) related applications.</li><li>Supports the GIS applications by assisting in the development of new functionality. This involves preparing documentation, coding as required, testing, and problem solving.</li><li>Maintain the technologies that are used to support Con Edison's Gas or Electric or both organizations. Support responsibilities involve software configurations, as well as customizations to enable integrations with critical systems at Con Edison.</li><li>Requirements collection, design and implementation of new systems and/or improvements to existing systems, both in-house built and vendor packaged applications.</li><li>Works with users to prepare technical analyses for enhancements to existing systems or new systems. Develops functional scope and design documentation for enhancements of existing systems.</li><li>Designs application enhancements and small projects according to departmental standards. Writes programming technical specifications based on system design.</li><li>Codes applications or supports some aspect of the application development environment (such as tools or common components). Performs system testing.</li><li>Troubleshoots problems found in testing development projects, production applications, or the development environment. Prepares work for production implementation.</li><li>Performs integration and system testing and assists in acceptance testing. Performs high level applications testing for technology upgrades.</li><li>Creates logic flow charts and process diagrams.</li><li>Ability to communicate effectively to technical and non-technical audiences.</li><li>Provides training for Systems Users.</li><li>Participates in the Company's emergency management processes and storm plans.</li><li>Perform other related tasks and assignments as required</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Required Education/Experience</u></strong></p><ul><li>Bachelor's Degree Preferably in a technical field (e.g. Computer Science, Math/Science, or Engineering) and three (3) years Demonstrated work experience in a related Information Technology field. or</li><li>Associate's Degree Preferably in a technical field (e.g. Computer Science, Math/Science, or Engineering); or technical certification and four (4) years Demonstrated work experience in a related Information Technology field. or</li><li>High School Diploma/GED Preferably in conjunction with a technical certification and 5-7 Years Demonstrated work experience in a related technology field</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Relevant Work Experience</u></strong></p><ul><li>Experience writing formal documentation and requirements. Some experience preparing and conducting presentations</li><li>Experience with object oriented programming languages. Python, or Java, or .NET experience is a plus</li><li>Experience with GIS related technologies such as ArcGIS is a must</li><li>Must be well organized, detail oriented and flexible to handle multiple assignments and deadlines</li><li>Be proficient querying relational databases; some level of Server administration skills, including low level operating system troubleshooting skills on either Windows or Linux platform, are a plus</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Skills &amp; Ability</u></strong></p><ul><li>Must be proficient in Microsoft Office including Word, Excel, Outlook and PowerPoint, etc.</li><li>Effective interpersonal skills</li><li>Demonstrated analytical skills</li><li>Strong written and verbal communication skills</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Work Authorization</u></strong></p><ul><li>Must be legally authorized to work in the United States without requiring sponsorship</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Licenses &amp; Certifications</u></strong></p><ul><li>Driver's License Required</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Other Physical Demands</u></strong></p><ul><li>Must be able to respond to Company emergencies by performing a System Emergency Assignment to restore service to our customers</li><li>Must be able and willing to travel within Company service territory, as needed</li><li>Must have the ability and willingness to work and travel to various Company locations within the Company's service territory</li><li>Must be available to work off hours as operationally required.
