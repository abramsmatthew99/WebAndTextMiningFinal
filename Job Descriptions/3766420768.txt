<strong>Overview<br/><br/></strong><strong>Who we are<br/><br/></strong>At Toyota, we are reimagining mobility through innovative, high-quality technology solutions designed to enhance lives and meet our company mission of “Producing Happiness for All.” If you are interested in reimagining mobility with us in an inclusive environment built on teamwork that puts respect for people first, we want to talk to you.<br/><br/><em>To save time applying, Toyota does not offer sponsorship of job applicants for employment-based visas or any other work authorization for this position at this time.<br/><br/></em><strong>Who We Are<br/><br/></strong>An important part of the Toyota family is Toyota Financial Services (TFS), the finance and insurance brand for Toyota and Lexus in North America. While TFS is a separate business entity, it is an essential part of this world-changing company – delivering on Toyota’s vision to move people beyond what’s possible. At TFS, you will help create best-in-class customer experiences in an innovative, collaborative environment.<br/><br/><strong>Who We’re Looking For<br/><br/></strong>Toyota Financial Services (TFS) is looking for a highly motivated person to fill a role as a Domain Operations Owner, Chief of Staff &amp; Portfolio Lead.<br/><br/>This role will play a pivotal role in supporting the TFSB CIO organization’s strategic initiatives and ensuring the organization’s efficient operation. This multifaceted role combines executive-level support, strategic planning, portfolio management, and cross-functional collaboration to drive innovation and excellence in technology endeavors.<br/><br/><strong>Key Responsibilities Include<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Strategic Support: Collaborate closely with the TFSB CIO to define and execute the technology vision, goals, and strategies. Provide insights, recommendations, and data-driven analysis to facilitate informed decision-making.</li><li>Portfolio Management: Oversee the technology portfolio, including projects, initiatives, and investments. Prioritize and align projects with the technology roadmap, business objectives, and resource availability. Monitor project progress and ensure timely delivery.</li><li>Budget Management: Collaborating with the TFSB CIO to forecast, establish, allocate, monitor, and report the technology budget. Collaborating with finance and relevant stakeholders to forecast and monitor budgets.</li><li>Cross-Functional Collaboration: Foster strong relationships with other senior leaders, department heads, and stakeholders across the organization. Facilitate communication, alignment, and coordination to drive synergies and achieve shared goals.</li><li>Operational Efficiency: Identify opportunities for process optimization and operational improvements within the technology organization. Implement best practices and frameworks to enhance efficiency and effectiveness.</li><li>Executive Communication: Prepare and deliver executive-level communications, presentations, and reports on technology strategy, performance, and initiatives. Synthesize complex technical information into easily understandable formats for a variety of audiences.</li><li>Change Management: Lead change management efforts related to technology transformations, ensuring smooth transitions and minimized disruption to ongoing operations.</li><li>Risk Management: Collaborate with the TFSC CIO to identify and mitigate risks associated with technology projects and initiatives. Develop contingency plans and strategies to address potential challenges.</li><li>Innovation and Research: Work with team to drive innovation and research through internal and external engagements and partnerships.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>What You Bring<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in a relevant field (Computer Science, Engineering, Business, etc.). Master's degree is a plus.</li><li>Proven experience in a senior leadership role within the technology sector, demonstrating a deep understanding of technology trends, strategies, and operations.</li><li>Excellent interpersonal and communication skills, with the ability to interact effectively with executives, technical teams, and cross-functional stakeholders.</li><li>Strong analytical and problem-solving abilities, coupled with a data-driven mindset.</li><li>Project management expertise, including portfolio management, resource allocation, and project delivery.</li><li>Change management and organizational transformation experience.</li><li>Proficiency in strategic planning and execution.</li><li>Ability to work in a fast-paced environment and manage multiple priorities.</li><li>High level of discretion and confidentiality.</li><li>Familiarity with relevant software tools and applications for project management and communication.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>What We’ll Bring<br/><br/></strong>During your interview process, our team can fill you in on all the details of our industry-leading benefits and career development opportunities. A few highlights include:<br/><br/><ul><li>A work environment built on teamwork, flexibility, and respect.</li><li>Professional growth and development programs to help advance your career, as well as tuition reimbursement.</li><li>Vehicle purchase &amp; lease programs.</li><li>Comprehensive health care and wellness plans for your entire family.</li><li>Flexible work options based on business needs.</li><li>Toyota 401(k) Savings Plan featuring a company match, as well as an annual retirement contribution from Toyota regardless of whether you contribute.</li><li>Paid holidays and paid time off.</li><li>Referral services related to prenatal services, adoption, childcare, schools, and more.</li><li>Tax-Advantaged Accounts (Health Savings Account, Health Care FSA, Dependent Care FSA).</li><li>Relocation assistance (if applicable).<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Belonging at Toyota<br/><br/></strong>Our success begins and ends with our people.
