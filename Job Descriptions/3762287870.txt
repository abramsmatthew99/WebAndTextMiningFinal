Note: We hire interns throughout the year. Please let us know when you apply when you'll be available to start. We strongly prefer 12+ week internships.Our name, Slingshot AI, reflects our mission: AI for all, not just Goliath. We don't want AI to be "Winner Takes All," because every high-value use case deserves a high-performing model.We're building the Model Studio for Generative AI, for machine learning developers to develop, through training, evaluation and iteration, top-tier specialized generative models. If we succeed, we’ll become the homepage for every machine learning engineer responsible for getting AI use cases to actually work. And ultimately, we’ll be defining the next generation of ML engineering for Gen AI.We're a well-funded, seed-stage startup backed by top-tier tech investors involved in Docker, Shopify, Plaid, Notion, Canva, Opendoor, Twitch, Airtable, and others. We're building a powerful team to revolutionize ML Engineering by empowering our engineers with the autonomy, flexibility, and resources to do their best work.The RoleAs a machine learning intern, you’ll conduct applied research projects involving deep learning models from start to finish. You’ll work collaboratively with our ML engineers and take ownership of your own project. You’ll work at a faster pace than you have before while writing high-quality, production-ready code and producing meaningful scientific insights.As an early user of the Slingshot platform, your feedback will also inform product decisions and influence the way we grow as a company, giving you a direct impact on the next generation of machine learning.You’ll be working with 5-100B parameter models and working predominantly in the NLP domain. You’ll predominately be written in PyTorch, utilizing the modern tooling in the ML space, including some that we’ve developed in-house. We write high-quality, typed, Zen code.About you:You’re either a PhD student, a Master’s students, or an exceptional undergraduate studentYou have experience developing deep learning models in PyTorch, TensorFlow or JAX. You also have experience with software engineering best practices and have a deep appreciation for what good code looks likeYou're fast-paced and pragmatic. You'd rather prove out an idea through quick MVP code than present a slide deck to explain it. You have a demonstrated track record of excellence, including coming up with new ideas or improving upon existing ideas, especially in machine learningYou understand why machine learning will bring about the greatest turning point in human historyRequirement: Able to work in New York City or LondonWhat We OfferCompetitive stipendHybrid environment, highly collaborative, fast-paced cultureUnparalleled growth opportunity - learn faster than you would anywhere elseHave a massive impact on the next generation of ML engineeringWork with a passionate team that cares about creating an AI world that's not winner-takes-allValuesBias towards ActionAuthenticity - we keep it real and we say it like it isStrong opinions, loosely heldWe're nowhere near the end of history
