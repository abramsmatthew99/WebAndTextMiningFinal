        Do you have a passion for innovative ideas and groundbreaking discoveries? With over $1 billion invested annually in R&amp;D, at Thermo Fisher Scientific you’ll help solve some of the world’s toughest challenges, from giving cancer patients hope, ensuring safe drinking water and helping law enforcement tackle cases through forensics. We empower our teams to put science into meaningful action and give our R&amp;D colleagues the autonomy, resources and tools they need to take science a step beyond. LOCATION/DIVISION SPECIFIC INFORMATION The Data Science and Machine Learning Internship position provides an opportunity for students who are currently pursuing a computer science, bioinformatics, computational biology, or similar degree to get hands on experience working with scientists and software engineers in a fast-paced research and development environment. The intern will participate in the development and testing of machine learning models including large language models to improve the performance of our algorithms to support genetic science research and development. The position starts in May or June, paid working 40 hours per week through summer. DISCOVER IMPACTFUL WORK: Through improving and developing trusted machine learning models, we can ensure accuracy of our genetic science software and establish a competitive advantage in the global marketplace with the goal of making a positive impact on the world. A DAY IN THE LIFE: - Training and testing on different machine learning models - Brainstorming new improvements to streamline current techniques - Understanding our group's standard practices to support genetic science research and development KEYS TO SUCCESS: EDUCATION - Currently enrolled in a master's program or a Ph.D. program EXPERIENCE - Knowledge about data science and machine learning - Large scale data science programming project - Deploy and evaluate machine learning models KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, ABILITIES - Proficient in Python, Java or related and willing to learn - Modern Data Science and Machine Learning - Knowledge of cloud computing with AWS PHYSICAL REQUIREMENTS / WORK ENVIRONMENT Work environment is an in-office stable 9 to 5 with some flexibility. The work itself is supported by a team effort in which we encourage engagement from a pool of different contributors in order to develop useful tools that will further progress our common goal of supporting genetic science research and development.<br/><br/><strong>Compensation And Benefits<br/><br/></strong>The hourly pay range estimated for this position based in California is $14.75–$45.75.<br/><br/>This position may also be eligible to receive a variable annual bonus based on company, team, and/or individual performance results in accordance with company policy.
