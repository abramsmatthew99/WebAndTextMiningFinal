</li><br/></ul> <p><strong>Requirements<br/></strong> </p> <ul><li>Bachelors in Computer Science or equivalent</li><li>Proven ability to complete projects in a timely manner while clearly measuring preferred</li><li>Strong experience with revision control (Git)</li><li>Experience with cloud-based systems (Azure / AWS / GCP).</li><li>High level understanding of big data design (data lake, data Monitoring and Alerting tools</li><li>Strong communication skills</li><li>Strong experience with use of RESTful APIs</li><li>High level understanding of HL7 V2.x / FHIR based interface messages.</li><li>High level understanding of system deployment tasks and technologies. (CI/CD Pipeline, K8s, Terraform)</li></ul>
