<strong>Who We Are:<br/><br/></strong>With offices in Los Angeles, Newport Beach, Boston and Sarasota, Aristotle is a family of employee‐owned investment management firms that collectively manage equity and fixed income strategies. Aristotle attracts and retains talented investment professionals through a culture focused on research, investment management and client success.<br/><br/>Aristotle’s experienced corporate leadership team oversees a centralized support infrastructure to ensure that our distinct teams of investment professionals can focus on what they do best—adding value to client portfolios. The company’s support infrastructure includes shared services such as finance, compliance, trading, operations, client communication, marketing, and distribution.<br/><br/>We are currently seeking a Sr. Service Desk Support Technician to join our team. The Sr Service Desk Support Technician will play a pivotal role in ensuring the efficiency and reliability of our IT services by providing exemplary customer service, attention to detail, and follow through, as well as efficient and effective technical assistance and support to our employees, ensuring the smooth operation of our technology systems and contributing to the overall success of the organization. The Sr Service Desk Support Technician role is a valuable opportunity to develop technical skills, while contributing to the efficient functioning of Aristotle Capital and contribute to the continual improvement of our IT operations.<br/><br/><strong>What You Will Do:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Provide best-in-class, white-glove customer service and technical support to end-users (onsite, offsite, and remote) by responding to and resolving inquiries, diagnosing, and troubleshooting hardware, software, and network issues promptly to minimize downtime.</li><li>Assist in installing, configuring, and maintaining hardware and software, including workstations, laptops, multi-functioning printers and scanners, mobile devices, video conferencing equipment, and other peripherals.</li><li>Document and maintain accurate records of support requests, solutions provided, and follow-up actions taken in our IT Support ticketing system (Jira Service Management) as well as escalate complex technical issues to members of other IT teams.</li><li>Perform IT device management including inventory, monitoring, reporting, patching, and planning to ensure the stability and security of the IT environment with remote tools (Intune, ConnectWise, Ninja RMM).</li><li>Administration of Windows Active Directory, Azure Active Directory, Microsoft Endpoint Management (Intune), Microsoft Office365 (including Teams, SharePoint, Exchange, and OneDrive), and other business-critical applications.</li><li>Perform user onboarding and offboarding processes, including provisioning and de-provisioning of accounts, access rights, and hardware.</li><li>Identify persistent problems in the environment and work with relevant team members and product owners to deliver effective solutions.</li><li>Interact with hardware vendors and service providers as needed, to close or open issues.</li><li>Collaborate with team members to create documentation to update and maintain a comprehensive knowledge base(Jira Confluence) for the team and end-users.</li><li>Participate in IT projects, such as software rollouts, hardware upgrades, and system migrations under the guidance of other Infrastructure, Cyber Security, and Enterprise Application engineers.</li><li>Stay up to date with industry trends and best practices to continuously enhance technical skills and contribute to the improvement of the service desk function.</li><li>Participate in after-hours support, and able to work some weekends when needed.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>What You Will Need:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Outstanding communication skills, both written and verbal with an emphasis on clear and concise technical communication with the ability to communicate technical information to non-technical users.</li><li>Highly self-motivated and directed.</li><li>Exceptional organizational skills and attention to detail.</li><li>Exceptional interpersonal skills and a customer-focused attitude with a passion for helping others.</li><li>Extensive and in-depth computer hardware, software applications, and operating system (Windows and MacOS) troubleshooting experience with a comprehensive understanding of Microsoft’s Office products and Cloud services (including Azure AD and Endpoint Management).</li><li>Strong working technical knowledge of current IT best practices, protocols, operating systems, and standards.</li><li>Advanced knowledge of remote desktop support tools (ConnectWise/Ninja RMM) and IT Service Management.</li><li>Proven ability to manage multiple tasks, prioritize effectively, and meet deadlines in a fast-paced environment.</li><li>Strong analytical and troubleshooting skills, including the ability to diagnose and resolve complex technical issues and develop effective solutions.</li><li>Team-oriented approach, with the ability to collaborate effectively with colleagues and management, creating and nurturing a teamwork environment.</li><li>Flexibility to work outside regular business hours when necessary.</li><li>Four-year degree from an accredited college or university in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field, or equivalent work experience. Relevant certifications and technical training (e.g., CompTIA A+, ITIL, Microsoft Certifications) are highly desirable.</li><li>5-7 years of experience in a technical support role or related field.</li><li>1-2 years of experience in a financial firm or asset management firm is preferred.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>What We Have to Offer:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Robust Health, Dental, and Vision insurance for you and your dependents</li><li>401(K) Retirement Plan with Employer Matching</li><li>A culture that values inclusivity</li><li>Flexible Paid time off, including annual Employee Volunteer Day</li><li>Life, Critical Illness, Disability, and Accidental Injury Insurance</li><li>Employee Assistance Program</li><li>Various Company events throughout the year</li><li>Employee Discount Program<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Is Aristotle Right for You?<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>At Aristotle, we stand by our core values.</li><li>We place our clients’ interests first.</li><li>We act with integrity.</li><li>We achieve excellence through accountability and collaboration.</li><li>We empower our employees.</li><li>We are respectful.</li><li>We value community.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul>Compensation Range: $98,000.00 - $120,000.00, plus discretionary bonus.<br/><br/>Location: The position will be based out of our Los Angeles and Newport Beach offices and is expected to be on-site Monday-Friday.<br/><br/>We are looking for exceptional individuals who are intelligent, enthusiastic, and curious, someone who will contribute to our expanding business. An ideal candidate would not limit their contribution to the provided job description but would help us surpass our goals by continually finding ways to improve themselves and the firm. If you would like the stability of a successful, established company and the growth opportunities of a dynamic environment, we look forward to hearing from you.<br/><br/>Note: The above description identifies the essential job functions and skills needed by the person or persons assigned to this position.
