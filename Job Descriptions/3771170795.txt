The team partners with stakeholders across the organization, primarily within Product, in order to implement strategic initiatives that drive growth via A/B Testing. Within this role, an intern will have access to projects that drive such growth and also interact with key stakeholders within Product and Decision Sciences. Interns can apply a wide range of analytical techniques, including SQL, Python, Statistical Modeling, and Data Visualization. The intern’s contribution to major Product changes will be tangible and realized. </li><li>Peacock, Data Engineering: We are seeking a motivated and enthusiastic Data Engineering Intern to join our dynamic team. This internship offers a unique opportunity to apply your academic knowledge in a real-world setting while contributing to the success of our data-driven initiatives. </li><li>Peacock Decision Sciences, Consumer Insights: The Peacock Consumer Insights Team supplies insights in support of strategic objectives to stakeholders across the organization, including the Content Strategy, Content Development &amp; Marketing teams. As an intern on our team, you will be explosed to a variety of quantitative and qualitative primary research methodologies and gain experience in analyzing the output to provide actionable insights for the business. We’re looking for someone to be genuinely curious, smart, and motivated; ready to learn and help with primary research analysis supporting the strategic business initiatives of the Peacock streaming service within NBCUniversal. They become active members of existing teams developing data science projects, acquiring specific responsibilities, goals and timelines which are interdependent with the goals and timelines of the teams they are embedded in. Upon the completion, future Data Scientists will have gained experience in real-world projects on a typical data science environment, using state of the art data science tools, and broad exposure to the media industry. As the data science intern, you’ll get to build machine learning and statistical models to understand and predict content performance on the Peacock platform, explore and transform data (e.g., cleansing, discretization, imputation, selection, generalization) to create high quality inputs for the modeling process, collaborate with the team to build scheduled pipelines for productizing data science solutions, use big data and relational data sources to access data at the appropriate level of granularity for the needs of specific analytical projects, work on improving the codebase and machine learning lifecycle infrastructure and actively participate in all aspects of the teamwork, and present work to and communicate with non-technical stakeholders to inform business decisions. </li><li>NBCU Analytics, Insights &amp; Measurement, Data Engineering: Our Data Engineering program is designed to provide interns with real-world data engineering experience, using state of the art tools and technologies. Working as part of our data engineering team, interns will help to design and develop custom decision sciences product for the media industry. Interns will acquire specific responsibilities, goals, and timelines aligned with the teams in which they are embedded. Join us as we collaborate across our business to design and implement engineering solutions that drive data-enhanced strategic decisions. </li><li>Media Group HQ, Data Science: Our Data Science program is designed to provide interns with real-world experience working on projects on a typical Data Science environment, using state of the art tools and technologies, while gaining broad exposure to the media industry. Interns will acquire specific responsibilities, goals, and timelines aligned with the teams they are embedded in. Join us as we collaborate across our business to support and inform data-enhanced strategic decisions. As the intern, you’ll get to assist with research &amp; development to inform new methodologies, implement predictive and inferential stastical and/or machine learnign models, contributre to the development of tools and data pipelines, and develop interative visualizations that communicate complex ideas. </li><li>Peacock Decision Sciences, Recommendation: We are building a world-class team of smart, hungry and fearless professionals who are energized by the possibility of working at the epicenter of content, technology and culture. ability to conduct advanced statistical analysis like factor or conjoint analysis is a plus but not required. </li><li>Experience with a statistical analysis tool like R, SPSS and/or survey analysis software like Marketsight, Alchemer, or SurveyMonkey preferred. </li><li>Working knowledge of Distributed Computing environments. </li><li>Experience working in cloud computing environment, designing solution architecture. </li><li>Working knowledge of at least two of the following programming languages: R, Python, Julia, Java, Scala or similar. </li><li>Familiarity with Spark, Databricks and/or AWS. </li><li>Familiarity with Google Cloud Platform. </li><li>Hands on experience in statistical methods and machine learning with special emphasis on the advanced algorithms like neural networks, SVM, random forests, bagging, gradient boosting machines, k-means++, reinforcement learning, etc. </li><li>Familiarity with deep learning modeling using Tensorflow or Pytorch. <br/><br/></li></ul>The hourly rate for undergraduate student interns is $30.00<br/><br/>The hourly rate for graduate student interns is $35.00
