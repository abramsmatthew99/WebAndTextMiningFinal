<strong>Job Description<br/><br/></strong>ADVANCEMENT OPPORTUNITIES<br/><br/>Geospatial Information Services Analyst, Senior<br/><br/>Manager, Geospatial Information Services<br/><br/>Manager, Executive, Technical Services Basin Planning<br/><br/><strong>Position Summary<br/><br/></strong>Under the general supervision of the Geospatial Information Services Manager, this position assists in several aspects of maintaining and supporting the Authority's enterprise-wide Geographic Information System (GIS). This includes creating and providing technical support for GIS-centric solutions; performing spatial analyses, geoprocessing, and modeling using ESRI's ArcGIS tools and techniques; creating and maintaining geospatial databases and maintaining data relationships with GIS-centric asset management systems. Oversees data integrity and assists in the generation, compilation, and documentation of reports and GIS procedures. This position is customer-oriented and focuses on serving the needs of a varied customer base.<br/><br/><strong>Essential Duties And Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Creates and implements GIS-centric application solutions. </li><li> Creates and maintains data in enterprise geodatabases. </li><li> Performs standard geospatial analysis and data modeling. </li><li> Makes information available to management in the form of reports and maps for decision-making and responds to information requests. </li><li> Write scripts and queries to automate and enhance workflow. </li><li> Lead in the implementation and testing of new GIS applications to meet customer needs and enterprise management requirements. </li><li> Participates in the design and implementation of new data schemas for enterprise geodatabases and individual GIS solutions. </li><li> Oversees data integration and integrity between geodatabases and GIS-centric work orders and asset management systems. </li><li> Act as a consultant to customer departments with a focus on promoting the use of geospatial technology, uncovering opportunities where geospatial technology will provide value, and providing guidance. </li><li> Make recommendations for improvements to procedures supporting the agency’s enterprise GIS. </li><li> Trains users on the use and adoption of GIS technologies. </li><li> Collaborates with appropriate parties, such as customer cities, IT, and vendors to resolve problems and investigate opportunities. </li><li> Uses analytical and clearly thought out processes to troubleshoot and resolve technical problems. </li><li> Adheres to departmental, personnel, and safety policies and procedures. </li><li> Stay abreast of developments in the geospatial fields. </li><li> Assists with special projects and studies as needed. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Supervisory Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong>This position does not regularly provide work leadership or full personnel management to any employees.<br/><br/>FINANCIAL RESPONSIBILITY<br/><br/>No significant financial responsibilities.<br/><br/><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong>EDUCATION<br/><br/>Bachelor’s degree in Geographic Information Science, Geography, Planning, Computer Science, or a closely related field that required equivalent coursework in Geographic Information Systems.<br/><br/><strong>Experience<br/><br/></strong>One (1) year of professional experience as a provider of geospatial services using Esri’s suite of products to include administering components of enterprise GIS software; and one year using Microsoft Office Products.<br/><br/>CERTIFICATES, LICENSES, REGISTRATIONS<br/><br/>Valid Texas driver’s license. The following certifications are desired but not a requirement: CompTIA Security+ or equivalent.<br/><br/>KNOWLEDGE<br/><br/>Knowledge of the theories and principles of GIS software and database manipulation and product/output development.<br/><br/>Knowledge of database design principles with experience designing geodatabases<br/><br/>Familiarity with geoprocessing tools in the ArcGIS software toolkit.<br/><br/>Experience with object-oriented programming languages with a preference for Python or JavaScript.<br/><br/>Experience with manipulating data using a functional language such as Structured Query Language (SQL).<br/><br/>Knowledge of GIS-centric asset management principles for utilities.<br/><br/>Proficient using server-side GIS tools such as ArcGIS Server, and Portal for ArcGIS to create web services and web maps.<br/><br/>Knowledge of the Esri technologies and processes for deploying and supporting GIS solutions intended for field use.<br/><br/><strong>Skills And Abilities<br/><br/></strong>Ability to maintain effective working relationships with others.<br/><br/>Ability to develop realistic plans needed to accomplish objectives and team goals.<br/><br/>Proven analytical and problem-solving skills.<br/><br/>Encourages innovative and new ways of looking at problems, processes, and solutions.<br/><br/>Proficient at effective oral and written communications.<br/><br/>Ability to interact with individuals at all levels in a diplomatic and professional manner while exercising good emotional control.<br/><br/>Ability to effectively manage multiple projects, assignments, and duties.<br/><br/>Proficient at resolving customer complaints and concerns.<br/><br/>GUIDANCE RECEIVED<br/><br/>Guided by priorities and policies.<br/><br/>Is guided primarily by established priorities and policies, along with professional standards and best practices to guide decisions and any direction or advice given to others.<br/><br/>Typically makes recommendations to managers on policy and process improvements after considering/analyzing various alternatives.<br/><br/>Proactively addresses needed changes to policies, methods, and procedures and obtains approval.<br/><br/>WORKING CONDITIONS<br/><br/>Most duties are carried out in an office environment but may include limited fieldwork, possibly under inclement weather.<br/><br/>TOOLS AND EQUIPMENT<br/><br/>Office machines include personal computer equipment, computer peripheral devices such as large format plotters, GPS devices, and business and productivity
