<p><strong>LHH </strong>is partnering with a client to find an experienced <em>Network Engineer </em>to join their team in Los Angeles, CA.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong>:</p><ul><li>Design, configure, and implement complex network solutions using Cisco products and technologies.</li><li>Plan and execute network upgrades, migrations, and optimizations.</li><li>Troubleshoot and resolve network issues, providing timely and effective solutions.</li><li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams to gather requirements and ensure alignment with business goals.</li><li>Provide technical guidance and mentorship to junior network engineers.</li><li>Stay up-to-date with the latest industry trends and emerging Cisco technologies.</li><li>Perform network capacity planning and scalability assessments.</li><li>Document network configurations, processes, and procedures.</li></ul><p><strong>Qualifications</strong>:</p><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or related field (or equivalent work experience).</li><li>Proven experience as a Network Engineer, with a strong focus on Cisco products and technologies.</li><li>Cisco certifications (e.g., CCNA, CCNP, CCIE) are preferred.</li><li>Expertise in configuring and managing Cisco routers, switches, and firewalls.</li><li>Proficiency in network protocols, routing, and switching (e.g., BGP, OSPF, VLANs).</li><li>Strong problem-solving and analytical skills.</li><li>Excellent communication and interpersonal abilities.</li><li>Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a team.</li><li>Strong attention to detail and commitment to delivering high-quality solutions.</li></ul><p><strong>Nice to Have:</strong></p><ul><li>Experience with other networking vendors and technologies (Juniper, Palo Alto, etc.).</li><li>Familiarity with software-defined networking (SDN) and network automation.</li><li>Knowledge of cloud networking and hybrid cloud environments.</li><li>Experience with network monitoring and management tools (e.g., SolarWinds, Cisco Prime).</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Salary</strong>: 100k-140k</p><p><br/></p><p>If you feel that you are a good fit for the <em>Network Engineer</em> role, please apply through the link.</p><p><br/></p><p>Equal Opportunity Employer/Veterans/Disabled</p><p><br/></p><ul><li>To read our Candidate Privacy Information Statement, which explains how we will use your information, please visit https://www.parkerlynch.com/candidate-privacy/</li></ul>
