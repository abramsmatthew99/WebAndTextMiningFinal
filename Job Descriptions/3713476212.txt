Adjunct faculty are an integral part of the University’s learning community and courses online and/or on campus.<p><br/></p>The College of Arts and Sciences is accepting applications for an academically qualified adjunct faculty member to teach different Computer Science courses including:<p><br/></p><ul><li>COSC 130 - Introduction to Programming</li><li>COSC 140 - Introduction to Operating Systems</li><li>COSC 375 - Applications of Blockchain Technology</li><li>COSC 421 – Introduction to Robotics</li><li>COSC 423 – Computer Vision</li><li>COSC 435 – Introduction to Reinforcement Learning</li><li>COSC 440 – Artificial Intelligence</li><li>COSC 443 – Ethics of Artificial Intelligence</li><li>COSC 445 – Applications of Artificial Intelligence</li></ul><p><br/></p><strong>Essential Job Functions/Responsibilities:</strong><p><br/></p><ul><li> Teach assigned course as specified in the schedule and course contract.</li><li> Participate in welcome orientation to become familiar with the University’s mission, academic policies and procedures,</li><li> expectations, and Canvas (Learning Management System).</li><li> Clearly communicate course objectives and learning outcomes, teaching methodology, and assignments and deadlines.</li><li> Employ a variety of teaching styles in order to effectively deliver course content feedback and grade assignments in a timely and thorough manner (within 24-36 hrs.).</li><li> Establish digital office hours to provide additional assistance with student questions/concerns.</li><li> Report early alerts for students who are not actively engaging in the course.</li><li> Additional duties as assigned.</li></ul><p><br/></p><strong>Minimum Qualifications</strong><p><br/></p><ul><li> Master's degree in Computer Science or related field from a regionally accredited institution.</li><li> Student-centered approach and passion for teaching and mentoring a diverse student population.</li></ul><p><br/></p><strong>Preferred Qualifications</strong><p><br/></p><ul><li> PhD in Computer Science or related field from a regionally accredited institution.</li><li> Experience in online teaching.</li></ul><p><br/></p><strong>Physical Demands</strong><p><br/></p><strong>Open Until Filled</strong><p><br/></p>Yes<p><br/></p><strong>Special Instructions to Applicants</strong><p><br/></p>An offer of employment is contingent upon successful completion of a background screening.<p><br/></p>Applicants requiring University sponsorship to obtain employment
