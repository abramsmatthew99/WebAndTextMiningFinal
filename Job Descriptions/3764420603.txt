Act as back-up for the Customer Service team for payment calls</li><li>Coordinate and support project teams through research, testing, development and implementation phases</li><li>Update and maintain Project and PMO documentation and other related project deliverables. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in business or a technical discipline supplemented by college level course work in Information Technology, Computer Science, or closely related technical field</li><li>Any equivalent combination of education and experience</li><li>Interest in Project management techniques, tools and reports to acquire project management skills</li><li>Understanding of Windows, Microsoft Office suite of products, current internet browsers, e-mail and business applications in a networked environment</li><li>Ability to interface and communicate effectively with non-technical personnel, as well as professional Information Technology staff. </li><li>Property &amp; Casualty Insurance experience desired but not required<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Benefits<br/><br/></strong>Concord Group employees are eligible for benefits including medical, vision, dental, life, and disability insurance, sick time and holiday pay, <strong>three weeks’ paid vacation,</strong> and a generous <strong>401(k)</strong> plan with a 6% <strong>company match.<br/><br/></strong><strong>About Us<br/><br/></strong>As a leading regional provider of property and casualty insurance, The Concord Group helps protect the families and small businesses that enable our communities to thrive.
