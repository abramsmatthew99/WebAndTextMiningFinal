<strong>Description<br/><br/></strong>The Nanotronics Machine Learning Engineer in our Control Lab is a smart, creative and passionate engineer who will work with our team in the development of the world’s ultimate integrated manufacturing platform. This position will be responsible for building the most implemented supervision and training platform across multiple industries, such as hypercar manufacturing, semiconductor manufacturing and personalized medicine.<br/><br/><strong>Salary Range: $132-297k per year <br/><br/></strong><em>A Note About Salary Ranges</em>: Nanotronics is committed to hiring both rising and advanced talent. We are a creative and flexible organization, with niche needs, so early-career potential can sometimes be as important as senior-level experience. Therefore, our broad salary ranges are meant to encourage applicants from a wide range of experience levels to apply.<br/><br/><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong><strong>Education and Experience<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>2+ years’ experience and advanced degree (Bachelor’s at minimum)</li><li>Background in computer science or engineering</li><li>Primary focus on DevOps, working with ML tools, setting up training pipelines, retaining and testing artifacts<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Required Skills<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Content knowledge in Statistics/Machine Learning through a formal degree, bootcamp, or self-guided study</li><li>Experience in Python, PyTorch, RNN, LSTM, multiprocessing, autoencoders, transformers, reinforcement learning, adaptive controls </li><li>Knowledge of core data science, data visualization, and graphing techniques</li><li>Applied mathematics</li><li>Strong technical communication and problem-solving skills </li><li>Ability to prioritize and manage time without excessive oversight<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Automate the ML model lifecycle, i.e., develop, deploy, monitor and maintain scalable services and applications for our stakeholders to handle their machine learning training and inference needs in a quick, reliable and reproducible way</li><li>Error analysis of trained machine learning models</li><li>Design, train, improve and launch AI models</li><li>Continually work on improving the codebase and have active participation in all aspects of the team</li><li>Integrate unstructured and timeseries data into production pipelines</li><li>Collaborate with DevOps engineers to plan and deploy data storage and processing systems, especially for text, timeseries or financial data<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>More About The Team<br/><br/></strong>Nanotronics’™ nControl™ fueled by AIPC™ solution brings AI Technology to factories, both increasing productivity and better securing processes. Our technology learns processes, then can suggest, and implement adjustments in real time to monitor and improve both efficiency and security.<br/><br/>The solutions we build push the envelope of what can be done, so we need engineers who are highly intelligent, hardworking, and creative. Flexibility and an ability to grasp high order concepts in physics, control theory, hardware design, automation, and robotics can be helpful for understanding and resolving specific issues.<br/><br/>The ideal candidate will be a self-starter, who work well with in fast-paced, ever-evolving environment.<br/><br/><strong>What We Offer<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Catered lunches every Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday</li><li>Company stock options</li><li>Comprehensive health benefits package, including, free dental, vision, short- and long-term disability and life insurance for you</li><li>401(k) match</li><li>Bright Horizons child/elder care benefits</li><li>Employer HSA contribution</li><li>Possible eligibility for city and state tax exemption under the START-UP NY program</li><li>Casual work environment</li><li>Direct exposure to C-level management</li><li>Interesting and challenging projects that truly make an impact at the company</li><li>Entrepreneurial spirit to grow, learn and thrive<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Application Process<br/><br/></strong>Please apply here. After submitting your application, our review process will begin with a review of your resume. If you meet or exceed the education, experience and required skills above, or demonstrate an equivalent to these things, you might be sent an assessment before being invited to participate in a phone interview. A successful assessment and/or phone interview will result in interviews with the hiring team, after which a decision about hiring will be made.<br/><br/><strong>What is Nanotronics?<br/><br/></strong>Nanotronics is an advanced machines and intelligence company that helps customers across the public, private, and nonprofit sectors solve for the unique inspection and process control challenges of precision manufacturing. A leading developer of optical inspection tools for the semiconductor industry, Nanotronics uses hardware and software to provide industrial-scale factory control solutions. Deployed across fifteen countries and industry agnostic, Nanotronics works with leading-edge companies, from aerospace, to electronics, to healthcare, to drive up yield, reduce footprint and waste, lower costs, and speed up design iteration, while eliminating laborious manual inspections.<br/><br/>We are an intellectually curious group of motivated and passionate innovators, bringing together the brightest minds in physics, optics, robotics, artificial intelligence, and software engineering as we work to lay the foundation for the 4th Industrial Revolution.<br/><br/>Armed with visionary leadership and strong financial backing, we foster an environment of both entrepreneurship and accountability.<br/><br/><strong>COVID-19 Vaccination Policy<br/><br/></strong>The essential functions of all positions at Nanotronics include a physical presence in the workplace, including but not limited to, in-person interactions with other employees, customers, and the community. Therefore, in accordance with Nanotronics’ duty to safeguard the health of our current employees and new hires, their families, our customers and visitors, our business and the community at large from COVID-19, all employees are required to present proof of vaccination and new hires must present proof of vaccination prior to their first day as a condition of employment. This policy will comply with all applicable laws and is based on guidance from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC), the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission (EEOC), and is designed to comply with all applicable federal, state, and local laws.<br/><br/><strong>Why We Work In The Office<br/><br/></strong>Our culture is built on innovation, quality and collaboration. We believe that working together and among other departments in person supports a sense of community, connection and belonging. So much of our communication as individuals is nonverbal—easier to discern and appreciate when we’re physically together. This synchrony not only gives us the power to innovate, but it helps us foster empathy, learning, and engagement. We want you to be a part of impromptu ideas dreamed and decisions made as people cross paths; to be in the right place at the right time to contribute your perspective; to look around and take pride in the diverse group of people with whom you work; to be seen by others when you’re recognized for a job well-done; and to look around, see things and help us build the best products and workplace we can. Our fast-paced environment pivots quickly, and we don’t want you to miss a thing.<br/><br/><strong>You’ll Make a Great Nanotron If<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>You are passionate about science, engineering, technology, and AI</li><li>You are looking for a challenge</li><li>You collaborate well with others</li><li>You are intellectually curious, eager to learn, and excited to contribute<br/><br/></li></ul>Visit our press page to learn more.<br/><br/><em>Nanotronics is an equal opportunity employer that is committed to diversity and inclusion in the workplace.
