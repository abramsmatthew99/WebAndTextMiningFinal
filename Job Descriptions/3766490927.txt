<ul><li>This position is located in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma and is not eligible for remote work ***<br/><br/></li></ul>The <strong>IT Specialist</strong> is primarily responsible for the management of the technology ticketing system and to align technology with the business goals of the organization. This individual will plan, coordinate, and design IT-related activities of the organization, as well as provide administrative support for daily operational activities of the IT department. The IT Manager will work closely with the IT Director to create and implement cost-effective technology solutions for all aspects of the organization. This person will support implementation of policies, procedures, and best practices.<br/><br/><strong>Principal Duties And Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Manages all aspects of the Technology Help Desk ticketing system and the 24/7 Technology Hotline</li><li>Logs and manages all IT assets and inventory, including warranties and replacement schedules.</li><li>Manages end-user creation and termination, including administration, security, and management for all database and business productivity tools.</li><li>Conducts weekly IT Orientation for all new staff members, including working with Director of IT to develop training material as identified by staff/end user need.</li><li>Creates and maintains documentation related to current and future state of IT Infrastructure</li><li>Manages technology service contracts – internet, telephone, mobile data, copier/printer, and all others as applicable to IT department.</li><li>Manages the deployment, monitoring, maintenance, development, upgrade, and support of all end user hardware.</li><li>Manages and record technology service contracts entered into by the Association and manage the renewal process.</li><li>Maintains working relationships with vendors and third-party providers, ensuring contracts are kept up to date; Abilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in the field of computer science, information systems or similar field preferred</li><li>2 years related experience in the field of Information Technology and Account Management preferred.</li><li>Understands and supports the Association’s mission, vision and values, always aspiring to serve the end user regardless of their technical skill.</li><li>Strong technical knowledge of network and PC operating systems, including Windows Server, Windows desktop operating systems and Cisco IOS</li><li>Strong technical knowledge of current network security, hardware, protocols, and standards, including data protection.</li><li>Knowledge of software and database systems management</li><li>Experience with VoIP phone systems and provisioning</li><li>Application support experience with Google Apps Suite and MS Office Suite</li><li>Proven experience in IT infrastructure planning and development and project management</li><li>Excellent understanding of the Association’s goals and objectives</li><li>Ability to adapt quickly to a dynamic workplace and has self-initiative.</li><li>Generates new ideas, challenges the status quo, takes risks, supports change, encourages innovation, solves problems quickly.</li><li>Recognizes problems and response, systematically gathers information, and sorts through complex issues.</li><li>Has an awareness of researches and adopts effective tools &amp;
