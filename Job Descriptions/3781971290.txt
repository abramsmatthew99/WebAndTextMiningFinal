        JT4, LLC provides engineering and technical support to multiple western test ranges for the U.S. Air Force, Space Force, and Navy under the Joint Range Technical Services Contract, better known as J-Tech II. JT4 develops and maintains realistic, integrated test and training environments and prepares our nation's war-fighting aircraft, weapons systems, and aircrews for today's missions and tomorrow's global challenges.<br/><br/><strong>Job Summary - Essential Functions/Duties<br/><br/></strong>JT4 is an engaging, family-oriented, fast-paced organization with multiple locations on the Western half of the United States. If you're a well-versed software developer who's capable of programming in multiple languages and ready to elevate your career to the next level, we have an opportunity that will be hard to pass up. A Computer Scientist provides software and systems engineering support to a variety of projects or customer efforts. The incumbent will be responsible to perform and demonstrate proficiency with the following functions/duties:   <br/><br/><ul><li> Proficiency in the following programming languages:</li><ul><li> Python</li><li> C++</li><li> Java</li><li> C#</li></ul><li> Proficiency with the following aircraft data:</li><ul><li> ICD's </li><li> DAR v3</li><li> 1553 data bus</li><li> 10</li><li> 8</li><li> 4 </li></ul><li> Proficiency in the following data analysis support activities:</li><ul><li> Building data analysis tools.</li><li> Reviewing and validating data conversion software.</li><li> Helping engineers validate and troubleshoot data discrepancies.</li><li> The ability to train engineers and data management staff on utilizing software applications for data processing.</li><li> The ability to develop flowcharts and diagrams to illustrate a sequence of steps that a program must follow and describe logical operations involved.</li><li> The ability to design and write basic to moderately complex computer programs.</li><li>Familiarity with systems engineering and software development lifecycles</li></ul><li>Perform other job-related duties, as required<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Requirements - Education, Technical And Work Experience<br/><br/></strong>A bachelor's degree in an associated discipline and either 2 years of related experience or a master's degree in an associated discipline are required for this position.<br/><br/>In addition, a Computer Scientist II must possess the following qualifications:<br/><br/><ul><li> Experience with computer-based systems and applications</li><li> Programming skills in languages used for job-specific programming tasks</li><li> Familiarity with the application of industry standard hardware and software design and documentation techniques<br/><br/></li></ul>The incumbent must possess a valid, state issued driver's license. Must be able to obtain and maintain security clearance.
