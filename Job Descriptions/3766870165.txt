This is a unique opportunity to change how drugs are developed and increase the speed of early drug discovery research. Interns will work with a team of AI Scientists at various levels and specialties and interact with Protein Engineers and Wet Lab Scientists.<br/><br/>Applications will be accepted until all positions are filled. Interns can choose to work in our New York, NY, Vancouver, WA offices or remotely. Internships will last 10-12 consecutive weeks. Identify and address data quality issues, propose next steps, and design experiments<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Working towards a BS, MS, or Ph.D. in computer science, biology, chemistry, physics, mathematics or a related field. Advanced degrees preferred</li><li>Experience working with large-scale protein design models such as AlphaFold2, ESMFold, RFDiffusion, etc</li><li>Experience with large language models (LLMs) and/or and geometric/3D deep learning</li><li>Proficiency in general-purpose programming languages. We primarily use Python and pytorch. </li><li>Proven ability to work collaboratively in a multidisciplinary team</li><li>Demonstrated experience in NGS data analysis and familiarity with FACS is a plus<br/><br/></li></ul>The salary range for this position is $8000 - $9200/month.
