<strong>Job Description<br/><br/></strong>We (Digital Member Experience team) are responsible for making Delta Dental of California's new Member Self Service and Provider Search experience delightful. We are looking for a proactive Quality Engineer interested in developing deep technical expertise in JavaScript based automation frameworks, strong problem-solving skills and a passion for quality.<br/><br/>The Quality Engineer will work alongside both business and technical team members to ensure the usability and functionality of products, services and systems. Your work will contribute to the team's overall strategic plan to maintain and improve production quality and processes by creating automation design and implementation using the approved automation test suites used in the organization.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Reviews requirements and specifications and provides timely feedback</li><li>Creates detailed and well-structured test plans and test cases</li><li>Prioritizes, plans and coordinates testing activities</li><li>Develops and maintains automated tests to execute the test plans and works to reduce the amount of manual testing by automating as much of the testing process as possible. </li><li>Executes test cases and / or automation scripts</li><li>Configures automated tests to run on demand and on-schedule and monitors the results of test runs to ensure that tests are running correctly and that environments are in order. </li><li>Identifies and documents defects thoroughly</li><li>Performs thorough regression testing</li><li>Installs, configures and maintains testing programs</li><li>Applies test standards and testing processes (i.e. SDLC, Agile)</li><li>Investigates and document the causes of routine defects</li><li>Tracks quality assurance metrics like defect totals, type and frequency and create test summary reports<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>2 to 5 years of professional experience specifically in software automation testing with Bachelor's degree in Computer Science or a closely related field. </li><li>3+ years of experience with software test automation tools and technologies such as: WebdriverIO, Protractor, Cypress, Pytest, xUnit, Unified Functional Testing, Postman, SOAPUI, Mockito, etc. </li><li>Proficient with JavaScript</li><li>Experience with CI/CD technologies such as Jenkins, Chef, Bamboo, etc. </li><li>Experience with SQL, XML and JSON</li><li>Experience with source code version control, Git, SVN, etc. </li><li>Experience using defect tracking systems, JIRA</li><li>Experience with test management and reporting tools, such as Quality Center, Zephyr or qTest</li><li>Experience working in an Agile environment</li><li>Experience in cross browser testing</li><li>Knowledge of QA principles, concepts, systems, industry practices, and standards. </li><li>Strong analytical and problem solving skills </li><li>Knowledge and understanding of software development life cycle (SDLC). </li><li>Ability to work independently with minimal supervision </li><li>Knowledge of root cause analysis techniques. </li><li>Excellent communication skills both written and verbal. </li><li>Strong time management and organizational
