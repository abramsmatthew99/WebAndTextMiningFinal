Associate's degree is preferred</li><li>Minimum two years of experience working in an office environment as a secretary or administrative assistant</li><li>Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite, especially Word, Excel, and Forms</li><li>Must be self-directed, flexible, and have excellent communication, organizational and interpersonal skills<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Perform general secretarial and clerical tasks such as the following:</li><ul><li>Answer phones and assist callers, transferring as necessary</li><li>Pick up and distribute office mail to both divisions</li><li>Prepare documents and memos for Chair and faculty as directed</li><li>Reserve rooms as needed for events</li><li>Collect syllabi and other course materials for Chair and faculty &amp;
