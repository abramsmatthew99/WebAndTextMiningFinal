You will work closely with cross-functional teams to define the product strategy, create a roadmap, and execute on it to ensure the success of our games in a live environment. If you are passionate about gaming and have a strong blend of technical and business skills, we want you to join our team and contribute to our mission of delivering top-tier gaming trends, player feedback, and competitive analysis.</li><li>Feature Definition: Work closely with cross-functional teams, including game designers, developers, artists, and QA, to define and prioritize monetization strategies.</li><li>Monetization Strategy: Develop and execute monetization strategies, including in-game purchases and virtual economies, while maintaining a fair and enjoyable player experience.</li><li>Competitive Analysis: Stay up-to-date with industry trends and competitors to ensure our titles remain competitive and innovative.</li><li>Team Collaboration: Work closely with cross-functional teams, including marketing, community management, and customer support, to coordinate efforts and deliver on project goals.</li><li>Player Engagement: Build and maintain strong relationships with the player community, gather feedback, and address concerns to enhance player satisfaction and retention.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>The Essentials<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g., business, computer science, game design) or equivalent experience.</li><li>1-3 years of experience in product management, preferably in the gaming industry.</li><li>Passion for gaming and a deep understanding of the gaming market, trends, and player preferences.</li><li>Strong analytical and data-driven decision-making skills.</li><li>Excellent communication and collaboration skills, with the ability to work effectively in cross-functional teams. Articulate and crisp in conveying the final message</li><li>Experience with project management tools and methodologies.</li><li>Knowledge of monetization strategies and virtual economies in gaming.</li><li>Ability to adapt to a fast-paced, dynamic environment and manage multiple priorities.</li><li>Problem-solving mindset and a proactive approach to challenges.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>The Nice to Haves<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Familiarity with live operations and game development processes is a plus. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>How We Get Things Done…<br/><br/></strong>This last bit is probably the most important!
