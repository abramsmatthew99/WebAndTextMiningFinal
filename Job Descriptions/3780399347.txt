        The Data Engineer is responsible for development and support for SFBLI’s enterprise data warehouse (EDW). This position will utilize various methods and technology to transform raw data into useful data systems. The Data Engineer will create and maintain all ETL processes for the EDW along with providing operational support in production. The Data Engineer will also work with business areas to help design and load data marts/data sets as needed.<br/><br/><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's Degree (Math, Computer Science, Information Systems, Statistics Preferred) or a combination of education and experience</li><li>Graduate Degree in Computer Science, Math, Statistics, Information Systems, Data Science preferred</li><li>Minimum of 2 years of experience in data engineering or comparable programming/design experience</li><li>Experience working at Southern Farm Bureau Life in a role requiring an understanding of the underlying data for various SFB systems preferred.</li><li>Experience with ETL process, database design and deploying/supporting in a production environment</li><li>Advanced working SQL knowledge and experience working with relational databases as well as working familiarity with a variety of databases</li><li>Experience performing root cause analysis on internal data and processes to answer specific business issues and identify opportunities for improvement</li><li>The ability to establish measurable outcomes and create processes for monitoring progress towards established goals.</li><li>The ability to make well-informed, effective and timely decisions, even when data is limited or solutions produce unpleasant consequences; perceive the impact and implications of decisions.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Builds, enhances, and scales out infrastructure to ingest, process and extract meaning from data</li><li>Collaborates with analytics and business teams to improve data models that feed business intelligence tools, increasing data accessibility and fostering data-driven decision making across the organization</li><li>Implements policies and procedures that ensure production database-related issues (EDW) are handled in a timely manner</li><li>Evaluates new data development, ensuring consistency and providing for integration with existing warehouse structure.</li><li>Identifies opportunities for process improvement and additional data sources throughout the company</li><li>Assures compliance with regulatory requirements governing privacy, data collection, and reporting</li><li>Assures that all data processes adhere to the Data Governance guidelines<br/><br/></li></ul>At Southern Farm Bureau Life Insurance Company, our Mission is to be the life insurance company of choice for our Farm Bureau family. Our focus is to provide competitive products and superior customer service to our Farm Bureau policyholders and agents, while observing the highest ethical standards. Southern Farm Bureau Life Insurance Company continues to build on over 75 years of success with an outlined strategic vision, defined core values and above all, our employees. Our Company strives to provide a workplace with an outstanding culture, focusing on the development, growth and engagement of its employees.<br/><br/>We are looking for candidates who model our Company’s Core Values which are:<br/><br/><ul><li>COMMITMENT TO SERVICE - recognizes that serving our agents, policyholders, and employees is the foundation of our success</li><li>RESPECT FOR PEOPLE - values individual’s unique talents, backgrounds, and experiences</li><li>INTEGRITY- upholds the highest ethical standards in all of their actions</li><li>ACCOUNTABILITY FOR RESULTS - commits to getting the job done by taking ownership of their work</li><li>COMMITMENT TO TEAMWORK - understands, appreciates, and respects each individual's roles on their teams</li><li>COURAGE TO ACT - acts in the best interest of the Company</li></ul>
