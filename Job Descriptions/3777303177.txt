<p><strong>SUMMARY: </strong></p><p>Responsible for project planning, coordinating, and supervising the activities of the Applications Development Group in the Information Technology Department involving the analysis, design, development, testing, and implementation of information systems and software applications. Also responsible for maintaining, supporting, and upgrading existing systems and applications as well as database administration. </p><p><br/></p><p><strong>ESSENTIAL FUNCTIONS AND RESPONSIBILITIES</strong> include the following. Other duties may be assigned. </p><p>• Supervises, coordinates, and prioritizes the schedules, projects, and activities of the software &amp; ATM development team. </p><p>• Applies communication and problem-solving skills, and knowledge of best practices to guide team on issues related to the design, development, and deployment of mission-critical information and software systems. </p><p>• Consults with end users and peers to analyze requirements, problems, and procedures, evaluate alternatives, and specify preferred solutions to automate processing or to improve existing systems. </p><p>• Creates functional requirements, technical and design specification documents, using flow charts and diagrams into sequence of detailed instructions and logical steps for coding into computer processable language for the use of Web Programmers and Analysts. </p><p>• Troubleshoots application systems problems and performs web and client server maintenance programming and testing in support of the company's internet, intranet, and extranet applications. </p><p>• Conducts system walk-throughs and trains staff on the use of new and modified programs and applications. </p><p>• Handles database upgrades, capacity planning, and fine tuning. </p><p>• Utilizes database design and management tools and apply entity-relationship design expertise to create and maintain relational database applications. </p><p>• Utilizes various programming languages to create online forms or create interfaces to existing applications that link users to databases. </p><p>• Strictly adheres to new and modified code staging and testing guidelines in close coordination with the IT Network and Desktop Support Group. </p><p>• Regular attendance and punctuality as a full-time employee in order to daily exercise all of the essential functions of the position. </p><p><br/></p><p><strong>ESSENTIAL SKILLS AND ABILITIES </strong></p><p>Must have strong verbal and written communication skills, as well as excellent planning and organizational skills. Must have the ability to work with individuals of all levels with varying technical skills. Ability to work under pressure with minimal supervision, managing multiple projects simultaneously. Working knowledge of Microsoft Windows Operating System, Outlook, Internet Explorer, Excel, Word, and PowerPoint. Expert knowledge of Microsoft SQL, IIS, ASP. Net, JAVA, Visual Studio, DevOps, and BI tools. To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions. Directly supervises applications development personnel in the IT Department. Carries out supervisory responsibilities in accordance with the organization's policies and applicable laws. Responsibilities include interviewing, hiring, and training employees; planning, assigning, and directing work; appraising performance; rewarding and disciplining employees; addressing complaints and resolving issues. </p><p><br/></p><p> </p><p><strong>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE</strong></p><p>Bachelor's or Master’s Degree in Information Technology, Computer Science or related required; or equivalent combination of education and experience. MCP, MCDBA, and/or equivalent certification a plus. 1 to 2 years of supervisory experience preferred. </p><p><br/></p><p><strong>PHYSICAL DEMANDS AND WORK ENVIRONMENT</strong></p><p>The physical demands and work environment characteristics described here are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of the job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions. </p><p><br/></p><p>Physical Requirements: Sitting, 5-8 hours per shift; talking, hearing and eye/hand/foot coordination, lift up to 10 lbs. up to 2 hours per shift. The following activities are performed, but rarely: standing, walking, lifting, carrying, pushing, pulling, climbing, balancing, stooping, kneeling, crouching, crawling, reaching, handling, grasping, feeling, and repetitive motions. Does not require strenuous physical effort. Overall work time is spent 95% in the office and 5% outside of the office. </p><p><br/></p><p>Mental Requirements: While performing duties of this job, employee is regularly required to do the following: read, do simple arithmetic, analyze data or report information, create methodologies for accomplishing a goal, implement recommendations by coordinating persons and/or other resources and direct the activities of others to accomplish a goal 5-8 hours per shift; writing, drawing conclusions from written or computer generated materials, developing plans, procedures, goals, strategies or processes based on data analysis or experience and evaluating the performance of others, 2-5 hours per shift. The following activities are performed, but rarely: Weighing and/or measuring. </p><p><br/></p><p>Work Environment: While performing duties of this job, employee is rarely exposed to extreme cold, extreme heat, temperature changes, wet or humid conditions, noise, vibration, hazards or atmospheric conditions. </p><p><br/></p><p><strong>SERVICE STANDARDS</strong></p><p>• Maintain a highly effective team by developing each employee to their highest potential through coaching and leadership.</p><p>• Responsible for ensuring that exceptional service is delivered to both internal and external clients by "doing what's right for credit unions." </p><p>• Demonstrate enthusiastic support of corporate mission, core values and long-term objectives. </p><p>• Perform job duties within our policies procedures, service standards and state and federal laws.</p>
