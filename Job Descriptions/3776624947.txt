<p><strong><em><u>Cyber Security Business Analyst 2 (HYBRID)</u></em></strong></p><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Our client, a leading Pacific Northwest utility provider committed to public service and environmental preservation, is looking for a Business Analyst for their <strong>Vancouver, Washington</strong> location. This position will provide project management and change control associated with several compliance and cybersecurity initiatives: Firmware tracking, Vulnerability assessments and password management; as well as providing detailed analysis to determine where equipment is located and how much of a risk the company has for non-compliance with NERC requirements and standards. This role also entails identifying the extent of the missing data in the system, as well as developing and implementing new processes or procedures to track compliance. Because there are limited existing databases, the BA in this position will need to utilize creative approaches to compliance documentation and verification. In addition, this position must provide recommendations for solutions to problems or gaps in data. </p><p><br/></p><p><strong>This position is a one-year renewable contract that includes employee benefits! If you think you’d be a good fit, we want to hear from you!</strong></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Pay Range</strong>: 45.00 – 48.00</p><p><strong>Location: Vancouver, WA. (Hybrid)</strong></p><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Key Responsibilities for Business Analyst:</strong></p><ul><li>Coordinate activities for manager-assigned projects that enable goals or objectives to be accomplished within the prescribed time frame and funding parameters.</li><li>Propose and monitor tasks and deadlines associated with new regulatory requirements, compliance violations, and process changes.</li><li>Build project sites, workflows, and document libraries in SharePoint.</li><li>Schedule meetings, prepare agendas, take, and distribute minutes, and track action items.</li><li>Monitor new and modified equipment to verify that configurations comply with requirements.</li><li>Prepare plans to verify that compliance requirements are defined and documented, compliance plans are formally approved, and milestones are accomplished.</li><li>Interview Field personnel to identify what equipment is installed in the field, update system information as appropriate.</li><li>Design, draft and recommend forms, reports and/or tools to track progress and verify completion of mitigation activities for compliance violations.</li><li>Perform research in asset and maintenance databases to gather and organize information (i.e., recent maintenance, missing data, and trouble reports).</li><li>Assemble data from multiple sources and utilize advanced Excel, Power BI, or reporting applications to analyze data for potential violations, and to present data in a user-friendly format for evidence reviews.</li><li>Mark documents and maintain filing system(s), files, emails, and records in accordance with compliance requirements.</li><li>Monitor and track versions and revisions of specialized software and firmware, verifying that updates are authenticated and processed in a timely manner.</li><li>Prepare for external and internal audits: searching databases, running reports, compiling evidence into electronic packets to be sent to auditors in advance of audits, compiling physical notebooks for auditors that contain evidence of who visited a substation, attending audit interviews, taking notes, and pulling reports.</li><li>Create and improve processes used by immediate team for efficiency, consistency, and quality management.</li><li>Study existing procedures and processes; document findings and prepare recommendations for new systems or procedures to facilitate compliance with requirements; assist personnel with implementation of management-approved recommendations.</li><li>Gather and document requirements.</li><li>Develop tools, such as spreadsheets, Access databases, Power BI, and SharePoint sites to support implementation of approved solutions, plans or efforts.</li><li>Assist with drafting new or revising existing processes and procedures as requested.</li><li>Monitor project schedules to track completed tasks and forecast additional work that may be required.</li><li>Arrange, coordinate, and facilitate requirements elicitation workshops and present findings to appropriate manager / personnel.</li><li>Analyze, facilitate, model, and organize information to elicit requirements; work with appropriate parties to facilitate resolution of conflicting team positions; and distinguish solution ideas from business needs.</li><li>Decompose high-level information into details and abstract up from low-level information to assist with resolution of solution vs. requirements conflicts.</li><li>Import data from various sources such as databases, SharePoint lists, and technical drawings; manipulate the data into automated or manually repeatable reports to be used for evidence reviews.</li><li>Facilitate resolution of project priorities and requirement conflicts among various stakeholders and project team members.</li><li>Complete documentation for project development efforts (including use cases, requirements, test plans, process maps, and desk procedures).</li><li>Compile, review and analyze information in order to collaborate with team members to, create/draft technical documentation to support system requirements, system design, system security, and operational manuals for a wide variety of software applications.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Requirements for Business Analyst: </strong></p><ul><li>A degree in Business Management, Business Administration, Computer Science, Cyber Security, Engineering, Organizational Development, or a closely related field is preferred. </li><li>5+ years’ experience w/bachelor’s degree.</li><li>7 years’ experience w/associate degree.</li><li>9 years' experience w/no degree.</li><li>5+ years of work experience in Information Technology business analysis or Cyber-Security business analysis.</li><li>Advanced computer skills and knowledge of automated data systems.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p>**We are unable to accommodate corp. to corp. candidates**</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>About Motus Recruiting and Staffing, Inc:</strong></p><p><em>Founded in 2006, Motus is an award-winning recruiting and staffing firm in the Pacific Northwest,
