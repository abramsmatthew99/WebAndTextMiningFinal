Responsibilities will include the professionalization and integration of software developed by graduate students/postdocs/MLPDS team members; maintaining and continuing the back-end development of a modern web application for submitting and processing long-running ML tasks and storing, retrieving, visualizing, and analyzing results; defining and codifying reproducible and transferable ELT pipelines for training ML models on chemical data; preparing scripts for facilitating the automatic retraining and deployment of ML models on user-provided chemical and reaction datasets; identifying shortcomings of existing models and researching new machine learning models with improved performance for various chemistry tasks; packaging the application into containerized microservices for deployment using Docker and Kubernetes; developing benchmarking tests; serving as a technical expert, helping transfer software to member companies, and troubleshooting/debugging issues; maintaining a development roadmap; assisting with documentation/manuals; maintaining and improving CI/CD pipelines for software testing/release/deployment; and tracking MIT-company interactions/software.
