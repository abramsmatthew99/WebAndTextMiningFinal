vulnerabilities, plan for, and implement remediation actions. </li><li>Investigate security events using logs, SIEM solutions, EDR solutions and other security tools to identify and prevent incidents. needed by our clients. </li><li>You will consult with clients and internal teams regarding security controls and baseline security for operating systems, database, cloud solutions and applications. help desk tickets. </li><li>You will work collaboratively with teams of smart, thoughtful people to best meet the needs of our clients. </li><li>You will work in and solve issues in modern service skills, both written and oral. </li><li>You should be able to explain just about anything technical to anyone, regardless of their background. </li><li>In addition to Security+, you should </li><li>You should be familiar with HIPAA, PCI DSS and other cybersecurity frameworks such as NIST CSF, NIST 800/53, ISO 2700x, and SOC. </li><li>Possess the ability to draft content related to policies, standards, and procedures in alignment with cybersecurity frameworks. </li><li>Create and execute audit plans. </li><li>Monitor and maintain security controls in support of our clients’ environments. </li><li>Possess knowledge of operating systems security features. </li><li>Possess knowledge of network and device security features. </li><li>Be able to execute vulnerability scanning with tools such as Qualys, RapidFire and provide in-depth analysis of results. </li><li>Possess knowledge on modern authentication and identity protection offerings such as Azure AD, Google Cloud Identity, JumpCloud, etc. </li><li>Have experience investigating security incidents with firewalls, SIEMs, and endpoints. </li><li>Familiarity and knowledge of Zero Trust
