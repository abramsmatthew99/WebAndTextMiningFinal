The successful candidate will be responsible for overseeing all IT operations, ensuring the efficient and effective use of technology to support our organization's objectives and drive business growth.</p><p><br/></p><p>Responsibilities:</p><p>1. Strategic Planning:</p><p>o Develop and execute the IT strategy aligned with the organization's goals.</p><p>o Collaborate with executive management to identify and prioritize IT initiatives that enhance business processes, increase efficiency, and drive innovation.</p><p>o Stay up-to-date with emerging technologies and industry trends relevant to the retail sector, and provide guidance on their potential application within the organization.</p><p><br/></p><p>2. Infrastructure Management:</p><p>o Oversee the design, implementation, and maintenance of a reliable and secure IT infrastructure, including networks, servers, storage, and other critical systems.</p><p>o Ensure high availability, scalability, and performance of infrastructure components, addressing any issues or vulnerabilities promptly.</p><p>o Evaluate and select appropriate hardware, software, and cloud solutions, negotiating contracts with vendors to optimize IT operations and reduce costs.</p><p>o Experience with AWS or Azure Cloud.</p><p><br/></p><p>3. Application Development:</p><p>o Lead the application development team, guiding them in the design, development, testing, and deployment of software applications.</p><p>o Collaborate with stakeholders to identify business requirements, define project scopes, and manage application development projects within established timelines and budgets.</p><p>o Promote software development best practices, quality assurance, and adherence to industry standards, ensuring the delivery of reliable, secure, and user-friendly applications.</p><p><br/></p><p>4. eCommerce Systems:</p><p>o Utilize your deep understanding of the Financial and eCommerce Industry to evaluate, implement, and enhance eCommerce systems, ensuring their seamless integration with existing infrastructure and software applications.</p><p>o Collaborate with cross-functional teams, such as finance, operations, and sales, to identify system requirements and drive continuous improvement.</p><p><br/></p><p>5. Team Leadership:</p><p>o Provide strong leadership to the IT team, fostering a collaborative and innovative work environment.</p><p>o Recruit, develop, and retain top IT talent, building a skilled and motivated team capable of meeting current and future business needs.</p><p>o Mentor team members, providing guidance and support for their professional growth and fostering a culture of learning and knowledge sharing.</p><p><br/></p><p>Qualifications and Skills:</p><p>• Bachelor's or master's degree in computer science, information technology, or a related field.</p><p>• Proven experience as an IT Director within the Financial or eCommerce industry.</p><p>• Strong knowledge and understanding of IT infrastructure, including networks, servers, storage, security, and cloud technologies.</p><p>• Extensive experience in application development, with a track record of managing complex projects and delivering high-quality software applications.</p><p>• Deep understanding of POS and lease retail systems, including lease accounting, contract management, and reporting.</p><p>• Familiarity with emerging technologies and trends, such as mobile payments, omnichannel retail, and e-commerce.</p><p>• Strong leadership and management skills, with the ability to motivate and inspire a diverse team.</p><p>• Excellent problem-solving and decision-making abilities, coupled with a strategic mindset.</p><p>• Outstanding communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively with stakeholders at all levels.</p>
