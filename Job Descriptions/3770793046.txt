Required knowledge, skills and abilities: Strong attention to detail; Ability to develop creative solutions to complex problems and develop critical thinking skills; Strong communication (verbal and written), teamwork and follow-up skills; Skilled in using Microsoft software, specifically Office and SharePoint. Familiarity with Meaningful Use (ARRA) as well as HIPAA, JCAHO and other regulations that impact clinical practice preferred.
