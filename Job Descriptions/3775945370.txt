<p><strong>Innovate to solve the world's most important challenges</strong></p><p><br/></p><p>The System Engineer Manager reports into Director – IGS Project Engineering excellence leader and is responsible for successful engineering execution and engineering management of all IGS projects assigned to them. This role is also responsible to ensure time bound quality engineering deliverables meeting the customers’ expectations, driving cost, right tools and processes, value engineering &amp; thus supporting in achieving business results - Revenue, GM, OI, and Cash flow to plan.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Major Areas Of Responsibilities</strong></p><ul><li>Establish professional relationships and communications with customers, Program directors, Operational leaders, Project Managers, project engineering discipline leaders, concept &amp; estimation, ISC and Install team leaders.</li><li>Provide day-to-day consultation, leadership and direction to the systems engineering group comprising professional engineering staff. Assign System engineering supervisors, system engineers to projects and manage the day-to-day resource conflicts. Also encourage by example and coaching, a highly professional approach to all other engineering activities.</li><li>Drive culture of change identification, change management, technical risk identification &amp; mitigation, value engineering ideas, techniques &amp; solutions within and across the system &amp; project engineering organization.</li><li>Drive efficiencies in the project execution methodology to bring down cost and or increase the value delivered to the customer. Develop and enhance the work processes and procedure as required.</li><li>Actively reviews, monitors, and controls the projects health &amp; the readiness compliance from the engineering perspective for the phase gate reviews. Ensures 100% compliance to phase gate reviews and all deliverables are submitted to the customer on time. Participate in monthly project reviews with Project managers / Program managers.</li><li>Ensures that required prerequisites (e.g., design inputs, tools, documentation, deliverables, schedule, budget, low-cost engineering content) are in place prior to commencing the work.</li><li>Ensures that the engineered solution meets project requirements in terms of functionality, standards, quality, and supporting documentation.</li><li>Drives billability of the system engineering group, project cost forecast accuracy, monitors engineering hours &amp; financials to ensure projects are completed within agreed engineering cost budget, any EAC changes due to engineering are logged in CORA and have been approved before the EAC variance is shown in the forecast.</li><li>Ensure common methods, procedures, techniques, tools, and products as agreed by the knowledge communities / Leaders are being effectively applied and provide feedback for future improvement. Ensures skill deployment, training &amp; knowledge transfer within the system engineering group and ensures processes &amp; procedures are updated with lessons learned on regular basis.</li><li>Establish and maintains good working relationships with numerous personnel in management and engineering all of whom are greatly dependent on the system engineering organization insights, analytical ability, and judgment.</li><li>Escalates customer feedback, internal/external risks, and schedule changes within the organization and work closely with Project managers / Program managers for possible mitigation.</li><li>Ensures customer feedbacks are collected on all the IGS projects at various customer touch points (KOM, design, Training, SAT etc.) of the project during project engineering execution.</li><li>Ensure project teams work in compliance with quality / safety procedures. Responsible for adherence to HSE Policy and Guideline</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>YOU MUST HAVE</strong></p><ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in engineering (System Engineering /Computer Science / Instrumentation / Information Technology /</li><li>Electrical / Mechanical Engineering or any other equivalent)</li><li>7-10 years of hands-on engineering management experience in managing large &amp; complex Wearhouse automation projects (Project value above $20M) / material handling projects.</li><li>Minimum 5 years of experience as a people leader of global teams, and experience of interacting with senior executive leaders in a corporate environment.</li><li>Areas of expertise needed in Leadership &amp; Team Development, Engineering management, Customer relationship management, Process Improvement, Procurement / Sub-contract management, Technical Risk Management.</li><li>Excellent interpersonal skills with strong verbal &amp; written communication skills</li><li>Ability to travel 15-20% of the time.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>WE VALUE</strong></p><ul><li>Drive for Results: The ability to demonstrate energy and determination to achieve results and improvement by overcoming obstacles, remaining effective despite setbacks, and challenging current thinking.</li><li>Change Leader: The ability to embrace change and guide the team through periods of change in a positive and proactive way.</li><li>Demonstrated ability to lead a diverse engineering team and have a solid track record of delivering high complex projects.</li><li>Strong presentation and communication skills to manage multi levels of internal stakeholders and influence the teams.</li><li>Coaching and Developing Others: The ability to enhance the performance of individuals and teams through setting challenging performance standards, providing constructive feedback on performance, and collaboratively discussing improvement strategies.</li><li>Experience in operate in a true multi-cultural and international organization, collaborating with culturally diverse customers/partners.</li><li>Organized and able to manage multiple priorities effectively.</li><li>Effectively demonstrates ability to deliver on complex situations or problem. Creative and collaborative problem - solving capability.</li><li>Comfort with developing solutions in ambiguous environments. Consistently makes timely decisions even in the face of complexity. </li></ul><p><br/></p><p>Additional Information</p><ul><li>JOB ID: HRD209081</li><li>Category: Engineering</li><li>Location: 7901 Innovation Way,Mason,Ohio,45040,United States</li><li>Exempt</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>Global (ALL)</p><p>Honeywell is an equal opportunity employer.
