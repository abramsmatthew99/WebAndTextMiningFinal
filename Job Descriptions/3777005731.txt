<p><strong>Software Engineer II (Java Backend Development)</strong></p><p><strong>6 Month Contract-to-Hire (W-2, Full-Time Hours, Paid Weekly)</strong></p><p><strong>100% Remote (Must be available to work 9AM-5PM CST) </strong></p><p><br/></p><p>As a Java Backend Developer, you will be developing new backend functionalities to enhance the customer buying experience. You will be working on a team with 7-8 software engineers within the Supply Chain Technology – Delivery Systems organization. This team is collectively building out innovative solutions to improve the visibility and tracking operations for product delivery, and you will play a critical role in developing and testing the backend systems and services. This is a highly modern and Agile environment following test driven development (TDD) with a Fortune Top 20 company leading in the specialty retail industry.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Required:</strong></p><ul><li>1-2 years developing, testing, and deploying production-grade Java backend applications.</li><li>Experience building scalable microservices using Spring Boot and RESTful APIs.</li><li>Cassandra or another NoSQL database management system.</li><li>Experience deploying applications to Google Cloud Platform (GCP) or similar cloud platform.</li><li>Preferred exposure to Kubernetes, Elasticsearch, Groovy, Prometheus, and Micronaut. </li><li>Bachelor of Science in Computer Science.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Responsibilities:</strong></p><ul><li>Develop, test, and deploy backend systems and services using Java.</li><li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design and implement scalable and reliable solutions that improves visibility and tracking for product delivery.</li><li>Design and implement data models and databases for efficient data storage and retrieval, contributing to enhanced product tracking capabilities.</li><li>Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code that aligns with the team's focus on improved product visibility and tracking.</li><li>Follow test driven development (TDD) practices.</li><li>Lead and participate in code reviews.</li><li>Ensure the security, scalability, and performance of high-volume backend systems.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Why should you be interested?</strong></p><ul><li>Gain experience working on one of the largest supply chain transformations with a Fortune 500 company recognized on Forbes’ lists of World’s Best Employers and Most Innovative Companies.</li><li>Develop in a true Agile and TDD environment, working with some of the best open-source technologies on the market with access to continuous learning opportunities.</li><li>All of the IT development is done in-house, leading to a consistent track record of growth for employees.</li><li>Receive a weekly paycheck, holiday pay, and the option to enroll into health benefits (medical, dental, vision, life insurance).</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><em>*W-2 employment only (no corp-to-corp or third-party vendors).</em></strong></p>
