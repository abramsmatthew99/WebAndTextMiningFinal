<p>Are you looking for an opportunity that impacts people in your neighborhood?</p><p>Do you have an engineering, architectural and product acumen that can drive an enterprise solution for the betterment of lives?</p><p>Are you willing to grow your talent by partnering with stakeholders from various verticals and building products that need a wholistic perspective?</p><p>Do you feel empowered by feeding the spirit and impacting all the communities where we as Kroger make a difference using the acumen of talented and authentically driven technology champions like you?</p><p>If any of these questions motivate your passion to create deliverables that guide the direction, development, and delivery of software solutions that meet business needs and most of all our customer needs, this is the exact opportunity for you. This is a highly collaborative role requiring technical ownership and product alignment along with leadership in analysis, design and implementation of tasks required to deliver critical business solutions. You will partner with engineering team, product management, architecture, information security and infrastructure teams to deliver software and technology products that meet requirements and produce desired business outcomes by adhering to established architectural principles and associated processes for adopting new technologies or integrating with a new third-party solution. You will advise product management on technology specific aspects of product roadmap development and recommend adoption of emerging technology appropriate to stated goals. You will be expected to demonstrate Kroger’s core values of respect, honesty, integrity, diversity, inclusion, and safety.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p><ul><li>Develop a high-quality codebase, collaborate on design discussions, execute development against design, and navigate complex codebase</li><li>Work closely with various teams in the domain of impact and strategy</li><li>Ensure the implementation of enterprise standards across domains: operations, infrastructure, data, applications, development, security &amp; risk, and business continuance</li><li>Provide technical leadership to ensure clarity between ongoing initiatives and the strategic objectives of the company</li><li>Drive digital innovation by leveraging innovative new technologies and approaches to renovate, extend, and transform the existing core technology base and IT estate</li><li>Work with the team and leadership to design implantation plans for high-level </strong></li><li><strong>Mentor team members in software development principles, patterns, processes and, practices </strong></li><li><strong>Promote the capture and reuse of intellectual capital, including code objects and components </strong></li><li><strong>Draft and review architectural diagrams, interface specifications, class structures and, other design documents </strong></p><ul><li>Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science or in a STEM major</li><li>8+ of software development experience.</li><li>6+ years of successful and applicable hands-on experience in high-quality systems</li><li>Familiarity and Experience in many of the following: - API First - REST - Java - Spring Boot - Azure - Cosmos DB - Redis - SQL Server - Docker – Kubernetes - Kafka</li><li>Senior level experience on Java development, object-oriented programming, advanced software engineering practices, and utilization of industry standard design patterns.</li><li>Extensive Experience in building and integrating with RESTful APIs and a deep understanding of REST principles.</li><li>Experience and understanding of Event Driven Design and Implementation using technologies like Kafka, Solace, etc.</li><li>Experience in building solutions with monitoring, logging, tracing, and visualization supporting a robust structure of debugging and mitigating customer issues in live products and services</li><li><strong>Demonstrated written, oral, and presentation/public speaking communication skills; along with ability to contribute at a strategic level. </strong></li><li>Experience being a contributor and a mentor in a team that has built or maintained high volume, highly available, fault tolerant applications/services that are embedded in a larger infrastructure.</li></ul><p><strong>Bonus Qualifications </strong></p><ul><li>Retail operations and/or Fleet Management systems is a plus.</li><li>Shipping/Carrier/Last Mile Delivery integration software experience also a plus.</li><li>Experience in cloud environments (preferably Azure), including good understanding of cloud architecture, functions, Logic Apps, Serverless architecture, application development and containerization are a big plus but not required.</li></ul><p>· Any experience in Infrastructure as Code (Cloud specific) is preferred but not required.</p><ul><li>Experience with using Kubernetes on Cloud, with CI/CD for highly dynamic and scalable solutions is preferred.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Company Overview: </strong> Kroger Family of Companies employs nearly half a million associates who serve over 11 million customers daily through a seamless shopping experience under a variety of banner names.
