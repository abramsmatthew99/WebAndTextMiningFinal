Use results from research and experimentation to support architecture or product direction.</li><li>Identify areas of dependency and overlap with other teams or team members. Provide constructive input so the work is integrated and timely. Communicate the status of deliverables and dependencies to meet schedule.</li><li>Engage in measures to protect tools, techniques, information, and results of security practices. Specific start dates are shared during the recruiting process.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Skills, Experience, Education, &amp; Training<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Current enrollment in Computer Science with Cybersecurity major degree-seeking program at the Master’s level. Bachelor’s level students would also be considered based on specific relevant experience.</li><li>Experience programming with Java, Python, JavaScript, or other related object-oriented programming languages</li><li>Demonstrated interest in the security field through academic coursework, previous work or internship experience, or personal projects.</li><li>Comfortable writing code to accomplish goals and does not depend solely on third-party products.</li><li>Have an interest in software engineering demonstrated by previous internships, work experience, projects, or publications.</li><li>Knowledge of data structures, how they are implemented and how to apply them to meet security challenges.</li><li>Familiarity with the application of common design patterns<br/><br/></li></ul>Additional Information<br/><br/>Due to the nature of our business and the role, please note that Intuitive and/or your customer(s) may require that you show current proof of vaccination against certain diseases including COVID-19.
