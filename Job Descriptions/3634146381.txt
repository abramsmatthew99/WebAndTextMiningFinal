<strong>Job Details</strong><p><br/></p><strong><u>Description</u></strong><p><br/></p><strong>School Summary</strong><p><br/></p>Great River Connections Academy (GRCA) is a tuition-free public online eSchool serving students in grades K-12 throughout Ohio beginning in the 2018-19 school year. GRCA is authorized by the Ohio Council of Community Schools and governed by an independent Board of Directors. The school is operated through a contract with Connections Academy of Ohio, LLC, to provide its educational program and other services. Connections Academy, a division of Connections Education, is accredited by Cognia.<p><br/></p><strong><u>Position Summary And Responsibilities</u></strong><p><br/></p>The CTE Teacher will be responsible for the successful completion of the following tasks:<p><br/></p><ul><li>Complete all grading, create progress reports and conduct parent conferences in a timely manner;</li><li>Support the instructional program with asynchronous web conferencing sessions and synchronous instruction;</li><li>Review curriculum and devise alternate approaches to presenting lessons to increase student understanding (working directly with students and parents);</li><li>Communicate with parents, students and other teachers on a regular basis to develop and update Personal Learning Plans and schedules, score assessments, provide feedback on student work, suggest instructional approaches and strategies, monitor completion of assignments and coach special projects;</li><li>Work collaboratively with other teachers to ensure that all students are successfully progressing through the program, that parents have a central point of contact, and that tasks are distributed among the teachers;</li><li>Develop a general knowledge of the entire program’s K-12 curriculum and a very detailed knowledge of the courses for which responsible; </li><li>Support students and parents with alternate strategies and provide additional assistance with daily assignments and projects;</li><li>Communicate regularly with parents, students, and curriculum specialists through use of computer and telephone;</li><li>Keep student records and data up-to-date, including cumulative files, online student and family information, attendance accounting, and logging all student and parent contacts;</li><li>Consult with other teachers and staff learning specialists to develop alternate enrichment activities and modifications to students’ programs to increase student understanding;</li><li>Work with other teachers to coordinate social activities and relevant field trips for students;</li><li>Manage regional field trips and make efforts to integrate trips into the curriculum; </li><li>Devise and implement virtual methods of creating and maintaining a “school community”;</li><li>Participate in the organization and administration of the State Testing, as directed;</li><li>Participate in student recruiting sessions and other marketing efforts that require teacher representation;</li><li>Work with Advisory Teachers and School Counselors to ensure students and families are receiving appropriate communications, students are making adequate progress and established goals are being met;</li><li>Attend field trips and other community activities implemented for families; and</li><li>Other duties as assigned.</li></ul><p><br/></p><strong><u>Requirements</u></strong><p><br/></p><ul><li>Highly qualified and certified to teach CTE Computer Technology and Science (appropriate to content area and grade level responsibilities)</li><li>Experience teaching applicable CTE courses</li><li>Strong technology skills (especially with Microsoft Office products and Google Suite)</li><li>Excellent communication skills, both oral and written</li><li>Highly organized and punctual</li><li>Customer focused approach</li><li>High degree of flexibility</li><li>Demonstrated ability to work well in fast paced environment</li><li>Team player track record</li><li>Willingness to travel for marketing and state testing events (may require occasional overnight travel)</li><li>Ability to work some occasional evening hours, as needed to support some families</li><li>Ability to work remotely</li></ul><p><br/></p><strong> Application Contingency: ANY PERSON WHO KNOWLINGLY MAKES A FALSE STATEMENT IS GUILTY OF A FALSIFICATION UNDER SECTION 2921.13 OF THE OHIO REVISED CODE, WHICH IS A MISDEMEANOR OF THE FIRST DEGREE. </strong><p><br/></p><strong> Want to know more? Please watch our new On Demand Teacher Information Session and join us for a Q&amp;A session held every other Wednesday at 5:00 p.m. Eastern. We hope to see you there!</strong>
