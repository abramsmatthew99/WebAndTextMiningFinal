        What you'll do<br/><br/>When our square shaped burgers made their first sizzle on the scene more than 50 years ago, people knew our approach wasn’t like any other. Same goes for the way we support our employees. Our culture of openness, flexibility, and inclusiveness allows everybody to flourish in their own way. If you’re looking for a career where you can be part of the action as we continue to grow our iconic brand - We got you!<br/><br/>The <strong>System Administrator - Payment Systems</strong> will provide leadership, supervision and oversight of activities related to maintaining accurate administration of Linux based restaurant payment systems, including estate management, performance and compliance, fraud management, and system configuration. In this role, you will supervise a team of analysts, ensuring seamless operations, compliance, and efficient problem analysis. You will serve as a SME on payment processes and requirements while ensuring smooth implementation of payment-related changes, including both system updates and process improvements.<br/><br/><strong>This position will have a hybrid work schedule, the selected candidate can work remotely, but would be required to come into our office in Dublin, OH as needed for testing purposes on a monthly basis. <br/><br/></strong><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Estate Management:</li><li></li><ul><li>Oversee chain of custody asset tracking and serial number validation processes to maintain accurate and up-to date records of assets across restaurants and geographies</li><li>Ensure PCI compliance and adherence to P2PE implementation manual guidelines, conducting regular audits and implementing necessary corrective actions</li><li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams to streamline asset management procedures, reducing discrepancies and improving efficiency</li></ul><li>System Performance &amp; Compliance:</li><ul><li>Monitor system performance, manage alerts, and promptly respond to system incidents to maintain optimal payment processing operations</li><li>Conduct performance analysis and log analysis to identify trends, bottlenecks, and potential issues, proactively recommending and implementing improvements</li><li>Lead documentation and reporting efforts, providing clear insights into system performance and compliance metrics to stakeholders</li><li>Manage upgrade and patch management activities, ensuring timely and seamless system updates to maintain security and compliance</li><li>Handle escalated in-restaurant support incidents related to transaction and funding variances, reprocessing, new restaurant opening setups, and data requests</li></ul><li>Fraud Management:</li><ul><li>Configure fraud detection tools and systems, integrating them with existing systems and databases to bolster the organization's fraud prevention capabilities</li><li>Conduct data analysis and incident investigations to identify fraudulent activities and patterns, implementing strategies to mitigate risks and losses</li><li>Establish Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) to measure the effectiveness of fraud management systems and continuously optimize strategies</li><li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams, such as IT security and data analytics, to enhance fraud detection and response capabilities</li></ul><li>System Configuration:</li><ul><li>Perform system acceptance and hardware profile configuration, ensuring accurate setup and compatibility with Wendy's payment systems</li><li>Manage user provisioning and maintenance for managed systems, adhering to Wendy's access control policies</li><li>Collaborate with IT and Operations teams to configure and test new systems and enhancements, ensuring seamless rollouts and hardware conversions</li></ul><li>Supervisory responsibilities, actively engaging in guiding and supporting team efforts across the above responsibilities in collaboration with direct manager to achieve desired outcomes.<br/><br/></li></ul>What we expect from you<br/><br/><ul><li>Education: Bachelors Degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field or equivalent work experience</li><li>At least 3 years of experience in Linux based system administration, preferably in the payments or financial industry</li><li>Prior experience overseeing Linux system performance, data analysis, and incident investigation</li><li>Experience overseeing estate management, fraud management, and compliance processes</li><li>Proficiency in system configuration and user provisioning for managed systems</li><li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills to effectively troubleshoot and resolve system performance issues</li><li>Excellent communication skills to collaborate with cross-functional teams and provide support for escalated incidents</li><li>Knowledge of payment processing systems, including familiarity with PCI DSS and P2PE requirements is highly desirable</li><li>Previous QSR, retail, or franchise experience preferred<br/><br/></li></ul>Wendy’s was built on the premise, "Quality is our Recipe®," which remains the guidepost of the Wendy's system. Today, Wendy's and its franchisees employ hundreds of thousands of people across more than 7,000 restaurants worldwide with a vision of becoming the world's most thriving and beloved restaurant brand.<br/><br/>The base pay range for this position is listed below.
