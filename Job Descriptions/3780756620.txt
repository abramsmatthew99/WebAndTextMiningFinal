<p><strong>Overview</strong></p><p>The Information Security and Compliance Team are responsible for identifying and analyzing technology based security risks in our environment and maintaining a robust security posture that aligns with our corporate risk profile and compliance objectives. This position serves as an internal Information Security Analyst that will assist in implementing, supporting and maintaining, security solutions and policies for Corporate and Cloud hosted environments.</p><p><br/></p><p>The Information Security Analyst will perform ongoing security operations tasks and help engineer security solutions across the enterprise. The Information Security Analyst will investigate and respond to incidents escalating as necessary. This position demands an organized, detail oriented team player with the ability to prioritize tasks and support multiple initiatives simultaneously; strong technical ability and excellent communication is required.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p><ul><li>Monitor and analyze systems for indication of security incidents.</li><li>Initiate security incident handling procedures and create incident handling reports.</li><li>Perform log correlation and analysis. </li><li>Provide maintenance and reporting for various security tools including vulnerability management solutions, SIEM, UTMs, HIDS, etc.</li><li>Support continuous monitoring programs in Cloud and Corporate environments to ensure control activities are operating effectively.</li><li>Document security processes and operational tasks as needed.</li><li>Analyze and evaluate new technologies.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Qualifications</strong></p><ul><li>B.S. in Computer Science, Management Information Systems or similar discipline.</li><li>Strong understanding of security principles, policies and industry best practices.</li><li>Networking knowledge – understand networking essentials, data flows, architecture, ports and protocols, wireless, etc.</li><li>Experience with engineering enterprise-grade security solutions. </li><li>Ability to effectively troubleshoot security issues with technical team members.</li><li>Excellent analytical and communications skills.</li><li>Prior experience with SOX ITGC compliance and/or other regulatory frameworks.</li><li>Demonstrated incident response and handling capabilities. </li><li>Solid understanding of identity management, authorization and multi-factor authentication technologies and solutions. </li><li>Application and web security knowledge. </li><li>Hands on experience with network security solutions to include NGFW, IDS/IPS, network and host-based forensics. </li><li>Email security gateway administration experience. </li><li>Experience with endpoint security threat monitoring and detection. </li><li>Prior systems engineering experience preferred. </li><li>3+ years’ experience in IT security.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Company Overview</strong></p><p>The RMR Group (Nasdaq: RMR) is a leading U.S. alternative asset management company, unique for its focus on commercial real estate (CRE) and related businesses. RMR’s vertical integration is supported by nearly 600 real estate professionals in over 30 offices nationwide who manage over $37 billion in assets under management and leverage 35 years of institutional experience in buying, selling, financing and operating CRE. RMR benefits from a scalable platform, a deep and experienced management team and a diversity of direct real estate strategies across its clients.</p><p><br/></p><p>RMR’s mission is to create long term value for our clients by managing their investments and assets “like we own it” – an approach that consistently and repeatedly generates opportunities for all our employees, investors and stakeholders. We are guided by <em>six core values</em>:</p><ul><li><em>Integrity at Our Core.</em></li><li><em>Perform Passionately and Effectively.</em></li><li><em>Inspired Thinking.</em></li><li><em>Like We Own It.</em></li><li><em>Power of We.</em></li><li><em>Mutual Respect.</em></li></ul><p><br/></p><p>RMR has been rated a Top Place to Work by The Boston Globe and GlobeSt.com, and has been recognized by the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency (EPA), the Building Owners and Managers Association (BOMA) International and the Institute of Real Estate Management (IREM), among others (listed here), as a CRE industry leader.</p>
