<strong>About Us<br/><br/></strong>The School of Engineering &amp; Technology is looking for a dedicated Part-Time Faculty Member to join our dynamic team. re-engagement.<br/><br/>Host a minimum of two one-hour synchronous office hour sessions per week, with details provided in each course.<br/><br/>Offer high-quality feedback to students, including substantive content focus, knowledge sharing, constructive edits, and supportive language.<br/><br/><strong>Scholarship<br/><br/></strong>Contribute to the advancement of knowledge in your discipline Handbook.<br/><br/><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong>Doctorate in Data Science, Engineering, Computer Science, Statistics, Business Analytics, or a related field.<br/><br/>Proficiency in Python, problem-solving.<br/><br/>Proficient in office management/systems, particularly in a higher education environment.<br/><br/>Goal-oriented and capable of achieving measurable objectives.<br/><br/>Excellent interpersonal, verbal, and written communication skills.<br/><br/>Adaptable, able to work both independently and in diverse teams.<br/><br/>Familiarity with Microsoft Office Suite and Student Information Systems (SIS).<br/><br/>Strong analytical and critical thinking abilities.<br/><br/>Mastery of APA style.<br/><br/>Experience with online education delivery and
