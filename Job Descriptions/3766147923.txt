<strong>Overview<br/><br/></strong><strong>Who we are<br/><br/></strong>Collaborative. Respectful. A place to dream and do. These are just a few words that describe what life is like at Toyota. As one of the world’s most admired brands, Toyota is growing and leading the future of mobility through innovative, high-quality solutions designed to enhance lives and delight those we serve. We’re looking for diverse, talented team members who want to Dream. Do. Grow. with us.<br/><br/>An important part of the Toyota family is Toyota Financial Services (TFS), the finance and insurance brand for Toyota and Lexus in North America. While TFS is a separate business entity, it is an essential part of this world-changing company - delivering on Toyota's vision to move people beyond what's possible. At TFS, you will help create best-in-class customer experiences in an innovative, collaborative environment.<br/><br/><em>To save time applying, Toyota does not offer sponsorship of job applicants for employment-based visas or any other work authorization for this position at this time.<br/><br/></em><strong>Who We're Looking For<br/><br/></strong>The Analytics &amp; Risk Management teams at Toyota Financial Services are looking for a highly motivated and detail-oriented <strong><em>Senior Analyst </em></strong>to join the <strong>Analytics Job Family</strong>; someone who shares our passions for data, creative problem solving, and continuous improvement.<br/><br/><strong>This role will be embedded within a specific business unit with the primary responsibility of delivering real-time insights and rapid turnaround analysis for the assigned business unit. Candidates should be as comfortable quickly packaging and presenting their ideas to leadership as they are analyzing complex data sets, creating insightful visualizations and writing SQL/R/Python code.<br/><br/></strong>Leaders across the Analytics Job Family are committed to creating an engaging space for talented individuals to grow their careers and do their best work. Successful candidates will need to possess high levels of innate curiosity, persistence, resourcefulness, initiative, aptitude for learning complex topics quickly and a desire to make a tangible impact and grow within the Analytics Job Family.<br/><br/><strong>What Is The Analytics Job Family<br/><br/></strong>The posting for the <strong>Analytics</strong><strong> Job Family</strong> serves to help fill open "technical analytics” roles across a wide array of TFS business units including:<br/><br/><ul><li>Analytics</li><ul><li>Business includes sales, marketing, wholesale/remarketing metrics</li><li>Finance includes profitability and performance metrics</li><li>Insurance and voluntary protection products</li><li>Pricing of retail and lease products</li></ul><li>Data Analysis</li><ul><li>Support business units with analytical initiatives &amp; data exploration</li></ul><li>Risk Management </li><ul><li>Residual Value risk from vehicle valuations</li><li>Commercial risk from dealership loans</li><li>Credit risk from consumer loans<br/></li></ul></ul>Note that specific job postings may also be available for other roles in these business units which are outside of the Analytics Job Family.<br/><br/><strong>What You'll Be Doing<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Become a subject matter expert for the business unit by developing a comprehensive understanding of how the department’s strategies, data, and systems work together for the benefit of our stakeholders and customers.</li><li>Develop actionable insights through data analysis, synthesis of disparate data sources, creative problem solving and collaboration with key stakeholders across the Analytics Job Family and the broader Toyota organization incl. Sales, Finance, Marketing, Servicing, Manufacturing, Data Science, etc. </li><li>Use SQL, R and/or Python to clean and structure data prior to analysis.</li><li>Propose actions to department, executive and international audiences through effective storytelling / presentations (PowerPoint), data visualizations (Tableau, Power BI), and clear written / verbal communication.</li><li>Build your analytical, technical, communication, and people skills through training, mentorships, and on-the-job experience for advancement into analytical leadership roles.<br/><br/></li></ul>As this posting covers an array of different positions, frequency/depth of above activities may vary.<br/><br/><strong>What You Bring<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree or higher in Analytics, Statistics, Finance, Accounting, Math, Computer Science, Economics, Engineering, or similar analytical / STEM field</li><li>3+ years of relevant professional work experience (paid, non-academic, non-intern)</li><li>Demonstrated experience with both coding and analysis tools (SQL, R, Tableau, Python and/or similar) and a high level of proficiency with both Excel and PowerPoint (testing of coding and analytic abilities will be conducted as part of the application process)</li><li>Working knowledge of statistics and modelling techniques with experience identifying trends and indicators through quantitative/qualitative analysis</li><li>Project management and presentation skills including proposing strategic recommendations to management and clearly articulating technical concepts to non-technical audiences.</li><li>Possess both the curiosity and resourcefulness needed to master complex concepts, methods, and systems in order to improve existing processes and develop creative solutions to new business challenges.</li><li>Ability to be self-directed and work independently while in a hybrid work environment. While these roles are predominantly work from home, team members are required to join us in-person at our Plano, TX headquarters on a regular basis. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Added bonus if you have<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>MBA or Master’s degree in an analytical field</li><li>Background in Auto Finance or Consumer Lending industries</li><li>Professional experience in one or more areas of the Analytics Job Family (see list above)</li><li>Intermediate to advanced statistics knowledge<br/><br/></li></ul>Note that specific job postings may also be available for other roles in these business units which are outside of the Analytics Job Family.<br/><br/><strong>What We’ll Bring<br/><br/></strong>During your interview process, our team can fill you in on all the details of our industry-leading benefits and career development opportunities. A few highlights include:<br/><br/><ul><li>A work environment built on teamwork, flexibility, and respect.</li><li>Professional growth and development programs to help advance your career, as well as tuition reimbursement.</li><li>Vehicle purchase &amp; lease programs.</li><li>Comprehensive health care and wellness plans for your entire family.</li><li>Flexible work options based on business needs.</li><li>Toyota 401(k) Savings Plan featuring a company match, as well as an annual retirement contribution from Toyota regardless of whether you contribute</li><li>Paid holidays and paid time off</li><li>Referral services related to prenatal services, adoption, childcare, schools and more</li><li>Tax Advantaged Accounts (Health Savings Account, Health Care FSA, Dependent Care FSA)</li><li>Relocation assistance (if applicable).<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Belonging at Toyota<br/><br/></strong>Our success begins and ends with our people. We embrace diverse perspectives and value unique human experiences. Respect for all is our North Star. Toyota is proud to have 10+ different Business Partnering Groups across 100 different North American chapter locations that support team members’ efforts to dream, do and grow without questioning that they belong.
