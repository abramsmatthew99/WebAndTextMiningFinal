<p>Happiest Minds, the Mindful IT Company, applies agile methodologies to enable digital transformation for enterprises and technology providers by delivering seamless customer experience, business efficiency and actionable insights. We leverage a spectrum of disruptive technologies such as: Big Data Analytics, AI &amp; Cognitive Computing, Internet of Things, Cloud, Security, SDN-NFV, RPA, Blockchain, etc. Positioned as “Born Digital . Born Agile”, our capabilities spans across product engineering, digital business solutions, infrastructure management and security services. We deliver these services across industry sectors such as retail, consumer packaged goods, edutech, e-commerce, banking, insurance, hi-tech, engineering R&amp;D, manufacturing, automotive and travel/transportation/hospitality.</p><p>Headquartered in Bangalore, India; Happiest Minds has operations in USA, UK, The Netherlands, Australia and Middle East.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong><em>Summary:</em></strong></p><p>An Embedded Software Engineer works on software for embedded systems. Embedded systems typically use specialized micro-controllers that have constrained resources. In addition to having general software engineering skills, this engineer will need to have experience working with micro-controllers, digital, and analog circuits.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong><em>Essential Duties &amp; Responsibilities: </em></strong></p><ul><li>Design, develop, and test software for new products and maintain software for existing products.</li><li>Work collaboratively. Support technical lead engineers in larger projects. Work with researchers in development and implementation of new functions. Work with product managers, UI designers, and other engineers to define requirements and user interfaces. Work with project leads in managing scope, schedule, task definitions, prioritization, and risk mitigation strategies. Work with electrical engineers in selecting components, pin assignments, schematic reviews, and bringing up prototype boards. Work with verification engineers in developing test strategies and procedures. Work with manufacturing engineers to design automated calibration and test fixtures for production. Assist other engineers with task-related and troubleshooting problems.</li><li>Ensure the software quality and function according to the requirements and application in the field. Meet and apply global standards, and comply with security regulations</li><li>Continuous improvement of development processes.</li><li>Perform other duties as assigned.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><em>Required Skills/Abilities:</em></strong></p><ul><li>Ability to build teams and communicate on international level</li><li>Knowledge of Software Engineering principles</li><li>Software development using modern C and C++</li><li>Software Configuration management tool like Azure or Git</li><li>Familiarity in working with micro-controllers and related tool-chains</li><li>Knowledge of test frameworks</li><li>Knowledge of RTOS concepts</li><li>Knowledge of user interface design principles</li><li>Familiarity using logic analyzers or mixed signal oscilloscopes</li><li>Familiarity analyzing serial communication protocols</li><li>Knowledge of industrial communication protocols and PLCs are a plus</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><em>Education and Experience:</em></strong></p><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science/Engineering, or Electrical Engineering</li><li>2-6 years of professional experience in the developing embedded software</li></ul>
