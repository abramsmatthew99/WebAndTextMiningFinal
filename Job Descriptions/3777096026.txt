Algorithms that can handle immense volumes of data.</p><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>What you’ll do for us</strong></p><ul><li>Developing advanced models for time-series forecasting and anomaly detection specifically tailored for the CPG and Retail Industry.</li><li>Ensure the scalability of our solutions and continuously optimize the performance of existing algorithms.</li><li>Assure seamless integration of our libraries with the o9 Platform.</li><li>Support Data Scientists using our algorithms.</li><li>Keep us up-to-date with the latest academic research in the field.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Essential Qualifications &amp; Skills</strong></p><p><strong>Experience:</strong></p><ul><li>3+ years of experience in the field of Data Science and Machine Learning</li><li>Experience with time series forecasting at scale in a retail environment is a big plus.</li><li>Experience with open source contributions is a plus.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Education:</strong></p><ul><li>Bachelors Degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Economics, Engineering or related field or</li><li>Masters Degree in Computer Science, Applied Mathematics, Statistics, Engineering, Business Analytics, Operations, or related field</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Skills:</strong></p><p>Algorithms and Domain Knowledge:</p><ul><li>Deep knowledge of fundamentals, and the state-of-the art, in relevant areas of ML, particularly time-series forecasting.</li><li>Understanding of Supply Chain Concepts will be preferable</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>Language, Tools and Best Practices:</p><ul><li>Strong Python programming skills and experience with at least one “lower level” language C/C++ or C#.</li><li>Knowledge of professional software engineering practices and best practices for the full software development lifecycle, including coding standards, code reviews, and source control management.</li><li>Knowledge of parallelizing frameworks such as Multiprocessing, Spark, Ray, etc and optimizing code for such frameworks is preferred.</li><li>Experience working with Hadoop and Hive would be preferable.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Characteristics</strong></p><ul><li>Strong presentation and communications skills.
