        Title: Desktop Engineer<br/><br/>Location: Kennewick, WA<br/><br/><strong>About Lamb Weston<br/><br/></strong>Lamb Weston is a leading supplier of frozen potato, sweet potato, appetizer and vegetable products to restaurants and retailers around the world. For more than 60 years, we’ve produced innovative, inventive products that make life better for our customers and their customers.<br/><br/><strong>Job Description Summary<br/><br/></strong>A Desktop Engineer in this role will manage, analyze, create, and modify desktop applications, software packages and images. This position requires expertise in SCCM and configuration modifications. The role will lead efforts in planning, coordinating, and executing complex desktop projects. This role will provide high level technical support to Lamb Weston staff. This position requires strong communication skills and will interact with various levels of IT and other LW employees. An individual in this role will be relied upon to improve the overall desktop experience and ensure that the Lamb Weston is keeping up with the modern technologies. This will include but isn’t limited to testing, integration with standard software packages, integration with automated patching and deployment technologies, documentation, training, and automating application\configuration deployments. Responsible for process and procedures for image build and support checklist</li><li>Develop and maintain Enterprise Software and Hardware Catalog</li><li>Responsible for addressing gaps in desktop support workflows</li><li>Responsible for desktop support tool selection and efficiency</li><li>Create and maintain automated deployments and working on software version updates</li><li>Develop software installation scripts</li><li>Follow organization security policies and best practices</li><li>Ability to develop reports, metrics, and stories surrounding scope of responsibilities</li><li>Participate in hardware purchasing discussions and make recommendations</li><li>Assist the Service Desk in advanced level software installation package distribution troubleshooting</li><li>Design, implement and configure software needed to support our enterprise SCCM current infrastructure</li><li>Update/Create documentation as needed (user guides, admin guides, knowledge base articles, FAQs, quick reference guides)</li><li>Create standard operating procedures on all solutions implemented</li><li>Research, evaluate, and remain current on emerging tools, techniques, and IT technologies</li><li>Work closely with client to perform technical and requirements analysis</li><li>Working effectively in a collaborative and innovative team-oriented environment</li><li>Closely work with multiple groups to review work and to remove impediments or roadblocks</li><li>Provide world class customer service to internal teams and end users customers</li><li>Provides weekly statuses &amp; estimates of all priority and non-priority projects along with recommended scope or schedule changes based on capacity and unforeseen challenges</li><li>Overseeing/Working with the service desk in image and package management. </li><li>Coordinates and Supports application deployments</li><li>Provides technical support including participation in on call rotation. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Application Services<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Actively contribute to requirements gathering process focusing on infrastructure components. In production support scenarios, support identification of incident trends and build proactive plans to eliminate. </li><li>Collaborate with application designers and vendor solution providers to proactively model optimized infrastructure solutions. </li><li>Comprehensively test all infrastructure components of IT solutions to ensure business requirements are met. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>IT Process<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Adhere and comply with System Development Lifecycle (SDLC) and Release Management processes and procedures. </li><li>Manage stages of projects through uniquely assigned project manager roles and responsibilities. Execute project management disciplines to ensure project activities and tasks are delivered on time, within budget and according to approved scope and quality requirements. </li><li>Conduct effective investigation, diagnosis and root cause analysis of Incidents / problems. Develop, Implement and document solutions in a timely manner and validate with system users that service has been restored. </li><li>Conduct proper urgency, impact and risk assessment of change requests. Plan, schedule, implement and document changes in accordance with core SDLC and Change Advisory Board processes. </li><li>Contribute to accurate estimation of work activities and tasks. </li><li>Performs other duties as assigned<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Position Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><strong>Basic &amp; Preferred Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree preferred. Focus on Computer Science, Software Engineering, Management Information Systems or a related field with 5+ years of experience in a Desktop Engineering function. </li><li>Five years’ experience with desktop / server hardware and software installation and support required</li><li>Exceptional oral and written communication and customer service skills required</li><li>Display a high sense of urgency, adaptability and flexibility in a fast-changing environment</li><li>Possess excellent problem-solving abilities including analytical, prioritization, and escalation</li><li>Ability to work within the multiple departments internal to IT</li><li>Planning skills</li><li>Knowledgeable of Microsoft Operating Systems, Office, Networking, Mobile Devices, and computer hardware</li><li>ITIL v3 Foundations Certification is preferred</li><li>Prior experience with Windows migrations is preferred</li><li>Microsoft Certified Solutions Associate (MCSA) Certification or Microsoft Certified Solutions Expert (MCSE) Certification is preferred</li><li>Experience managing and or deploying Mobile Device Management software</li><li>Experience with Intune and group policy preferred</li><li>Experience using the ServiceNow Ticketing System or similar</li><li>Ability to be on a regular on-call rotation and willing to travel as needed for projects. </li><li>Ability to lift 50 lbs. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Industry-Competitive Benefits<br/><br/></strong>Coupled with our compensation and bonus incentive programs, our benefits deliver rewards that are market competitive.
