<strong>Ready to help us transform healthcare? Bring your true colors to blue.<br/><br/></strong>The <strong>Omni Channel Customer Experience Journey Manager</strong> is an important role responsible for executing on the Experience Transformation strategy to ensure members stay healthy and manage costs along the way. The Experience Manager’s primary goal will be to transform identified key journeys by leveraging a product mindset, reimagining those journeys to drive behavior change and member value. you will own one of a discreet number of customer journeys at BCBS MA. You will work closely with your Technical Product Management counterpart focused on the same end to end journey as well as leaders in our customer advocate (call center) team to discover, design, develop, iterate, measure and continuously improve delightful customer journeys that result in happier, more healthy consumers.<br/><br/>The <strong>Omni Channel Customer Experience Journey Manager</strong> believes that the Experience is the Product we deliver to the people we are privileged to serve. This leader will own, manage and lead the identification, creation and execution of the end-to-end experience of a key journey. They will work with human-centered experience designers responsible for creating future-state journeys to improve the consumer experience and evolve and reimagine current-state journeys and strategies, positioning new journeys for market success which align to business imperatives. Also, this role will champion customer experience as a core component of the omni-channel experience transformation by partnering with technology and operational areas to identify the products and capabilities available and needed to execute the experience roadmap across multiple channels<br/><br/>Success in this role will be achieved by identifying and understanding what needs to transform across the journey, working with other Experience Managers, operations, other functional areas, and Product Managers (digital and traditional) to define objectives and key results to execute on reimagined journeys through a product management discipline. Identifying, measuring and tracking consumer success metrics and outcomes is an imperative, as is working cross-functionally with technology partners, operations, product and clinical colleagues to drive better results faster.<br/><br/>The <strong>Omni Channel Customer Experience Journey Manager</strong> will lead the transition to a customer mindset in end-to-end processes and delivery. This leader must instill an “outside-in” approach across the organization, prioritizing what matters most to members which also support business priorities.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities And Accountabilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Own the end-to-end member experience for a key journey, and gain alignment on reimagining it.</li><li>Own the journey roadmap, driving its execution by leading a cross-functional Team of Teams, collaborating across the company to execute the roadmap.</li><li>Partner with Senior Leaders, executives and technology partners in our business space to reimagine journeys, leveraging complex solutions / products to solve business problems or to enable strategic priorities, objectives &amp; key results.</li><li>Support digital product idea conceptualization, vision, identification of business value, product design, product road mapping, planning, and delivery of digital products and solutions for all digital products, applications, and platforms that are in service to the member journeys.</li><li>Manage and orchestrate the Consumer Ecosystem for the Journey. The Consumer Ecosystem consists of the necessary experiences, sub-journeys, products, capabilities, components, widgets, and data interchange to deliver a comprehensive, delightful consumer experience.</li><li>Partner with Enterprise Architecture and Solution Architecture from Value Stream and Shared Services to recommend solutions &amp; options supporting sizing, cost, &amp; timelines to socialize &amp; bring forward for funding &amp; approval.</li><li>As the "Voice of the Customer", oversee ongoing review and elaboration of features with business stakeholders, including recommending any changes required to meet critical business, customer, or operational requirements.</li><li>Planning and Execution: Define customer outcomes, business impacts, and roadmap for the Journey. Build the business case, ROI analysis and lead stakeholder alignment. </li><li>Delivery: Oversee the experience lifecycle from conception to release, iteration &amp; maintenance, determining the right product portfolio for the experience &amp; prioritizing work in the backlog to ensure products are delivering to experience expectations. Work within a matrixed &amp; complex organization across multiple functional areas to achieve results.</li><li>Use knowledge of product management and ability to understand the P&amp;L impacts across multiple functional areas to develop and source alignment for the journey. Proactively identify new initiatives/themes and build out the backlog items based on quantified business value and in partnership with technology partners. </li><li>Maintain a clear view of the highest value experience backlog items and make trade-offs based on team capacity to collaborate on the sprint backlog. Items could be focused on technology, digital experience, marketing, operations, product or clinical. </li><li>Results &amp; Metrics: Define, track and monitor Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) based on outcomes.</li><li>Use Data to Drive Outcomes: Seek to deliver viable ideas or learn fast and pivot by understanding pain points &amp; experience analytics. Leverage consumer, operations, clinical data &amp; feedback/behavior to drive business outcomes.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Required Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>BA/BS required, in computer science/technology or business preferred; MS/MBA preferred.</li><li>8-10 years experience in product management roles, driving and implementing product and experience design.</li><li>3+ years in management/consulting experience preferred; Healthcare experience preferred.</li><li>Comprehensive understanding of Customer Experience, and how new tools and technologies are being utilized to improve the customer experience.</li><li>Experience using agile methods in the product management discipline; Experience with project management tools (JIRA, Clarizen)</li><li>Understanding of the product lifecycle, capabilities, and direction of consumer-facing technologies (including mobile apps) and their impact</li><li>Extensive experience defining and measuring product management objectives that span MVP, roadmap, quality, business value, time to deliver, adherence to agile principles, and cost-to-value ratio.</li><li>Highly strategic: demonstrated track record in assessing complex, competitive markets and situations and in developing related initiatives in partnership with other stakeholders to drive business results.</li><li>A systems-oriented thinker with the ability to analyze complex relationships and situations, think outside the box, act, and promote entrepreneurial and innovative approaches. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Minimum Education Requirements<br/><br/></strong>High school degree or equivalent required unless otherwise noted above<br/><br/>Location<br/><br/>Boston<br/><br/>Time Type<br/><br/>Full time<br/><br/>Salary Range: $126,000.00 - $154,000.00<br/><br/>The job posting range is the lowest to highest salary we in good faith believe we would pay for this role at the time of this posting.
