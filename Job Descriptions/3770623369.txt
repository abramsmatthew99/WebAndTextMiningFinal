discipline to proactively design outcomes in a smart and timely manner. </li><li> Be responsible for ensuring that there is a consistent approach to modelling of graphical (3D) data and inclusion of non-graphical data across, including consistent and appropriate technologies are in use. </li><li> Ensure BIM processes/workflows are communicated and followed across the project for the federation and quality assurance of design models (model graphical and data checking and verification, clash mitigation and control) and verification of general information needed for reports, model production and delivery. </li><li> Ensure utilization of federated project information models for safety in design and technical reviews, stakeholder and client engagement, design and interface issue tracking and resolution and milestone reporting. </li><li> Managing and delivering scheduled model, drawing and data to external parties and the client according to the project schedule. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications And Experience<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Tertiary degree or certificate in Engineering, Computer Science, or related fields. </li><li> Minimum of 5 years of
