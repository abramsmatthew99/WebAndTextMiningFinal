        PDG Consulting (PDGC) is a Los Angeles, California based software consulting firm specializing in custom Web Application Development, Business Intelligence, and Digital Transformation for the Media & Entertainment and Content Distribution industries. We are seeking a Business Analyst with a solid foundation in defining, analyzing, and documenting complex business functionality and processes for enterprise-scale applications. The candidate must be a motivated self-starter and have the ability to operate in a highly collaborative team environment.Responsibilities:Solicit requirements from business stakeholders through interviews, workshops, and analyzing existing system functionality, data, processes, and documentationDocument business requirements via industry-standard analysis techniques and artifacts including user stories, data/process flow diagrams, object modeling, UI mockups and/or wireframes, and data conversion mapping and transformation rulesProvide analysis and recommendations for improving application workflows and manual processesPlan and manage User Acceptance Testing (UAT) including UAT planning, scheduling and coordination, developing test scenarios and conditions, and managing UAT execution including defect tracking and status reportingPlan and manage user training including the creation of system documentation and training materials and conducting user trainingTake ownership and accountability for client deliverablesCollaborate with stakeholders, business analysts, and developers in an agile environmentProvide mentorship and guidance for other team membersRequired Skills:B.A./B.S. in Computer Science, Engineering, Information Systems or related degree program with an emphasis on analytical skills5+ years of Business Analysis experienceExperience working in an Agile SCRUM environment.
