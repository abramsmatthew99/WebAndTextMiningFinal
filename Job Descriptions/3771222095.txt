        Wisetek Solutions, Inc. is a leader in the IT Asset Disposal (ITAD), reuse and data destruction services worldwide. We strive to provide world class services to achieve maximum financial return from advanced reuse, remanufacturing, remarketing and recycling of retired IT equipment. Our processes enable clients to be legally and ethically responsible for their IT asset disposal and data destruction services, giving them environmental responsibility for their redundant IT equipment. Our services reduce the risk, cost and complexity associated with securely managing IT assets and consumer electronics throughout their lifecycle in compliance with environmental and data security regulations.<br/><br/><strong>Overview:<br/><br/></strong>Wisetek Solutions, Inc. is looking for an entry-level IT Test Engineer to be based out of our Sacramento, CA facility. The IT Test Engineer will support our ITAD operations and manage on-premise network and server infrastructure. This position requires excellent communication and problem solving skills and demands the candidate to be able to independently research and interpret technical documentation.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Install, patch, secure, and monitor servers, workstations, routers, switches</li><li>Provide first level support to users in-person and remotely across multiple locations</li><li>Work with third-party vendors to deploy software updates and troubleshoot issues</li><li>Perform routine data backups of on-premise servers and databases</li><li>Write and maintain documentation for procedures, networks, and systems</li><li>Develop and support test methods for testing new and refurbished hardware</li><li>Research and implement software automation tools and techniques</li><li>Research, identify, and support continuous improvement and innovation activity - opportunities to enhance or transform our ITAD operations</li><li>Other duties as assigned<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Minimum Requirements:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Familiarity with computer hardware components</li><li>Experience with Linux (Ubuntu, Debian, RHEL, Rocky Linux) and Windows</li><li>Comfortable with Bash and PowerShell</li><li>Basic networking knowledge (routing, switching, DHCP, DNS, WiFi, Ethernet, firewalls)</li><li>Knowledge in SQL and databases</li><li>Problem solving and analytical skills - need to be able to take the lead in dealing with technical and business problems encountered with products and processes, root causing and correcting them</li><li>Self-motivated and results driven - self-starter with a sense of urgency, capable of working in a multi-tasking environment and shifting focus appropriately</li><li>Clearly identify, document, and find solutions for any possible issues and product problems. </li><li>Willingness and ability to travel, to other Wisetek sites, customers, and suppliers</li><li>Good interpersonal and team skills along with a customer focused attitude - capable of leading or working as an integral part of a team and delivering on commitments made<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Preferred Qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Management Information Systems, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related discipline; or equivalent experience</li><li>At least 1 year of experience in an IT help desk, IT system admin, or related role<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Annual Salary Range:<br/><br/></strong>$65,000 to $90,000<br/><br/>Wisetek-USA provides equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants for employment and prohibits discrimination and harassment of any type without regard to race, color, religion, age, sex, national origin, disability status, genetics, pregnancy, marital status, protected veteran status, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, or any other characteristic protected by federal, state or local laws
