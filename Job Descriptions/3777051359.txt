You will report to the <em>Associate Director of Customer &amp; Product Insights </em>and will be a part of the larger Product Innovation team.<br/><br/><strong>You Will…<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Lead responsive, descriptive, and exploratory data analytics (through SQL queries / Python scripts, spreadsheets, visualizations, and automations) to enable strategic coming data from multiple sources (primary research, third-party data, trend monitoring, internal behavioral, sales, product, operational, financial) for a holistic view on the customer to derive actionable insights</li><li>Communicate findings and recommendations in a clear and compelling way to inspire others to use data to take action<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>You are…<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Analytical &amp; Detail Oriented: you can tackle vague, complex problems using data and logic; You ensure precision and thoroughness in your work products</li><li>A Holistic Thinker: you don’t just report numbers; you understand business context; you’re entrepreneurial, collaborative, creative, and can tie everything back to strategy and actionable recommendations</li><li>Curious: you are naturally curious and stay in tune with what’s happening in the food space, and are able to synthesize trends into product ideas aligned to our business; you have a genuine interest in using data and analytical techniques to solve real business and consumer challenges</li><li>An Influencer &amp; Communicator: you are a strong communicator who is comfortable supporting the needs of multiple stakeholders and balancing multiple projects simultaneously, and who can distill and explain complex data down to insights and recommendations to a variety needs</li><li>Collaborative: you foster cooperation among diverse stakeholders to achieve common objectives and seek opportunities to enhance cross-functional process efficiency.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>You have… <br/><br/></strong><ul><li>A degree in Business Administration or related field, Business Analytics, Operations Research, Statistics, Applied Mathematics, Data Science, Computer Science OR certificate in an aforementioned quantitative field with 1-2+ years of work experience delivering analytical solutions to complex problems</li><li>1-2 years in an analytically-driven role in tech, consulting, food/CPG, or related field</li><li>Passion for driving clear insights paired with business context so teams can take action</li><li>Proficiency in SQL, Python, DataBricks, and Tableau (Bonus: Snowflake, S3)</li><li>Experience in e-commerce or subscription-based companies (preferably)<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>You’ll get… <br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Competitive salary, 401k with company match that vests immediately upon participation, and company equity plan based on role</li><li>Generous PTO and parental leave of up to 16 weeks</li><li>Comprehensive health and wellness benefits with options at $0 monthly, effective first day of employment</li><li>Tuition
