<strong>Description<br/><br/></strong><strong>Job Title:</strong> Sr. Director of IT<br/><br/><strong>Department:</strong> Information Technology<br/><br/><strong>About the Role:<br/><br/></strong>As Sr. Director of IT, you will be responsible for designing and building ApolloMed’s IT infrastructure. This includes creating and maintaining hardware and software systems, ensuring network security and connectivity, managing data storage and backup, and implementing disaster recovery and business continuity plans. The Sr. Director of IT will play a critical role in leveraging technology to improve patient care, drive operational efficiency, and achieve ApolloMed’s business goals.<br/><br/><strong>What You’ll Do:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Own and execute the vision for IT at ApolloMed</li><li> Partner with stakeholders across the organization including Executive Leadership and Engineering teams to assess and anticipate business needs related to technology that will advance the company’s mission, vision, and strategic goals</li><li> Manage and build a high performing and diverse in-house team of IT professionals and leaders to ensure our end-user support, physical and virtual infrastructure, application management, and more thrive as the company grows</li><li> Develop, socialize, and implement a vision and roadmap for IT at ApolloMed including project &amp; service roadmaps, stakeholder management, cost controls, and internal comms.</li><li> Successfully achieve Objectives and Key Results (OKRs) within ambitious timelines in a fast-paced organization</li><li> Manage and oversee all aspects of IT at ApolloMed including project management, planning &amp; roadmaps, stakeholder management, service management, end-user support, compliance, policies, tooling, and more</li><li> Grow, build, and manage a team of IT professionals including 1:1 meetings, Career Development, Logistics (e.g.: Expenses &amp; Timecards), Hiring, and Performance Management</li><li> Manage ApolloMed’s IT compliance position including reviewing and approving change to policies, implementation of controls, and enforcement while partnering with the broader Security organization on all relevant compliance frameworks (SOX, HITRIST, HIPAA, NIST, etc.)</li><li> Develop and implement an IT work management process to increase predictability for incidents, enhancements, and longer-term projects while factoring in stakeholder timelines, internal IT initiatives, as well as team well-being and growth<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Education: bachelor's degree in computer science, engineering, or related field; graduate degree in a related field (preferred); related experience</li><li> Experience: 10+ years of experience in IT management</li><li> Industry/Environment: 5+ years of experience in a high-growth, entrepreneurial organization highly preferred; healthcare, clinical, or provider organization experience a bonus but not required</li><li> Strong technical skills, including experience with software development, database management, networking, and security</li><li> Strong understanding of IT infrastructure, cloud operations, networking, applications, operational excellence, regulatory and compliance requirements</li><li> Experience influencing and executing on strategic technology objectives, while communicating the business value and broader vision to the company</li><li> Deep experience in leading, guiding, and executing projects from inception to production and further scaling, using a modern technology stack<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>You’re a great fit for the role if:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Deep understanding of the healthcare industry and the unique challenges and opportunities in health tech startups</li><li> Proven experience identifying areas where technology can add value to the business and work closely with other executives to develop and implement IT strategies that align with the company's overall business goals</li><li> Prior experience working with healthcare-specific technologies, such as electronic health records (EHRs) and health information exchanges (HIEs).</li><li> Track record of innovation and problem-solving, and should be able to identify and implement IT solutions that improve patient care, operational efficiency, and business outcomes<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Who We Are:<br/><br/></strong>ApolloMed (NASDAQ: AMEH) is a physician-centric, technology-powered healthcare company accelerating the path towards a future where high-quality, coordinated healthcare is available to all. We are building and operating an integrated, value-based healthcare delivery platform to empower our physicians to provide the highest quality of end-to-end care for their patients in a cost-effective manner, especially patients who are traditionally underserved and those most vulnerable.<br/><br/>Our platform currently empowers over 12,000 physicians to provide care for over 1.3 million patients nationwide and is growing revenue over 40% a year. We have demonstrably improved patient outcomes: seniors on our platform spend 54% fewer days in the hospital than the national average and are readmitted 63% less often, and serve an extremely diverse community, with over 75% of our patients identifying as BIPOC and over 80% enrolled in Medicare or Medicaid. Our rapid growth and unique position at the intersection of all major healthcare stakeholders (payer, provider, and patient) gives us an unparalleled opportunity to combine clinical and technological expertise to improve patient outcomes, increase access to quality healthcare, and reduce the waste in the US healthcare system.<br/><br/><strong>Our Values:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Put Patients First</li><li> Empower Entrepreneurial Provider and Care Teams</li><li> Be Innovative</li><li> Operate With Integrity &amp; Excellence</li><li> Work As One Team<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Environmental Job Requirements and Working Conditions: <br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Our organization follows a hybrid work structure where the expectation is to work both in office and at home on a weekly basis. The office is located at 1668 S Garfield Avenue, Alhambra, CA 91801</li><li>The total compensation target pay range for this role is: $150,000 - $170,000. The salary range represents our national target range for this role. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Additional Information:<br/><br/></strong>ApolloMed is proud to be an Equal Employment Opportunity and Affirmative Action employer.
