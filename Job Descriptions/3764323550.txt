<p><strong>Montgomery County Government</strong></p><p><strong>Position Description</strong></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Job Title:</strong> Software Manager</p><p><strong>Department:</strong> Information Technology</p><p><strong>Class Code:</strong> 125</p><p><strong>Reports To:</strong> Director of Information Technology</p><p><strong>FLSA Status: </strong>Exempt</p><p><strong>Prepared By:</strong> Information Technology</p><p><strong>Prepared Date: </strong>July 2023</p><p><strong>Remote Options:</strong> Hybrid </p><p><br/></p><p>*Montgomery County Government adopted the State of Tennessee’s Hybrid Retirement Plan to be effective January 1, 2017. Any new full-time employee hired on or after January 1, 2017 will be required to enroll. As a member of the Hybrid Plan, you are required to contribute at least 5% of your salary to the defined benefit portion of the Hybrid Plan. Contributions are made on a tax deferred basis.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>SUMMARY</strong></p><p>This position is responsible for providing leadership, technical programming, and system support. The ideal candidate brings a diverse range of expertise in project management, data migration, software support, and software development. They will use this experience to vet new vendor software solutions, lead our team in providing these solutions to our users, and ensure timely budget-conscious delivery of said systems.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>EXPECTATIONS OF EMPLOYEE</strong></p><p>• Adheres to County Government Policy and Procedures</p><p>• Acts as a role model within and outside the work environment</p><p>• Performs duties as workload necessitates</p><p>• Maintains a positive and respectful attitude</p><p>• Communicates regularly with supervisor about department issues</p><p>• Demonstrates flexible and efficient time management and ability to prioritize workload</p><p>• Demonstrates awareness and understanding of the various internal and external cultures that utilize department services</p><p>• Consistently reports to work on time prepared to perform duties of position</p><p>• Meets County Government productivity and quality standards</p><p>• Maintains appropriate customer relations</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES</strong> include the following. Other duties</p><p>may be assigned.</p><p>• Manage vendor software projects, leading cross-functional teams, coordinating project activities, and ensuring successful delivery of high-quality software solutions within established timelines and budgets.</p><p>• Conduct comprehensive evaluations of new vendor and in-house software solutions to make informed recommendations.</p><p>• Perform data migrations with emphasis on maintaining data accuracy and minimizing business disruption.</p><p>• Generate data reports based on department needs.</p><p>• Provide training and support on the use of data reporting services.</p><p>• Support transition from legacy systems to Oracle Fusion Cloud Products.</p><p>• Support current and future HCM and ERP packages.</p><p>• Support Public Safety software packages, including installation, configuration, software patching, user training, data reporting, and issue troubleshooting.</p><p>• Work with County departments to evaluate their software needs, analyzing feasibility, cost and time required, and compatibility with current systems</p><p>• Create and maintain documentation of system hardware and software configuration of all systems.</p><p>• Participate in on-call rotation for supporting County Emergency Services.</p><p>• Answer calls and provide information, advice, and guidance to handle problems. Take and relay messages and/or direct calls to appropriate personnel.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>PROFICIENCIES and TOOLS</strong></p><p>• Visual Studio, Git VCS, MS SQL Management Studio, MS Excel</p><p>• Expertise in at least one of C#, VB, or Python</p><p>• Proficient in MS SQL or equivalent DBMS, with extensive experience in database management, data retrieval, and query optimization.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>QUALIFICATIONS</strong></p><p>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>EDUCATION and/or EXPERIENCE</strong></p><p>This position requires a Bachelor’s degree in computer science or programming required with three to five years of computer programming/systems analysis (or related) experience; or any equivalent combination of education, training, and experience that provides the requisite knowledge, skills, and abilities for this job.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>SPECIAL KNOWLEDGE and SKILLS</strong></p><p>This position requires effective oral and written communication skills, excellent interpersonal skills, and advanced computer literacy.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>LANGUAGE SKILLS</strong></p><p>Incumbent must possess the ability to read, analyze, and interpret general business periodicals, professional journals, technical procedures, or governmental regulations. Incumbent must possess the ability to write reports, business correspondence, and procedure manuals; effectively present information and respond to questions from</p><p>groups of managers, clients, customers, and the general public.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>MATHEMATICAL SKILLS</strong></p><p>Incumbent must possess the ability to work with mathematical concepts such as probability and statistical inference, and the ability to apply concepts such as fractions, percentages, ratios, and proportions to practical situations.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>REASONING ABILITY</strong></p><p>Incumbent must possess the ability to solve practical problems and deal with a variety of concrete variables in situations where only limited standardization exists. Incumbent must possess the ability to interpret a variety of instructions furnished in written, oral, diagram, or schedule form.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>PHYSICAL DEMANDS</strong></p><p>While performing the duties of this job, the incumbent is regularly required to sit and talk or hear. The incumbent frequently is required to use hands to finger, handle, or feel; reach with hands and arms. The incumbent is occasionally required to stand, walk, stoop, kneel, crouch, crawl and climb or balance. The incumbent must</p><p>occasionally lift and/or move up to 25 pounds. Specific vision abilities required by this job include close vision, and ability to adjust focus.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>WORK ENVIRONMENT</strong></p><p>While performing the duties of this job, the incumbent is occasionally exposed to outside weather conditions.
