Contact us today, and we look forward to connecting with you!</p><p><br/></p><p><strong> </strong></p><p><strong>Senior Director of IT Applications</strong></p><p><strong>Direct Hire/ Contract/ or Contract to Hire</strong></p><p><strong>Higher Education Industry</strong></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>ROLE AND REPORTING STRUCTURE:</strong></p><p>As the Senior IT Applications Director, your role involves spearheading and overseeing the IT Applications Team to enhance the organization's technological capabilities for streamlined processes and improved outcomes. Maintaining a high level of technical expertise and industry knowledge, you will function as a trusted partner to the organization. With a strong emphasis on project management and continual process enhancement, you will take charge of multiple priorities and develop the technical team to meet objectives. This position reports directly to the VP of IT and involves collaboration with the entire IT staff and key business stakeholders.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>PRIMARY RESPONSIBILITIES:</strong></p><ul><li>Oversee the implementation, management, and support of enterprise-level systems, ensuring the entire application lifecycle is optimized.</li><li>Manage integrations, both for new projects and existing systems, across the organization.</li><li>Lead exploration and analysis of new application functions and technologies.</li><li>Communicate with various cross-departmental stakeholders, taking a lead role in all application projects.</li><li>Define processes and tools for effective application management.</li><li>Manage, mentor, and develop a cohesive team of highly trained professionals, external technical experts, and key users.</li><li>Coordinate efforts in supporting existing production systems and executing new system projects. Ensure detailed project plans, accurate requirements, efficient system design/coding or purchase, successful test and training plans, and comprehensive system/user documentation.</li><li>Support existing production systems by defining and prioritizing system problems and enhancement requests, overseeing development activities, ensuring successful testing, providing user training, updating documentation, and meeting service level agreements.</li><li>Perform business analysis activities at the strategic, operational, and organizational levels to contribute to the continual improvement of business processes.</li><li>Undertake other duties as assigned.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>QUALIFICATION REQUIREMENTS:</strong></p><ul><li>In-depth understanding of business requirements with the ability to translate them into technical documentation.</li><li>Strong collaboration with senior leadership, senior technologists, and business partners to define business processes and implement creative solutions.</li><li>Demonstrated ability to convert strategic goals into tangible steps and programs.</li><li>Ability to lead technology roadmap development and facilitate planning for IT-wide initiatives.</li><li>Experience with IT portfolio management concepts.</li><li>Ability to interface with and influence people at all levels within the organization.</li><li>Capability to translate and present technical concepts into practical business terms for leaders in both large and small group settings.</li><li>Strong knowledge of all facets of systems development and operations.</li><li>Experience with Learning Management Systems, Student Information Systems, and CRM Systems is highly desired.</li><li>Willingness and ability to travel for work assignments.</li><li>Willingness and ability to work additional hours during the week and/or weekends, as required.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>EDUCATION and EXPERIENCE REQUIREMENTS:</strong></p><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science, software engineering, a closely related field, or equivalent experience.</li><li>Experience with Salesforce is a plus.</li><li>Experience working in a higher education industry is preferred.</li><li>Minimum of seven years of progressive experience in managing all aspects of the application lifecycle.</li><li>Minimum of 5 years’ experience managing a high-performance technical team.</li><li>Experience with Windows operating systems, relational databases (specifically SQL Server), and modern computer languages such as C, Visual Basic, .Net.</li><li>Experience with Salesforce is preferred.</li><li>Experience with SharePoint, Campus Management, and Canvas is preferred.</li><li>Experience providing technical leadership at the enterprise level for the design of information systems.</li><li>Experience with complex technical environments using multiple
