<strong>Overview<br/><br/></strong><strong> Overview: <br/><br/></strong>At HealthEdge, we have a unique opportunity to help revolutionize the health care payer/provider industry. As a member of our executive leadership team, you will take the lead to ensure HealthEdge achieves our vision by leveraging technology that rapidly evolves to meet changing customer expectations. Your entrepreneurial and systems approach will equip HealthEdge to scale our tech solutions effectively. You are an executive, a cross functional leader, a product and tech strategist with a strong engineering background. You understand broad technology trends, are able to align innovation with business goals and help HealthEdge solidify its competitive advantage through technology. You will bring respected technical credibility and lead decisively while also empowering your team members with the right degree of autonomy to solve problems with their specialized skills. That’s why HealthRules Payer has been ranked number one its class among CAP Solutions.
