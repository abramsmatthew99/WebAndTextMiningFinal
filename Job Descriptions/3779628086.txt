<p><strong><u>Senior Software Engineer - Medical Devices</u></strong></p><p><br/></p><p>Are you a seasoned Senior Software Engineer with a passion for innovation and a drive to make a difference in the medical field?</p><p><br/></p><p>Do you thrive in a fast-paced environment and enjoy collaborating with cross-functional teams to bring groundbreaking medical devices to life?</p><p><br/></p><p>If so, then we have the perfect opportunity for you!</p><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p>Our client, a leading innovator in the medical device industry, is seeking an experienced Senior Software Engineer to play a pivotal role in the design, development, and implementation of software solutions for our medical devices and systems. As a key member of the engineering team, you will contribute to the advancement of groundbreaking technologies that directly impact patient well-being.</p><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Responsibilities:</u></strong></p><ul><li>Software Design and Development - Design and implement innovative features for our robotic medical imaging platform.</li><li>Develop multi-threaded programs and testing tools using Python or C++.</li><li>Design and implement robust data structures to enhance software performance.</li><li>Ensure high-quality documentation, including program readability, code flow diagrams, and sequence diagrams.</li><li>Collaborate with software, imaging, product, data science, and clinical teams to understand needs and implement features for image-guided navigational tools in robotic systems.</li><li>Explore, understand, and integrate open-source APIs for software development.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Required Qualifications:</u></strong></p><ul><li>Master's degree in computer science or related technical field</li><li>6 years of professional experience in programming using Python, C++, and related languages in a Linux-based environment.</li><li>3 years of experience in the Medical Device industry or related field. </li><li>Hands-on experience with low-level code development for embedded systems and instrumentation control, particularly with multithreading.</li><li>Proficiency in using software development tools such as gdb, gcc, git, and IDEs.</li><li>Strong technical skills, including a deep understanding of data structures and algorithms.</li><li>Excellent problem-solving and debugging capabilities.</li><li>Outstanding collaboration and communication skills.</li><li>Work effectively both independently, taking ownership of coding solutions, and collaboratively, seeking input from the team when necessary.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Desirable Experience (not required):</u></strong></p><ul><li>Experience with MRI-guided technologies and/or Robotics systems.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>The typical base pay range for this role is USD $150,000 - $190,000 per year. Compensation offered will be determined by level, job-related knowledge, skills, and experience.</p><p><br/></p><p>If you are a highly motivated and experienced Senior Software Engineer who is passionate about making a difference in healthcare, then we encourage you to apply!</p>
