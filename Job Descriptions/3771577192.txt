<strong>SUMMER 2024<br/><br/></strong>Shure offers a challenging, fun and rewarding summer internship program. The twelve-week program is offered to undergraduate and graduate students. We offer internships with a variety of work arrangements from onsite interns to fully remote in US. Each intern will receive a competitive salary. Additionally, Interns who relocate to Illinois for onsite internships will receive a housing stipend to cover living expenses. Applications will be collected, reviewed, and selected candidates will be contacted in late fall/early winter.<br/><br/>The Shure Strategic Technology Team is looking for an Audio Data Science Intern to help research, develop, and demonstrate novel product features and use cases enabled by machine learning and artificial intelligence. You will to AI-assisted enhancements to our applications in audio or joint audio/video, such as blind room characterization, speech enhancement, audio feature analysis, speaker identification, and more. The project may target devices such as microphones, conference call systems, audio plug-ins, audio processing software, and more.<br/><br/>We are a diverse group of engineers, scientists, designers, and people with other backgrounds who invent and prototype useful, playful, and beautiful things. Our vision is to make life easier, more connected, more entertaining, more personal, and more productive. This is your opportunity to join our talented professionals as an intern to work on cutting-edge technologies and cool projects.<br/><br/>This Internship can be Onsite or Hybrid.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Work collaboratively with data scientists and engineers to identify audio use cases of value that leverage machine learning and artificial intelligence.</li><li>Research, build, train, and quantitatively/qualitatively assess deep learning models.</li><li>Identify and capture datasets from different sources for model development, which may include discovering, recording, simulating, cleaning, and pre-processing the data.</li><li>Provide demonstrations of the concept, design, and results to peers and stakeholders.</li><li>Document all design, experiments, and results.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Completion of at least two years of coursework in Electrical or Computer Engineering, Computer Science, Mathematics, or related field with an emphasis in Data Science or Machine Learning preferred.</li><li>An interest in audio is required, experience with audio of any kind preferred.</li><li>Experience with Python’s scientific libraries (numpy, scipy, matplotlib, etc</li><li>Experience with training and verifying machine learning and deep learning models in frameworks such as TensorFlow or PyTorch.</li><li>Good verbal and written communication skills.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Who We Are<br/><br/></strong>Shure’s mission is to be the most trusted audio brand worldwide – and for nearly a century, our Core Values have aligned us to be just that. Founded in 1925, we are a leading global manufacturer of audio equipment known for quality, reliability, and durability. We engineer microphones, headphones, wireless audio systems, conferencing systems, and more. And quality doesn’t stop at our products. Our talented teams strive for perfection and innovate every chance they get. We offer an Associate-first culture, flexible work arrangements, and opportunity for all.<br/><br/>Shure Incorporated is headquartered in Niles, Illinois, with remote and hybrid opportunities throughout the United States. We have more than 35 regional sales offices, engineering hubs, and manufacturing facilities throughout the Americas, EMEA, and Asia.<br/><br/><strong>THE MIX MATTERS<br/><br/></strong>Don’t check off every box in the job requirements? No problem! We recognize that every professional journey is unique and are committed to providing an equitable candidate experience for all prospective Shure Associates. If you’re excited about this role, believe you’ve got the skills to be successful, and share our passion for creating an inclusive, diverse, equitable, and accessible work environment, then apply!
