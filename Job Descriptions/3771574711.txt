<strong>SUMMER 2024<br/><br/></strong>Shure offers a challenging, fun and rewarding summer internship program. The twelve-week program is offered to undergraduate and graduate students. We offer internships with a variety of work arrangements from onsite interns to fully remote in US. Each intern will receive a competitive salary. Additionally, Interns who relocate to Illinois for onsite internships will receive a housing stipend to cover living expenses. Applications will be collected, reviewed, and selected candidates will be contacted in late fall/early winter.<br/><br/>The Shure Strategic Technology Team is looking for a Software Prototyping Intern to help prototype and demonstrate forward-leaning use cases in audio and video machine learning and artificial intelligence. You will contribute to creating and executing a pipeline to migrate Machine Learning, Artificial Intelligence, and DSP audio and video algorithms to embedded and/or cloud targets. You will work closely with data scientists and DSP engineers to optimize algorithms in consideration of latency, computational, and memory constraints. This role additional supports demonstration of technology by producing SW and embedded HW prototypes by considering end-user experience in prototyping activities.<br/><br/>We are a diverse group of engineers, scientists, designers, and people with other backgrounds who invent and prototype useful, playful, and beautiful things. Our vision is to make life easier, more connected, more entertaining, more personal, and more productive. This is your opportunity to join our talented professionals as an intern to work on cutting-edge technologies and cool projects.<br/><br/>This Internship can be Onsite or Hybrid.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Help prototype and demonstrate forward-leaning audio and video use cases in machine learning and artificial intelligence.</li><li>Develop embedded and/or web-based code bases to implement machine learning models created by data scientists</li><li>Work with data scientists and DSP engineers to jointly optimize algorithms around latency, computational complexity, and memory constraints</li><li>Document all designs and results. Follow established code development and archiving practices</li><li>Leverage machine learning frameworks and implement bottleneck operations or models directly, as needed</li><li>Target platforms may include x86, ARM, NPUs, GPUs, Mac, mobile apps, and cloud/IoT applications and services.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Completion of at least 3 years of coursework in computer science, electrical engineer, computer engineering, or related field with coursework or experience in computer programming and/or data science.</li><li>Experience with embedded software development, Mac &amp; PC application development, mobile apps, and cloud-based applications and services</li><li>Experience with full stack development with languages such as Python, Java, JavaScript, and C++.</li><li>Hands-on experience with machine learning and deep learning models and frameworks, and training deep learning models leveraging GPUs with tools such as Keras, TensorFLow, PyTorch, MXNet, Arm-NN, etc.</li><li>Good verbal and written communication skills<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Who We Are<br/><br/></strong>Shure’s mission is to be the most trusted audio brand worldwide – and for nearly a century, our Core Values have aligned us to be just that. Founded in 1925, we are a leading global manufacturer of audio equipment known for quality, reliability, and durability. We engineer microphones, headphones, wireless audio systems, conferencing systems, and more. And quality doesn’t stop at our products. Our talented teams strive for perfection and innovate every chance they get. We offer an Associate-first culture, flexible work arrangements, and opportunity for all.<br/><br/>Shure Incorporated is headquartered in Niles, Illinois, with remote and hybrid opportunities throughout the United States. We have more than 35 regional sales offices, engineering hubs, and manufacturing facilities throughout the Americas, EMEA, and Asia.<br/><br/><strong>THE MIX MATTERS<br/><br/></strong>Don’t check off every box in the job requirements? No problem! We recognize that every professional journey is unique and are committed to providing an equitable candidate experience for all prospective Shure Associates. If you’re excited about this role, believe you’ve got the skills to be successful, and share our passion for creating an inclusive, diverse, equitable, and accessible work environment, then apply!
