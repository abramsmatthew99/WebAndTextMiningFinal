Join us.<br/><br/>About the Team<br/>The data analytics team will be responsible for data analysis of TikTok e-commerce's US e-commerce logistics operations. The e-commerce industry has seen tremendous growth in recent years and has become a hotly contested space amongst leading Internet companies, and its future growth cannot be underestimated. With millions of loyal users globally, we believe TikTok is an ideal platform to deliver a brand new and better e-commerce experience to our users. <br/><br/>We are looking for passionate and talented data analysts to join our product and operations team, together we can build an e-commerce ecosystem that is innovative, secure and intuitive for our users.<br/><br/>Responsibilities <br/>1. Responsible for data analysis of TikTok e-commerce's US logistics operations.<br/>2. Maintain &amp; improve operation performance index; assist top management to monitor the weekly and bi-monthly operation performance; analyze results using statistical techniques and provide ongoing reports.<br/>3. Cooperate with logistics team to set strategy, e.g. Leadtime improvement, cost reduction, logistics experience rating, etc; communicate findings to influence others to take actions.<br/>4. Follow-up data analysis of local operation teams in several overseas markets, support operation and provide optimization suggestions.<br/>5. Cooperate with Products and ETL teams to define system requirements, build data products, improve and roll-out product features to support business. <br/><br/><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong> 1. Degree holder in Logistics and Supply Chain Management, Operation Research, Mathematics, Statistics, Computer Science is preferred.<br/>2. At least 3 years of working experience in data analysis.<br/>3. Proficient in SQL and familiar with Python or R.<br/>4. High energy, self-motivation and ability to work in a fast-growing environment.<br/><br/>TikTok is committed to creating an inclusive space where employees are valued for their skills, experiences, and unique perspectives.
