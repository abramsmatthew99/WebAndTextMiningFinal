<strong>Description<br/><br/></strong>Job Description<br/><br/><strong>Company Overview<br/><br/></strong>As the nation's largest producer of clean, carbon-free energy, Constellation is a company purposely-built to meet the challenges of the climate crisis. Constellation has been the leader in clean energy production for more than a decade and we are growing our company and capabilities. Now, we're accelerating, speeding our low-carbon or no-carbon power to more people in more places, day and night, providing our customers and communities with options to buy, manage and use energy as part of their decarbonization mission. The race is on to confront the climate crisis and Constellation is ready to meet the challenge. Come join us as we lead energy, together.<br/><br/><strong>TOTAL REWARDS<br/><br/></strong>Constellation offers a wide range of benefits and rewards, designed to help our employees thrive professionally and personally. In addition to highly competitive salaries, we offer a bonus program, 401(k) with company match, employee stock purchase program; comprehensive medical, dental and vision benefits, including a robust wellness program; paid time off for vacation, holidays and sick days; and much more.<br/><br/>Expected salary range of $110,700 to $123,000, varies based on experience, along with comprehensive benefits package that includes bonus and 401(k).<br/><br/><strong>Primary Purpose Of Position<br/><br/></strong>The Software Asset Management team works across a number of disciplines to allow Corporate Systems and Services (CSS) to deliver services of a high standard to Constellation IT. Accountable for Software Asset Management and compliance, it is a multi-dimensional team with responsibility for the way in which services operate and the way in which data and information is used to support service delivery. The Software Asset Management Lead will lead the team ensuring compliance across IT. Experience in procurement, completion of vendor audits including major suppliers, and IAITAM certification with ITIL qualifications desirable.<br/><br/><strong>Primary Duties And Accountabilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Maintain an inventory of all software licenses and subscriptions across the organization. </li><li>Ensure compliance with software licensing agreements and regulations. </li><li>Monitor software usage to identify potential compliance risks and optimization opportunities. Assist in budget planning for future software licensing needs and costs.</li><li>Identify opportunities to reduce software costs through license consolidation, renegotiation, or elimination of unused licenses. </li><li>Implement strategies to minimize over-licensing and non-compliance penalties.</li><li>Maintain accurate and up-to-date records of software licenses, contracts, and related documents. Generate regular reports on software asset status, compliance, and cost savings for management and stakeholders. </li><li>Provide greater transparency, compliance, and continual improvement across the organization while showing demonstrable value generation.</li><li>Develop and enforce well-defined policies and processes for life cycle management and risk mitigation around software assets.</li><li>Educate employees about software compliance and asset management best practices.</li><li>Prepare for software audits by conducting internal audits and ensuring all necessary documentation is readily available. </li><li>Collaborate with auditors to provide required information and resolve audit findings.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><strong>MINIMUM QUALIFICATIONS<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelors degree in Computer Science or related discipline and typically 5 to 8 years experience in Information Technology or equivalent combination of education and work experience.</li><li>Proficiency with data analysis in Microsoft Excel</li><li>Experience leading small IT projects or sub-teams and knowledge of IT project management.</li><li>Strong problem solving and analysis ability.</li><li>Excellent communications skills (written and verbal)</li><li>Ability to work with remote project teams<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Preferred Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Utilize understanding of customer's business needs to determine requirements</li><li>Applies technical expertise to plan, design, build or support required products and processes</li><li>Help other team members learn appropriate theories, practices and principles that relate to their skill set portfolio</li><li>As assigned, manage budget for area of responsibility</li><li>As assigned, manage working relationship with outsourcing partners</li><li>ServiceNow, Documentum, AssetSuite 8, and Passport experience</li><li>ServiceNow certification in Software Asset Management (SAM), Hardware Asset Management (HAM), Configuration Management, or Discovery preferred.</li><li>ITIL, IAITAM, CPIM, or CSCP certifications</li><li>Knowledge of IT agreements and legal terms<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>EEO<br/><br/></strong>At
