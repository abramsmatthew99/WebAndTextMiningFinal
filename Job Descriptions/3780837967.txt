<strong>Job Summary:<br/><br/></strong>DT Professional Services is seeking a mid-level Test Automation Engineer in a remote capacity! As a Test Automation Engineer, you will design automation tests, including designing automation scripts and finding solutions for automation problems. The ideal candidate will be committed to developing skills in order to provide up-to-date solutions. To be successful as a test automation engineer, you must be exceptionally accurate and possess a superior mathematical aptitude, as well as be proficient in a variety of programming languages.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities</strong>:<br/><br/><ul><li>Provide quality assurance and quality control on in-house applications including black box testing, regression testing, load testing, stress testing, functional testing, and interoperability testing</li><li>Develop test plans, test cases, test scripts and automated test reports for manual and automated tests on multiple software projects of varying size</li><li>Contribute to establishing procedures and processes for the quality control of products, taking initiative to identify areas to improve internal processes related to product releases and bug fixes</li><li>Develop a scalable / maintainable automation framework from scratch</li><li>Communicate effectively with all stakeholders in written and verbal formats<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Basic Qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Clearance</li><li>3+ years of experience in developing and executing automation test scripts in Windows environment</li><li>3+ years of experience in building and implementing automation frameworks from scratch</li><li>3+ years of hands-on experience with object-oriented programming languages (JavaScript or Typescript is preferred)</li><li>3+ years of experience in software development practices (TDD or ATDD is preferred)</li><li>Knowledge of standard practices and techniques in software QA testing including black/white box testing, regression testing, load testing, stress testing, functional testing, interoperability testing</li><li>Ability to write test plans and conduct program testing of all programs within a module, including the interaction between web/client-based applications, database, and external data sources</li><li>Ability to write automation scripts for black-box testing of commercial web-based applications</li><li>Experience and strong understanding of automation tools such as Selenium Web Driver, Cypress, Playwright</li><li>Experience in translating software specifications into test cases</li><li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team</li><li>Experience giving technical presentation to technical and non-technical attendees</li><li>Experience providing surge support to other programs as needed</li><li>Ability to travel as needed</li><li>Ability to work after hours and weekends as needed<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Preferred Qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Current CompTIA Security+ Certification or ability to complete certification within 30 days</li><li>Bachelor's degree in computer science, software development, or a related field</li><li>3+ years of experience implementing test automation frameworks on COTS applications in cloud environments</li><li>Experience with Jira and Azure Dev Ops for defect tracking, bug reporting and task creations</li><li>Proficiency with cloud technologies (Azure)</li><li>Previous experience with logistics and/or supply chain systems<br/><br/></li></ul>The compensation for this position: $85k - $100k annually.
