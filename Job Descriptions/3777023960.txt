<p><strong>Machine Learning Engineer (Independent Contractor) - U.S. Citizen </strong></p><p>San Francisco Bay Area (Remote with occasional in-person meetings)</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Introduction to Datasophical and Our Requirements:</strong></p><p>Datasophical is at the forefront of AI-driven cybersecurity innovation, creating advanced solutions for complex challenges in the field. We are committed to developing resilient, efficient, and ethically-aligned AI technologies. Our focus is on enhancing cloud, network, and application security and compliance with rigorous standards like CMMC and FedRAMP by utilizing AI to revolutionize cybersecurity practices. We are now seeking a highly skilled Machine Learning Engineer, required to be a U.S. Citizen due to compliance requirements of the Department of Defense <span>CMMC</span> cybersecurity program. </p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Role Overview:</strong></p><p>You will be instrumental in improving both our online and offline AI models, using popular Python libraries to create robust Large Language Models (LLMs). Your proficiency in Python and expertise in feature engineering will be crucial in developing high-performance, low-latency models. In your role, you'll be responsible for configuring Nvidia GPUs and fine-tuning model parameters. A crucial aspect of your duties will be to effectively communicate complex AI model concepts to clients. This includes not only explaining the underlying mathematical principles but also articulating how large language model architectures, like transformers, arrive at specific answers.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Responsibilities:</strong></p><ul><li>Create and enhance LLMs for cybersecurity applications.</li><li>Develop models resilient to adversarial attacks. </li><li>Test models for accuracy, reproducibility, and ethical AI use. </li><li>Set up and optimize Nvidia GPU hardware for model performance. </li><li>Communicate complex model functionalities to customers and stakeholders. </li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Qualifications:</strong></p><ul><li>Candidates should be currently enrolled in, or have graduated with a Master’s or PhD degree in Machine Learning, Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence, Data Science, Engineering, Mathematics, or a related field. Equivalent work experience in these fields will also be considered. </li><li>Experience in making AI and machine learning models reproducible and explainable.</li><li>Skilled in testing models for accuracy and ethicality.</li><li>Proven experience in writing and publishing research papers in the field of artificial intelligence. </li><li>Demonstrated ability to comprehend and stay on top of cutting-edge AI research literature. </li><li>Expertise in Python and machine learning libraries. </li></ul><p><br/></p>
