Find your future with us.<br/><br/><strong>The Boeing Company</strong> is looking for an <strong>Entry Level Industrial Engineer </strong>to join our team in <strong>Mesa, AZ</strong>.<br/><br/>Boeing is the world's largest aerospace company and leading manufacturer of commercial airplanes and defense, space and security systems. We are engineers and technicians. Skilled scientists and thinkers. Bold innovators and dreamers! Join us, and you can build something better for yourself, for our customers and for the world.<br/><br/><strong>Position Responsibilities: <br/><br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Applies basic industrial engineering concepts, approaches and techniques to product, process and services to meet program requirements. </li><li>Supports the development of plant and equipment layouts, capacity plans, accident prevention measures, sequencing of operations and work flows. </li><li>Participates in the evaluation of new techniques and technological developments for integration into the production process and service operations. </li><li>Assists with designing, maintaining, and optimizing the production system by integrating design, supply base, facilities, equipment, and personnel. </li><li>Conducts basic work flow design and analysis. </li><li>Develops simple models, databases and spreadsheets to analyze data and provides summary analysis and metrics for consultation to leaders. </li><li>Supports research, design, develop, improve, and implementation of processes and tools to enhance schedule performance, lower cost, and improve quality, for large scale systems integration and asset utilization. </li><li>Supports implementation plans and programs for facility/factory modifications and revisions to operating methods. </li><li>Documents product assembly instructions and standardizes assembly practices. </li><li>Supports the preparation of operations plans, includes work statement analysis, budgeting and forecasts, capacity and constraint analysis and production change, to ensure technical goals and objectives are met. </li><li>Supports execution planning by using project management, planning and scheduling and shop load practices. </li><li>Provides production, supply chain logistics, performance visibility and recovery planning support. </li><li>Follows compliance requirements with established industry safety, design standards and ergonomic guidelines.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>This position is expected to be 100% onsite.
