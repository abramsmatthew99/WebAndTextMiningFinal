<p><strong>Job Title: Mid Project Manager</strong></p><p><strong>Location: Washington, DC, Wilmington, DE, Atlanta, GA, Philadelphia, PA</strong></p><p><strong>Duration: 12+ Months</strong></p><p><strong>Client: Amtrak</strong></p><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Job Descriptions:</u></strong></p><p><strong>The Project Manager – Mid is a member of the Infrastructure Platform services, department specific PMO, managing mainframe migration related technology projects within the Information Technology department. The Project Manager follows the best practices and utilizes Project Management tools, methodologies, and processes to successfully keep projects on budget and schedule through to full implementation and rollout of all hardware and software. These are senior level, complex programs and projects involving multiple departments at Amtrak as well as different outside vendors and sub vendors depending upon the program/project. The Project Manager utilizes formal project management monitoring and control processes including, but not limited to, managing the project schedule, tracking and mitigating project risks and issues, formal change management, configuration management, quality assurance processes, budget preparation, tracking and reporting</strong></p><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Essential Functions</u></strong> </p><ul><li>Lead and coordinate the planning, execution, and monitoring of the TPF mainframe migration project, ensuring that the project meets the business objectives, timeline, and budget.</li><li>Data center migration experience.</li><li>Manage the communication and collaboration with internal and external stakeholders, including the TPF development team, the datacenter team, the network team, the security team, and the vendors.</li><li>Apply the best practices and methodologies of TPF mainframe development, testing, deployment, and maintenance, as well as the DevOps principles and tools for TPF mainframe.</li><li>Identify and mitigate the risks and issues that may arise during the migration project, such as the performance, availability, and reliability of the TPF mainframe system.</li><li>Directs technical leads and other project team members and consultants/contractors. </li><li>Development and maintenance of all required project documents including 1) A Project Charter 2) A Project Management Plan 3) Requirements documents 4) Design documents 5) Input to architecture documents 6) Test Plan 7) Test results 8) Implementation Plans 9) Post Implementation and Lessons Learned 10) Project schedules and budgets 11) Resource management plans </li><li>Produces weekly program status reports for each program. </li><li>Hold project status and other meetings as necessary. </li><li>Manages the project schedule via the use of MS Enterprise Project Management (EPM) and MS Project Professional 2010. </li><li>Reports schedule and budget variances. </li><li>Creates and maintains SharePoint project site for each project within the EPM system. </li><li>Ensure there are quality and well-maintained project schedules, baselines and estimates to complete program/project and report variances. </li><li>Reports on risks and provides ongoing risk management plan, risk assessment and mitigation within a program/project. </li><li>Reports on ongoing issues and changes utilizing existing processes and templates and publishing reports within a program/project. </li><li>Creates and publishes weekly status report of activities, accomplishments, issues and action to resolve issues. </li><li>Identifies and ensures all activities required to successfully meet the operational readiness review are completed on time. </li><li>May create a Statement of Work (SOW) for the Request for Proposal (RFP) and associated vendor response evaluation. Verify the creation of all documents from vendors as outlined in the SOW or creation of new ones as required. </li><li>Provides a SOW for ongoing support services as necessary. </li><li>Evaluates criteria for product and proposal analysis. </li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Deliverables</u></strong> </p><ul><li>Deliver programs and projects on schedule and on budget </li><li>Present projects at the Quality Checkpoints </li><li>Present projects at the Department Level Project Reviews </li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Requirements</u></strong> </p><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in project management, Accounting, Business Administration, Finance, Economics, Computer Science, Information Systems or an equivalent combination of work experience, training and education. </li><li>At least seven years’ experience and significant hands-on Program and Project management experience from project initiation to implementation using formal project management methodologies and processes. </li><li>At least seven years’ experience and significant experience in technology management, analysis and administration. </li><li>Demonstrated ability to interact with internal and external stakeholders with evidence of willingness and ability to express and protect departmental interests. </li><li>Demonstrated ability to apply strong analytical skills. </li><li>Demonstrated effective communicator and writer, able to tailor message to receiving audience. </li><li>Demonstrated experience managing multiple projects simultaneously. </li><li>Demonstrated experience using MS Project Office Professional and MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint and Visio. </li><li>Experience with project management tools such as MS Project Server (EPM), Primavera, etc. </li></ul><p> </p><p><strong><u>Preferred</u></strong> </p><ul><li>At least four years’ experience with project management tools such as Microsoft Project Server (EPM), etc. </li><li>PMP Certified</li></ul>
