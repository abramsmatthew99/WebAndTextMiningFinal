<strong>Company Overview<br/><br/></strong>Kairos Power is a new nuclear energy technology and engineering company whose mission is to enable the world’s transition to clean energy, with the ultimate goal to dramatically improve people’s quality of life while protecting the environment. In this role you will support engineering teams in the development of code and compute infrastructure related to the design and analysis of nuclear power systems. This includes developing code for a variety of software projects, deploying CI/CD pipelines, and building out cloud-based compute infrastructure. You will play a crucial role in optimizing our code development processes, ensuring smooth and consistent code integration, and deploying code into production. The position requires a strong self-starting mentality and the ability to develop and deploy novel solutions in support of the company mission.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Collaborate with nuclear and other non-software engineers to define and develop software projects based on their support efficient code development and deployment.</li><li>Contribute to the documentation of code, ensuring clarity for current and future team members.</li><li>Review existing code and recommend
