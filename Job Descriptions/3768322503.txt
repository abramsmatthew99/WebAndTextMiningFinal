        Job Description: Entry-Level Help Desk Person at a Healthcare Clinic<br/><br/>Position Overview: We are seeking a highly motivated and customer-focused individual to join our healthcare clinic as an Entry-Level Help Desk Person. This role will be responsible for providing technical support and assistance to our staff members, ensuring the smooth operation of our computer systems and software applications. The ideal candidate will have excellent communication skills, a strong problem-solving ability, and a passion for delivering exceptional customer service.<br/><br/>Responsibilities:<br/><br/><ul><li>Provide first-level technical support to clinic staff, including troubleshooting hardware, software, and network-related issues.</li><li>Respond to help desk tickets and phone calls in a timely and professional manner, ensuring that all incidents are properly logged and documented.</li><li>Assist with the setup and configuration of computer systems, peripherals, and software applications for new staff members.</li><li>Conduct user training sessions to educate staff on basic software usage and best practices.</li><li>Collaborate with the IT team to diagnose and resolve more complex technical issues, escalating problems when necessary.</li><li>Assist in maintaining an inventory of computer equipment, ensuring that all assets are properly tracked and accounted for.</li><li>Perform routine maintenance and updates on computer systems to ensure optimal performance and security.</li><li>Stay up-to-date with the latest technology trends and advancements in the healthcare industry to provide effective support and recommendations.</li><li>Any other duties as assigned.<br/><br/></li></ul>Educational Requirements: To be considered for this position, candidates must meet the following educational requirements:<br/><br/><ul><li>High school diploma or equivalent.</li><li>Associate's degree in information technology, computer science, or a related field is preferred.</li><li>Relevant industry certifications (such as CompTIA A+, Network+, or Microsoft Certified Professional) are a plus.<br/><br/></li></ul>Skills and Qualifications:<br/><br/><ul><li>Strong technical aptitude and familiarity with computer hardware, software, and networking concepts.</li><li>Excellent problem-solving and analytical skills to diagnose and resolve technical issues.</li><li>Exceptional customer service skills with the ability to communicate technical information in a clear and concise manner.</li><li>Strong organizational skills with the ability to prioritize tasks and manage time effectively.</li><li>Attention to detail and accuracy in documentation and record-keeping.</li><li>Ability to work independently and collaboratively as part of a team.</li><li>Knowledge of healthcare industry regulations (e.g., HIPAA) and experience in a healthcare environment is desirable but not required.<br/><br/></li></ul>We offer a competitive salary and benefits package, as well as opportunities for growth and professional development.
