        Driven by growth and new technology initiatives, PrismHR has an opening for a Manager, Applications and Infrastructure Operations. This role has direct management for a team of infrastructure engineers with varied disciplines across both our legacy and go forward technology initiatives.<br/><br/>This role will require someone with a hands-on type of leadership style, someone that can guide others and still roll up their sleeves to contribute to our growing infrastructure. You will collaborate across multiple groups within the Product Delivery organization including development, qa, architecture and program management to help deliver the best service for our clients.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Lead and manage a team of 3 to 6 application and infrastructure engineers, providing guidance and mentorship to foster individual and team growth.</li><li>Lead team members in configuring, monitoring, maintaining and troubleshooting the company’s cloud-based SaaS applications.</li><li>Deploy and administer cloud infrastructure in Azure and AWS using Infrastructure as Code.</li><li>Administer and leverage various administrative and observability platforms used in conjunction with delivering PrismHR’s applications.</li><li>Manage containerized technologies like Docker and Kubernetes to enhance application portability and agility.</li><li>Troubleshoot application configurations and diagnose operational issues, ensuring timely resolution and minimal impact on user experience.</li><li>Implement security best practices for application and infrastructure environments.</li><li>Monitor system performance and identify opportunities for improvement, analyzing trends and proposing solutions for optimization.</li><li>Stay abreast of current trends and emerging technologies in SaaS delivery and public cloud proactively evaluating and implementing solutions for continuous improvement.</li><li>Provide formal, less formal and ‘as needed’ continual performance and personal growth feedback to assigned engineers</li><li>Facilitate incident response for critical issues and outages which could include after business hours response.</li><li>Create and maintain comprehensive documentation as it relates to application and configuration configurations.</li><li>Continue to evolve the use of automation and configuration management tools.</li><li>Participate in the creation, maintenance, testing, and execution of the Disaster Recovery plans.</li><li>Oversee capacity planning activities as it relates to public cloud spend and efficiency.</li><li>Participate in after-hours software deployments, maintenance windows, and on-call availability as needed.</li><li>Function as a project lead to ensure major projects are kept on track and key deliverables met.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Experience<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>10+ years of infrastructure and application support in a production SaaS environment</li><li>4+ years in a management role leading technical resources in a 24/7 environment.</li><li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, MIS or Engineering.</li><li>Experience with Docker, Kubernetes or running containers on PaaS.</li><li>Experience with automation and IaC tools such as Puppet and Teraform.</li><li>Experience working in a GitOps/DevOps environment.</li><li>Experience troubleshooting application configurations and diagnosing operational issues.</li><li>Ability to prioritize effectively and work independently in a fast-paced environment.</li><li>Solid understanding of public cloud concepts and services in AWS and Azure.</li><li>Scripting experience (Powershell, Bash, Python) a plus.</li><li>Experience supporting security/compliance programs such as SOC1, SOC2, or PCI.</li><li>Excellent problem solving and effective research skills.</li><li>Strong customer service skills and communication as must.</li><li>Experience with Palo Alto NGFW’s and F5 Load Balancers a plus.</li><li>Dedication and willingness to work alternative schedules when necessary.</li><li>Industry certifications are strongly desired: Red Hat (RHCSA/RHCE), Windows (MCSA / MCSE), or Azure/AWS certifications.<br/><br/></li></ul>PrismHR is a fast-paced SaaS company which provides customers with a cloud-based payroll process software application. PrismHR also provides professional services including system implementation consulting, custom configurations, and training.
