The Service Desk Team supports all Ares employees, including those in other locations, remotely as needed. They will also be responsible for aligning themselves with the relevant business units and departments and proactively strategizing business needs as it pertains to Technology-related requirements. The Service Desk Engineer will also participate in understanding, solutioning and deploying updates or fixes to the firm.<br/><br/><strong>Primary Functions And Essential Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Maintains a high degree of customer service and confidentiality (as needed) for all support queries while adhering to all service management principles</li><li>Manages day to day incident submissions; deploying solutions for recurring issues at the workstation level.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong>Education<br/><br/><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree (or advanced) in business administration, Computer Science or related technical field<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Experience Required<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>5+ years<br/><br/></li></ul>General Requirements<br/><br/><ul><li>Experience with using ticketing systems (ServiceNow preferred)</li><li>Operating within an ITIL framework</li><li>Support within a high-volume, regulated enterprise environment (preferably finance)</li><li>Creating / advocating user enabling technologies</li><li>Technical Depth in supporting Widows 10 &amp; common software (productivity, security) for an enterprise environment</li><li>Experience using Active Directory</li><li>Remote support using BeyondTrust (Bomgar) remote access appliance or similar</li><li>Asset management principles and software use</li><li>Support of the O365 suite</li><li>Use of System Center Configuration Manager</li><li>IOS/Android support</li><li>Experience with Microsoft Intune (preferred)</li><li>Experience using PrinterLogic (preferred)</li><li>Minimum basic demonstrable knowledge with one at least one of the following:</li><li>PowerShell Scripting</li><li>ServiceNow development</li><li>Statistical Analysis</li><li>Process design</li><li>Knowledge management (KCS)</li><li>HTML</li><li>Process design, Analysis &amp; Metrics</li><li>Able to understand and articulate the benefits of technical implementations sufficient to drive adoption</li><li>Someone who is not constrained by ‘what is’ but instead thinks of ‘what could be’<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Reporting Relationships<br/><br/></strong><strong>Compensation<br/><br/></strong>The anticipated base salary range for this position is listed below.
