<strong>Introduction<br/><br/></strong>Introduction to the job<br/><br/>ASML US brings together the most creative minds in science and technology to develop lithography machines that are key to producing faster, cheaper, more energy-efficient microchips. We design, develop, integrate, market and service these advanced machines, which enable our customers - the world’s leading chipmakers - to reduce the size and increase the functionality of their microchips, which in turn leads to smaller, more powerful consumer electronics. Our headquarters are in Veldhoven, the Netherlands, and we have 18 office locations around the United States including main offices in Chandler, Arizona, San Jose and San Diego, California, Wilton, Connecticut, and Hillsboro, Oregon.<br/><br/>This position reports to the Manager of the US Development &amp; Engineering (D&amp;E) Business Planning team. Through effective collaboration and innovation, the Business Planning team supports the Wilton D&amp;E community with strategic and logistical tools to achieve optimized processes amidst changing business needs. We are currently searching for a highly motivated, self-starter who can help our team provide effective coordination and analysis.<br/><br/>This position requires access to controlled technology, as defined in the Export Administration Regulations (15 C.F.R.<br/><br/><ul><li>730, et seq.). Qualified candidates must be legally authorized to access such controlled technology prior to beginning work. Business demands may require ASML to proceed with candidates who are immediately eligible to access controlled technology.<br/><br/></li></ul>Roles and responsibilities<br/><br/><ul><li>Leading, supporting end to end trouble shooting of issues that effect D&amp;E processes.</li><li>Drive, facilitate, and evaluate various non-product planning needs.</li><li>Analyze problems and set priorities between different alternatives or deliverables</li><li>Specify and maintain business planning data sources, procedures and guidelines; Ensure data integrity and effective data management practices.</li><li>Model the impacts of a variety of business decisions, obtain stakeholder buy-in, and develop reporting to communicate results.</li><li>Serve as an analytical and process “hub;” provide guidance and training to D&amp;E staff regarding planning processes and tools.</li><li>Define, plan, manage, and coordinate assigned projects to ensure execution and delivery.</li><li>Act as a recognized business partner by enabling effective decision making on all levels.</li><li>Pro-actively assist, enable and challenge all stakeholders.</li><li>Provide guidance to D&amp;E stakeholders around standard process and proactively develop continuous improvement strategies.</li><li>Build and maintains relationships with D&amp;E project teams to ensure effective collaboration.</li><li>Contribute to the team by living the 3Cs (challenge, collaborate, care) and building a sustaining function.<br/><br/></li></ul>Education and management knowledge.</li><li>Proficiency (expert) in Microsoft Office Excel, Outlook, Word, PowerPoint, SharePoint, Teams, Forms (Power BI, Spotfire skills a plus).</li><li>Experience building and maintaining SharePoint sites.</li><li>Ability to solve problems and implement solutions to complex problems with available resources.</li><li>Attention to detail and continuous improvement mindset.</li><li>Excellent communication and influencing skills – written and verbal.</li><li>Self-motivated and flexible to changing priorities and project needs.</li><li>Ability to achieve short/mid-term results while maintaining focus on long-term strategy.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Skills<br/><br/></strong>Working at the cutting edge of tech, you’ll always have new challenges and new You won’t work in a silo. Instead, you’ll be part of a creative, dynamic work environment where you’ll collaborate with supportive colleagues. There is always space for creative and unique points of view.
