<strong>Job Number: R0184277<br/><br/></strong>Network Engineer<br/><br/><strong>The Opportunity: <br/><br/></strong>A well-designed network is critical to move data and enable organizations to achieve their missions, but how can an organization make sure their network will fit their current and evolving needs? Crafting the right network, with the right equipment and software, requires a combination of technical skill and careful planning. That’s why we need you, an experienced network engineer who knows how to develop the exact network the Navy needs.<br/><br/>As a network engineer on our team, you’ll use your experience in network design and architecture, system interoperability, network transport layers, and virtual routing and forwarding to support the nation’s defense and respond to evolving adversaries. You’ll manage enterprise infrastructure, troubleshoot and resolve complex network issues, and increase security, reliability, and availability for mission-critical network systems. You’ll apply your expertise in routing and switching, integration of encryption gear, network design and architecture, and system interoperability as you join our team of problem-solvers to help the US Navy evolve and deploy new capabilities and technologies. Further your career with us as we help protect our country’s naval fleet.<br/><br/>Join us. The world can’t wait.<br/><br/><strong>You Have: <br/><br/></strong><ul><li>7 years of experience with IT and network hardware such as servers, routers, switches, and in-line network encryption devices </li><li>Experience with DoD networks both WAN and LAN, IPv4 and IPv6 address allocation, and Cisco Command Line Interface (CLI)</li><li>Experience with network testing hardware from vendors such as Spirent or Linktropy</li><li>Experience with General Dynamics KG series TACLANES</li><li>Experience with network testing, troubleshooting software tools such as iPerf, Nmap, or Wireshark</li><li>Knowledge of networking protocols and their implementation to include L2VPN, L3VPN, OSPF, BGP, DMVPN, and static routes</li><li>Knowledge of network security via firewalls including software and hardware, ACLs, and port security</li><li>TS/SCI clearance</li><li>Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Computer Science, or Information Technology</li><li>IAT Level II including Security+ CE Certification <br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Nice If You Have:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Experience with BASH, Ansible, Python, and Cisco Embedded Event Manager Scripting</li><li>Experience with ADNS and IC networks</li><li>Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) Certification <br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Clearance:<br/><br/></strong>Applicants selected will be subject to a security investigation and may need to meet eligibility requirements for access to classified information; TS/SCI clearance is required.<br/><br/> <br/><br/><strong>Create Your Career: <br/><br/></strong><strong>Grow With Us <br/><br/></strong>Your growth matters to us—that’s why we offer a variety of ways for you to develop your career.
