<p><strong>Overview</strong></p><p>Looking for an EMR Implementation and Optimization specialist to join our team. This role is required to be onsite, at least four days a week, working with our team in a brand new and modern office in Portsmouth, NH. If you are an energetic, driven, and hardworking individual with EMR implementation experience who is looking to be a part of an innovative and forward-thinking healthcare organization where your passion and ideas can flourish, then this may be the right role for you.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Responsibilities</strong></p><p>• Mobile Integration: Assist the team with the implementation and optimization of mobile EMR capabilities.</p><p>• Provide training and support to staff, fostering a culture of continuous improvement.</p><p>• Address mobile EMR issues in the dynamic and supportive onsite work environment.</p><p>• Coordinate with CSMs on client issues and follow through until the issues are resolved.</p><p>• Provide customer guidance, recommendations, and solutions throughout the entire implementation process.</p><p>• Share industry knowledge, proven process flows, and best practices to assist them in meeting their business needs.</p><p>• Leverage effective troubleshooting techniques (call-based and written form) to support clients during implementation.</p><p>• Communicate and give feedback to the broader business (including sales, product, client success, and support teams) throughout the implementation lifecycle.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Requirements</strong></p><p>• Bachelor's degree in healthcare informatics, IT, or a related field.</p><p>• Proven experience in EMR implementation, specifically with the NextGen Healthcare platform.</p><p>• Familiarity with mobile healthcare applications and integrations.</p><p>• Strong problem-solving and communication skills.</p><p>• Proven experience working with EMR systems in a healthcare setting, including implementation, training, and optimization.</p><p>• In-depth knowledge of EMR systems, their functionalities, and relevant regulatory requirements, such as HIPAA, HiTRUST, etc.</p><p>• Demonstrated knowledge of interoperability standards such as HL7, FHIR, and CCD/CDA.</p><p>• Solid problem solving and time management skills.</p><p>• Great interpersonal skills.</p><p>• Excellent communication skills (written and verbal).</p><p>• Strong attention to detail &amp; highly organized.</p>
