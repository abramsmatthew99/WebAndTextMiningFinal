As an Industrialization KPI Dashboard Intern, you will work closely with our team to assist in the development of Key Performance Indicator (KPI) dashboards aimed at improving industrial processes. This role involves gaining hands-on experience in data collection, analysis, and visualization to support informed decision-making within our organization.<br/><br/>This position requires access to controlled technology, as defined in the Export Administration Regulations (15 C.F.R.<br/><br/><ul><li>730, et seq.).
