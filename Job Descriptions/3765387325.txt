Job DescriptionCape Aegis, LLC in support of Arrow Alliance Industries is seeking a Network Administrator to support the Bureau of Indian Education (BIE) with architecture, setup, and installation of upgraded network equipment and infrastructure on BIE school campuses in the West. The Network Administrator Administrator is responsible for providing IP Network Engineering services to both manage and document the back-end and on-premises WAN/LAN, and provide IP Network Engineering configuration, services, and support to the Sr. IT Telecommunications Administrator, the Sr. Network and Systems Administrator and the client manager. Travel may be required to the campuses.Job Hours ClassificationFull-time Contract PositionTypeRemote (with some travel)Primary Responsibilities:Provides network engineering services including but not limited to network management and analytics, firewalls and security, networking, load balancing, programming languages, hardware and infrastructure, DNS, IoT, Virtualization and Automation, MPLS, ZTN, Hybrid WAN Networks. Prepares and maintains BIE Network Architecture documents. Automates maintenance as required. Tests and troubleshoots as required. Provides ongoing support as directed. Create and maintain documentation as it relates to network configuration, network mapping, Strategy & PlanningCollaborate with management and to assess near- and long-term network capacity needs.Design and deploy company LANs, WANs, and wireless networks, including servers, routers, switches, UPSs, and other hardware.Design, implement, and support firewalls, site-to-site VPNs, and remote-access VPNs Conduct research on network products, services, protocols, and standards to remain abreast of developments in the networking industry.Configure networks to ensure their smooth and reliable operation for fulfilling business objectives and processes.Monitor network performance and troubleshoot problem areas as needed.Oversee or perform installation, configuration, maintenance, and troubleshooting of end user workstation hardware, software, and peripheral devices.Ensure network connectivity of all servers, workstations, and other appliances on the network.Practice network asset management, including maintenance of network component inventory and related documentation and technical specifications information.Position RequirementsExperience in network management and analytics, firewalls and security, networking, load balancing, programming languages, hardware and infrastructure, DNS, IoT, Virtualization and Automation, MPLS, ZTN, Hybrid WAN Networks.Proven experience and success with LAN, WAN, and WLAN design and implementation.Proven experience with network capacity planning, network security principles, and general network management best practices.Working technical knowledge of current network hardware, protocols, and Internet standards, including routers, switches (layer 2/3), firewalls, remote access, DNS, BGP, OSPF, EIGRP, VLAN, QoS, DSL, Frame Relay, and related technologies.Experience designing, implementing, managing and supporting enterprise level IP networks that include support of a heterogeneous distributed desktop computing environment required.Excellent hardware troubleshooting experience.Extensive application support experience with network monitoring and analysis software,Competence with testing tools and procedures for voice and data circuits.Strong interpersonal, written, and oral communication skills.Able to conduct research into networking issues and products as required.Proven analytical and problem-solving abilities.Strong customer service orientation.Experience working in a team-oriented, collaborative environment.Education & Certification Requirements:Bachelor’s degree in computer science related field OR 5 years work experience.Certification preferred in one or more of the following: Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) certification required,Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP) preferred).Microsoft Certified Solutions Associate (MCSA)Has a strong knowledge of current industry standards such as NIST and ISO. Additional Requirements:Valid Driver’s LicenseComplete applicable BIE security and safety training and obtains relevant certifications.Personal Protective Equipment (PPE) required to be used while on campus as necessary.Must be able to write English effectively, using correct spelling and grammar to generate reports and documentation (e.g., status, trip, and deficiency reports).Experience documenting tasks using MS Word, Excel, and related software.Work Environment:This position is primarily remote, however, when requested, work locations include but are not limited to cable vaults, ladders, scissor lifts, building entrance facility, telecommunication facility, telecommunication rooms/closets, data centers, central office, administrative office locations, warehouses, outdoor storage areas.Work conditions include data centers but may also include on-campus environments including seasonal conditions typical for work areas located outdoors and indoors with non-climate-controlled facilities, dampness, adverse lighting conditions.On-site installation occurs at remote locations primarily in AZ and NM but also throughout the west.Travel may be required.Security Clearance Requirement:Background investigations for public trust or higher level positions are conducted to gather information to determine whether applicants are reliable, trustworthy, of good conduct and character, and loyal to the U.S. The information that provided on the Questionnaire for Public Trust Positions (SF-85P) and the Declaration for Federal Employment (OF 306) may be confirmed during the investigation. To avoid such delays, the applicant must request that the consumer reporting agencies lift the freeze in these instances.
