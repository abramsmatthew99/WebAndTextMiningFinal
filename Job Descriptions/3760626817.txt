<p><strong>Job Overview:</strong> We are seeking an experienced and highly skilled Infrastructure/Systems Engineer to join our dynamic IT team. The ideal candidate will have expertise in Windows Server patching, security agent management, backup solutions, email infrastructure, and Office 365/Google Workspace administration. As an Infrastructure/Systems Engineer, you will play a critical role in ensuring the stability, security, and efficiency of our organization's IT systems and infrastructure.</p><p><strong> </strong></p><p><strong>Key Responsibilities:</strong></p><p> </p><p><strong>Windows Server Patching and Security Agents:</strong></p><ul><li>Plan, schedule, and implement regular patching and updates for Windows Server environments.</li><li>Configure and manage security agents to protect systems and networks from threats.</li><li>Monitor system security and respond to security incidents and vulnerabilities.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Backup Management:</strong></p><ul><li>Design and maintain backup solutions using Veeam to ensure data protection and recovery.</li><li>Conduct regular backup testing and optimization to minimize downtime in case of data loss.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Email Architecture/Infrastructure:</strong></p><ul><li>Manage and optimize email architecture (exposure or knowledge in Proofpoint, Linux routing servers, and bulk mailing servers preferred)</li><li>Monitor email systems for performance, security, and reliability.</li><li>Troubleshoot and resolve email-related issues promptly.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Office 365 and Google Workspace Administration:</strong></p><ul><li>Administer and maintain Office 365 and Google Workspace accounts, including directory synchronization.</li><li>Collaborate with end-users to provide support and configure email, file sharing, and collaboration tools.</li><li>Implement and manage security policies and access controls.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Qualifications:</strong></p><p> </p><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field</li><li>Strong expertise in Windows Server administration, patching, and security.</li><li>Proficiency in managing backup solutions, with Veeam or other related tools.</li><li>Knowledge of email infrastructure</li><li>Experience in Office 365 (required) and Google Workspace (nice to have, not required) administration, including directory synchronization.</li><li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.</li><li>Excellent communication and teamwork skills.</li></ul><p></p>
