Job Overview: We are seeking an experienced and highly skilled Infrastructure/Systems Engineer to join our dynamic IT team. The ideal candidate will have expertise in Windows Server patching, security agent management, backup solutions, email infrastructure, and Office 365/Google Workspace administration. As an Infrastructure/Systems Engineer, you will play a critical role in ensuring the stability, security, and efficiency of our organization's IT systems and infrastructure. Key Responsibilities: Windows Server Patching and Security Agents:Plan, schedule, and implement regular patching and updates for Windows Server environments.Configure and manage security agents to protect systems and networks from threats.Monitor system security and respond to security incidents and vulnerabilities.Backup Management:Design and maintain backup solutions using Veeam to ensure data protection and recovery.Conduct regular backup testing and optimization to minimize downtime in case of data loss.Email Architecture/Infrastructure:Manage and optimize email architecture (exposure or knowledge in Proofpoint, Linux routing servers, and bulk mailing servers preferred)Monitor email systems for performance, security, and reliability.Troubleshoot and resolve email-related issues promptly.Office 365 and Google Workspace Administration:Administer and maintain Office 365 and Google Workspace accounts, including directory synchronization.Collaborate with end-users to provide support and configure email, file sharing, and collaboration tools.Implement and manage security policies and access controls.Qualifications: Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related fieldStrong expertise in Windows Server administration, patching, and security.Proficiency in managing backup solutions, with Veeam or other related tools.Knowledge of email infrastructureExperience in Office 365 (required) and Google Workspace (nice to have, not required) administration, including directory synchronization.Strong analytical and problem-solving skills.Excellent communication and teamwork skills.
