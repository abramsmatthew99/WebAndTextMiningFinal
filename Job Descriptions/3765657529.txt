The Lead may also be asked to work independently on projects and tasks while accomplishing these and other critical functions:</p><p> </p><p>Providing end user support for the financial departments of the firm for the 3E and Electronic Billing Hub (eBH) financial and billing systems, respectively</p><p>Acting as a senior resource to the firm on various financial technology questions for staff and other users; testing resolutions and making recommendations to accounting for corrections in Production</p><p>Writing and running SQL’s for 3E balancing schedules, reviewing and resolving balancing variances, including running SQL’s for tax related balancing schedules and troubleshooting variances.</p><p>Assisting the supervisor with developing junior team members</p><p>Working independently with the DBA group to schedule and monitor tasks for evening or weekend maintenance windows</p><p>Working with the Financial Development team to help analyze and assist with developing 3E enhancement requests</p><p>Acting as a liaison to the accounting group to communicate deadlines and deliverables</p><p><strong>Qualifications:</strong></p><p>Display the ability to quickly analyze and interpret information from a variety of sources</p><p>Apply critical and creative thinking in order to draw conclusions or develop solutions to complex problems</p><p>Have the ability to adapt to changing priorities and maintain a productive and committed work effort, meeting deadlines and managing a demanding workload</p><p>Possess the ability to work independently without supervision and be able to prioritize responsibilities</p><p>A Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Accounting, Finance or Business</p><p>A minimum of five (5) years of increasingly responsible relevant experience, during which the knowledge, skills and abilities applicable to the position were demonstrated</p><p>3E experience is required</p><p>Knowledge of accounting concepts, procedures, and advanced experience in SQL Server, including T-SQL programming.</p><p>Experience with administration of the 3E system</p><li></li>
