technology</li><li>Zoom and Microsoft Teams meetings</li><li>Meeting room video conferencing</li><li>VPN and Citrix remote access</li><li>VOIP telephones</li><li>Strong technical skills and knowledge of hardware, software, and network systems</li><li>A deep understanding of the Windows operating system is a plus</li><li>Application packaging experience is a plus</li><li>Strong understanding of help desk functions and technical support experience</li><li>Strong knowledge of desktop/laptop hardware and BIOS upgrades</li><li>Strong knowledge of Windows 10, Windows 11, iPhones, iOS, and general productivity applications</li><li>Networking knowledge (TCP/IP, DHCP, DNS, Wireless)</li><li>Must be able to lift desktops, laptops, monitors, printers, and other IT-related equipment</li><li>Knowledge and understanding of Microsoft Active Directory<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Personal Attributes:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Excellent people and communication skills and a passion for exceptional customer service</li><li>Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills</li><li>Flexibility in working in a fast-paced, high-expectations environment</li><li>Ability to work well under pressure and handle multiple tasks simultaneously</li><li>Working independently and as a member of a worldwide support team</li><li>Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills</li><li>Excellent listening, interpersonal, written, and oral communication skills</li><li>Pays extreme attention to details</li><li>Highly self-motivated and passionate about technology and people</li><li>Ability to prioritize and manage time effectively</li><li>Eager to learn new technologies and applications, and ability to absorb new topics quickly<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Education:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>A Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Management Information Systems, or a related field (and/or equivalent CompTIA A+, CompTIA Network+, Microsoft MTA, etc.)</li></ul>
