Your work will span the spectrum from quick exploratory/experimental models to scaled, production processes.</p><p>Your work may start from simple models, but the ultimate goal is to push the boundaries of what is scientifically and technically possible, and share those advances with the greater scientific community via publication and/or open source.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Basic qualifications:</strong></p><ul><li>Deep passion for AI and building machine learning systems</li><li>Interested in the full ML stack from initial R&amp;D through deploying models into production</li><li>Comfortable working with data in Python and SQL</li><li>Passionate about good engineering, clean code, and scalability</li><li>Able to communicate technical concepts clearly at all levels, whether that be engaging deeply on a topic with other Machine Learning Scientists or sharing key insights with business stakeholders</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Nice to haves:</strong></p><ul><li>Experience with active learning, reinforcement learning, causal inference and/or online learning approaches</li><li>Experience with serverless or highly distributed Machine Learning model training on massive datasets</li><li>Expertise in experimental design and Bayesian uncertainty estimation</li><li>Familiarity in deep learning frameworks (e.g. PyTorch/TensorFlow)</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><em><span class="ql-cursor"> </span>The base salary range for this position is $135,000 - $200,000 yr.
