<p>***Candidates for this role must be local to San Francisco Bay area to physically support installations and troubleshoot incidents at the local office communication rooms and data centers. This position will require candidate to be on an onsite/remote rotation with a typical rotation of 3 days on site 2 days remote unless otherwise required.</p><p><br/></p><p>Join our dynamic San Francisco team, where your expertise shapes the future of our network security. We value work-life balance and believe in the pivotal role our employees play in our success and growth.</p><p>As a Network Security Engineer, you will:</p><ul><li>Administer and support enterprise network infrastructure, focusing on high availability and meeting business objectives.</li><li>Implement, troubleshoot, and maintain network solutions, with a primary emphasis on Network Security Services, DMZ/Extranet Services, Remote Access/VPN Services, and User Internet Services.</li><li>Automate network deployment and data collection tasks for enhanced productivity.</li><li>Create and document Support Strategies, ensuring alignment with business expectations.</li><li>Maintain comprehensive documentation for all implemented networks and support data center design and implementation.</li><li>Select and implement security tools in collaboration with the security team.</li><li>Lead problem management events, conduct root cause analyses, and provide complex technical support to end-users.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>Qualifications:</p><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Information Technology, Computer Science, Engineering, or related field.</li><li>5 years of experience in a Cisco environment and supporting/managing hosted Data Centers.</li><li>Advanced knowledge and hands-on administration experience with Cisco Networking Equipment, Firewall Management Console, Netskope, Cisco ISE, Cisco Remote Access, and Azure Firewalls</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>Preferred Skills:</p><ul><li>CCNA or CCNP certification.</li><li>Experience with Forcepoint, Network Access Control solutions, Forescout, Cisco DNA, Cisco Nexus Dashboard, Python, Ansible or Terraform, and automating network deployments.</li><li>Familiarity with monitoring tools such as Dynatrace and Forward Networks administration.</li><li>Expertise in Cisco Wireless solutions, SD-WAN with Cloud Integration, InfoBlox administration, Configuration Drift Detection and Remediation, Security Posture Assurance, and Cloud platforms like Azure.</li><li>ITIL Certification and experience.</li><li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Perks of working with our Client:</strong></p><ul><li>Inclusive culture, providing a great work/life balance</li><li>Cigna Health benefits, Medical, dental &amp; vision coverage for employee</li><li>PTO</li><li>401K Match</li><li>Educational benefits for trainings, conferences, certifications, etc.</li></ul>
