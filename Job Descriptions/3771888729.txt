<strong>About<br/><br/></strong>Macy’s is proudly America’s Department Store. For more than 160 years, Macy’s has served generations at every stage of their lives. Customers come to us for fashion, value and celebration. Now is an exciting time to join Macy’s, Inc. The face of retail is changing, and change requires innovation.<br/><br/>Macy’s Tech provides modern tools, platforms, and services to all parts of the business. Our team supports millions of customers in connected commerce across the technology hub at Macy’s Join our team to help shape the future of e-commerce and set the pace in retail technology. Whether focused on store technology, supply chain tech, application security, merchandising systems, or the mobile app – you’ll have opportunities to grow your career while finding meaningful ways to make a difference.<br/><br/><strong>Job Overview<br/><br/></strong>We are seeking an experienced and dynamic Principal Technical Program Manager to lead and oversee complex technical projects and initiatives within our organization. The successful candidate will be responsible for driving cross-functional teams, managing project timelines, budgets, and resources, and ensuring successful delivery of high-impact technical solutions. The Principal Technical Program Manager will play a critical role in aligning business goals with technical execution, fostering innovation, and maintaining a strong focus on quality and efficiency.<br/><br/><strong>Essential Functions<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Lead Complex Technical Projects: Oversee the end-to-end lifecycle of complex technical projects, from initiation to delivery. Coordinate cross-functional teams including engineering, design, product management, and other relevant stakeholders to ensure successful project execution.</li><li>Strategic Planning: Collaborate with senior leadership to define project goals, objectives, and strategies that align with the organization's long-term vision. Translate high-level business objectives into actionable project plans.</li><li>Program and Project Management: Develop and maintain comprehensive project plans, including scope, schedule, budget, and resource allocation. Monitor and track progress against milestones and adjust plans as necessary to ensure on-time and within-budget delivery.</li><li>Risk Management: Identify potential project risks, assess their impact, and implement mitigation strategies to minimize disruptions and ensure project success.</li><li>Stakeholder Communication: Facilitate effective communication among project stakeholders, ensuring transparency, alignment, and regular updates on project status, risks, and milestones.</li><li>Technical Expertise: Provide deep technical insights and guidance to project teams, enabling informed decision-making and fostering innovation in solution design and implementation.</li><li>Continuous Improvement: Drive process improvements and best practices across the organization, promoting efficiency, scalability, and quality in project execution and delivery.</li><li>Team Leadership: Mentor and guide project team members, fostering a collaborative and high-performance culture. Encourage skill development and career growth within the team.</li><li>Budget Management: Manage project budgets, track expenses, and allocate resources efficiently to optimize project outcomes while adhering to financial constraints.</li><li>Post-Implementation Review: Conduct thorough post-project evaluations to identify successes, areas for improvement, and lessons learned. Share findings with relevant teams to enhance future project execution.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s or master’s degree in a relevant technical field (e.g., Computer Science, Engineering, Information Technology) or equivalent practical experience.</li><li>We encourage candidates with either a bachelor’s degree or equivalent work experience in a related field to apply.</li><li>Proven track record of successfully managing and delivering complex technical projects or programs, ideally within a fast-paced, technology-driven environment.</li><li>Strong technical background with a deep understanding of software development methodologies, systems architecture, and emerging technologies.</li><li>Excellent leadership, communication, and interpersonal skills, with the ability to influence and collaborate with cross-functional teams at all levels.</li><li>Exceptional problem-solving and critical-thinking abilities, with a keen attention to detail.</li><li>Project management certifications (e.g., PMP, PMP, PMI-ACP) are a plus.</li><li>Experience with Agile or other iterative development methodologies is highly desirable.</li><li>Previous experience in mentoring and developing junior program or project managers is advantageous.<br/><br/></li></ul>Join our team of dedicated professionals and contribute to our organization's success by driving the delivery of cutting-edge technical solutions. Apply your expertise and leadership to shape the future of our products and services.<br/><br/>TECH00<br/><br/>This position may be eligible for performance-based incentives/bonuses.
