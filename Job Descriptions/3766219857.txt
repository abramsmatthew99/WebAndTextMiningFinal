Architect and network security team for secure network design.<br/><br/>Provide information security expertise to system development teams.<br/><br/>Document and generate reports of detections and response actions for review.<br/><br/>Assist in the analysis of vulnerabilities.<br/><br/>Monitor security platforms for errors, misconfigurations, or performance alerts.<br/><br/>Utilize the SIEM platform for threat identification and investigations.<br/><br/>Support end-users and stakeholders’ requests related to information security service.<br/><br/>Perform control testing and other risk management activities.<br/><br/>Provide information in response to assessments and audits.<br/><br/>Maintain an understanding of deployed systems, solutions, and network technologies.<br/><br/>Work with network monitoring and management applications, creating reproducible configuration scripts and templates.<br/><br/><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong>Experience in Information Security and networks.<br/><br/>Familiarity with SIEM platforms and Cisco security products.<br/><br/>Awareness of Information Security best practices and regulatory requirements.<br/><br/>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, IS, or Information Security (CISSP, CISM, GIAC certifications are a plus).<br/><br/>Strong professional communication skills, both verbal and written.<br/><br/>Strong understanding of computer systems and networking principles.<br/><br/>Analytical skills and knowledge of data analysis tools.<br/><br/>Ability to parse logs, create queries, and perform root cause analysis.<br/><br/>Understanding of logical security, user access, and identity management.<br/><br/>Detail-oriented and process-focused.<br/><br/>Ability to prioritize work independently with minimal oversight.<br/><br/>Team player with a passion for problem-solving.<br/><br/><strong>Additional Information<br/><br/></strong>This 12-month contract opportunity is based in Nashville, TN, with a
