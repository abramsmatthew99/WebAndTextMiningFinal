<strong>Job Summary<br/><br/></strong>The IT Security Analyst is responsible for maintaining security operational readiness of James Avery systems, including compliance with James Avery security policies. Performs network and system vulnerability testing, configuration validation, security event analysis, vulnerability management and remediation. Works closely with IT and business teams to support incident response and vulnerability management efforts.<br/><br/><strong>What You Will Be Doing<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Perform daily security operations task management and security event analysis in coordination with other teams and event correlation using existing security systems to determine risk and exposure. Conduct vulnerability scans and configure scanning tools to assist with identifying vulnerabilities.</li><li>Perform basic administration of endpoint security and vulnerability management platforms. Working knowledge of endpoint security controls such as endpoint detection and response, data leakage prevention, vulnerability management.</li><li>Assist with the security incident response process including analysis and remediation of malware infections, reformatting/reimaging of systems.</li><li>Understand company security policies, procedures and guidelines and take security based actions to meet policy needs. Verify systems and configurations comply with James Avery information and cybersecurity policies.</li><li>Coordinate remediation efforts associated with third-party audits.</li><li>Support security education and training awareness efforts by creating content for email-based campaigns, presentations, and the company intranet. Identify process, documentation and training opportunities that enhance the customer experience or optimize support delivery.</li><li>Provide project support on company initiatives, events or incident investigations when required.</li><li>Identify potential areas of process improvement and automation to minimize reoccurrence of common issues. analyzing incidents and identifying root causes, developing resolution plans and executing respective tasks.</li><li>Assist with creation of technical documentation in internal Wiki and customer guides.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>What You Will Need<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in Information Technology, Cybersecurity, Computer Science, Engineering or related field; or equivalent combination of education/experience.</li><li>Knowledge of IT structures and services including Windows 10+ desktop operating systems, Windows Server 2010+ end-user related administration.</li><li>Knowledge of system network and cloud protocols such as Telnet, TCP, IP, UDP, SMTP, FTP, POP, HTTPS and HTTP, Containers, Kubernetes, etc.</li><li>Strong organizational and problem-solving skills.</li><li>Proven ability to coordinate multiple tasks simultaneously, manage frequent interruptions and work effectively under pressure.</li><li>Ability to work flexible hours and travel to various work locations when business need requires.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Preferred Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>2 years’ experience in Information Systems Security, Cybersecurity or relevant field.</li><li>Experience with vulnerability management and security scanning tools.</li></ul>
