We love going to work each day - <em>especially when it's hard</em>. We are constantly looking to identify opportunities for improvement in our people, our processes and our product.</p><p><br/></p><p>Previous CSM experience is definitely preferred, but not necessary.</p><p><br/></p><p>The CSM role at Kixie is a great opportunity to hone develop skills in relationship-building, teamwork, software, personal accountability, and customer success strategy.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Ideal Candidate:</strong></p><p><br/></p><p>The ideal candidate for this position demonstrates strong interpersonal and relationship management skills, good technical skills/instincts, and the ability to learn our tech/product relatively independently and quickly.
