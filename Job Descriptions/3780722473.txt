We have a unique culture where transparency, teamwork, and collaboration aren’t just words on a page – they are central to how we do business every day. If you join our team as a Premier Software Engineer, you will get to work with our own proprietary software, Cargas Energy. You’ll be working with existing customers who already use our product every day. Your main objective in this role will be to help troubleshoot and configure the software to suit the customer’s needs as they evolve over time. You’ll work through a ticketing system to prioritize bug fixes and make recommendations for SQL server updates. As you resolve each request or bug, it will be important to communicate with the customer to let them know the status of the issue.
