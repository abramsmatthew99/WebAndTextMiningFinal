<p><strong>Job description</strong></p><p><strong>POSITION SUMMARY:</strong></p><p>The End User Support Specialist’s responsibilities include providing on-site and remote support for all end-user issues. Their responsibilities include but are not limited to the installation, configuration, and maintenance of firm-approved applications, peripherals, and desktop technology. It is important to have a great attitude, strong problem-solving abilities, and excellent customer service skills. The End User Support Specialist must also be able to travel occasionally to other offices to provide on-site support if needed.</p><p><strong>KNOWLEDGE, SKILLS, AND ABILITIES REQUIRED:</strong></p><p>1. EDUCATION:</p><p>Associate’s degree, computer certification, or equivalent education in a computer science field.</p><p>2. WORK EXPERIENCE:</p><p>Must have at least two years of experience supporting users in a helpdesk or customer service technical role. Law firm experience preferred.</p><p>Excellent knowledge and ability to support software, not limited to, Microsoft Office Suite.</p><p>Excellent researching and troubleshooting skills.</p><p>Ability to interact professionally with all levels of internal and external contacts.</p><p>Customer Service oriented person with knowledge of PC hardware, Windows 10 operating system, and general hardware accessories and software installation and troubleshooting methods.</p><p></p>
