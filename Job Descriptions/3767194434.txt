</li><li>Manage and coordinate test data collection efforts. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Preferred Skills:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>5 or more years of test development experience in R&amp;D and failure analysis report and other relevant test development documents. </li><li>Develop manufacturing test requirements: work with hardware and software design engineers to develop test cases, identify test hardware, software tools and acceptance criteria. </li><li>Project management skills: ability to manage all assigned tasks in a project time-line manner from start to finish. create and work with small teams to complete assigned tasks in a timely manner. </li><li>Design test fixtures for production test; robust, state-of-art interconnect methods, data acquisition switching, analog and digital signal conditioning modules, etc. </li><li>Test system design for functional testing using GPIB, RS-232 and USB interfaces is essential. </li><li>Proven knowledge and extensive experience with analog and digital electronics. </li><li>A/D and D/A circuits. Analog – power management, exposure to Li-on battery interfaces. digital – microcontroller, FPGA, RAM, EEPROM. addressing and control I2C bus. familiarity and experience using cadence/mentor design software tools. </li><li>Knowledge of JTAG interfaces and experience with development of boundary scan tests is highly desirable. Familiarity with asset or JTAG technologies tools is a plus. </li><li>Work with quality and reliability teams to improve/enhance test methods, data collection, knowledgebase and feedback to customer service to close the loop. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Basic Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Electrical Engineering or Computer Science. </li><li>5 + years of test development experience. </li><li>Legal authorization to work in the U.S. indefinitely is required.
