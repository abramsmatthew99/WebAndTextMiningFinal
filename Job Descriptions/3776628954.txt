<p><strong>Job Requisition:</strong> IT Systems Administrator </p><p><strong>Location</strong>: ForeThought Inc., Ocean Springs, Mississippi</p><p><strong>Company Overview: </strong></p><p>ForeThought Inc. is committed to embracing cutting-edge technologies to enhance our operations and deliver innovative solutions to our clients. For over 10 years, we have provided IT services and solutions to the Department of Defense which include: the development and management of Product Lifecycle Management and SharePoint Collaboration applications, Wide Area Networks, Help Desk support, and Cloud Infrastructure Administration. </p><p><strong>Job Summary: </strong></p><p>We are seeking a full-time highly skilled and experienced IT Systems Administrator to join our dynamic IT team. The ideal candidate will have a proven track record of successfully managing and maintaining AWS Cloud Based and On-Prem IT systems, ensuring their reliability, security, and optimal performance. The chosen candidate will play a crucial role in supporting our company's operations by implementing and managing robust IT infrastructure. The position is full-time, includes a benefits package and affords telework when appropriate. </p><p><strong>Key Responsibilities:</strong></p><p><strong>1.      <em>System Administration: </em></strong></p><p>·        Administer, monitor, and maintain servers, networks, and other IT systems.</p><p>·        Ensure the availability, performance, and security of systems through proactive monitoring and troubleshooting.</p><p>·        Install and integrate applications within an AWS Cloud Based and On-Prem environment, including Windows Server, Microsoft SQL Server, Microsoft SharePoint Server and other commercial (COTS) products.</p><p>·        Create and maintain interfaces to external systems.</p><p><strong>2.      Infrastructure Management:</strong></p><p>·        Design, implement, and manage IT infrastructure to support the organization's growth and technological advancements.</p><p>·        Collaborate with other IT professionals to plan and execute system upgrades and enhancements.</p><p><strong>3.      <em>Security and Compliance:</em></strong></p><p>·        Implement and enforce security measures to safeguard systems and data.</p><p>·        Stay abreast of industry best practices and ensure compliance with relevant regulations.</p><p><strong>4.      <em>User Support:</em></strong></p><p>·        Provide technical support, addressing hardware and software issues promptly.</p><p>·        Collaborate with the helpdesk team to resolve escalated support tickets.</p><p><strong>5.      <em>Backup and Recovery:</em></strong></p><p>·        Develop and maintain backup and disaster recovery plans to minimize downtime and data loss in both an AWS Cloud Hosted and On-Prem system.</p><p><strong>6.      <em>Documentation:</em></strong></p><p>·        Create and maintain comprehensive documentation for system configurations, processes, and procedures.</p><p><strong> </strong></p><p><strong>Qualifications:</strong></p><p>1.      Bachelor's degree or equivalent experience in Computer Science, Information Technology, or a related field.</p><p>2.      10 years’ experience managing and implementing Windows Server, Windows SharePoint Server and Linux environments is <strong>required</strong>.</p><p>3.      10 years’ experience scripting and automation using tools such as PowerShell, Python, or Terraform is <strong>required</strong>.</p><p>4.      5 years’ experience with management and configuration of networking devices including routers, firewalls, switches, etc. is <strong>required</strong>. </p><p>5.      Knowledge of the National institute of Standards and Technology (NIST) Risk Management Framework (RMF) cybersecurity process is <strong>required</strong>. </p><p>6.      Experience in designing and implementing cloud solutions, with expertise in one or more cloud platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure, Google Cloud) is preferred.</p><p>7.      Experience with containerization technologies and AWS GovCloud is a plus.</p><p>8.      Relevant certifications (e.g., Windows Server, Linux Server, CompTIA Security +, VMware Certified Professional, AWS Certified Solutions Architect, Azure Solutions Architect, etc.) are <strong>required</strong>.</p><p>9.      Excellent communication and interpersonal skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively with technical and non-technical stakeholders.</p><p>10.  The ability to achieve and maintain a Secret Level Clearance is <strong>required</strong>.</p><p><strong><em> </em></strong></p><p><strong><em>Candidates who do not meet the “required” qualifications above need not apply.</em></strong></p><p><strong> </strong></p><p><strong> </strong></p><p><strong>How to Apply</strong>: Qualified candidates are invited to submit their resume and cover letter to <strong>careers@forethought.pro</strong>. The deadline for applications is December 31, 2023.</p><p>ForeThought Inc. is an equal opportunity employer. We encourage applications from candidates of all backgrounds and experiences.</p><li><br/></li>
