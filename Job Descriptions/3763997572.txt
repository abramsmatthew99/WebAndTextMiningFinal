<strong>Description<br/><br/></strong>Aragen Bioscience is a leading contract research organization focused on accelerating product development. Aragen’s offerings include antibody discovery, recombinant protein production and purification, stable cell line development as well as cell-based assays and in vivo models of disease. We offer clients high quality service and superior R&amp;D partnerships.<br/><br/>The Part Time System Administrator/Support Engineer will be responsible for the following:<br/><br/><ul><li> Provides technical support to users</li><li> Troubleshoots hardware and software issues</li><li> Provides instructions or directly sets up desktop hardware</li><li> Assists with installation of computer software and operating system updates</li><li> Ensuring all Network and Systems related issues and tasks are resolved in a timely manner.</li><li> Working extensively on corporate infrastructure (both on-prem and in the cloud) of networks and systems, including firewalls, switches, WAPS, Microsoft 365, Exchange, Azure, Intune and more.</li><li> Manage Active Directory, DNS, DHCP, Group Policies Objects.</li><li> Monitor and troubleshoot infrastructure components to identify and resolve performance issues, security vulnerabilities, and other system-related problems.</li><li> Troubleshoot, maintain integrity, and configure network components along with implementing operating systems enhancements to improve reliability and performance.</li><li> Implementing and managing VMware virtualization in the most current versions in a high availability enterprise environment.</li><li> Perform backup operations or replications ensuring all required systems and data are successfully backed up including off site backups, as necessary.</li><li> Working with monitoring and performance tuning tools, such as Nagios, Zabbix, and MRTG.</li><li> Implement and test software patches and security fixes.</li><li> Coordinate, set up, and support technologies used in meetings including presentations, audio, and video conferences.</li><li> Work closely with 3rd party vendors providing various technology services.<br/><br/></li></ul>Other tasks may be assigned<br/><br/><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong>Qualifications<br/><br/><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or similar</li><li>Prior experience of 2-5 years in desktop support or related field</li><li>In-depth knowledge of desktop hardware and software systems</li><li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills<br/><br/></li></ul>Aragen is an equal opportunity employer. We offer a stimulating work environment with excellent salary. No Sponsorship available.<br/><br/>Salary Description<br/><br/>$38.00 to $45.00 per hour
