All persons hired must be a current U.S. citizen or current lawful permanent resident of the U.S.<br/><br/>POSITION<br/><br/><strong>Satellite Flight Software Engineer<br/><br/></strong>Xplore is hiring a Satellite Flight Software Engineer to develop software for the various flight software segments across the Xplore spacecraft software architecture. This individual will play an integral role in software development, integration, and test and must have experience developing flight software for an operational spacecraft. The successful candidate must be able to demonstrate an ability to work with minimal supervision while being responsive and flexible within the framework of an agile space start-up.<br/><br/>Work for this role will be performed at Xplore in Redmond, Washington.<br/><br/>The Satellite Flight Software Engineer will report to the Spaceflight Software Director and work with the Systems Engineering, Mission Operations, and satellite subsystem Leads for the development of the flight software segments needed to control the satellite and manage its payloads.<br/><br/>As an Xplore Satellite Flight Software Engineer, you will help to shape the future of space exploration, working with an exceptional team that is passionate about creating revolutionary technology.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Develop, test, integrate, and maintain software and firmware that performs spacecraft executive-level and management functions</li><li>Develop requirements in coordination with systems engineering, hardware engineering, software engineering and subsystem owners</li><li>Assume ownership of these software products, including verification testing and writing documentation</li><li>Perform development activities throughout the software development lifecycle</li><li>Support integrated firmware and software testing and verification</li><li>Author tests, documentation, and participate in design reviews</li><li>Support software readiness, planning, preparation, execution, and evaluation for all phases of spacecraft mission; from launch to mission to de-orbit<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong>REQUIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE<br/><br/><ul><li>All persons hired must be a current U.S. citizen or current lawful permanent resident of the U.S</li><li>Bachelor’s degree (B.S.) from an accredited institution in Software Engineering or Computer Science and 5+ years of relevant experience, OR equivalent combination of education and experience</li><li>Demonstrated experience developing operational software for a space mission</li><li>Demonstrated experience connecting satellite subsystems and payloads with onboard computers</li><li>Demonstrated strong programming skills in multiple languages including C/C++</li><li>Demonstrated development experience with Unix operating systems and ARM processors</li><li>Demonstrated experience and knowledge related to software discipline process development and deployment</li><li>Demonstrated ability to communicate technical information, challenges, and guidance to varied audiences</li><li>Demonstrated ability to work effectively in a dynamic environment with changing needs and requirements</li><li>Ability to work on own initiative and prioritize tasking to align with business goal<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Desired SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Five years of experience developing avionics software</li><li>Working knowledge of DO-332, DO-178C and DO-278A qualification</li><li>Working knowledge of real-time operating systems</li><li>Experience with instrumentation interfaces including RS-485, I2C, SPI, or LVDS<br/><br/></li></ul>The top 4-6 measurable priorities to be accomplished in the first 6 months<br/><br/><ul><li> Develop software and firmware and
