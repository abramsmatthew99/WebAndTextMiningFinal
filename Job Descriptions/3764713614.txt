        The Regional Information Technology Director position is a hands-on leadership role that reports directly to the Group ICT head with dotted-line reporting to the Regional Leadership is responsible for deploying Group IT strategy in region, Roll Rollout (D365 F&amp;O), managing IT applications and infrastructure technologies and a group of highly technical IT personnel supporting the Ammega Group and its business unit IT systems, projects, and IT operations in the Americas. The Regional Director of IT develops and establishes IT department technology/application roadmaps with alignment with group IT’s standards, and procedures, and assigns work to the operations staff. The position also has responsibilities for the analysis, planning, design, development, deployment, coordination, communication and operations of IT software/hardware systems and IT services supporting the business. This individual will work closely with his/her peers in the business and team members within IT to ensure overall betterment of the Americas IT and compliance with the IT policies, procedures, and Group standards. The Head of Information Technology will lead an entire IT department for the Ammega Group in the Americas to provide 24x7 IT support services to businesses located within the region. The ideal candidate must have both leadership, Global ERP rollout experience and IT technical know-how to effectively and efficiently manage Information Technology services and personnel that promote alignment with business strategies and support the Company’s customer satisfaction, quality and profit objectives. This position will be required to work in a globally diverse workforce as a team player promoting one single global vision for the Ammega Group IT department.<br/><br/>Requirement<br/><br/><ul><li>Global ERP Rollout experience.</li><li>Bachelor's or master's degree in computer science/information technology or equivalent.</li><li>Minimum 15 years of Information technology experience with at least 8 years in a senior IT leadership role leading the IT department for a business.</li><li>Proven track record of hiring and leading highly technical senior-level IT professionals to support a global multinational firm.</li><li>Sound knowledge of all aspects of IT services and operations.</li><li>Experience managing or leading major IT projects for a multinational firm is preferred.</li><li>Proven management skills with the ability to mentor staff members.</li><li>Good people skills and the ability to interact and communicate effectively across all levels of the organization.</li><li>Ability to travel globally approximately 25%.</li><li>Must be able to work in the US.</li></ul>
