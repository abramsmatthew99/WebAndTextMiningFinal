<p>The Department of Energy (DOE) Office of the Chief Information Officer (OCIO) is looking for an experienced IT Support Analyst who will be responsible for managing a broad spectrum of duties related to the identities and credentials of people and devices. The candidate will operate as part of a larger team supporting enterprise IT services for a nation-wide customer base. Familiarity with Public Key Infrastructure, smart cards, and One-Time Password token technology is desired.</p><p><strong>Job Responsibilities:</strong></p><p>• Level 2 (Intermediate Level) IT Support for applications and infrastructure systems.</p><p>• Diagnose and resolve problems independently.</p><p>• Data quality control. Error diagnosis and resolution. Event auditing.</p><p>• Able to collaborate with cross-functional teams and peers outside of the organization.</p><p>• Administrative work managing sensitive information about identity and credentials. </p><p> </p><p><strong>Minimum Qualifications</strong></p><p><br/></p><p>Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Science or a related field or equivalent experience</p><p>3+ years of relevant work experience.</p><p>Ability to obtain a “Q” Level Clearance, so active TS/SCI strongly preferred</p><p><strong>Other Job Specific Skills </strong></p><p><br/></p><p>• Ability to communicate effectively and exceptional customer service skills. </p><p>• Ideal candidate is someone that has worked on a helpdesk for a few years and is ready for the next level. </p><p>• Excellent attention to detail.</p><p>• Must be able to balance multiple tasks simultaneously.</p><p>• Independent worker who can diagnose and resolve problems on their own and possess the capacity to operate as part of a larger team. </p><p>• Strong verbal and written communication skills that work effectively with both technical and non-technical audiences. </p><p><br/></p><p><br/></p><p>#Cjpost</p>
