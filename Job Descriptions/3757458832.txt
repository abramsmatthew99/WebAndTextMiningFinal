Additionally, this position will be responsible for identifying raw client data, importing and modeling it, and analyzing and presenting it. strong work ethic and attention to detail.</li><li>The ability to work with all levels of management and anticipate support requirements are critical skills needed to be successful in this role.</li><li>The ability to exercise independent judgment with minimal supervision and maintain a high degree of confidentiality with regard to client matters.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Preferred Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in related field (statistics, mathematics, computer science, or similar) or relevant work experience preferred.</li><li>Preferred candidates will have demonstrated skills in all areas and be willing to take on additional responsibilities as needed.</li><li>Experience within the finance and investment management industries a plus.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>About Us<br/><br/></strong>Cassaday &amp;
