Whether that’s evolving our streaming and digital products in new and immersive ways, powering worldwide advertising and distribution to maximize flexibility and efficiency, or delivering Disney’s unmatched entertainment and sports content, every day is a moment to make a difference to partners and to hundreds of millions of people around the world.<br/><br/><br/><ul><li>Building the future of Disney’s media business: DE&amp;E Technologists are designing and building the infrastructure that will power Disney’s media, advertising, and distribution businesses for years to come.</li><li>Reach &amp; Scale: The products and platforms this group builds and operates delight millions of consumers every minute of every day – from Disney+ and Hulu, to ABC News and Entertainment, to ESPN and ESPN+, and much more.</li><li>Innovation: We develop and execute groundbreaking products and techniques that shape industry norms and enhance how audiences experience sports, entertainment &amp; news.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul>Platform Software Engineering oversees the core technical platforms and systems for Disney’s massive suite of streaming products – including Disney+ and Hulu – and digital products and experiences – including ESPN, Marvel, Disney Studios, NatGeo, and ABC News. When you’re watching SportsCenter and you’re impressed by how awesome the studio looks, you have this team to thank!<br/><br/>The Lifecycle Engineering Organization creates scalable foundations and platforms to power messaging and in-app experiences across the consumer lifecycle, spanning our various product offerings - Disney+, Hulu, ESPN+, STAR+ and Bundles.<br/><br/>We are hiring a Senior Software Engineer to join the team that is responsible for the Unified Messaging Portal which will be the central hub for managing all messages and campaigns delivered to hundreds of millions of customers across Disney+, Hulu, ESPN+, STAR+ and Bundles. In this role, you will collaborate cross-functionally with teams including Engineering, Product Management, Marketing, Data, Design, Program Management, and more to help shape the vision for the Unified Messaging Portal and convert that vision into code. If you love to solve problems in unique ways, are passionate about building the best product for consumers, thrive on problem solving in the face of ambiguity, and drive towards outcomes, then this is a great role for you!<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities:<br/><br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Build high-performance scalable code for deployment both internally and externally.</li><li>Work with a strong team culture of transparency and collaboration, contributing meaningful feedback to the rest of the development team, and being open to receiving feedback as well.</li><li>Participate in the on-call rotation, supporting systems and services during production incidents.</li><li>Create strong relationships with engineering and cross-functional partners.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Basic
