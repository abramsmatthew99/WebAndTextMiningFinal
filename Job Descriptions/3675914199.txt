More information about the Romain College of Business and the Computer Science Program can be found on the Web at http://business.usi.edu/ and https://www.usi.edu/business/computer-science.<br/><br/><strong>Application Process<br/><br/></strong>Click “Apply Now!” near the top right of this page to complete an application and upload application materials to the attention of the Search Committee Chair.
