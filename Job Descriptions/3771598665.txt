SSH.</li><li>Hands-on experience in Windows Server and Virtualization using VMWare.</li><li>Familiarity with SAN, NAS, and DAS storage implementations.</li><li>Understanding of IP networks, network components, and troubleshooting.</li><li>Knowledge of system, application, and network security.</li><li>Experience working in a co-location Data Center environment.</li><li>Commitment to delivering
