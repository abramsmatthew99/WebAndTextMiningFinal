<strong>Title: Test Engineer <br/><br/></strong><strong> About Us </strong><strong> : <br/><br/></strong>AMETEK is using innovation to make the world a better place, technology to improve lives, and talent to solve challenges that matter. We need people like you to help make it happen!<br/><br/><strong> Job Summary: <br/><br/></strong>As a Test Engineer, you have the opportunity to work closely with talented individuals from various departments, including Engineering, QA, and Manufacturing. Your role is crucial in every step of the product life cycle, where you will design and execute testing strategies in collaboration with Engineering. By providing support to factory test technicians, you will troubleshoot and resolve non-conformities. Your expertise in handling and utilizing test instruments will enable you to create improvement plans for the management team. Your contributions as a Test Engineer will be invaluable to the success of the company and the satisfaction of our customers.<br/><br/><strong> Key Responsibilities: <br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Develop, deploy, execute, and maintain test processes, plans, instructions, fixtures, calibration logs, and documentation for acceptance and production testing of components and systems in a factory environment </li><li> Identify, troubleshoot, and coordinate the resolution of factory test failures.
