        Skydio is the leading US drone company and the world leader in autonomous flight, the key technology for the future of drones and aerial transportation. The Skydio team combines deep expertise in artificial intelligence, best-in-class hardware and software product development, and operational excellence to empower a broader, more diverse audience of drone users - from first responders to insurance claims adjusters, utilities, and more!<br/><br/><strong>About The Role<br/><br/></strong>By combining best-in-class autonomy and cloud-connected real-time visibility, drones will become critical and ubiquitous infrastructure that can be deployed and monitored in real-time to solve a variety of problems faster, cheaper, and safer than ever before. As a full-stack intern, you will work on important and exciting projects related to building out cloud features and infrastructure required to turn this vision into a reality.<br/><br/>An ideal intern candidate is a junior, senior, or MS-level college student with practical project and/or internship experience developing complex web applications or backend data systems.<br/><br/><strong>How You’ll Make An Impact<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Leverage your past experiences, best practices, and open-ended problem solving skills to help make the world more productive, creative, and safe with autonomous drones. <br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Project Examples<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Build out customer-facing product features to enhance existing capabilities and/or enable new use cases on the Skydio Platform</li><li>Develop web front-ends, APIs, and drone and server-side applications for fleet management, photo and video asset management, 3D reconstruction, and more</li><li>Develop secure, efficient, observable, scalable, and robust software for dealing with real-time data and video from fleets of Skydio drones<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>What Makes You a Good Fit<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Solid software engineering foundation and a commitment to writing clean, well-architected code</li><li>You are a junior, senior, or MS-level student with meaningful project and/or internship experience developing complex web applications or backend data systems.</li><li>Bonus points for experience shipping products or features within a collaborative engineering team environment.</li><li>Proficiency with Python and/or Javascript/Typescript</li><li>Familiarity with REST-ful APIs, Linux operating system configuration, build tools, relational databases (Postgres, MySQL) and caching systems (Redis, Memcached)</li><li>Understanding of cloud service offerings (AWS, GCP), deployment techniques, and configuration management</li><li>Bonus points for familiarity with robotics software development concepts or real-time networking<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Compensation Range:</strong> The hourly rate for this position is $41/hr for Undergrad, $53/hr for Graduate students, and $58 for PhD students*.
