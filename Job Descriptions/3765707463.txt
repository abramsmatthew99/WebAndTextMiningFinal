<p><strong>Overview</strong></p><p>Welcome to the nexus of connectivity! As a Network Systems Administrator, you're not just handling data and voice; you're the linchpin of EMF's network ecosystem. Reporting to the Senior Manager of Infrastructure Support, your responsibilities span hardware planning, configuration, integration, and routing maintenance, ensuring our Data, Voice, Media, and Wireless services operate seamlessly. It's not just about networks; it's about fortifying the digital infrastructure that powers our mission to move people closer to Christ!</p><p>Here's some of what you will be doing:</p><ul><li>Plan, design, implement, and support Local Area Networks, including development of configurations.</li><li>Implement and support Voice over IP (VoIP) Networks, including maintenance of configurations.</li><li>Plan, design, implement and support Wide Area Networks, including development of configurations.</li><li>Implement routing protocols, including IP, EIGRP, OSPF,GLBP, and BGP.</li><li>Packet level troubleshooting, analysis and problem solving for Data, Voice, Video and Wireless network issues.</li><li>Configure and assist with implementation of network hardware, including routers, switches, VoIP equipment, wireless, security, appliances, etc.</li><li>Create and maintain network documentation as it relates to network topology, equipment, and configuration.</li><li>Interact and negotiate with vendors, consultant, and contractors to design and implement network solutions.</li><li>Work with vendor performance as it relates to EMF’s standards. </li></ul><p>Here's some experience and background we are seeking:</p><ul><li>Experience with network performance and optimization tools a must. SolarWinds desired.</li><li>Experience with Cisco, HPE Aruba, Palo Alto Networks desired.</li><li>Practical experience with network troubleshooting tools.</li><li>Excellent customer service focus and analytical problem-solving abilities.</li><li>Ability to document all aspects of EMF’s-IT network and services provided to users.</li><li>Strong interpersonal, written, and oral communication and presentation skills are also required.</li><li>Highly self-motivated and directed, with keen attention to detail.</li><li>Demonstrated ability to work independently, establishing and meeting performance/ministry goals.</li><li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science, information systems or equivalent. </li></ul><p><strong>Why work for Educational Media Foundation, K-LOVE/Air1?</strong></p><p>Educational Media Foundation (EMF) is a nonprofit, multi-platform media company on a mission to draw people closer to Christ. Founded in 1982 in Santa Rosa, CA, with a singular radio station, EMF today owns and operates the nation's two largest Christian music radio networks (K-LOVE and Air1) with over 1,000 broadcast signals across all 50 states, streaming audio reaching around the world, and a growing family of media ministries including podcasts, books, films, concerts, and events. EMF employs nearly 500 team members between its offices in Nashville, TN, Rocklin, CA, and field locations around the country. You can view our mission and values here Mission, Beliefs &amp; Values.</p><p><strong> What can we offer you? </strong></p><ul><li>Industry leading Medical, Dental &amp; Vision coverage</li><li>Short/long term disability and life insurance</li><li>Robust 401K with company match</li><li>Parental leave with Baby Bonding pay</li><li>Generous PTO, holiday and sick pay</li><li>Unique company culture that includes exclusive access to concerts, movie premieres, media industry events, and more</li><li>Leadership and Career Development Programs including free access to LinkedIn Learning platform</li></ul>
