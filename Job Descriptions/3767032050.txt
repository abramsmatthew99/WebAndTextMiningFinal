<p><strong>Overview</strong></p><p><br/></p><p>1-800-FLOWERS.COM, Inc. is seeking a Data Scientist, who will be responsible for analyzing our vast amounts of customer data. This role is for professionals who can extract data, build and interpret machine learning models, make recommendations, and support decision-making. You will be part of a cross-functional team and collaborate with various stakeholders to design, develop and execute data-driven solutions that will directly influence our personalized customer contact strategy and experiences.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Key Responsibilities:</strong></p><p><br/></p><ol><li>Apply statistical and machine learning methods to specific business problems and data.</li><li>Implement and validate predictive models; create and maintain statistical models with a focus on big data.</li><li>Use customer data to provide insight into customer behavior, preferences, and trends to enhance the overall customer experience.</li><li>Identify new areas where customer data can be leveraged to drive business value.</li><li>Collaborate with cross-functional teams to design experiments, test hypotheses, and build models.</li><li>Use visualization tools to represent data and insights.</li><li>Generate reports, annotated code, and other projects artifacts to document, archive, and communicate your work and outcomes.</li><li>Drive data-driven decision making across the organization.</li></ol><p><br/></p><p><strong>Requirements:</strong></p><p><br/></p><ol><li>A Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, or a related discipline. Master's degree or PhD is preferred.</li><li>Minimum of 3 years of experience in a similar role, preferably in the retail sector.</li><li>Strong knowledge of statistical modeling, machine learning, and AI methodologies.</li><li>Experience in data mining and using databases in a business environment with large-scale, complex datasets.</li><li>Proficiency in SAS and SQL; Python and/or R is a plus.</li><li>Experience with data visualization tools like Tableau, Power BI, or similar.</li><li>Exceptional analytical, quantitative, problem-solving, and critical thinking skills.</li><li>Excellent communication skills with the ability to present complex information to non-technical stakeholders.</li></ol><p><br/></p><p><strong>Preferred Skills</strong></p><p><br/></p><ol><li>Familiarity with cloud platforms such as Snowflake (preferred), AWS, GCP or Azure.</li><li>Experience with big data platforms like Hadoop or Spark is a plus.</li><li>Understanding of retail business metrics and key performance indicators is highly desirable.</li></ol><p><br/></p><p>The expected salary for this position is $100,000-$125,000. The actual compensation will be determined by experience and other factors permitted by the law.</p><p></p>
