        Lazarus Naturals is on a mission to make quality hemp-based CBD accessible to everybody. It’s why we own our manufacturing process from start to finish—beginning at our hemp farms in Central Oregon to our extraction, formulation, and packaging—all to deliver safe, effective CBD at an affordable price. We provide a 60% Assistance Program to veterans and individuals with disabilities or low-income because we believe everybody should be able to experience the benefits of CBD regardless.<br/><br/>We have a close-knit, fast-paced work environment with a fully stocked kitchen, competitive pay and benefits. We are searching for an Information Technology Manager to join our team. This position is located in Portland, Oregon.<br/><br/>Beyond technical qualifications and sufficient experience requirements, we at Lazarus Naturals look for people who can thrive in our work environment and actively challenge themselves. We prioritize applicants who can illustrate their capacity to think critically, approach mistakes positively and constructively, as well as the ability to claim responsibility for their actions, whether good or bad. It is equally important that any applicant is able to exhibit an appreciation for proactive honesty and marked flexibility in the face of abrupt change. Moreover, we prefer applicants who share our passion and pride for the work and the impact we have.<br/><br/>In the Technology organization, we love what we do, and we're searching for a like-minded individual to manage the company's business systems. This is an exciting opportunity to be part of a leading company in a growing industry. As an Information Systems Manager, you will be take lead on the administration of corporate business systems and technology infrastructure.<br/><br/>This position reports directly to the Senior Director of Technology and, at the onset, will be directly responsible for the following:<br/><br/><ul><li>Develop and implement management information systems policies and procedures to ensure data accuracy and security</li><li>Develop, document, and maintain a broad knowledge of the company’s business and technology requirements and needs</li><li>Develop, document, and maintain an information system plan</li><li>Gather departmental system requirements and ensure they are met, including organizational compliance requirements</li><li>Perform periodic maintenance and servicing of IS systems to improve operational efficiency</li><li>Troubleshoot problems and failures in information systems</li><li>Monitor information system performance regularly to ensure availability and avoid unplanned outages and downtime</li><li>Identify problematic technology-related areas and recommend the implementation of cost-effective solutions</li><li>Plan, schedule, train, and supervise employees in best practices, procedures, and techniques related to the proper utilization of software systems. </li><li>Serve as the lead adviser and trainer on the use of productivity software (Google Workspace, Microsoft Office, etc.)</li><li>Provide assistance to employees with issues related to the proper utilization of software systems</li><li>Develop change management strategies to lessen the negative impacts of new system implementations</li><li>Conduct regular internal audits of business systems for areas of improvement</li><li>Assist with budget planning to ensure cost effectiveness</li><li>Execute budget activities in accordance with authorized budget plan</li><li>Collaborate on system implementations and integrations</li><li>Take direction and meeting deadlines in an efficient and timely manner</li><li>Communicate effectively with all team members<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science, Management Information Systems, or similar field (preferred, but not required)</li><li>3+ years working as an Information Systems Manager or related role</li><li>Experience monitoring the performance and usage of information systems to determine cost and productivity levels</li><li>Experience with cybersecurity compliance, controls, and incident response management</li><li>Experience analyzing business requirements, through stakeholder partnerships, to identify and/or develop technology solutions</li><li>Experience with vendor/supplier relationship management</li><li>Excellent knowledge of technical management, information analysis and of computer hardware/software systems</li><li>Demonstrable ability to successfully troubleshoot break/fix scenarios regardless of previous experience</li><li>Ability to coach and manage personnel<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Benefits<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Comprehensive benefits (medical, vision, and dental insurance)</li><li>Employee discount program</li><li>Fully stocked kitchen</li><li>401K plan</li><li>FSA &amp; commuter benefits</li><li>Employee development opportunities</li><li>Company events<br/><br/></li></ul>This is a full-time role based in Portland, OR. Please note, we will not consider applicants outside the immediate area. Interested parties are encouraged to include a cover letter detailing why you think you could be a great fit, along with an updated resume. Thanks, and we look forward to hearing from you.<br/><br/>Veterans strongly encouraged to apply.<br/><br/><em>Lazarus Naturals is proud to be an equal opportunity employer and is committed to creating a diverse environment.
