<strong>Summary<br/><br/></strong>Responsible for the day-to-day performance and operation of both the PC and LAN hardware and software; providing technical support for the installation, trouble shooting, monitoring performance, maintenance and administration of personal computers and desktop application software, premises printers, computer related peripheral devices, and company approved and supported mobile devices. This position supports the division.<br/><br/><strong>Essential Functions<br/><br/></strong>This job description lists the major duties and requirements of the position and is not all-inclusive. Applicable essential functions may vary by site. Incumbent(s) may be expected to perform job-related duties other than those contained in this document and may be required to have specific job-related knowledge and skills.<br/><br/><ul><li> Provides intermediate administrative support to end users via the Service Desk. </li><li> Manages user accounts, access, and passwords; creates user groups, accounts, and computers in directory services and email for users/sites. </li><li> Maintains and tracks inventories of hardware for desktops, laptops, thin clients, and peripherals in the appropriate systems. </li><li> Executes intermediate projects related to the department or office to which assigned. </li><li> Participates in sourcing of IT equipment needs. </li><li> Assists users in determining their hardware and software needs. </li><li> Installs, tests, documents, and maintains personal computer systems and company supported cell phones. </li><li> Installs intermediate instrument software on workstations. </li><li> Assists end users in the intermediate operation of hardware and software. </li><li> Monitors scheduled backups of premises servers and workstations data, adhering to adopted back-up and retention policies; escalates outages to management; assists on determining resolution. </li><li> Troubleshoots simple data and internet service outages (e.g., Wi-Fi); escalates outage to management as needed; communicates outage as directed to impacted users/sites. </li><li> Provides on-call support for site/division as assigned. </li><li> Contributes to the efficiency and effectiveness of the department's service to its customers by offering suggestions and directing or participating as an active member of a work team. </li><li> Promotes and supports the overall mission of PaceÂ® by demonstrating courteous and cooperative behavior when interacting with customers and staff; acts in a manner that promotes a harmonious and effective workplace environment. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong>To perform this job successfully, an individual must be able to perform each essential duty satisfactorily. The requirements listed below are representative of the knowledge, skill, and/or ability required.<br/><br/><strong>Education And Experience<br/><br/></strong>Associate degree in computer science, information systems, or a related field; AND zero to two (0-2) years of experience installing, maintaining and troubleshooting personal computer/network/server hardware and software; OR an equivalent combination of education, training, and experience.<br/><br/><strong>Required Certificates, Licenses, And Registrations<br/><br/></strong>None.<br/><br/><strong>Required Knowledge And Skills<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Intermediate familiarity with Microsoft operating systems and software, Windows operating systems, networking switches and data networks, IP telecommunications systems and audio/visual systems and equipment.</li><li>Intermediate knowledge of personal computer system hardware and business application software operating capabilities and limitations.</li><li>Intermediate use of specified computer applications involving word processing, office automation and/or standard report generation.</li><li>Intermediate techniques regarding the installation and maintenance of personal computer and network hardware and software.</li><li>Intermediate techniques regarding local area network hardware and software capabilities and installation.</li><li>Correct business English, including spelling, grammar, and punctuation.</li><li>Intermediate familiarity with TCP/IP, best practices in cabling/cable management, Cisco switches, routers, and network cards.</li><li>Troubleshooting intermediate network hardware and software.</li><li>Installing, maintaining, and troubleshooting intermediate personal computer hardware and software including peripheral equipment.</li><li>Basic monitoring and troubleshooting of backup and restoration services.</li><li>Recognizing basic security/virus issues and solutions.</li><li>Working without close supervision in standard work situations.</li><li>Using initiative and independent judgment within established procedural guidelines.</li><li>Record keeping principles and practices.</li><li>Maintaining accurate records of work performed.</li><li>Preparing clear and concise documentation, user instructions, reports, correspondence, and other written materials.</li><li>Basic skills in managing active directory accounts and group policies.</li><li>Fundamental familiarity with network connection switches.</li><li>Contributing effectively to the accomplishment of team or work unit goals, objectives, and activities.</li><li>Establishing and maintaining effective working relationships with a variety of individuals contacted in the course of the work.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Physical/Mental Requirements<br/><br/></strong>The physical demands described herein are representative of those that must be met by an employee to successfully perform the essential functions of the job. Reasonable accommodations may be made to enable individuals with disabilities to perform the essential functions.<br/><br/>Mobility to work in a lab and office setting, use standard office and lab equipment and stamina to sit for extended periods of time; exerting up to 40 pounds of force occasionally and/or up to 40 pounds of force frequently to lift, carry, push, pull or move objects; vision to read printed materials and computer screens; and hearing and speech to communicate in person or over the telephone.<br/><br/><strong> WORKING ENVIRONMENT <br/><br/></strong>Work is performed primarily in an office setting with occasional work in a lab setting. Work is subject to chemicals, fumes, gasses, noxious odors and related items in a lab setting.
