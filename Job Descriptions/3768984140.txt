We are seeking someone with excellent customer service skills and the ability to make quick decisions in resolving technical problems. Responsibilities: - Provide onsite support to a fast-growing business group of 1000+ employees - Troubleshoot and resolve software and hardware issues - Assist with laptop support for Windows PC’s, Macs, and Mobile Devices - Ensure excellent customer service in handling internal user's technical issues - Collaborate with team members to resolve more complex technical problems Requirements: - High School Diploma, GED, or equivalent certification - At least 1 year of experience in remote administration and end user support - Familiarity with ticketing and traffic reporting tools (Zendesk, HPSM, ServiceNow) Preferred Qualifications: - Associate’s Degree in Business, Information Systems, Computer Science or Military experience - Experience with Active Directory services integration - Knowledge of change, incident, and problem management functions - Apple certification(s) - Experience with virtualization technologies (VMWare tools) - macOS Server administration experience - Experience with platform management and integration (JAMF Casper Suite or SCCM) - Familiarity with productivity tools on Mac (MS Office, Lync or Skype) - Experience supporting end users on Mac systems - Knowledge of image deployment, software and system testing, and encryption and security tools Salary Range: New York City (Hybrid On-Site): $71,400 - $81,500 for Principal Coordinator, Support Operations.
