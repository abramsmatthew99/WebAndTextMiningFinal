<strong>Job Description<br/><br/></strong>My Traverse City, MI client is in need of a IT Systems Administrator to join their team. This role will be to provide and assist in a variety of services in the installation, operation, repair and maintenance of the company’s computers, networks and related equipment; monitor, evaluate and modify network systems to assure proper security and operation; install, maintain and repair computer hardware, software and peripheral equipment; provide technical user support assistance.<br/><br/>Monitor and maintain network stability, integrity, connectivity and availability.<br/><br/>Monitor and maintain network security and security solutions, including; firewall, anti-virus, and intrusion detection systems.<br/><br/>Monitor and maintain networked servers such as file servers, e-mail and database systems.<br/><br/>Monitor and maintain infrastructure routers, switches, phones, software’s, PC drivers and configuration settings to support the computing environment.<br/><br/>Monitor and maintain LAN, WAN, WLAN, DNS, WINS, LDAP, DHCP, Group Policy management, and Microsoft Active Directory services.<br/><br/>Monitor and maintain system backups and recovery processes.<br/><br/>Troubleshoot, evaluate, repair and upgrade company computers and peripheral hardware.<br/><br/>Support telecommunications infrastructure including telephone services, voicemail, VoIP systems and audio/video technologies.<br/><br/>Performs server security audits, maintain license compliance and comply with adopted Sarbanes Oxley policies &amp; procedures.<br/><br/>Assists with the maintenance and documentation of network configurations, cabling layouts, and PC hardware/software inventories.<br/><br/>Implements networking solutions ensuring effective and efficient network operations.<br/><br/>Provide Help Desk support, responding to incoming calls, pages, e-mails, and after hours on-call support, regarding end-user computer issues, documenting problems and providing diagnosis and resolution.<br/><br/>Providing coverage as backup to iSeries (AS400) staff.<br/><br/><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong>Minimum of 3 years’ experience in Information Technology support; 5 years’ experience preferred<br/><br/>Bachelor’s Degree in Information Systems Management/Computer Network/Computer Science or equivalent<br/><br/>Highly self-motivated and directed, with attention to detail.<br/><br/>Strong customer service orientation and ability to support remote users.<br/><br/>On-call availability for emergency issues.<br/><br/>Ability to conduct research into enterprise networking issues and products as required.<br/><br/>Minimal travel may be required for the purpose of on-site installation of hardware/software and troubleshooting.<br/><br/>Understanding of advanced concepts, such as virtualization, share storage, networking, PowerShell scripts, MIIS and thin-client technology.<br/><br/>Excellent technical knowledge and understanding of Windows Operating systems (server&amp; client), Virtual environments (VMware), Exchange, SQL, and Microsoft Office suites.<br/><br/>Excellent technical knowledge and understanding of Microsoft Active Directory, DNS,DHCP and Group Policy management.<br/><br/>Strong technical knowledge and understanding of enterprise-level LAN, WAN and WLAN infrastructure.<br/><br/>Industry standard certifications such as; CCNA, CCENT, MCP, etc. preferred, but not required.<br/><br/>Additional Information<br/><br/><strong>This is a On site position in Traverse City, MI. Company offers a great relocation package.<br/><br/></strong><strong>No C2C or sponsorship is available for this position. Must be authorized to work in the US without expiration.</strong>
