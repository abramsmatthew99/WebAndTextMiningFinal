Collaborate with UI/UX designers to translate wireframes and visual designs into responsive web interfaces. Ensure the technical feasibility of UI/UX designs and optimize them for maximum performance and scalability. Work closely with back-end developers to integrate front-end components with server-side logic and APIs.
