        The Commercial Service Representative is responsible for assisting in the input and actualization of commercial activities (midstream and marketing) within company’s TIES (Trilogy Effective’s The Integrated Energy System) transactional software. This position requires timely and adequate management of TIES transactions of commercial activities along with assisting in the monthly settlement process.<br/><br/>This role will be based in our Dallas, Texas Corporate Office.<br/><br/>Aethon Energy is a leading private investment firm focused on acquiring, operating, and developing onshore energy assets across North America. Founded in 1990 and headquartered in Dallas, TX, AEM has a 30-plus year track record of generating attractive returns for its fund investors over multiple commodity price cycles. Aethon employs more than 300 employees across its businesses, which drives large-scale upstream development with vertically integrated midstream assets and disciplined risk management to provide capital efficient growth. We are looking for talented and motivated people to join us on our mission of creating exceptional value through sustainable energy solutions that make the world better.<br/><br/><strong>DUTIES &amp; JOB RESPONSIBILITIES:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Obtain and verify data from internal and external sources and update TIES to accurately reflect all (term, monthly and daily) buy / sell transactions, transportation and cash-outs on 3rd party midstream and transmission pipelines</li><li>Obtain and verify data from internal and external sources and update TIES for midstream activities on company assets</li><li>Assist in the TIES setup for monthly activities and accounting accrual process</li><li>Perform pipeline settlements for assets in East Texas, ensuring volumes, rates, cash outs are correct in TIES</li><li>Ensure contracts for East Texas assets are working properly in TIES including all pertinent inlet and delivery meters, fuel rates, fees and allocations</li><li>Monitor 3rd party producer’s nominations and flows on Aethon owned midstream systems to minimize imbalances throughout the month utilizing TIES Electronic Bulletin Board</li><li>Send actualized imbalance statements with FMS volume statements backup data to 3rd party producers after the month of flow</li><li>Assist in monthly close process by obtaining all settlement statements related to Midstream commercial activities (invoices, volume statements, buy / sell, transportation, 3rd party gathering / processing, oba / cash-outs, etc.) and ensure they are reflective of the commercial terms and actual monthly activities before submitting to Midstream Manager for approval then to accounting for processing</li><li>Assist in the actualization of TIES transactions based on verified settlement statements</li><li>Work closely with TIES administrator regarding setup of new facilities and contracts</li><li>Assist in resolving monthly settlement discrepancies</li><li>Calculate and monitor pipeline system physical balances to ensure minimal gas losses for each system</li><li>Be available to assist customers with on system scheduling<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in Business, Marketing or Finance / Accounting or a technical field (e.g. Computer Science or Engineering) is required</li><li>Commercial experience with natural gas midstream assets, exploration and production companies is preferred, but not required</li><li>Proficient in the use of computers and software, especially Microsoft Office</li><li>Must be a self-starter, highly motivated, detail oriented and flexible as priorities and direction may change on short notice</li><li>Demonstrated strong organizational and customer service skills along with the ability to communicate both verbally and in writing</li><li>Ability to establish priorities, manage multiple activities and communicate at all levels in the organization</li><li>Ability to work both independently with minimal guidance and as part of a team</li><li>Must be eligible to work in the United States </li><li>Successful candidate will be able to meet company standard background check specific to individual role prior to employment and will be subject to Company Drug &amp; Alcohol Program</li><li>Position is an indoor office position which will require remaining in a stationary position, often sitting or standing for prolonged periods of time <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Benefits<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Premiums for health, dental, and vision insurance for employee and children are 100% paid for by Aethon and premiums for spouse are 75% covered</li><li>401k match 100% up to 7% of annual base salary</li><li>Health Advocate to assist navigating your medical, dental, and vision insurance</li><li>Company provided: </li><li>Basic Life and AD&amp;D Insurance</li><li>Short- and Long-Term Disability Insurance</li><li>Hybrid work schedule (currently WFH on Monday and Friday)</li><li>Paid vacation time </li><li>11 paid holidays annually</li><li>40 hours paid sick time annually</li><li>Casual dress code</li><li>Unlimited access to workout facilities within the building</li><li></li><li>Wellness program with earned incentives for completed activities<br/><br/></li></ul>Please note this job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties, or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. Employees must be willing and able to perform other duties as assigned.
