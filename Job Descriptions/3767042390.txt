Physical positions will include but not be limited to walking, sitting or standing for extended periods of time, crawling, kneeling, stooping or cramped working places and work requiring repeated or frequent climbing. Environment will include customer contact, extended work days, an office laboratory work space.<br/><br/><strong> REQUIRED DEGREE/EDUCATION/CERTIFICATION: <br/><br/></strong><ul><li> High School Diploma or equivalent+ 5 years IT experience </li><li> or Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related discipline + 3 years of cybersecurity experience </li><li> 3 years of Navy accreditation or RMF experience; Equivalent years of experience may be substituted in lieu of education. </li><li> DoD 8570/8140: IAM I <br/><br/></li></ul><strong> REQUIRED SECURITY AND CLEARANCE: <br/><br/></strong><ul><li> Must be a U.S. Citizen </li><li> Secret security clearance is required.
