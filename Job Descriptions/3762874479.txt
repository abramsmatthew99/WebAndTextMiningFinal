        The Enviva team is driven by our shared vision for a renewable energy future. We are a fast-growing, purpose-driven, global energy company specializing in delivering sustainable wood bioenergy solutions. We are the world's largest producer of sustainable wood pellets, which provide a low-carbon alternative to fossil fuels.<br/><br/>Enviva is looking for a Data Analyst to join our team. As a Data Analyst, you will have the chance to shape data analytics at Enviva and make a real impact on our organization. You will be responsible for working with stakeholders to drive decision-making and problem solving. The ideal candidate is highly motivated with strong quantitative and analytical skills, as well as a deep understanding of data and analytics. You should have experience working with a wide range of data sources, and an ability to work in a fast-paced environment. Previous experience in a data analyst role for a large organization is desirable.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong>Data Collection &amp; Analysis:<br/><br/><ul><li>Extracting and aggregating data from multiple disparate sources including SQL, Azure DW/DL, and Excel</li><li>Ensuring data quality and integrity by cleaning and preprocessing the data. </li><li>Performing analysis to derive insights from the data. </li><li>Developing and applying data analysis techniques, including regression analysis, clustering, and classification. </li><li>Provide assistance with other ad hoc reporting requests<br/><br/></li></ul>Data Visualization:<br/><br/><ul><li>Creating visual representations of data using tools like charts, graphs, and dashboards. </li><li>Presenting data in a clear and understandable format for non-technical stakeholders. <br/><br/></li></ul>Report Generation:<br/><br/><ul><li>Gather, define and document business requirements and turn them into meaningful solutions for leadership audiences</li><li>Developing business driven reports, dashboards and data visualization using PowerBI including DAX. </li><li>Creating actionable recommendations based on data insights. </li><li>Automating reports for real-time self-service consumption<br/><br/></li></ul>Data Modeling:<br/><br/><ul><li>Building predictive models to forecast trends or outcomes. </li><li>Evaluating and fine-tuning models to improve their accuracy and performance. <br/><br/></li></ul>Data Management:<br/><br/><ul><li>Organizing and maintaining databases and data repositories. </li><li>Ensuring data security, privacy, and compliance with relevant regulations. <br/><br/></li></ul>Communication and Collaboration:<br/><br/><ul><li>Collaborating with cross-functional teams to understand their data needs and provide data-driven insights. </li><li>Effective communication of findings and insights to non-technical stakeholders. </li><li>Desire and ability to take ownership and drive programs successfully with minimal guidance or supervision. <br/><br/></li></ul>Problem Solving:<br/><br/><ul><li>Identifying business problems and using data analysis to propose solutions and make data-driven decisions. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Required Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's Degree in Data Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Econometrics, Economics, Operations Research, Computer Science, Business, or related field</li><li>3+ years data-analytics experience (e.g., managing structured and unstructured data, developing dashboards, and reporting results)</li><li>Advanced skills and experience developing business driven reports, dashboards and data visualization using PowerBI including DAX. </li><li>Experience extracting and aggregating data from multiple disparate sources including SQL, Azure DW/DL, and Excel</li><li>Experience automating reports for real-time self-service consumption</li><li>Strong background in data analysis, data validation and data quality assurance. </li><li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills</li><li>Proven ability to gather, define and document business requirements and turn them into meaningful solutions for leadership audiences</li><li>Ability to pivot to provide assistance with other ad hoc reporting requests</li><li>Strong analytical, critical thinking, and organizational skills</li><li>Excellent written and verbal communication and documentation skills</li><li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office</li><li>Proactive, customer-service oriented approach in engaging with stakeholders<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Preferred Qualifications - What Will Set You Apart<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Desire and ability to take ownership and drive programs successfully with minimal guidance or supervision. </li><li>Strong aptitude and curiosity for technology to enable understanding of relevant data to help develop business insights and/or identify significant anomalies</li><li>Experience/proficiency with project management software such as Cora and Smartsheet is preferred<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Travel Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Minimal, as
