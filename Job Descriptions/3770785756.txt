<p>The BI Administrator position is responsible for configuration, administration, maintenance, and support of US Cellular’s Enterprise Business Intelligence implementations in a 24x7 production environment. This position requires working with key business partners and analysts to develop and deploy BI solutions meeting the needs of the business; delivering the information needed to make critical business decisions in support of providing our customers the best possible experience. This role includes monitoring system stability, availability, and performance including identifying and implementing improvements.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Location Note</strong></p><p><em>This role is a hybrid work arrangement where the candidate can perform this role in-office or remote. In-office expectations within our operating footprint will be limited and based on an on purpose, for a purpose model should a meeting or collaborative session be scheduled. For this role, we prefer candidates that either live in the Chicagoland area or can reasonably commute to our office in Chicago should a meeting or collaborative session be scheduled.</em></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Essential Functions</strong></p><ul><li>Provide ongoing administrative support of USC’s BI environment.</li><li>Responsible for supporting BI environment installs, administration, and operations.</li><li>Manage BI environment following standards and best practices.</li><li>Works with other teams to implement improvements.</li><li>Aids business teams on matters relating to system connections, security, user accounts, and creation of data models.</li><li>Assists users with use of BI applications to drive business value.</li><li>Aids in development of BI Semantic Layers (Cognos Packages/Models)</li><li>Monitors and maintains system performance.</li><li>Participates in reporting requirement sessions.</li><li>Assists with BI test executions.</li><li>Communicate effectively in verbal and written English.</li><li>Demonstrate ability to work in team environment and help resolve issues.</li><li>Communicate status / risks to manager regarding delivering solution on time.</li><li>Participates in on-call schedule; off-hours support as required.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Required Experience</strong></p><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in the following areas of study: Computer Science, Information Systems, Electrical Engineering or related field or equivalent work experience required.</li><li>BI Administrator: 4+ years relevant work experience required.</li><li>Experience with BI Report &amp; Dashboard development required.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Preferred Experience</strong></p><ul><li>Experience understanding performance and tuning impacts preferred.</li><li>Experience with installing/upgrading BI applications preferred.</li><li>Experience with various data sources for BI applications preferred.</li><li>Development experience working with SAS, Cognos, Tableau, Hyperion, Unica, or others preferred.</li><li>Specific experience with maintaining SAS, Cognos, Tableau, Hyperion, or Unica preferred.</li><li>Experience with Symantec Layers in BI Applications preferred.</li><li>Knowledge of Oracle SQL development techniques, including PL/SQL preferred.</li><li>O/S &amp; Network: IBM AIX, Sun Solaris, HP/UX preferred.</li><li>Agile Experience preferred.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong><span class="ql-cursor"> </span>Job</strong></p><p>Information Technology</p><p><strong>Location(s)</strong></p><p>Illinois-CHICAGO_IL</p><p>UScellular® is an EEO employer and gives consideration to qualified applicants without regard to race/color/age/religion/sex/sexual orientation/gender identity/national origin/disability/veteran status, pregnancy or genetic information.</p>
