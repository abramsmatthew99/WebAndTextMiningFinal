Utilizing a variety of software platforms, the Decision Support Data Analyst assists in the preparation and development of robust reporting for the Umpqua Health leadership team, managers and other organizational stakeholders. Under management directive, this individual may evaluate reports and make recommendations which impact business decisions. Position will support the Senior Director of Decision Support.<br/><br/><strong>Essential Job Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Run queries and create analysis utilizing all data warehouse systems.</li><li>Provide ad hoc financial and operational reporting to finance, clinical and executive staff.</li><li>Perform complex financial analysis related to special projects.</li><li>Perform sources to serve as research initiatives financial data.</li><li>Develop and provide complex reporting and analysis on revenue and health plan claims data.</li><li>Track population and health trends for purposes of identifying target areas of utilization and case management.</li><li>Oversight of metrics components related to healthcare claims processing that fall outside normal claims guidelines.</li><li>Assist data extracts and validation for the purposes of state mandated reporting requirements.</li><li>Document processes for preparing any monthly or routine reporting.</li><li>Create tools, spreadsheets and systems to improve financial data analysis and reporting. </li><li>Provide administrative and operational support to the Senior Director of Decision Support.</li><li>Expand analytical reporting skills with 20 hours per year of continued professional education. </li><li>Comply with organization’s internal policies and procedures, Code of Conduct, experience in business and financial analytics.</li><li>Familiarity with report analysis</li><li>No suspension/exclusion/debarment from participation in federal health care programs (eg.
