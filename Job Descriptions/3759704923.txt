        Games continue to evolve and push the boundaries of entertainment as we know it. As a Recruiter for the Gaming team (Xbox) at Microsoft, you will have the distinction of partnering with the studios that make games such as Minecraft, Forza, Gears of War and Halo, among many others. We are looking for a Recruiter who has in-depth knowledge of the gaming industry, balanced with client management experience, candidate engagement skills and a passion for recruiting. An ideal candidate for this role will have experience recruiting for AAA Gaming Studios focusing on various genres, platforms, and titles. The opportunities of this role stretch beyond hands-on recruiting, to strategic projects, coaching leaders to be the very best in the industry at hiring, mentoring others and driving impact across our region.<br/><br/>Microsoft’s mission is to empower every person and every organization on the planet to achieve more. As employees we come together with a growth mindset, innovate to empower others, and collaborate to realize our shared goals.
