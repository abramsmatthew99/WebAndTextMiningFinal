Lilly is investing over $1 billion to create a new state-of-the-art manufacturing site, in Concord, North Carolina. The brand-new facility will utilize the latest technology to increase the company's manufacturing capacity in parenteral (injectable) medications, device assembly and packaging operations. This is an exciting once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to help build and operate a new site. The Lilly Concord site will be built using the latest high-tech equipment, advanced highly integrated and automated manufacturing systems, and have a focus on minimizing the impact to our environment. This is an opportunity you don’t want to miss!<br/><br/><strong>What You’ll Be Doing<br/><br/></strong>You will be part of Tech at Lilly MQ (Manufacturing and Quality), an organization that drives manufacturing operational excellence through utilization of technology. Tech at Lilly MQ strives to enable the making of medicine “with safety first and quality always”.<br/><br/>As the Manager-IT Service Delivery, you will play a key role to oversee and manage the IT support at our Concord manufacturing site. This position plays a critical role in ensuring that all IT systems are maintained, supported, and continuously improved to achieve the aspirational goal of zero downtime.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Ensuring the reliable and secure operation of IT systems, including networks, servers, and storage, to minimize downtime and maximize productivity.</li><li>Ensure round-the-clock availability of IT support to address critical issues and minimize downtime. Implement an on-call rotation schedule for support staff.</li><li>Oversee the handling and resolution of IT incidents, ensuring timely and effective responses to minimize operational disruptions. Ensuring that all support requests are efficiently logged, categorized, prioritized, and resolved within established SLAs.</li><li>Handle changes and updates to IT systems and services with minimal disruptions, following best practices and change management processes.</li><li>Identify and implement opportunities to streamline support processes and reduce response and resolution times. Continuously improve IT support.</li><li>Maintain high levels of customer satisfaction by delivering exceptional IT support to end-users and addressing their needs promptly.</li><li>Implement and maintain a comprehensive knowledge base for the IT support team to access relevant information and solutions for common issues.</li><li>Implement quality control and quality assurance measures to ensure that IT support services meet or exceed established standards.</li><li>Manage IT support resources, including hardware, software, and tools necessary for providing uninterrupted services.</li><li>Regularly monitor and report on the performance of the IT support function, including KPIs and customer satisfaction metrics.</li><li>Manage relationships with third-party vendors and suppliers to ensure their services align with the site's zero-downtime objective.</li><li>Manage the budget for the IT support function, ensuring cost control and efficient financial planning.</li><li>Align IT support operations with the site's overall business goals and objectives, with a strong focus on achieving zero downtime.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Basic Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in Software Engineering, Computer Science, Computer Engineering, or a related field.</li><li>Minimum of 5+ years of IT leadership experience working in IT Support.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Additional Skills/Preferences<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Master’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Business Administration, or a related field is preferred.</li><li>The capability to align IT support services with the organization's overall strategic goals and industry-specific objectives.</li><li>Excellent problem-solving solutions.</li><li>Skills in vendor relationship management, contract negotiation, and compliance enforcement to ensure reliable third-party support.</li><li>Understanding of cybersecurity best international operations, diverse teams, global IT projects, or working in a global organization.</li><li>Previous LEAN and Agile experience.</li><li>Understanding of pharmaceutical manufacturing processes and compliance with industry-specific regulations, such as FDA guidelines for Good Manufacturing Practices (GMP).</li><li>Certification in relevant IT areas (e.g., ITIL, CompTIA A+, CompTIA
