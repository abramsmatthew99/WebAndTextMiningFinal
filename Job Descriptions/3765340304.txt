<strong>Position Description<br/><br/></strong>IT Service Desk Technician<br/><br/><strong>Department:</strong> Information Technology<br/><br/><strong>Location:</strong> Rochester<br/><br/><strong>Classification:</strong> Nonexempt<br/><br/><strong>Reports to:</strong> Director of Information Technology<br/><br/><strong>Company Overview<br/><br/></strong>Woods Oviatt Gilman LLP is a leading and reputable full-service law firm dedicated to providing exceptional legal services to our clients. With a team of highly skilled and experienced attorneys, we strive to deliver comprehensive and effective solutions to meet the diverse needs of our clients. Headquarters are located in Rochester, NY with additional offices in Albany and Buffalo, NY.<br/><br/>We foster a collaborative and inclusive work environment where every team member is valued and respected. We encourage open communication, teamwork, and professional growth. Our firm promotes a healthy work-life balance and supports the well-being of our employees.<br/><br/><strong>Summary<br/><br/></strong>This role will be a customer-facing role that provides technical support to our end users in a very fast-paced environment. It’s an entry-level position that will be open for growth for the right individual. The ability to handle customer requests promptly, problem-solving skills, and technical knowledge are required for this role.<br/><br/><strong>Duties And Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Serve as the first point of contact for customers seeking technical assistance</li><li>Perform troubleshooting to resolve issues</li><li>Create and manage tickets within the IT Service Desk ticketing system</li><li>Update best practices, solutions, and recommendations</li><li>Regularly overview tickets and provide updates</li><li>Document, track, and monitor problems to ensure a timely resolution and follow-up with end users are completed</li><li>Ability to escalate tickets to higher levels promptly</li><li>Convey any feedback and/or suggestions to the rest of the IT team</li><li>Provide feedback to management based on customer response and behavior</li><li>Contribute to end-user training by providing feedback and/or being actively involved</li><li>Be able to understand newer technologies and stay on top of new technologies</li><li>Troubleshoot basic network connectivity issues and application issues</li><li>Understand the role critically to set the tone for the whole IT team<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Technical Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Solid knowledge of basic MS Office applications, such as MS Word, MS Excel, and MS PowerPoint</li><li>Knowledge of Windows Operating Systems</li><li>Experience with Adobe 2017 Professional</li><li>Knowledge of MS Edge, Google Chrome, Firefox</li><li>Working knowledge of basic printer troubleshooting, mobile devices, and other computer hardware <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Excellent communication skills</li><li>Demonstrated problem-solving skills</li><li>Demonstrated ability to work collaboratively in a team</li><li>Produce high-quality accurate work</li><li>Ability to organize and prioritize issues and workload</li><li>Demonstrate a respectful, friendly, and helpful attitude toward co-workers</li><li>Ability to impact operations and effect change without being confrontational</li><li>Process &amp; detail-oriented, analytical, and inquisitive</li><li>Proficient in Microsoft Office, and Document Management software experience<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Physical Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Must have the ability to carry computer equipment, printers, and files</li><li>Bend and stand for extended periods</li><li>Must be able to lift to 50 lbs. to transport and move equipment if needed<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Education &amp; Experience<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor of Science in IT, Computer Science, or a relevant field</li><li>Two plus years of experience in an IT Service Desk support role<br/><br/></li></ul>Other Duties: Please note this job description is not designed to cover or contain a comprehensive listing of activities, duties, or responsibilities that are required of the employee for this job. This is a snapshot of the core functions and responsibilities. All inquiries will be handled with the utmost confidentiality. The compensation range for this position is $22-$26 per hour, representing our good faith and reasonable estimate of the potential compensation at the time of posting.
