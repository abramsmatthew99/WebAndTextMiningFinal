<strong>Who We Are<br/><br/></strong>Verkada is the largest cloud-based B2B physical security platform company in the world. Only Verkada offers six product lines — video security cameras, access control, environmental sensors, alarms, workplace and intercoms — integrated with a single cloud-based software platform.<br/><br/>Designed with simplicity and scalability in mind, Verkada gives organizations the real-time insight to know what could impact the safety and comfort of people throughout their physical environment, while empowering them to take immediate action to minimize security risks, workplace frustrations and costly inefficiencies.<br/><br/>Founded in 2016 with more than $460M in funding raised to date, Verkada has expanded rapidly with 15 offices across three continents, 1,600+ full-time employees and 20,000+ customers across 70+ countries.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Launching new products in collaboration with Design, Engineering, and Marketing. </li><li>Gathering customer feedback and digging into customer use cases to understand their needs and to prioritize feature development. </li><li>Defining internal processes and tracking metrics to optimize customer experience. </li><li>Training and educating our Sales team on new product features and releases. </li><li>Partnering with the Sales team to close strategic customer accounts. </li><li>Making data-driven decisions to define and optimize product pricing. </li><li>Establishing technology partnerships with complementary tech companies. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Minimum of 3 years of experience in a business oriented function</li><li>Undergraduate degree in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, or similar quantitative field.
