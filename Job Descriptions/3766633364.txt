<strong>Company Description<br/><br/></strong><strong>Our Mission<br/><br/></strong>At Palo Alto Networks® everything starts and ends with our mission:<br/><br/>Being the cybersecurity partner of choice, protecting our digital way of life.<br/><br/>Our vision is a world where each day is safer and more secure than the one before. We are a company built on the foundation of challenging and disrupting the way things are done, and we’re looking for innovators who are as committed to shaping the future of cybersecurity as we are.<br/><br/><strong>Our Approach to Work<br/><br/></strong>We lead with flexibility and choice in all of our people programs. We have disrupted the traditional view that all employees have the same needs and wants. We offer personalization and offer our employees the opportunity to choose what works best for them as often as possible - from your wellbeing support to your growth and development, and beyond!<br/><br/>At Palo Alto Networks, we believe in the power of collaboration and value in-person interactions. This is why our employees generally work from the office three days per week, leaving two days for choice and flexibility to work where you feel most effective. This setup fosters casual conversations, problem-solving, and trusted relationships. While details may evolve, our goal is to create an environment where innovation thrives, with office-based teams coming together three days a week to collaborate and thrive, together!<br/><br/><strong>Job Description<br/><br/></strong><strong>Your Career<br/><br/></strong>The Business Program Analyst - Google will pursue non-linear growth for Palo Alto Networks (PANW) through closer and smarter collaboration with Google. You will work closely with teams related to sales, technical integrations, marketing, go-to-market, product, engineering, and finance. You possess strong relationship-building and quantitative and qualitative problem-solving skills.<br/><br/>The Google Alliance team is a central conduit for the Google Cloud and Palo Alto Networks ecosystem with our joint customers and partners. We are responsible for the end-to-end experience that the customers and partners have in our ecosystem. The Business Program Analyst - Google will report to Director, Google Programs, but will also have several dotted line coordination requirements with various other stakeholders within the partnership.<br/><br/>As a member of our team, you will continuously gain a deep understanding of our ecosystem, joint solution, and go-to-market strategies to further our team’s mission. You will be a part of cross-functional initiatives including co-sell, strategic planning, technical demonstrations, campaigns, channel enablements, joint solutions, and partnership roadmaps.<br/><br/><strong>Your Impact<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Manage joint sell-with and go-to-market activities with Google</li><li>Understand and articulate the portfolio and strategies between Google and Palo Alto Networks to internal and external stakeholders with data</li><li>Streamline cross-functional communications between Engineering, Sales, Product, Marketing, and Finance</li><li>Build and maintain relationships with key personnel in Google Cloud and facilitate engagement</li><li>Attend and participate in technical GCP events and seminars</li><li>Provide an escalation point for pre-sales and post-sales issues for any activity with GCP<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><strong>Your Experience<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>BA/BS Degree in Business, Management Information Systems, Marketing, Computer Science, or a related field (or equivalent work experience or equivalent military experience required) </li><li>2+ years of work experience</li><li>Familiarity with BigQuery, MS Excel, or Google Sheets (analyzing spreadsheets and charts) </li><li>Public Cloud experience preferred (AWS, Google Cloud/GCP, Microsoft Azure)</li><li>Demonstrated ability to work both in-person and virtually</li><li>Proven ability to manage a myriad of different disciplines and tasks in a high pressure, time sensitive environment</li><li>Strong communication (written and verbal), negotiation and presentation skills </li><li>Have a "Whatever it takes" attitude and motivation to do whatever necessary to assist in winning business</li><li>Ability to understand technical concepts - have enthusiasm for technology and be able to articulate clearly to all levels of technical aptitude<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Additional Information<br/><br/></strong><strong>The Team<br/><br/></strong>The Google Alliance Team at Palo Alto Networks is a strategic pillar for our continued growth within cybersecurity, and a critical component to accomplishing our mission. Palo Alto Networks has ambitious plans to grow our Google sell-with business, both in markets where collaboration is already strong, as well as expanding our business in growth markets.<br/><br/>To fit in this role, you are personable, creative, willing to ask questions, always learning, and have a strong work ethic. You are confident in who you are and able to enjoy the personable interactions that come with a highly cross-functional job. Becoming a part of the team means you will be surrounded by amazing, smart and hardworking teammates and that’s a great place to be. This is a unique opportunity to make your mark by taking our growing Google business to the next level and beyond, while working in a dynamic and supportive environment.<br/><br/><strong>Our Commitment<br/><br/></strong>We’re trailblazers that dream big, take risks, and challenge cybersecurity’s status quo.
