As a member of the Technology leadership team, they will contribute to the development and execution of the enterprise-wide Technology strategy and ensure its alignment with the enterprise's business maintain platform sourcing strategy and provide strategic oversight for vendor/partner relationship management</li><li>Responsible for daily operations of Cloud infrastructure and development</li><li>Identify business process and practices that can be improved and develop service improvement plans that achieve these results</li><li>Manage all consumer technology related tests</li><li>Manage communication across the organization, to ensure effective collection and dissemination of information regarding consumer technology Master’s Degree preferred.</li><li>At least 12 years of IT experience in roles with increasing levels of leadership responsibilities</li><li>Extensive background in consumer technology transformations for a global company</li><li>Strong experience architecting and optimizing Consumer Technologies – eCommerce (SFCC, Shopify, Hybris), Retail/POS solutions, Marketing technologies (Salesforce, Liquid, Braze) and Data Warehousing/Analytics services (GCP Big Query, Tableau, AI/ML)</li><li>Expertise in modern cloud IT Infrastructure solutioning – Azure, Microsoft 365, ITSM (ServiceNow), Google Cloud, etc.</li><li>Experience working within corporate environments that are experiencing massive growth and global expansion, including scaling and integrating consumer technology platforms</li><li>Ability to create a consumer technology roadmap that will enable current and future business needs</li><li>Consumer product and retail industry experience required</li><li>Passion for building outstanding teams and creating an environment where individuals can do their best work</li><li>Executive presence and the ability to effectively communicate across varying audiences</li><li>Strong business and commercial acumen</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>US Workplace Approach</strong></p><ul><li>Onsite: Role requires you to be onsite full-time.</li><li>Hybrid: Role requires you to be located in Austin, Texas and is a mixture of onsite and remote.</li><li>Remote: Role is full-time remote.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><em>Approach may vary based on role.</em></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>YETI is proud to be an Equal Opportunity Employer.</strong></p><p><br/></p><p>YETI Global Workforce Data Privacy Notice</p><p><em>If you require accommodation in order to apply for a job, please contact us at accommodationrequest@yeti.com.</em></p>
