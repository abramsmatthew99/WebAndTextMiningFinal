<p>BASIC FUNCTION</p><p>Gathers business requirements, conducts need assessments, and develops functional specifications to ensure that developed information technology solutions support business objectives. Serves as a liaison between development teams and the internal/external customer or end user. Follows an established requirements gathering process to identify requirements. Lead all aspects of business applications projects. Communicates requirements to the development teams using specifications, statements of work, project plans, or other forms of documentation. Coordinates with developers, quality assurance teams, and users to test processes and ensure functionality and user acceptance. May develop user documentation or training materials. As a high-level specialist, provides ongoing advanced application support to business end-users. In this role you will function as the Project Manager and a Technical Lead and will collaborate with subject matter experts to translate project objectives and requirements into schedules and tasks and drive technical tasks to through delivery and testing cycles. Candidate needs to demonstrate experience managing medium to large scale projects and technical lead roles. The role will work close with the Director of IT. </p><p><br/></p><p> </p><p> <strong> </strong></p><p><strong>PRINCIPAL ACCOUNTABILITIES</strong></p><p> </p><p>In this role, you may perform multiple roles and should be able to demonstrate experience with the following:</p><p> <em> </em></p><p> <strong><em>Business Relationship Management and Applications Strategy</em></strong></p><ul><li>Serve as a liaison between business users and other IT groups, including external contractors.</li><li>Partner with Business Functional Leads and System Implementation Partner.</li><li>Build relationships with business users and help prioritize IT application initiatives.</li><li>Coordinate with technical applications groups to ensure business systems are kept current.</li><li>Direct activities, assign responsibilities, set expectations and motivate team members toward achieving program/project objectives.</li><li>Identify software solutions to ensure the applications and integrations align with the business strategy.</li><li>Maintain existing applications and implement new software solutions.</li></ul><p> </p><p><strong><em>Business Process Improvement / Applications Consulting</em></strong></p><p>· Work with internal customers and external business partners (customers, suppliers, etc.) to determine business needs, write system process specifications, and implement software solutions.</p><ul><li>Consult functional areas of the business to identify applications that drive business process improvement and add value to the business operations.</li><li>Maintain working knowledge of the business areas and the associated system applications</li><li>Uses advanced techniques, theories, and processes to analyze and understand multiple applications that can deliver measurable value to the business.</li><li>Provide direction in new and existing software technologies relevant to the business needs.</li><li>Leverage software applications to support business initiatives involving Operational Excellence, Continuous Improvement, Lean Manufacturing, etc.</li><li>Consult the business operations with identifying information delivery and data analytics strategies.</li></ul><p> </p><p><strong><em>Applications Project Management</em></strong></p><ul><li>Work on business application portfolio projects as team leader and/or technical resource.</li><li>Lead divisional business application projects, including internal business initiatives, external customer and supplier projects, and corporate / enterprise IT requirements.</li><li>Responsible for project management leadership, delivering projects on-time and within budget.</li><li>Facilitate applications projects with cross-functional business resources and IT technical staff.</li><li>Complete Project Snapshot Submissions, Statements of Work, timelines / Gantt Charts, status reports, etc.</li></ul><p> </p><p><strong><em>Business Requirements Analysis</em></strong></p><ul><li>Lead all facets of business requirements analysis, prepare Scope of Work (SOW) for software solutions.</li><li>Conduct systems analysis to study the business procedures and identify requirements for achieving them more efficiently. Use systems analysis to help determine whether to build or buy software solutions.</li><li>Ability to lead and/or facilitate any business requirement sessions with users.</li><li>Understand the impact of application requests and provide system direction</li><li>Translation of business needs into application solutions.</li><li>Design business systems by defining the application architecture, modules, interfaces, and data for a system to satisfy specified requirements.</li><li>Coordinate all division application design and development work, leveraging Enterprise Applications / Shared Services or outsourced service providers for technical development work.</li><li>Coordinate with IT directors, Business Managers, Resource Managers and IT PMO Manager for resource assignments, creation of project deliverables, issue resolution and escalation and overall delivery of work elements.</li></ul><p> </p><p> <strong> </strong></p><p><strong>PRINCIPAL ACCOUNTABILITIES (continued)</strong></p><p>Responsibilities for this role include (continued):</p><p><strong><em> </em></strong></p><p><strong><em>Application Configurations, Implementations, and Maintenance</em></strong></p><ul><li>Participate on divisional business application implementations, both purchased and internally developed software solutions, including setup and configuration of system parameters.</li><li>Leverage agile implementation techniques to deliver software solutions in a timely and cost-effective approach, while ensuring all business requirements are met.</li><li>Design, construct, and implement of new processes and business applications.</li><li>Oversee setup and configuration of all divisional business applications, including business critical ERP systems.</li><li>Responsible for application change management process controls, including post implementation audits.</li><li>Manage ongoing maintenance of business systems, including version upgrades and patching of software applications, databases, and operating systems.</li><li>Ability to identify potential system problems and propose functional solutions aligned with user needs. </li><li>Perform specialist functions in a multi-application landscape (tactical execution, installations, technical configuration, etc.)</li><li>Work with business users to ensure all application programming is adequately tested for Quality Assurance before deploying throughout the business operations.</li></ul><p> </p><p><strong><em>Applications Training, Support, and Administration</em></strong></p><ul><li>Train end-users, Subject Matter Experts (SME’s), and Business Process Owners (BPO’s) on using business applications effectively to perform their jobs.</li><li>Provide level II applications support, providing application resources to resolve IT problems escalated to the group and servicing the customers / end-users promptly.</li><li>Engage with Corporate / Enterprise IT groups to resolve IT incidents, requests, problems, and change management.</li><li>Train level I support staff and document resolutions to reduce recurring escalations.</li><li>Use ServiceNow for recording, tracking, and closing all work in a timely manner.</li><li>Perform ongoing business system administration and application user account administration.</li><li>Participate in Sarbanes-Oxley compliance activities (IT self-audits, Internal Audit risk assessments and General Computing Control testing, and external IT audits), particularly involving financial applications.</li></ul><p> </p><p><strong>NATURE AND SCOPE</strong></p><p> </p><p><strong><em> Education:</em></strong></p><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Systems, Business Administration, or related field</li><li>Master’s degree is a plus</li></ul><p> </p><p><strong><em> Experience:</em></strong></p><ul><li>Requires 8+ years total Information Technology experience.</li><li>Requires 4+ years working in applications roles, with a minimum of 2 years as a Business Applications Analyst.</li><li>Must have solid project management and ERP functional skills on Oracle NetSuite</li><li>Experience in an industrial / manufacturing company preferred.</li></ul><p> </p><p> <strong><em>Other </em></strong></p><ul><li>Oral and written communication skills, creation of presentation quality documents for management.</li><li>Performs work independently.</li><li>Ability to build collaborative relationships, strong leadership skills.</li><li>Analytical thinking and planning skills.</li><li>Ability to actively participate in a culture which moves to systems standardization.</li><li>Willingness to travel up to 25% of the time.</li></ul><p></p>
