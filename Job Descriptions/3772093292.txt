If you want to be part of tomorrow’s health today, we want to hear from you.<br/><br/>We are looking for a technology costing analyst to join our team at Medical-Surgical. This role will be responsible for costing out technology purchases in the cloud and on premises, as well as developing and managing cost models. The ideal candidate will have a strong understanding of data and AI technologies, as well as experience in cost analysis and tracking for SAAS data products. A background in Finance is needed.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Cost out data, software and AI purchases in the cloud and on premises to support the Data, AI, software and automation roadmaps.</li><li>Provide SAAS product pricing guidance.</li><li>Develop, manage and optimize cost models.</li><li>Analyze cost data and trends and provide regular reporting.</li><li>Identify cost-saving opportunities and technology substitutes.</li><li>Optimize technology purchase- identify cost drivers, track trends, identify opportunities for cost savings, and suggest innovative costing models.</li><li>Work with stakeholders to ensure that costs are aligned with business goals.</li><li>Prepare budget, forecasts and track spending.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelors with 3-5 years experience or Masters with 2-3 years exp. FinOps certification preferred.</li><li>3+ years of experience in cost analysis in both cloud and on-premises technologies. Background in finance, computer science, sourcing &amp; procurement, or related field</li><li>Candidate should have SAAS pricing experience.</li><li>Experience in 1 of the following cloud platforms (AWS, GCP, Azure).</li><li>Experience in 1 of the following (Tableau, Power BI, Looker) in reporting.</li><li>Strong understanding of cloud services and platforms, Saas, PaaS, IaaS. Ideal candidate will know how to right-size a tech stack and understand regional settings/zonal settings/ and compute/ storage instance machine cost options.</li><li>The ability to collect, analyze, and interpret data which is essential for costing out cloud costs. This includes understanding how to use cloud billing data to identify cost drivers, track trends, and identify opportunities for cost savings.</li><li>Demonstrative skills in budgeting , cost allocation, operational P&amp;L management.</li><li>Able to think critically and creatively, and to come up with solutions that meet business needs while minimizing costs.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Additional Skills:<br/><br/></strong>In addition to these skills, technology buyers should also be familiar with the following:<br/><br/><ul><li>Technology pricing models</li><li>The ability to communicate effectively with both technical and non-technical audiences is essential for costing out cloud costs. This includes being able to explain complex technical concepts concisely, and work with stakeholders to get buy-in for cost-saving initiatives.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Benefits:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Competitive salary and benefits package</li><li>Opportunity to work on cutting-edge data and AI projects</li><li>Chance to make a real impact on the business<br/><br/><br/></li></ul>If you are a highly motivated and experienced cost analyst with a passion for data and AI, we encourage you to apply.<br/><br/>At McKesson, we care about the well-being of the patients and communities we serve, and that starts with caring for our people.
