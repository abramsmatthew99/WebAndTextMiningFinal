A degree in computer science, engineering, information science, information systems management, mathematics, operations research, statistics, or technology management <strong>or</strong> degree that provided a minimum of 24 semester hours in one or more of the fields identified above and required the development or adaptation of applications, systems or networks.<br/><br/><strong>GS-12</strong>: There is no education substitution at the GS-12 grade level for this position.<br/><br/>Experience refers to paid and unpaid experience, including volunteer work done through National Service programs (e.g., Peace Corps, AmeriCorps) and other organizations (e.g., professional; philanthropic; religious; spiritual; community, student, social).
