<strong>Job Description<br/><br/></strong>If you are an <strong>INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY </strong>professional looking for an opportunity to grow, Emerson has an exciting opportunity for you! You will support a fast-paced manufacturing and laboratory environment by designing, programming, testing, implementing, and maintaining custom client/server, mobile devices, and web-based applications to allow our facilities to deliver products to our customers.<br/><br/><strong>AS AN IT SYSTEMS ANALYST, YOU WILL: <br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Respond to user issues in a timely manner to support a fast-paced manufacturing environment</li><li>Be responsible for designing, programming, testing, implementing, and maintaining a broad range of client/server, mobile devices, and web-based applications to support shop floor and engineering lab requirements</li><li>Attend meetings with other IT analysts to gather requirements for new applications, or modifications to existing applications</li><li>Conduct end-user training upon completion of software development</li><li>Assist in the documentation of new or existing applications and business processes<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>WHO YOU ARE:<br/><br/></strong>You can anticipate customer needs and provide services that are beyond customer expectations. You can quickly and decisively take action in fact-changing, unpredictable situations. You seek out experts and innovators to learn about the impact emerging technologies might have on the business. You look for ways to make new connections and build relationships in other areas and teams.<br/><br/><strong>REQUIRED EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE, &amp; SKILLS:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science/Information Technology plus one (1) year of relevant experience or equivalence (co-op experience may be considered)</li><li>Microsoft SQL Server Database and .NET framework—specifically VB.NET and ASP.NET</li><li>Ability to travel 15 to 20% of the time</li><li>Legal Authorization to work in the United States – Sponsorship will not be provided for this role. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>PREFERRED EDUCATION, EXPERIENCE, &amp; SKILLS: <br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science/Information Technology plus two (2) year of relative experience or equivalent experience</li><li>Experience with Visual Studio, SQL Server Management Studio, and Team Foundation Server</li><li>Proficient in developing SQL Server stored procedures and queries</li><li>Knowledge/experience with barcoding printers, such as Zebra, Printronix, SATO, and scanners is a plus<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Work Schedule – Core Hours<br/><br/></strong>General working hours for this position are 8:00 am - 5:00 pm; however, we offer a work-life balance to our employees that allows flexibility around start and end times as long as the core hours of 9:00 am - 3:00 pm are covered. Our teams work together to ensure our chosen work schedules enable our creativity and productivity as we serve the needs of our customers.<br/><br/><strong>Why Work in the Greater Miami Valley Area<br/><br/></strong>Our facility is located in Sidney, OH, conveniently located within driving distance to several larger cities, such as Dayton, Troy, and Columbus. The area’s generally low costs of living, excellent school systems, and low crime rates create a family-friendly environment. The area is home to several growing communities with restaurants, festivals, shopping, and arts scenes that make them exciting places to live and work.<br/><br/><strong>About Our Location<br/><br/></strong>Our newly renovated Sidney campus has 1M square feet of manufacturing floor with an additional 120k square feet of leading-edge lab facilities. This location is the headquarters for our HVACR Americas business unit. Through our many years of compressor expertise, our products help bring comfort and convenience to refrigeration, commercial, industrial, and residential spaces. Approximately 1600 employees work in Sidney, who all support a multitude of business functions. With the blend of businesses and functions represented under this one roof, there are many opportunities for advancement within the Sidney location. We believe that our culture of passion, openness, and collaboration empowers us to work toward the same goal - to make the world a better place. We invest in the end-to-end development of our people, beginning at onboarding and through senior leadership, so they can thrive personally and professionally.<br/><br/>Flexible and competitive benefits plans offer the right options to meet your individual/family needs: medical insurance plans, dental and vision coverage, 401(k), tuition reimbursement, and more. We provide employees with flexible time off plans, including paid parental leave (maternal and paternal), vacation and holiday leave.<br/><br/>Together, we have the opportunity – and the power – to continue to revolutionize the technology behind air conditioning, heating and refrigeration, and cultivate a better future.
