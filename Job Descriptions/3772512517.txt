</li><li>Monitor the remote monitoring and management system alerts and notifications, and respond accordingly through service tickets. </li><li>System documentation maintenance and review in ConnectWise. </li><li>Communication with customers as required: keeping them informed of incident progress, notifying them of impending changes, and agreed outages.
