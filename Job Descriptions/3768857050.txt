<strong>Job Summary:<br/><br/></strong>DT Professional Services is seeking an IT Business Development Director in a hybrid capacity in Ventura, CA. The ideal candidate will create, improve and execute strategies to drive sustained growth of the business. The growth can be organic or inorganic, within the same customer segment or expansion into new customers. It can also occur within existing technology expertise or outside of existing technology expertise, and could be driven by internal efforts or in collaboration with outside technology and service partners. The BD Director will focus on customer acquisition, lead generation, oversee capture efforts and conduct prospect management. The BD Director will be responsible for leading identification of opportunities, as well as coordinating preparations of proposals, plans, specifications, and assisting with financial conditions of contracts. Additionally, BD Director will oversee new business and product line expansion efforts, ensuring adherence to master plans, pipelines, and schedules.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Collaborating with management on annual strategic and revenue goals, planning, and forecasting; maintaining short-term (3 years) and long-term (5 years) business development plans</li><li>Building relationships with external companies/partners through professional interaction and timely response and keeping prospective client and business partner, and pipeline database updated</li><li>Meeting all quotas as set through mutual agreement</li><li>Providing ideas to attract new clients and keep senior management updated about marketplace and competitor activity, and retain team members</li><li>Leading BD team to ensure sustainable support from the G&amp;S team</li><li>Making cold calls for new business leads</li><li>Creating informative presentations; presenting and delivering information to potential clients at client meetings, industry exhibits, trade shows, and conferences</li><li>Overseeing the cross functional (Capture/Technical/BD) efforts in writing new business proposals; contributing directly by working on high priority proposals as needed</li><li>Maintaining knowledge of all product and service offerings of the company</li><li>Following company guidelines and procedures for acquisition of customers, submission of RFP etc.</li><li>Understanding client needs and offering solutions and support; answering potential client questions and follow-up call questions; responding to client requests for proposals (RFPs)</li><li>Facilitating retrospective(s) to ensure team members learn from previous efforts, continue what is working and change what is not working<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Basic Qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Information Technology or related</li><li>Ability to obtain and maintain a DoD Secret Clearance</li><li>5+ years of DoD Program Management experience </li><li>10+ years experience of IT/technology solutions Program/Project Management</li><li>7+ years’ experience with lead generation and prospect/proposal management</li><li>Demonstrated advanced experience with Office suites (Microsoft Office, G Suite), presentation software (PowerPoint, Keynote), communication and collaboration tools, graphics and multimedia, and spreadsheets</li><li>Demonstrated analytical and time-management skills</li><li>Demonstrated ability to work independently or as an active member of a team, leading team<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Preferred Skills:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Master’s Degree</li><li>10+ years’ Navy program experience</li><li>PMP (highly desired) or similar project management related certification<br/><br/></li></ul>The compensation range for this position: $160k - $200k annually.
