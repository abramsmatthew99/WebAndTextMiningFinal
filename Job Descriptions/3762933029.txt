<strong>Position Summary<br/><br/></strong>The Software Web Developer develops web-based software tools in multiple languages to improve the quality of the creation of high-definition maps for autonomous vehicles. They are familiar with full life cycle Software Development Life Cycle – SDLC and Agile development processes in a fast-paced, small team environment. The Web Developer will work in a primarily Open-Source environment.<br/><br/><strong>Main Duties And Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Develop software tools using web technologies by studying requirements, designing the software, developing, testing, and releasing. </li><li>Use Vue JS, JavaScript, and Node JS to develop and maintain software.</li><li>Deliver software for a complete full-stack application – front end and back end.</li><li>Coordinate workflow between backend/frontend development.</li><li>Communicate regularly with other teams and stakeholders within the company.</li><li>Follow the software development lifecycle in an Agile environment to develop source code.</li><li>Estimate the tasks and effort needed to prepare work plans to meet delivery dates.</li><li>Develop Requirements and attend requirements reviews.</li><li>Develop Object Oriented Design and conduct design reviews.</li><li>Develop good, reusable, high-quality code.</li><li>Merge and Integrate code with other developers for delivery.</li><li>Use good debugging skills to test the software tools and release them into production.</li><li>Work with internal and external customers to integrate, test, and launch tools successfully for internal and external stakeholders.</li><li>Regular use of Jira, GIT, Confluence, and MS Visual Studios. </li><li>Develop high-quality software and make all efforts to find and fix defects before the release.</li><li>Work in a team environment.</li><li>Experience in presenting design or software concepts to customers, management, and team members.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Skills, Qualifications, And Competencies<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>2+ years of experience in programming primarily around Vue JS, React JS, and JavaScript for application web development.</li><li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science or software engineering or equivalent experience.</li><li>Strong understanding of object-oriented design and development, Design Patterns, Multi-threading, and Software Development Life Cycle.</li><li>Solid understanding of Microsoft Visual Studio 2017/2019, Bitbucket, Jira, and Git version control.</li><li>Strong understanding of implementing/using REST API’s.</li><li>Knowledge or Experience in JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, C#.</li><li>Knowledge of Open-Source GIS Web technologies (GeoServer, OpenLayers) Preferred.</li><li>Experience with SQL, or PostgreSQL.</li><li>Familiarity with querying spatial relational databases using SQL or PostgreSQL.</li><li>Familiarity with JSON objects, Node JS, and Express JS.</li><li>Ability to think creatively and use your imagination to problem-solve.</li><li>Experience with unit testing.</li><li>Experience in an Agile/Scrum environment.</li><li>Strong written and verbal communication skills.</li><li>Prior experience working on developing enterprise web applications.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Work Environment<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Hybrid work environment, requiring individuals to work on-site a minimum of 3 days per week.</li><li>Works in a fast-paced office environment with multiple priorities and competing demands; potential setbacks in project completion due to internal or external issues, resourcing, and re-allocation of priorities.</li><li>M-Fri 8 – 5 PM ET expected office hours, occasionally requiring additional time.</li><li>May work for long periods reviewing data on a computer.</li><li>Work with other team members, management, and customers.</li><li>Occasional travel may be required.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Assets<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Experience with Automotive or Tools software development.</li><li>Knowledge of GIS, LIDAR, and HD MAPS.</li><li>Experience with AWS cloud development.</li><li>Self-starter, willing to solve hard problems (sometimes outside your domain).</li><li>Results and quality focused.</li><li>Passion to learn and teach/share new concepts and tools.</li><li>Comfortable tackling open-ended problems without requirements.</li><li>Expertise in developing good, clean, reusable code and software tools.</li><li>Experience working with
