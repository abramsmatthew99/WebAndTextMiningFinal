Assurance:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Conduct thorough testing of automation workflows to ensure accuracy, reliability, and performance</li><li>Debug and troubleshoot issues, making necessary Improvement:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Monitor and evaluate the effectiveness of implemented automation solutions</li><li>Identify opportunities for continuous improvement and optimization of existing Qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Certification in process automation tools</li><li>Experience with robotic process automation (RPA) and business process automation (BPA)</li><li>Knowledge of
