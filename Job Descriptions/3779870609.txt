and performance requirements of overall system</li><li>Develop test plans and detailed functional and usability test cases for each deliverable of the software and project implementation services</li><li>Develop and maintain a repository of regression test cases</li><li>Lead initiatives to automate testing and reduce test cycles and overall time to market</li><li>Plan and prioritize test efforts and resolution of defects based on tradeoff between severity of defect and change impact to committed release and delivery dates</li><li>Report test results to management team proactively</li><li>Track defects and verifying resolution</li><li>Work as part of a team which includes in-house software engineers and QA engineers on the day-to-day execution of quality assurance efforts to meet committed delivery dates</li><li>Other duties as assigned<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Requirements<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>A minimum of a bachelor's degree in computer science or related field of study is required. </li><li>Experience writing Selenium scripts in JAVA language</li><li>Demonstrated experience with current software languages, application code design, and writing (Java/Java Script/C++ preferred)</li><li>Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, and Word is required</li><li>Knowledge and experience in testing tools</li><li>Knowledge and experience in engineering problem solving</li><li>Ability to analyze and troubleshoot applications</li><li>Excellent communication skills with the ability to work in a team environment</li><li>Proficiency in Microsoft Excel, PowerPoint, and Word is required<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Benefits<br/><br/></strong>Resource Innovations offers competitive salaries based on candidate's qualifications.
