<p><strong>Location – San Jose, CA</strong></p><p>C Light Technologies is looking for a Software Engineer (I / II) to build the technological backbones for our new product, Retitrack, an ultrahigh-accuracy retinal tracking device.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>About C Light Technologies:</strong></p><p>We are a Berkeley spin-off startup team. Our mission is to deliver a Compact and Accessible AI-driven healthcare solution through the unique combination of ultrahigh quality of eye-brain data and a brain-inspired neuromorphic AI algorithmic system. Our first generation product, Retitrack, is targeted to revolutionize the process of how diseases such as Multiple Sclerosis can be detected as early as possible that can influence millions of people in the US. </p><p>We will be using everything we learn from Retitrack to expand our AI platform across different disease prognostics including Traumatic Brain Injury, Alzheimer and Parkinson Disease. To deliver on our mission, we need to expand our team of highly skilled and motivated team members to help us change healthcare for the better!</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>What We Will Need To See:</strong></p><ul><li>A BS / Master’s degree or above in Computer Science, Electrical Engineering, Computer Engineering, Physics or related fields.</li><li>Demonstrate excellent programming skills in C++ or Python, software design, and debugging skills in a Linux, embedded Linux (ARM) environment.</li><li>Experience in data structures, data base (SQL, Non-structured), and some knowledge of advanced algorithms, deep learning</li><li>Strong hands-on skills on software debugging, Linux dev-box skills (Bash), dev-ops skills (fundamentals of how a computer works)</li><li>Cloud use of Elastic Computing Service, Micro-service, Container Orchestration, Docker etc.</li><li>Occasional travel (&lt;15%) may be required.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>How To Stand Out</strong>:</p><ul><li>Experience with medical device development, and related systems design, system architect</li><li>Experience with robust, safety-critical, efficient code, code style, experience with development under ISO 13485, ISO 14971, IEC 62304 compliance is a plus</li><li>Experience in hands-on ML research and expertise in one or more of the following: computer vision, deep signal processing and fusion, object detection &amp; tracking and image embedding technologies</li><li>Expertise with database technologies (SQL, Non-structured), cloud use such as AWS Elastic Computing Service, Micro-services (Docker, Container Orchestration, etc.)</li><li>Strong collaborative and interpersonal skills</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Who We Are:</strong></p><p>Working for C Light Technologies offers you the opportunity to:</p><ul><li>Work with world-renowned neuroscientists, innovator in the AI entrepreneurial community in Silicon Valley, clinicians. Together we are pushing the limits of artificial intelligence and machine learning in medical space.</li><li>Tackle complex challenges and projects with the highest levels of quality and execution for audiences that include top technologists, the FDA, and world-leading healthcare providers.</li><li>Push the accessibility and quality of healthcare to new heights to improve the lives of millions of people.</li><li>Contribute to a rapidly growing startup that lives by its mission to deliver “Accessible AI Technology”.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>Who You Are:</strong></p><p>This role collaborates with teams across the organization including Hardware Engineering, Product Manufacture, Regulatory, IT, Sales, and Marketing. In this role, your responsibilities would include the following:</p><p>As a member of our software engineering team, you will work on implementing various AI algorithms at a production level that is ready to be integrated into Edge AI computing infrastructures:</p><ul><li>Productionize and optimize medical image perception algorithms, using deep learning, deep neural network based approaches (object detection, multiple-object-tracking, semantic segmentations)</li><li>Implement RNN based predictive models based upon various approaches of time series data, video sequences</li><li>Implement and maintain the development production pipeline on Cloud (AWS ECS) and on Edge AI (NVIDIA Jetson families), CICD pipelines on deploying the build, Docker image to ECS cloud computing and L4T embedded Docker environment</li><li>Consolidate different pieces or components that belong to our full AI stack, gluing between different applications, modules.</li></ul><p>You will collaborate with a diverse team of engineers encompassing the entire Medical Device Development domain. As such, you should have a track record of working well across organizations</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>What We Believe:</strong></p><p>We believe that transforming healthcare solution world will be a defining contribution of our generation to provide broader accessibility, better quality of healthcare service to the people. We have the funding and scale, but we need your help on our team. We value what makes each person unique and create a place for everyone to thrive as we build towards shared goals. We’re committed to continuous learning and are striving to create a principled workplace while continuing to impact the world around us.</p><p><br/></p><p>If this sounds like you, then apply to be our next Software Engineer!</p><p><br/></p><p>C Light Technologies offers competitive salaries with demonstrated experience.
