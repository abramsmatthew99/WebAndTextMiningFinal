<strong>SUMMER 2024<br/><br/></strong>Shure offers a challenging, fun and rewarding summer internship program. The twelve-week program is offered to undergraduate and graduate students. We offer internships with a variety of work arrangements from onsite interns to fully remote in US. Each intern will receive a competitive salary. Additionally, Interns who relocate to Illinois for onsite internships will receive a housing stipend to cover living expenses. Applications will be collected, reviewed, and selected candidates will be contacted in late fall/early winter.<br/><br/>The Front-End Web Development Intern will work with the Global Web Team, under the supervision of our front-end web developers. They’ll assist in the creation and maintenance of shure.com. The selected candidate also will work with back-end developers, designers, content managers, and stakeholders to understand how a fully functioning Global Web Team operates. The selected candidate also may have the opportunity to take the lead on a small project.<br/><br/>This Internship is 100% remote.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> The intern will assist the web team in creating new web pages as well as maintaining existing pages in both legacy and modern applications</li><li> The intern will meet with stakeholders and for multiple projects</li><li> The intern will work within Shure’s documented brand guidelines</li><li> The intern will work with all members of the Global Web Team<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li> In the process of a Bachelor’s degree in computer science OR experience with boot camps, online classes, or self-taught work</li><li> A firm grasp of the front-end fundamentals: JavaScript, CSS, HTML</li><li> Experience with JavaScript frameworks such as React, Vue, or Angular</li><li> Experience with integrating data from APIs into an application.</li><li> Experience with CSS preprocessors such as SCSS is a plus</li><li> Experience with GraphQL is a plus</li><li> Able to follow standard instructions, practices, and procedures</li><li> Self-motivated, team player, detail-oriented</li><li> Able to meet required deadlines</li><li> Ability to work independently with a high degree of accuracy and attention to detail.</li><li> Exposure to the Adobe Creative Suite, Zeplin, Sketch, and other modern web design tools is a plus<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Who We Are.<br/><br/></strong>Shure’s mission is to be the most trusted audio brand worldwide – and for nearly a century, our Core Values have aligned us to be just that. Founded in 1925, we are a leading global manufacturer of audio equipment known for quality, reliability, and durability. We engineer microphones, headphones, wireless audio systems, conferencing systems, and more. And quality doesn’t stop at our products. Our talented teams strive for perfection and innovate every chance they get. We offer an Associate-first culture, flexible work arrangements, and opportunity for all.<br/><br/>Shure Incorporated is headquartered in Niles, Illinois, with remote and hybrid opportunities throughout the United States. We have more than 35 regional sales offices, engineering hubs, and manufacturing facilities throughout the Americas, EMEA, and Asia.<br/><br/><strong>THE MIX MATTERS<br/><br/></strong>Don’t check off every box in the job requirements?
