<p><strong>Summary </strong></p><p>The Design Engineer III is responsible for developing, integrating, and supporting hardware and embedded firmware for new product development and sustaining engineering projects.</p><p><strong>Essential Duties and Responsibilities</strong></p><p>This list of duties and responsibilities is not all inclusive and may be expanded to include other duties and responsibilities as management may deem necessary from time to time.</p><p>Qualifications:</p><ul><li>Must hold a degree in BSEE, Computer Science, or similar</li><li>Must have a proficient knowledge of common electronic circuits</li><li>Must be able to communicate effectively and professionally in person or by other electronic means</li><li>Must possess strong time management skills</li><li>Must be committed to continual growth and improvement</li><li>Must be self-driven</li><li>Preferred: a knowledge of common manufacturing processes, as related to component insertion (placement) on printed circuit boards (PCBs), electronic assemblies and subassemblies and electro-mechanical assemblies and subassemblies</li></ul><p>Responsibilities:</p><p>· General and specialized circuit and system design</p><p>· Analysis of general and specialized components, circuits, signals, and systems</p><p>· Circuit simulation using SPICE modeling</p><p>· Schematic capture and PCB layout using Altium Designer</p><p>· Hardware and system development and integration</p><p>· Hardware troubleshooting and analysis using common electronic test equipment such as digital multimeters, oscilloscopes, and function generators</p><p>· Embedded firmware development, primarily in embedded C/C++ programming languages</p><p>· Embedded firmware debugging and analysis using ICE, JTAG debuggers, and data monitors</p><p>· Calculate voltage, current and power requirements for components, circuits, and systems</p><p>· Use both basic and complex math to solve problems</p><p>· Work closely with other engineering disciplines (mechanical, manufacturing, documentation, software) for the purpose of new and sustaining product development, troubleshooting, and manufacturing</p><p>· Work closely with engineers to select components, parts, and materials</p><p>· Participate in frequent group discussions related to technology and product development</p><p>· Participate in both peer and management-led design reviews</p><p>· Present design strategies and proposals to peers and management in both group and private settings</p><p>· Stay current on market related news, product use cases, and usage criteria as it pertains to new product development</p><p>· Stay current on the latest technical innovations as related to the electrical engineering discipline</p><p>· Perform advanced research, explore &amp; prove out new technologies in the market, microprocessors &amp; microcontrollers, and processes for inclusion in future projects</p><p>· Support pre-compliance testing for EMI/EMC compliance and certification of product at external certified labs</p><p>· Interface with diagnostics and communication systems (CAN/LIN) and Software tools (Kvaser/PCAN/CANalyzer)</p><p>· Aid in the mentoring of newer engineers</p><p>· Be an engineering representative across the organization and with customers</p><p></p>
