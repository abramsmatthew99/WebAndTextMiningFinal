<p>WHAT FLEXWARE DOES </p><p>Flexware Innovation is a leading technology integrator that helps forward thinkers in manufacturing and related industries build comprehensive and long-lasting solutions with ease. </p><p>Founded in 1996 and based in Indiana, our teams of talented engineers leverage technology to solve real business problems with teams of engineers focused on industrial controls, manufacturing systems integration, software development, business intelligence, and Internet of Things (IoT) devices. Our passion is helping our customers build solutions that stand the test of time by creating solid architecture and helping customers avoid costly design mistakes. </p><p>But we’re not just invested in technology – we’re also invested in people. Our internal promise to our Flexdogs is to have a positive and lasting impact on our families by providing a healthy and engaging work environment. Our environment is fun, family-friendly, energetic, and was nominated for TechPoint’s Mira Award for “Company Culture of the Year” in 2017, four Powderkeg awards in 2019, and 5 Powderkeg awards in 2022. </p><p><br/></p><p>WHAT YOU WILL DO</p><p>As a Data Analyst for Flexware Central Team, you will be responsible for building reports in PowerBI for all teams. You will work closely with Flexware’s BI Architect and team to make sure information is accurate and have viewable reports for teams to utilize.</p><p>This position will: </p><ul><li>Maintain, improve, and create Kimble and Salesforce reports and dashboards in PowerBI and Kimble</li><li>Work with and collaborate with Business Intelligence Architect to ensure data is accurate for reports</li><li>Provide support and troubleshoot issues as they arise with reports and dashboards</li><li>Redesign and manage integrations between Kimble, Salesforce, Microsoft Teams, and other systems as needed</li><li>Work with business units to standardize and improve data integrity and metric utilization</li><li>Work with different teams to understand data needs and provide proper reports and dashboards</li><li>Assist with miscellaneous projects associated with data and needs of the business</li><li>Perform other duties and special projects as needed</li></ul><p>Flexware is looking for a candidate that is servant minded and has a willingness to see a problem and find a solution.</p><p><br/></p><p>WHAT YOU MIGHT HAVE DONE BEFORE </p><p>Flexdogs are a rare breed. They come from varied backgrounds, but typically have <em>some</em> of the following traits:</p><ul><li>Received a bachelor’s degree in Informatics, Computer Information Technology, or simliar degree is highly preferred</li><li>2+ years experience in data analytics, data science, or relevant report and dashboard creation</li><li>Experience creating and maintaining reports and dashboards in PowerBI (highly preferred) or other tools such as Tableau, Qlik, etc.</li><li>Worked with Internal Customers to deliver reports</li><li>Experience working with different business units to understand their data needs in order to create reports and dashboards</li><li>Experience with SQL databases, creating complex queries, and working with complex data schemas</li><li>Experience with different systems integrations and understand of how data interacts with different systems</li><li>Exceptional verbal and communication skills</li></ul><p> </p><p>If you’re interested in this opportunity, we’re excited to start a conversation with you! Please reach out to Lindsay Imhoff at lindsay.imhoff@flexwareinnovation.com Your inquiry and conversation will be treated with confidentiality, and we will not share your information with others.</p>
