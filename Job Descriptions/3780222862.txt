<strong>Job Description<br/><br/></strong>Hawai'i Pacific Health is a not-for-profit health care network with over 70 locations statewide including medical centers, clinics, physicians and other caregivers serving Hawai'i and the Pacific Region with high quality, compassionate care. Its four medical centers - Kapi'olani, Pali Momi, Straub and Wilcox - specialize in innovative programs in women’s health, pediatric care, cardiovascular services, cancer care, bone and joint services and more. Hawai'i Pacific Health is recognized nationally for its excellence in patient care and the use of electronic health records to improve quality and patient safety.<br/><br/>Information Technology (IT) implements, supports and maintains all systems at Hawai‘i Pacific Health facilities. The department encompasses the people, records and activities that process the data and information in the organization. The team provides support to users, and acts as the key liaison between the business community and vendors. It also codes, defines criteria, performs testing and debugging, and maintains programs in order to meet the informational needs of Hawai‘i Pacific Health’s numerous facilities and administrative offices.<br/><br/>As the Business System Analyst, you will act as the key liaison between the business community, vendors and the IS Department. You will be responsible for Tier 2 support of applications and translating customers’ high-level requirements into functional specifications for the IS Department and managers changes to such specifications. You will use a variety of platforms to provide automated systems applications to customers. You would be responsible for gathering, compiling and synthesizing information with regard to technology processes and/or systems. In this role, you will code, define criteria and perform testing, debug and document to maintain programs. Lastly, we are looking for someone who will research and maintain knowledge in emerging technologies and possible application to the business.<br/><br/><strong>Location:</strong> Hawaii Pacific Health, Honolulu, HI<br/><br/><strong>Work Schedule:</strong> Day - 8 Hours<br/><br/><strong>Work Type:</strong> Full Time Regular<br/><br/>FTE: 1.000000<br/><br/>Bargaining Unit: Non-Bargaining<br/><br/><strong>Exempt:</strong> Yes<br/><br/><strong>Minimum Qualifications:</strong> Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, Business, Health Care, or related field, and/or an equivalent combination of education, training and experience. Valid driver's license and abstract. Current Hawai’i auto insurance. One (1) experience in functional analysis, system design and/or business applications.<br/><br/><strong>Preferred Qualifications:</strong> Experience with supporting information systems. Experience in analyzing workflow and implementing changes in workflow.<br/><br/>EOE/AA/Disabled/Vets<br/><br/><strong>Position</strong> Business Systems Analyst - IT<br/><br/><strong>Category</strong> Information Technology<br/><br/><strong>Employment Type</strong> Employee<br/><br/><strong>Location</strong> Hawaii Pacific Health, Honolulu, HI<br/><br/><strong>Req ID</strong> 23770
