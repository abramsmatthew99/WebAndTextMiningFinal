Provide technical leadership to an agile team of 5-7. Participate actively in Scrum team following Agile/TDD/BDD approach. Work with Product Line Leaders (PLLs) to understand product requirements and vision. Engage subject matter experts in the successful transfer of complex domain knowledge. Apply principles of SDLC and methodologies like Lean/Agile/XP, CI, Software and Product Security, Scalability, Documentation Practices, refactoring, and Testing Techniques. Write code that meets standards and delivers desired functionality using the technology selected for the project. Understand performance parameters and assess application performance. In the alternative, the position requires a Master’s degree, or foreign equivalent, in Computer Science or in “STEM” majors (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Math) and 2 years of progressively responsible experience. The position also requires 2 years of experience working with technologies such as C#, ASP.NET, REST, SOAP, JavaScript, JQUERY, HTML, and CSS and software design patterns; 2 years of experience working with database technologies such as Oracle and SQL; 2 years of experience with tools such as GIT, TFS, JIRA, Rally, and Salesforce; 2 years of experience working on software projects in the Oil and Gas domain; and 2 years of experience working with Asset Performance Management (APM) modules such as Mechanical Integrity (MI), Asset Strategy Management (ASM), and Safety Instrumented Systems (SIS). 100% fully remote position.
