<strong>We're Aspire, a ServiceTitan Company<br/><br/></strong>As a technical writer at Aspire Software, a ServiceTitan company, you’ll be part of the Product Management team, supporting our efforts to create engaging and compelling content, including new-feature documentation, content updates, release notes, internal reference materials, help deliverables, and other customer-facing material.<br/><br/><strong>Ready to make your career move?<br/><br/></strong>The Product Management team comprises talented product managers, product owners, technical writers, and UX designers to make our products easier to use. Reporting to the Product Operations Lead, you’ll contribute improvements to the content and maintenance of the Aspire User Guide and participate in internal documentation projects as needed.<br/><br/><strong>What You'll Do:<br/><br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Produce with impact: Develop effective documentation that promotes self-service and efficiency for our end-users and internal employees</li><li>Identify and track: Help the team track and identify documentation areas impacted by UI changes and new features</li><li>Audit and optimize: Participate in audits of existing documentation to ensure optimal content structure and accuracy of content</li><li>Write clean and clear copy: Author easy-to-understand, customer-facing guides and help articles, working with product managers, product owners, and subject matter experts to get an in-depth understanding of the product and documentation requirements</li><li>Collaborate and participate: Work with the Product Management team to improve content as we enhance the customer experience through new feature releases and improvements—including release notes creation and supporting docs for all new features and improvements</li><li>Manage and solve for assigned areas: Project manage tasks and needs for assigned areas, including tracking and updating tasks through JIRA, Confluence and similar tools.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>What You'll Bring:<br/><br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in English, Technical Communications, Computer Science, Engineering, or related fields</li><li>Experience in technical writing, professional editing, or grant writing (3+ years preferred)</li><li>Experience with software development/SaaS (2+ years preferred) or educational equivalent</li><li>Excellent grammar and proofreading skills</li><li>Ability to organize and simplify large amounts of data and information and multi-tiered processes</li><li>Ability to coordinate multiple ongoing projects and documentation</li><li>Ability to quickly grasp complex technical concepts and make them easily understandable in text and through images/media</li><li>Demonstrated ability to quickly learn new software and technical skills with little to no guidance<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Preferred skills (if you have one of the following skills, this will be a significant bonus)<br/><br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Experience with Confluence/JIRA and Microsoft Office productivity suite</li><li>Knowledge of Document360<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Portfolio submission<br/><br/></strong>Please submit a writing portfolio (3–5 writing samples) that demonstrates your ability to produce efficient and clearly written customer-facing and/or internal documentation. The portfolio should exhibit your ability to understand and implement content architecture and/or presentation of complex concepts. A diverse writing portfolio that includes online content and data visualization is preferred.<br/><br/>Note: We are unable to sponsor or take over sponsorship of employment Visa at this time. Applicants must have US work authorization.<br/><br/><strong>Where You’ll Work:<br/><br/></strong>Our headquarters is located in St. Louis, however, we’ll be remote until the time is right to return to the office. When that happens, St. Louis-based employees will be moving to a hybrid work model (three days in office with the option to work remotely for the remaining two). Fully remote employees will be able to stay remote.<br/><br/><strong><strong>Be Human With Us:<br/><br/></strong></strong>Being human isn’t about checking every box on a list. It’s about the experiences we have, people we meet, and the perspectives we share. So, if you have the skills but are hesitant to apply because of your background, apply anyway. We need amazing people like you to help us challenge the conventional and think differently about the problems that we’re solving. We’re in this together. Come be human, with us.<br/><br/><strong><strong>What We Offer:<br/><br/></strong></strong>When you join our team, you’re not just accepting a job. You’re making a career move. Here’s how we’ll support you in doing some of the most impactful work of your career:<br/><br/><br/><ul><li>Flextime, recognition, and support for autonomous work: Flexible time off with ample learning and development opportunities to continue growing your career. We offer a comprehensive onboarding program, leadership training for Titans at all levels, and other programs and events. Great work is rewarded through Bonusly, peer-nominated awards, and more. </li><li>Holistic health and wellness benefits: Company-paid medical, dental, and vision (with 100% employer paid options and 90% coverage for dependents), FSA and HSA, 401k match, and telehealth options including memberships to Headspace, Galileo, One Medical, Ginger and more. </li><li>Support for Titans at all stages of life: Parental leave and support, up to $20k in adoption reimbursement, on demand maternity support through Maven Maternity, free breast milk shipping through Maven Milk, pet insurance, legal advisory services, financial planning tools, and more.<br/><br/></li></ul>At ServiceTitan, we celebrate individuality and uniqueness.
