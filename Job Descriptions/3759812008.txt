<p><strong>Knowledge Services is seeking a Service Desk Technician for a long-term contract in South Bend, IN. </strong></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Hybrid work schedule working onsite at least 3-days per week in South Bend, IN and 2 days a week working from home. </strong></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Service Desk Technician Responsibilities:</strong></p><p>• Team member of the service desk to process the service desk tickets throughout the SDLC of assets.</p><p>• Provide technical support for contract- and company-related projects and products, including, but not limited to, providing system access and permissions, resetting passwords, and system administration.</p><p>• Participate in the onboarding of new clients, including the documentation of solutions, procedures, and troubleshooting steps to create knowledge base articles.</p><p>• Assign new user accounts and email addresses in Active Directory/Microsoft 365/ Microsoft Intune/Exchange.</p><p>• Ability &amp; flexibility to provide support for multiple office locations, including onsite visits when necessary.</p><p>• Administer and image laptops, desktops, printers, switches, firewalls, phones, software deployments, security updates and patches.</p><p>• Configure end user devices such as workstations tablet and/or phones for end users.</p><p>• Support technology assignments given from Management that may include Microsoft technology, as well as other specific lines of business applications, system management, and deployment.</p><p>• Work in collaboration with all team members to ensure completion of support requests and tickets.</p><p>• Create detailed documentation of systems configurations and set up. This will include updating as the setup changes or is modified.</p><p>• Responsible for installation and response to antivirus and malware threats detected on systems.</p><p>• Proficiency in Microsoft 365.</p><p>• Proficient time management skills.</p><p>• Excellent attention to detail.</p><p>• Works well in a team environment.</p><p>• Ability to maintain confidential information.</p><p>• Apply security patches, software updates, and deployments as directed by change control process.</p><p>• Work after hours and on weekends to troubleshoot and do required maintenance on technology.</p><p>• Provide exceptional customer service to the clients in a timely manner.</p><p>• Establish effective working relationships with end users and managers.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong><span class="ql-cursor"> </span>Service Desk Technician Requirements:</strong></p><p>• 2-5 years’ experience desired.</p><p>• Bachelor’s degree in computer science or equivalent work experience.</p><p>• Experience with the following technologies: Windows 10, 7, Microsoft 365 Administration, Microsoft Office, Active Directory, Microsoft Intune, Mobile Phone support using MDM, Cisco WebEx, Workstation Imaging Software, Enterprise Virus Protection and Encryption, Network Troubleshooting-VPN, LAN, Wi-Fi, Windows Servers, RSA.</p><p>• Additionally, these skills would be a plus: MacAfee ePolicy Orchestrator, Mass360, Cisco Phone Systems and Voicemail, ServiceNow, Paragon, VMware, Hyper-V, PowerShell, Mac, Adobe CC support.</p><p>• Certifications desired: A+, Security +, and MCSA.</p><p>• Certifications a plus: Network +, ITIL Foundations.</p>
