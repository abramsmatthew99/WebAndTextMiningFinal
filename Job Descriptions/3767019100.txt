<p><strong>Job Title: </strong>Site Reliability Engineer (SRE) - Apple Mac Engineering and Deployment</p><p><strong>Corporate Title:</strong> Assistant Vice President</p><p><strong>Location:</strong> Jacksonville, FL</p><p><strong>Overview</strong></p><p>We are seeking a talented and experienced Site Reliability Engineer (SRE) with a strong background in Apple Mac engineering and deployment. In this role, you will play a critical part in ensuring the reliability, performance, and scalability of our Apple Mac infrastructure. You will collaborate with cross-functional teams to design, implement, and maintain systems that enable seamless deployment and management of Apple Mac devices across our organization.</p><p><strong>What We Offer You </strong></p><ul><li>A diverse and inclusive environment that embraces change, innovation, and collaboration</li><li>A hybrid working model, allowing for in-office / work from home flexibility, generous vacation, personal and volunteer days</li><li>Employee Resource Groups support an inclusive workplace for everyone and promote community engagement</li><li>Competitive compensation packages including health and wellbeing benefits, retirement savings plans, parental leave, and family building benefits</li><li>Educational resources, matching gift, and volunteer programs</li></ul><p><strong>What You’ll Do </strong></p><ul><li>Support the design, implementation, and maintenance of Apple Mac engineering and deployment solutions, ensuring a smooth and efficient user experience</li><li>Collaborate with software engineering teams to integrate Mac-specific requirements into software development and deployment pipelines</li><li>Develop and maintain tools for automating provisioning, configuration, and management of Apple Mac devices at scale</li><li>Troubleshoot and resolve complex technical issues related to Mac hardware, software, and networking, applying a systematic problem-solving approach</li><li>Monitor system performance, conduct performance tuning, and implement proactive measures to ensure optimal uptime and responsiveness</li><li>Participate in incident response, root cause analysis, and continuous improvement initiatives to enhance system reliability and operational efficiency, as well as stay up to date with industry trends and best practices related to Apple Mac engineering, deployment, and SRE methodologies</li></ul><p><strong>Skills You’ll Need </strong></p><ul><li>Proven experience as a Site Reliability Engineer with a focus on Apple Mac engineering and deployment</li><li>Strong expertise in macOS and Apple ecosystem, including device management tools (e.g., Jamf), package management, and security practices</li><li>Proficiency in scripting and programming languages such as Python, Bash, or Swift</li><li>Experience with configuration management tools (e.g., Puppet, Ansible) and version control systems (e.g., Git)</li><li>Familiarity with cloud computing platforms (e.g., AWS, Azure) and containerization technologies (e.g., Docker, Kubernetes)</li></ul><p><strong>Skills That Will Help You Excel</strong></p><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science, Information Technology, or a related field. Relevant experience will be considered</li><li>Excellent problem-solving skills and a proactive approach to preventing and resolving technical challenges</li><li>Strong communication skills, with the ability to collaborate effectively across teams and convey technical information to non-technical stakeholders</li><li>DevOps/ITIL/Agile experience a plus</li></ul><p><strong>Expectations</strong></p><p>It is the Bank’s expectation that employees hired into this role will work in the Jacksonville office in accordance with the Bank’s hybrid working model.</p><p>Deutsche Bank provides reasonable accommodations to candidates and employees with a substantiated need based on disability and/or religion.</p><p>The salary range for this position in Jacksonville is $78,000 to $110,000.
