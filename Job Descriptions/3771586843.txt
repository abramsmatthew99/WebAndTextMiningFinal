Project Statuses</li><li>Maintenance Calendar<br/></li></ul></ul>Qualifications:<br/><br/><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science, information systems, business, or related field and 15+ years to various levels of staff with various levels of understanding of technology. </li><li>Experience with client negotiation and interfacing with senior management. </li><li>Must have proven knowledge of Change and Configuration Management best practices. </li><li>Must have experience leading and collaborating with technical teams of diverse IT related skillsets. </li><li>Must have demonstrated knowledge in infrastructure architecture. </li><li>Must have extensive knowledge of internet protocols, routing, server configuration and management. </li><li>ITIL 4 certification is highly desired.
