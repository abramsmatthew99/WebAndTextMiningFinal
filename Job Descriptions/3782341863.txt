You will be a catalyst for change and help implement systems, reporting and best practices to ensure that Bulbrite can remain an unparalleled disruptive force within the lighting industry. You will report directly to the COO.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>The role is located in NJ and is remote/hybrid. </strong></p><p><strong>You must be able to be in the office a couple days a week.</strong></p><p><br/></p><p><strong><u>Core Requirements and Responsibilities:</u></strong></p><ul><li>Develop, execute, and manage IT projects within the defined scope. Makes recommendations on system and process enhancements plus force behind data initiatives.</li><li>Support the implementation of new solutions or applications with a willingness to achieve.</li><li>Participate in business meetings to provide insight into technical requirements.</li><li>Educate/Train our population on new IT tools, enhancements &amp; reporting on a weekly, monthly basis.</li><li><strong><u>Description of ideal candidate:</u></strong></li><li>BA degree required, Computer Science or Management Information Systems.</li><li>5-10 yrs. plus experience in project management, business systems analysis and software implementation.</li><li>Capable of eliciting high-level requirements from both business and technical stakeholders and translating those requirements into detailed prioritized work.</li><li>Minimum five years of experience in managing data warehouse, reporting &amp; integration teams. Strong Knowledge of report building, creating dashboards, data analysis &amp; business operations. Experience in SAP Business One. (ERP). EDI experience would help.</li><li>Super Strong report building skills.</li><li>Technical Plus- SQL (Sequel) Queries, VBA and Power BI (We have DataRails)</li><li>Strong teammate and able to collaborate with and through others while building positive and effective relationships.</li><li>Able to work with tight timeframes in an evolving, fast paced environment.</li><li>Strong organizational and time management skills with an attention to detail</li><li>Excellent verbal, written and interpersonal communication skills.</li><li>Must be the “Brand Ambassador” for IT. Positive outlook in every project.</li></ul><p><strong><u>Preferred Knowledge:</u></strong></p><ul><li>Project Management Professional (PMP) or Program Management Professional Certification</li><li>Six Sigma green belt or above preferred</li><li>Proficiency in visualization software such as Power BI or Tableau- (We use Data Rails)</li><li>Industrial distribution domain plus Retail &amp; Distribution industry experience is a plus.</li></ul>
