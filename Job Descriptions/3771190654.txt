Here you are supported, here you are celebrated, here you can thrive.<br/><br/><strong>The Job<br/><br/></strong>As a Product Analyst in our Central Product Management team, you will play a crucial role in the success of our AAA titles by leveraging data and insights to inform product decisions. You will work closely with Product Managers and cross-functional teams to analyze player behavior, identify opportunities for improvement, and help shape the direction of our games. If you have a passion for gaming and a strong analytical mindset, we invite you to join our team and contribute to delivering exceptional gaming experiences.<br/><br/>This role can be FULLY REMOTE<br/><br/><strong>The Daily<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Data Analysis: Collect, analyze, and interpret data related to player behavior, in-game metrics, and performance to provide actionable insights.</li><li>Player Insights: Develop a deep understanding of player preferences and behavior through data analysis and player feedback.</li><li>Reporting: Create and maintain regular reports and dashboards to track key performance indicators (KPIs) and provide visibility into game performance.</li><li>A/B Testing: Collaborate with Product Managers to design and execute A/B tests to optimize game features, monetization strategies, and player engagement.</li><li>Competitive Analysis: Stay informed about industry trends, competitor performance, and market dynamics to help inform product decisions.</li><li>Feature Evaluation: Assist in evaluating the impact of new features and changes to the game, providing data-driven recommendations.</li><li>Product Optimization: Work with cross-functional teams to identify areas for product improvement and optimization, such as user interface enhancements and game balance adjustments.</li><li>Monetization Analysis: Analyze in-game monetization strategies, pricing models, and virtual economies to maximize revenue while maintaining player satisfaction.</li><li>Feedback Loop: Act as a bridge between the player community and the development team, conveying player feedback and concerns for consideration.</li><li>Continuous Learning: Stay up-to-date with the latest tools, techniques, and best practices in data analysis and product analytics.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>The Essentials<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in a related field (e.g., statistics, data science, business, computer science) or equivalent experience.</li><li>1-3 years of experience in data analysis, preferably in the gaming industry.</li><li>Strong proficiency in data analysis tools and languages such as Excel, SQL, Python and data visualization tools (e.g., Tableau, Power BI).</li><li>Excellent analytical and problem-solving skills, with a keen attention to detail.</li><li>Ability to translate complex data into actionable insights and recommendations.</li><li>Strong communication skills to convey findings and insights to non-technical stakeholders.</li><li>Passion for gaming and a deep understanding of the gaming market and player behavior.</li><li>Familiarity with A/B testing methodologies and statistical analysis.</li><li>Collaborative mindset and the ability to work effectively in cross-functional teams.</li><li>Eagerness to learn and adapt to a fast-paced, evolving environment<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>How We Get Things Done…<br/><br/></strong>This last bit is probably the most important!
