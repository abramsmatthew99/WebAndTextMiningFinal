As a Data Analyst within the Customer Success team, you will work directly with founders and other members of early stage and high-growth DTC companies to provide key insights about their business. This includes creating custom BI reports, running exploratory data analyses, building models based on client requests, and more. You will also have the unique opportunity to develop our product by improving and building data pipelines, automated predictions, and analytics platform.<br/><br/><em><strong>Outcomes</strong> <br/><br/></em><ul><li>Work with clients to deliver insights and analysis about their business. </li><li>Transform client requests into data deliverables, such as models, data transformations, and interactive reporting. </li><li>Build standard reports for our analytics platform that will be used by every client. </li><li>Actively monitor and improve the data accuracy of the analytics platform. </li><li>Contribute to building the data pipeline that powers our analytics platform and financial model. </li><li>Collaborate with other members of the Customer Success team to share learnings about the client’s business. </li><li>Create new data product features to provide further value and automation for our clients.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong><em>Competencies <br/><br/></em></strong><ul><li>Fantastic SQL skills. </li><li>Strong communication and interpersonal skills to effectively interact with clients and team members in a remote environment.
