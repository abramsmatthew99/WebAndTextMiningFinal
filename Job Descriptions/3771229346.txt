Working within a distributed agile team. You will participate in the entire quality assurance process, which includes standards implementation, requirements review, test plan creation, generating test cases and scripts, executing tests, managing issues, and reporting results. Prior experience developing and implementing automated regression testing suites is required.<br/><br/>The ideal candidate is someone who has a development background with solid knowledge of software quality engineering processes, is self-motivated and organized, and has a strong attention to detail.<br/><br/>Knowledge of the insurance or insurance services industry is desirable, but not required.
