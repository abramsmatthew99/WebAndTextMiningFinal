Biases to monitor and optimize model performance.</li><li>Follow best software development practices to ensure code quality, maintainability, and scalability.</li><li>Stay up-to-date with the latest developments in AI and machine learning, and apply them to real-world projects.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong><em>Qualifications &amp; Requirements:<br/><br/></em></strong><ul><li>A PhD degree or Masters in Computer Science, Information and Engineering Sciences, Applied Mathematics or Applied Sciences with an emphasis on machine learning and deep learning</li><li>Preferable experience of 1-3 year(s) for Ph.D or 3-5 years for MS with a strong record of accomplishments and project experience in applications of AI/ML</li><li>Expertise in Deep Learning architectures including CNNs, RNNs, GNN’s, Embeddings, Transfer Learning, Attention-based Networks, Transformers.</li><li>Familiarity with experiment tracking tools such as MLFlow or WandB</li><li>Solid understanding of software engineering practices, including code review and testing.</li><li>Proficient with GitHub for version control and collaborative development.</li><li>Proficient with Python development</li><li>Strong knowledge of CI/CD principles and experience with setting up pipelines</li><li>Expertise in at least one deep learning libraries such as PyTorch and TensorFlow</li><li>The ability to work with APIs and multi-GPU machines on the cloud</li><li>A change agent with a combination of business, science &amp;
