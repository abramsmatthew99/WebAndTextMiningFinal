systems.<br/><br/>This is a hybrid position.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Provide technical support to end-users via phone, email, or in-person</li><li>Troubleshoot operations</li><li>Administer user accounts, permissions, and access rights using JumpCloud</li><li>Manage Meraki wireless and security appliances, including access points, switches, and security cameras</li><li>Ensure compliance with IT security policies and procedures</li><li>Document IT processes and procedures<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Requirements<br/><br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, or at least 3 years experience in a service desk management systems</li><li>Extensive knowledge of Microsoft Windows, MacOS and Google Workspace essential</li><li>Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail</li><li>Jira service desk or equivalent experience</li><li>Slack experience</li><li>Ability to work independently and as part of a team</li><li>Strong communication skills, both verbal and written<br/><br/><br/></li></ul>Desirable knowledge in Crowdstrike, Confluence, asset control, TeamViewer remote control.<br/><br/>The base annual salary range for this position is $62,000 - $72,000.<br/><br/><strong>Benefits<br/><br/></strong>Alongside base salary we offer a range of benefits including:<br/><br/><br/><ul><li> Health insurance and an Employee Assistance Programme </li><li> 401k </li><li> Annual Compensation Reviews </li><li> 3 paid volunteer days per year </li><li> Free monthly LetsGetChecked tests as we are not only focused on the well being of our patients
