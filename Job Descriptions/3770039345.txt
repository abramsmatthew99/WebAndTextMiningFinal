<strong>Job Description<br/><br/></strong>We are searching for a Business Systems Analyst I – someone who works well in a fast-paced setting. In this position, you will develop clinical/business solutions by investigating processes to understand data and communications flow with individual tasks and transactions and to manage the ongoing maintenance of applications. This role will investigate processes to meet end-user needs within Texas Children's. Additionally, provide financial systems support, coordinate system upgrades, and provide other system support for Texas Children's.
