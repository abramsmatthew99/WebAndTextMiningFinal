<p><strong><em>CTI is Building A Better Future For Our Customers, Our People and Our Communities!</em></strong></p><p><br/></p><p><strong>SUMMARY:</strong></p><p><em>Concrete Technologies, Inc. (CTI), an established and growing concrete construction company in the Des Moines area, has an immediate opening for an IT Specialist. This role will be responsible for maintaining reliability and functionality of in-house computer hardware and software, all virtual technology for our remote job sites and RDP systems, and mobile device setups and distribution.</em></p><p><strong><em> </em></strong></p><p><strong>ESSENTIAL DUTIES AND RESPONSIBILITIES:</strong></p><ul><li>Oversees installation, configuration, maintenance and troubleshooting of end user workstation hardware, software, and peripheral devices.</li><li>Provides training to staff on various technical subject including Vista by Viewpoint, Microsoft Office and Adobe applications, and Windows operating systems.</li><li>Stays abreast of emerging technology to support future company needs. Validates new technologies proposed for use.</li><li>Schedules and performs all system equipment, hardware and software upgrades including security patches, operating system updates, application updates, and performance tuning on servers and end user devices.</li><li>Responsible for network security, including user accounts, permissions and access rights, firewalls, anti-virus, and intrusion detection systems.</li><li>Maintains up-to-date knowledge of applicable network and software installations.</li><li>Maintains a database/library of all supporting documentation for hardware and software.</li><li>Ensures IT project timelines and budgets are met.</li><li>Monitoring and on-call response for critical IT service disruptions</li><li>Manage Microsoft 365 environment including user and group management, Exchange online and cloud security applications.</li></ul><p> </p><p><strong>QUALIFICATIONS:</strong></p><ul><li>Associates or Bachelors in Information Technology, Computer Science or related field is preferred.</li><li>Proficiency with Microsoft Office and Windows operating systems.</li><li>Experience with Microsoft Active Directory, Group Policy, DHCP, DNS, and other domain services.</li><li>Attention to detail and ability to manage multiple projects in a timely manner.</li><li>Knowledge of construction processes and practices preferred.</li><li>Strong problem-solving and troubleshooting skills.</li></ul><p> </p><p><strong>About Us:</strong></p><p>In 2001, Concrete Technologies, Inc. ("CTI") opened for business with four employees and a focus on commercial foundations, floors, and parking lots. Since then, CTI has grown to over 250 employees and added mainline paving to its service line. Initially focused on Central Iowa, CTI has successfully completed projects throughout Iowa and the Midwest. Together with CTI Ready Mix and CTI Recycling, the CTI companies bring the resources to provide a diverse range of concrete construction services and ready mix designs.</p><p><br/></p><p><em>Concrete Technologies, Inc. provides equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants for employment and prohibits discrimination and harassment of any type without regard to race, color, religion, age, sex, national origin, disability status, genetics, protected veteran status, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, or any other characteristic protected by federal, state or local laws.
