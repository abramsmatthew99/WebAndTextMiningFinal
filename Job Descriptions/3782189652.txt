<p>The Lowcountry Center for Veterans Research (LCVR) is a forward-thinking non-profit organization dedicated improving the overall health and well-being of veterans through discovery and innovation of ground-breaking medical treatments and procedures. </p><p> </p><p>LCVR is searching for a Research Assistant to join our growing team of research professionals. Working closely with VA researchers and other LCVR staff members, you will assist with multiple research trials sponsored by federal agencies and private industries at the Ralph H. Johnson VA Health Care System’s Outpatient Clinic in Savannah, GA.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>Duties &amp; Responsibilities: </strong></p><ul><li> Research Operations: Assist with recruitment strategies; screen potential participants for study participation; obtain informed consent from study participants; file study documents per protocol; communicate with participants about study expectations and upcoming appointments; coordinate scheduling of clinical assessments</li><li>Site &amp; Study Coordination: Provide support to research staff; inventory office resource and supplies; procure additional materials as needed</li><li>Data &amp; Informatics: Enter data independently; detect issues related to data capture, collection, or management and suggest solutions</li><li>Communication: Participate in team meetings; take an active role in the decision-making process; trouble-shoot issues and communicate with other team members accordingly</li><li>Participant Safety &amp; Reporting: Collect adverse event information; under direction, employ safeguards for human subject protection</li><li>Leadership &amp; Professionalism: Liaise with mental health and medical professionals; provide education regarding study activities to patients and VAMC staff</li><li>Other: Complete additional tasks as requested by study investigators</li></ul><p> </p><p><strong>Supervision: </strong></p><ul><li>Must be able to work independently </li><li>Reports directly to Principal Investigator</li></ul><p> </p><p> <strong>Qualifications and Hiring Criteria: </strong></p><ul><li>Authorization to work in the US to apply for this job and are subject to a background/suitability investigation</li><li>Knowledge of MS Office Products (Excel, Word, Power Point, Access and Outlook) is required</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>Please send resumes and cover letters to hradmin@lcvresearch.org.</p>
