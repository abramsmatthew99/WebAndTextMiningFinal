teardown.</li><li>Work closely with unit CFSRs and S6 staff as well as other personnel to coordinate activities.</li><li>Positions may be subject to work an uncommon hours, including nights, weekends, and holidays to meet mission requirements.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Required Qualifications &amp; Experience<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Active Secret Clearance</li><li>Applicants must possess a Tactical Network equipment.</li><li>Knowledge of Systems Engineering processes and methodologies to include military network and communications systems engineering.</li><li>Knowledge of Tactical Network systems, to include REDCOM, Cisco routers, switches, firewalls, CallManager, PKI, and IP voice networks, is required.</li><li>Occasional travel - Travel requirements will services.</li><li>Specialized experience gained performing work as an engineer in industry or as an active, reserve or National Guard unit is desired.</li><li>Experienced in PdM Network Modernization
