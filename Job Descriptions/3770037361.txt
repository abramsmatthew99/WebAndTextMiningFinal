role, you will delve into extensive datasets to uncover insights and support executive reporting, business performance measurement, and customer segmentation to drive cross-functional strategy for problems</li><li>Support standard reporting needs, including dashboard development, to provide visibility into the health of the Peacock business</li><li>Lead ad hoc analyses to answer critical time-sensitive business questions, identifying areas of strategic opportunity for the business</li><li>Inform decision-making by visually and verbally synthesizing data into easy-to-consume storylines tools)</li><li>Strategic thinker with the ability to derive conclusions and recommendations from raw data</li><li>Self-starter with strong attention to detail, sense of curiosity, accountability, and ability to execute projects of all sizes with vision and purpose</li><li>Ability to deliver on multiple projects and meet tight deadlines</li><li>Team oriented and collaborative with an aptitude and
