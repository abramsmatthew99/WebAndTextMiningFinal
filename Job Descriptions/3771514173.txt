database management and monitoring tools</li><li>Optimize hardware and software configuration</li><li>Over time, assist developers/engineers in database and application tuning for optimal performance continuous integration/development<br/><br/></li></ul>To be successful in this role, we ask that you have:<br/><br/><ul><li>Minimum 5 years general developer/engineer experience, some DBA experience a
