Our CompanyChanging the world through digital experiences is what Adobe’s all about. We give everyone—from emerging artists to global brands—everything they need to design and deliver exceptional digital experiences! We’re passionate about empowering people to create beautiful and powerful images, videos, and apps, and transform how companies interact with customers across every screen.We’re on a mission to hire the very best and are committed to creating exceptional employee experiences where everyone is respected and has access to equal opportunity. We realize that new ideas can come from everywhere in the organization, and we know the next big idea could be yours!The Opportunity Adobe applications help you build, communicate, collaborate, and experience digital content wherever you are. Adobe’s Creative Cloud Engineering team is looking for a full-stack web developer to join the team in the San Jose or Seattle office. You will write sophisticated frontend and backend applications using world-class technologies that bring the creative Adobe experience to millions of users, anywhere and anytime!We are looking for someone with a strong computer science background and experience to develop the next generations of creative cloud applications. If you are interested in joining an outstanding team of hardworking people and industry veterans who love to build elegant products, we wish to hear from you!What you'll Do Work closely with our product and design teams to build the Home experience for Adobe.com serving millions of Adobe customers daily. Collaborate with a diverse and distributed team to implement new features and improvements based on business requirements. Stay updated and implement the best-in-class full-stack development methodologies and standards. Participate in and drive software architecture, design, and development. Maintain focus on performance, user experience, UI design principles, and making applications work intuitively. What you need to succeed Experience with several of the following: React, Redux, ES6, JavaScript, CSS3, SASS, HTML5, RESTful API, Node.js, GraphQL, and other web technologies. Experience with various JavaScript libraries and responsive web design. Familiarity with development tools such as Jenkins, GitHub, JS debugger, lint, Webpack, NPM/Yarn, etc. Familiarity with database technologies such as Amazon DynamoDB, MongoDB, SQL, etc. Familiarity with AWS, or other Content Delivery Network (CDN). Experience in understanding large and complex code bases, including API design techniques to help keep them clean and maintainable. Comfortable with Agile and Lean development processes. Experience in working with offshore teams. Love to learn new things, work hard, and have fun. Good communication and interpersonal skills. Minimum of 4+ years of experience in software development. B.S. or M.S. preferably in Computer Science or a related field or equivalent work experience. Our compensation reflects the cost of labor across several  U.S. geographic markets, and we pay differently based on those defined markets. The U.S. pay range for this position is $119,000 -- $232,700 annually. Pay within this range varies by work location and may also depend on job-related knowledge, skills, and experience.
