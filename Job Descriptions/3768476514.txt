<strong>Position Summary<br/><br/></strong>JetBlue is looking for an Analyst Airline Optimization to be based at JetBlue’s Support Center in Long Island City, NY. The Airline Optimization team is responsible for understanding and quantifying the effects of the Network on the Operation, and vice versa. The Airline Optimization team will then support planning decisions across the company to maximize the airline’s end-to-end profitability and operational robustness.<br/><br/>The team’s short-term responsibilities include helping to optimize schedules and operational targets and working across teams to prepare the schedule for deployment. Longer-term responsibilities focus on helping closely align JetBlue’s network plan with its operational footprint.<br/><br/>The ideal Analyst will have excellent quantitative and interpersonal skills, helping them to excel at working across teams to measure and balance planning requirements. Analytical and modeling experience are required.<br/><br/><strong>Essential Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Identify hidden inefficiencies in Network or operational plans </li><li>Work closely with Route &amp; Schedule Planning Crewmembers to build schedule scenarios, assess the revenue/strategic impact of decisions, and execute Network changes as needed </li><li>Use planning scenarios to measure the cost impact of schedule changes </li><li>Use tools to forecast operability outcomes and disruption risk </li><li>Build relationships with Operational teams to help align short- and long-term planning decisions </li><li>Manage metrics and reporting, including regularly updating decision support dashboards </li><li>Other duties as assigned <br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Minimum Experience And Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s Degree in Business, Economics, Mathematics, Finance, Engineering, Computer Science or analytical or related experience achieved via school or work experience </li><li>Strong capabilities with database querying (e.g., SQL) and/or high-level programming language(s) (such as Python or R) </li><li>Strong airline business acumen </li><li>Excellent data analysis and problem-solving skills </li><li>Strong written and verbal communication ability </li><li>Ability to work independently and
