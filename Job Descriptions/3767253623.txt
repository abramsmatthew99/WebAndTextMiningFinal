        Our team is responsible for software on the NICs in the fleet, Linux Kernel Networking and transport protocols associated with moving AI workloads across the Fabric. We develop our own products as well as work with vendor devices. We focus on reliability, performance and serviceability of transport functions associated with host traffic in the fleet.The AI Transport group within Host Networking is responsible for design and development of software that transports AI workloads between compute nodes within the DC. This also includes data path software on embedded systems, control software on Linux stack and additionally addressing congestion and typical network behavior in a highly scaled DC environment.
