<strong>Company Description<br/><br/></strong>“We’re not in the shipping business; we’re in the information business” -Peter Rose, Expeditors Founder<br/><br/>Global supply chain management is what we do, but at the heart of Expeditors you will find professionalism, leadership, and a friendly environment, all of which foster an innovative, customer service-based approach to logistics.<br/><br/><ul><li>18,000 trained professionals</li><li>350+ locations worldwide across 60+ countries</li><li>Fortune 500</li><li>Globally unified systems<br/><br/></li></ul>Job Description<br/><br/>Global logistics is complex. Accurate and timely accounting of services we purchase or provide is essential to our company's success. Expeditors' ongoing growth and success relies on providing exceptional customer service and reducing financial risk. We are looking for strong and driven candidates who want to help design and develop highly available, responsive, secure, and robust systems supporting our accounting and finance business functions. You will be part of an Agile Team to ensure our systems support our users, and ultimately shareholders by creating accounting transactions the right way, the first time. You will have a direct role in driving a shared understanding of business goals, objectives between Business and IT in delivering quality systems that meet business needs, with a reputation of on-time delivery and with a commitment to complete and accurate financial data. By employing a variety of business, process, data, and system analysis techniques, the Business Analyst aligns stakeholders, reduces ambiguity and risk, and defines solutions that meet defined goals and objectives. The successful Business Analyst in Accounting &amp; Data Governance group is passionate about solving complex business problems, aligning stakeholders to solutions, and modeling data and processes.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Collaborate with Product Manager(s) in building and prioritizing team backlog. </li><li>Take ownership of defining and driving the analysis process while proactively collaborate with the team. </li><li>Elicit and analyze functional and non-functional requirements. Craft meaningful requirements and specifications that align stakeholder needs to system capabilities. Clearly define user stories. </li><li>Lead requirements, specification reviews and backlog grooming sessions to achieve shared understanding. </li><li>Collaborate to design high-value and sensible IS solutions, making sure both functional and non-functional requirements are addressed in the design and the testing early on. </li><li>Assist the team in achieving team goals, e.g., assisting with testing activities when needed. </li><li>Proactively and continuously improve business and system knowledge and improve processes. <br/><br/></li></ul><em>This position is on-site and will reside in the greater Seattle, WA. area at one of the following locations depending on availability. Candidates must be local or willing to relocate. Relocation assistance is not offered for this position.<br/><br/></em><ul><li>Downtown Seattle</li><li>Federal Way</li><li>Lynnwood</li><li>Bellevue </li><li>Broad knowledge of database structures, data relationships, and data modeling. </li><li>Experience working with Reference Data, Data Governance products. </li><li>Effective at managing
