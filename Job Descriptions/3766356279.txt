EAM is core component of our Aftermarket Services Business, a key growth enabler for Honeywell. This role requires strategic planning, technical oversight, and driving results with leaders across the organization, including stakeholders in technology and the businesses. Ensure that changes are thoroughly tested and implemented smoothly.</li><li>Supplier Management: Diligent management of third-parties – platform and system integrators to ensure performance and delivery and value extraction.</li><li>Budget Management: Manage the costs for the Enterprise Asset Management program, including forecasting, expense tracking, and cost optimization.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>YOU MUST HAVE <br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field</li><li>Minimum 15 years of progressive IT, relevant IT/ business experience</li><li>Minimum 7 years of experience engaging executives and other stakeholders to support important business decisions</li><li>Previous experience leading large cross-functional, cross-service programs.</li><li>Proven experience leading/ deploying Enterprise Asset / Service Management solutions and relevant software tools, specifically with the Service Max platform (Asset 360 preferred)</li><li>Strong understanding of asset management principles and best practices.</li><li>Effective at building and leveraging cross-organizational networks.</li><li>Excellent leadership and project management skills.</li><li>Strong communication and collaboration abilities.</li><li>Ability to work effectively with cross-functional teams and stakeholders.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>WE VALUE<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Minimum 3 years of working in or with lean and agile software delivery teams (preferred)</li><li>Strong communication/ active listening skills – both written and verbal</li><li>Ability to influence others to move toward a common vision or goal</li><li>Flexible and adaptable;
