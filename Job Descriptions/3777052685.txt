<strong>Job Description<br/><br/></strong>Design, develop, troubleshoot and debug software programs for applications, tools, networks, databases, etc. As a member of the OCI software engineering division, you will perform professional software development tasks associated with the designing, developing and debugging of software applications or operating systems. BS or MS degree or equivalent experience relevant to the functional area. 4 or more years of software engineering or related experience.<br/><br/><strong>Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong>Oracle’s Cloud Infrastructure (OCI) team is building new Infrastructure-as-a-Service technologies that operate at high scale in a broadly distributed multi-tenant cloud environment. Our customers run their businesses on our cloud, and our mission is to provide them with best-in-class compute, storage, networking, database, security, and an ever-expanding set of foundational cloud-based services.<br/><br/>The Support organization in OCI is building new services that operate at high scale in a broadly distributed multi-tenant cloud environment. We are addressing complex, large scale and connected event platforms that manifests as the following services – Oracle Support Center and Oracle Support Digital Assistant. These services are cornerstones of the Customer experiences offered by OCI and offer a wide variety of technical problems to be solved and customer focused innovations to be created.<br/><br/>In the OCI Support team we are focused on building a scalable platform for ingestion of time series data that can be leveraged to build health and performance monitoring experiences. We are expanding our capabilities to support complex rule-based and machine learning based alerting and interactive bots, long term forecasting and data analytics for cloud environments. The team is focused on enhancing these core capabilities and improving customer experience.<br/><br/>We’re looking for hands-on engineers with expertise and passion in solving difficult problems in distributed systems and highly available services. If this is you, at Oracle you can design and build innovative new systems from the ground up. These are exciting times in our space - we are growing fast, still at an early stage, and working on ambitious new initiatives. An engineer at any level can have significant technical and business impact.<br/><br/>As a Software Engineer, you will own the software design and development for major components of Oracle’s Cloud Infrastructure. You should be both a rock solid coder and a distributed systems generalist, able to dive into any part of the stack, as well as design broad distributed system interactions. You should value simplicity and scale, work comfortably in a collaborative, agile environment, and be excited to learn.<br/><br/><strong>About You<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>You work backward, starting from the user. You care about creating usable, useful software that solves real problems and brings delight to users.</li><li>You have solid communication skills. You can clearly explain complex technical concepts.</li><li>You work well with non-engineers. You can lead a conversation in a room with designers, engineers, and product managers.</li><li>You are comfortable with ambiguity. You have a strong sense of ownership, and are able to drive development of new projects and features to completion.</li><li>You are comfortable working at all levels of the stack.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Minimum Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>BS in Computer Science, or equivalent experience</li><li>4+ years of object oriented programming (Java/C++)</li><li>Knowledge of data structures, algorithms, operating systems, and distributed systems fundamentals.
