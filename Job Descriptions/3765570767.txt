We went from selling everyone’s favorite Crunchy Tacos on the West Coast to a global brand with 8,200+ restaurants, 350 franchise organizations, that serve 42+ million fans each week around the globe. We’re not only the largest Mexican-inspired quick service brand (QSR) in the world, we’re also part of the biggest restaurant group in the world: Yum! Brands.<br/><br/>Much of our fan love and authentic connection with our communities are rooted in being rebels <em>with a</em> cause. You will also collaborate with technology leaders, functional partners, franchisees, vendors, and across the Yum ecosystem to deliver the best customer service to the entire system.<br/><br/>You are an innovative, intellectually curious problem solver who can quickly drive the conversation toward a productive solution by including the right actions and accountability. Automate as much as possible and leverage technology to improve the service desk capabilities and processes. </li><li>Lead the team to enhance and build upon existing standard operating procedures and best practices for technical support. Ensure that the team follows the appropriate escalation paths and communication protocols for resolving issues. </li><li>Manage the team’s performance, growth, and development.
