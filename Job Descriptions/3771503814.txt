<strong>Job Description<br/><br/></strong>Responsible for managing day-to-day operation and direct supervision of all Service Desk personnel. Ensure that contract requirements and contract SLAs and performance criteria are met. Supports continual development of the Service Desk function, to include promoting a service-oriented culture and maintaining Service Desk SOPs. Supports interactions with other teams and manages queues. Creates daily and weekly reports. Monitors incidents and requests, and possible causes for increases or deviations. Develops recommendations for process improvements. Facilitates incident escalations within the team and follow-ups as needed. Provides resource capacity management to ensure sufficient personnel are available to meet defined service levels.<br/><br/>Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:<br/><br/><ul><li>Manage Service Desk personnel (approximately 20 personnel) in the performance of daily responsibilities. </li><li>Promote a service-oriented culture within the Service Desk. </li><li>Maintain the SOPs used within the Service Desk. </li><li>Managing Service Desk ticket workflow. </li><li>Coordinate changes with incident and problem management processes. </li><li>Define and implement new or improved operating practices. </li><li>Provide resource capacity management to ensure sufficient personnel are available to meet defined service levels. </li><li>Addressing areas where Service Desk service level targets have not been met. </li><li>Delivering management level reporting. </li><li>Managing the Service Desk incident process, including queue management, problem management, ticket life cycle management, ticket closure, root cause, trending, knowledge management. </li><li>Ensuring efficient flow of tickets through Tiers 1 - 3. </li><li>Producing management information, including Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and reports, and performing trend analysis. </li><li>Tracking incidents escalated by the Service Desk to other queue owners and monitoring for closure / coordinate activities between multiple support groups to ensure Service Desk incidents are resolved. <br/><br/></li></ul>Qualifications:<br/><br/><ul><li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science, information systems, business, or related field and 7+ years management experience in the planning, directing, and managing Service Desks of similar size and scope. </li><li>Demonstrated excellence in performing Incident and Problem Management for organization of similar size and scope. </li><li>Comprehensive knowledge of ServiceNow or similar ITSM software, including the ability to create ad hoc reports, manage work/ticket queues, and workflow automation. </li><li>Demonstrated experience developing and utilizing ACD reports to provide metrics reporting to improve the Service Desk operations and support. </li><li>Strong interpersonal and time management skills and must have the ability to adapt and prioritize work in a dynamic environment. </li><li>Ability to effectively communicate verbally and electronically to various levels of staff with various levels of understanding of technology. </li><li>Must have a positive and patient customer service attitude. </li><li>Must be able to lead, inspire, and mentor a multi-disciplined team of both on-site and remote staff. </li><li>HDI Support Center Manager certification is desired.
