<p>OMNIA Partners has become the largest and most experienced purchasing organization for public and private sector markets by delivering unparalleled scale and solutions. Through further organic growth and strategic acquisitions, OMNIA Partners will continue to drive economies of scale to execute more contracts, in more verticals, with transparent, value-driven pricing for our membership of companies. Our success and growth have been unparalleled in this space.</p><p><br/></p><p>At the core of our success is a high energy, talented staff with diverse skills and a unified focus on delivering value and an emphasis on creativity, strategic thinking, and execution.</p><p><br/></p><p>We are currently seeking an IT Infrastructure Engineer to join our onsite IT Team in Franklin, TN. As a member of our IT Operations team, you will provide a wide variety of technical solutions aiding in the development and maintenance of a stable and mature computing environment. Additionally, you will have the opportunity to engage in security efforts to ensure IT Infrastructure is operating according to security best practices. The ideal candidate will be detail-oriented and highly motivated, with the ability to adapt and be comfortable in an ambiguous environment. Strong communication and time management skills and the ability to problem solve and develop strong partnerships are essential.</p><p><br/></p><p>This position is located fully onsite in Franklin, TN and remote applicants will not be considered.</p><p><br/></p><p><strong>KEY RESPONSIBILITIES</strong></p><ul><li>Provide Tier 3 IT support relating to technical infrastructure issues involving Microsoft’s core business applications, operating systems, and cloud infrastructure.</li><li>Create and maintain infrastructure-related documentation within the company's document management platform.</li><li>Monitor and support Local and Cloud Backups.</li><li>Monitor and Manage Microsoft and third-party patch management technologies and processes.</li><li>Management of Azure and Microsoft 365 Core Infrastructure including Azure Network &amp; Compute, Defender Suite, MCAS, Microsoft Endpoint Management (Intune), Exchange Online, SharePoint Online, Microsoft Teams Voice, One Drive for Business, Hybrid Azure AD, etc.</li><li>Management of on-premises infrastructure including Hyper-converged network &amp; compute.</li><li>Advanced technical support at the network level: WAN and LAN connectivity, routers, firewalls, Wi-Fi, and security.</li><li>Manage advanced remote access solution support: IPSEC &amp; SSL VPN, Remote Desktop Services, etc.</li><li>Monitor, track, and manage Network Monitoring and RMM system alerts and notifications including appropriate triage and resolution methodologies and documentation.</li><li>Communication with users/clients as required: keeping them informed of maintenance, incident progress, notifying them of impending changes, and agreed outages.</li><li>Track all incidents and support request resolution through enterprise ticketing systems.</li><li>Audit, and review vulnerability &amp; security logs and alerts.</li><li>Alert, Review &amp; Respond to security incidents.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>ADDITIONAL DUTIES AND RESPONSABILITIES</strong></p><ul><li>Resolute dedication to unparalleled customer service.</li><li>Ability to work in a team and communicate effectively.</li><li>Ensure requests are routed to the proper resource in order to be resolved quickly and efficiently.</li><li>Escalate service requests appropriately.</li><li>Vendor management.</li><li>Participate in On-Call rotation providing emergency after hours support.</li><li>Participate in Risk &amp; Change Management efforts and meetings.</li><li>Continuous education, expansion, and refinement of technical knowledge and credibility through industry standard certifications.</li><li>Act as a technical resource for support desk training and mentorship.</li><li>Interpret and discuss information with others and regularly provide advice and recommend actions involving complex issues.</li><li>Demonstrate professional attitude with client over the phone, on-site and through emails to resolve all client requests/questions.</li><li>Additional duties and responsibilities as required.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS</strong></p><ul><li>7+ years of progressive experience in the IT industry</li><li>5+ years of experience managing Azure environments</li><li>Cloud &amp; Local Virtualization Technologies including Azure, VMWare, and Hyper-V</li><li>Familiar with Security Technologies including Conditional Access, EDR, MDR/XDR, Managed SOC, etc.</li><li>Microsoft 365 Administration &amp; Licensing</li><li>Windows Server Administration</li><li>Ability to work independently and a strong desire for personal and professional development</li><li>Enterprise Network Technologies including Firewall, Route, Switch, Wireless, SD-WAN, etc.</li><li>Experience with SharePoint, Power Automate and/or PowerApps is a plus</li><li>Familiarity with ITIL processes</li><li>Ability to identify and evaluate trends in industry best practice policies</li><li>Excellent written and verbal communication skills</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>EDUCATION</strong></p><ul><li>Associate or bachelor’s degree in computer science or MIS from a reputable institute or 7 years of IT or related experience.</li></ul><p><br/></p><p><strong>PROFESSIONAL CERTIFICATIONS</strong></p><ul><li>Microsoft Server and/or Azure Certifications Desired</li><li>Network+ Desired</li><li>Security+ Desired</li></ul><p><br/></p><p>OMNIA Partners provides equal employment opportunities to all employees and applicants for employment and prohibits discrimination and harassment of any type without regard to race, color, religion, age, sex, national origin, disability status, genetics, protected veteran status, sexual orientation, gender identity or expression, or any other characteristic protected by federal, state or local laws.</p><p><br/></p><p>This policy applies to all terms and conditions of employment, including recruiting, hiring, placement, promotion, termination, layoff, recall, transfer, leaves of absence, compensation and training.</p>
