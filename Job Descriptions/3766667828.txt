        **Job Description: **<br/><br/><strong>Position Summary</strong>:<br/><br/>As a Network System Engineer, you will play a crucial role in the design, implementation, maintenance, and optimization of an organization's network infrastructure. Your primary responsibility will be to ensure the efficient and secure operation of the network, including local area networks (LANs), wide area networks (WANs), and other related systems. You will work closely with the IT Manager, IT Team and other stakeholders to ensure the network meets the organization's technical requirements and business objectives.<br/><br/>**Key Responsibilities: **<br/><br/><ul><li>** Network Design and Planning: **</li><li>Collaborate with stakeholders to assess network requirements and design network solutions that meet business needs. </li><li> Plan and implement network architecture, including switches, routers, firewalls, and other networking devices.</li><li> Evaluate and recommend network technologies to enhance performance and security.</li><li>** Network Implementation and Configuration: **</li><li>Deploy and configure network equipment, ensuring it operates efficiently and securely. - Install, maintain, and troubleshoot network hardware and software. </li><li> Implement security measures to safeguard network infrastructure from cyber threats and vulnerabilities.</li><li>** Network Monitoring and Maintenance: **</li><li>Monitor network performance and proactively address issues to ensure optimal uptime. </li><li> Conduct routine network maintenance and updates to keep the infrastructure current and secure.</li><li> Maintain network documentation, diagrams, and configuration files.</li><li>** Troubleshooting and Issue Resolution: **</li><li>Identify and resolve network-related problems, such as connectivity issues and performance degradation. </li><li> Respond to network outages and incidents promptly, minimizing downtime. - Investigate security breaches or vulnerabilities and implement corrective measures.</li><li>** Capacity Planning and Scalability: **</li><li>Analyze network traffic patterns and capacity requirements, and plan for future growth. </li><li> Recommend and implement scalability solutions to accommodate increased network demand.</li><li>** Collaboration and Teamwork: **</li><li>Collaborate with other IT teams and departments to align network solutions with organizational goals. </li><li> Work with vendors and third-party service providers for procurement and support.</li><li>** Documentation and Reporting: **</li><li>Maintain detailed records of network configurations, changes, and incidents. - Prepare reports on network performance, security, and recommendations for improvements. <br/><br/><br/></li></ul>**Qualifications: **<br/><br/><ul><li>Bachelor's degree in computer science, information technology, or a related field (or equivalent work experience). </li><li>Professional Information Certifications, or equivalent. </li><li>Proven experience in network design, implementation, and maintenance. </li><li>Strong knowledge of networking protocols and technologies, including TCP/IP, VLANs, VPNs, and others. </li><li>Familiarity with network security best practices and technologies. </li><li>Proficiency in network monitoring and troubleshooting tools. </li><li>Excellent problem-solving and communication skills. </li><li>Ability to work both independently and as part of a team. </li><li>Strong attention to detail and a commitment to maintaining network documentation.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul>The Network System Engineer plays a vital role in ensuring that an organization's network infrastructure is robust, secure, and capable of supporting the business's needs. This role requires a combination of technical expertise, problem-solving skills, and effective communication to maintain the integrity and functionality of the network.<br/><br/>ID:yUx7fb Ref #: 93868<br/><br/>
