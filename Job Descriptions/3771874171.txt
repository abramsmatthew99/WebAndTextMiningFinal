        LHH is actively seeking an accomplished Head of Technology for one of our long-time healthcare clients in Albuquerque, NM. In this role, you will spearhead the management and optimization of comprehensive information technology systems across various locations. As the Head of Technology, you will be at the forefront of ensuring seamless operations of our clinical systems throughout the organization. Your role involves overseeing a dedicated IT team, acting as a liaison between IT and business entities/partners, and strategically managing all IT projects and system implementations.<br/><br/><strong>Key Responsibilities:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Strategic Systems Management: Direct day-to-day operations of current Radiology Information Systems and applications, encompassing PACS, RIS, EHR, and advanced imaging applications. </li><li>Collaborative Innovation: Work closely with administration, leadership, and other key stakeholders to design, build, and test solutions aimed at enhancing clinical workflows and operational efficiency. </li><li>Leadership and Supervision: Provide effective management and supervision of the IT team, ensuring cohesion and collaboration. Oversee all IT-related projects with a keen eye on technical documentation and project requirements. </li><li>Continuous Improvement: Stay abreast of industry best practices, propose enhancements, and contribute to the development and implementation of policies and standards in clinical processes. </li><li>Compliance and Security: Ensure adherence to HIPAA privacy and security compliance, cybersecurity directives, and industry-specific measures. Provide justification for IT enhancements, privacy compliance, and cybersecurity initiatives. </li><li>Financial Oversight: Actively participate in budget-related activities, monitor expenses, and provide documentation for budget variances. Justify recommendations related to IT enhancements and improvements. </li><li>Data Management and Reporting: Compile and analyze data, prepare reports on quality measures, and monitor system statuses, identifying trends for continuous improvement. </li><li>Project Coordination: Lead and complete ad-hoc projects, actively participate in system upgrades and testing scripts, coordinate user groups and project teams, and facilitate effective communication between stakeholders. </li><li>Technical Expertise: Troubleshoot system and application problems, collaborate with vendors and support services, and provide ongoing communication to end users, administration, the Board, and vendors. <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Minimum Qualifications:<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Education and Experience: Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science, Technology, or related field, with a preference for a Master’s degree. Minimum of ten years of experience in systems analysis and design, including five years in a managerial capacity. </li><li>Healthcare Expertise: A minimum of ten years of experience with HIPAA, diagnostic, medical imaging experience, including proficiency with radiology-specific applications and systems. Knowledge of Change Healthcare PACS, GE and MedInformatix RIS, EPIC, PowerScribe 360, Corepoint, PenRad, and other radiology imaging applications/systems/equipment is essential. </li><li>Technical Proficiency: Solid experience with Microsoft Office 365 Suite, strong technical, software, and infrastructure expertise, and familiarity with industry standards such as DICOM, XDS, HL7, and Vendor Neutral Archive (VNA). </li><li>Security Expertise: At least 5 years of hands-on experience in designing, implementing, managing, and upgrading IT and cyber security systems; long-term planning and strategy surrounding data and system integrity and security. </li><li>Communication and Leadership: Exceptional communication skills, both written and verbal, strong leadership capabilities, and the ability to work independently or as part of a dynamic team. </li><li>Adaptability: Adaptive personality with exceptional follow-through, ability to react calmly and creatively to complex situations, and effective prioritization and execution in high-pressure environments. </li><li>Project Management: Proven project management experience, including collaboration with cross-functional teams. <br/><br/></li></ul>This position offers a unique opportunity to lead and innovate in the dynamic field of healthcare technology. If you have a passion for leveraging IT solutions to enhance healthcare services, we invite you to apply and contribute to our commitment to excellence in patient care.<br/><br/><strong>Salary: </strong>Starting at $150k<br/><br/><strong>Benefits:</strong> medical, dental, vision, 401k, paid time off, paid holidays,<br/><br/><strong>Location: </strong>onsite in Albuquerque, NM<br/><br/><strong>Pay Details:</strong> $150,000.00 to $190,000.00 per year<br/><br/><strong>Search managed by:</strong> Megan McQuay<br/><br/>Equal Opportunity Employer/Veterans/Disabled<br/><br/>To read our Candidate Privacy Information Statement, which explains how we will use your information, please navigate to https://www.lhh.com/us/en/candidate-privacy<br/><br/>The Company will consider qualified applicants with arrest and conviction records subject to federal contractor requirements and/or security clearance requirements.
