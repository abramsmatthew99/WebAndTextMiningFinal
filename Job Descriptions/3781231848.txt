The position analyzes moderately complex systems and, either independently or under broad supervision, develops changes to existing business applications and/or designs for new initiatives. This position may influence others within the job area through explanation of facts, policies and practices.<br/><br/><strong>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Develops and debugs software in support of new and existing applications</li><li>Develop, execute and thoroughly document unit test plans</li><li>Provides support for production applications (7x24)</li><li>Participate in Agile methodology and ceremonies<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Knowledge, Skills &amp; Abilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Results oriented, critical thinker who uses a sense of urgency to deliver results.</li><li>Must be a self-starter, adapt easily to change and have a collaborative personality.</li><li>Continuous learner able to identify skills gaps and acquire and employ new knowledge.</li><li>Offers and seeks mentoring.</li><li>Understanding of general business concepts and user procedures.</li><li>Excellent written, oral and listening communication skills.</li><li>Proactively identifies opportunities to improve current programs and procedures.</li><li>Employs test-driven development and integrates unit tests in development tasks.</li><li>Meet quality, performance, coding standards and delivery commitments.</li><li>Possesses and shares domain knowledge of at least one system.</li><li>Shows steady professional growth in technical and business skills that enhance the team’s capabilities.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Work Experience and/or Education<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>2+ years experience in software development in an IT organization or equivalent educational experience.</li><li>2+ years experience with a relational database (i.e., Oracle, MSSQL, Postgres, etc.).</li><li>Bachelor’s degree in computer science or related field, trade school or equivalent work experience.</li><li>Experience with a higher-level language (i.e. C#, Java, etc.) preferred.<br/><br/><br/></li></ul>#MOGUL#
