        Camping World Holdings, Inc., headquartered in Lincolnshire, IL, (together with its subsidiaries) is America’s largest retailer of RVs and related products and services. Our vision is to build a long-term legacy business that makes RVing fun and easy, and our Camping World and Good Sam brands have been serving RV consumers since 1966.<br/><br/>We strive to build long-term value for our customers, employees, and shareholders by combining a unique and comprehensive assortment of RV products and services with a national network of RV dealerships, service centers and customer support centers along with the industry’s most extensive online presence and a highly trained and knowledgeable team of employees serving our customers, the RV lifestyle, and the communities in which we operate. We also believe that our Good Sam organization and family of programs and services uniquely enable us to connect with our customers as stewards of the RV enthusiast community and the RV lifestyle. With RV sales and service locations in 42 states, Camping World has grown to become the prime destinations for everything RV.<br/><br/><strong>Pricing Data Analyst<br/><br/></strong><strong>Position Location:</strong> Hybrid (Lincolnshire, IL)<br/><br/>Camping World is seeking a Pricing Data Analyst to work at the intersection of pricing and data science in our recreational vehicle (RV) business. In this role, you will use business and analysis experience to create pricing analytics, analyze competitor data, develop pricing and discounting models, and compare outcomes of different pricing actions.<br/><br/>You will report to the Director of RV Pricing Strategy and be part of a new, dedicated pricing team focused on implementing new pricing policies and processes.<br/><br/><strong>Job Responsibilities<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Perform daily tasks to monitor and adjust prices and pricing models to optimize profit and volume of sales.</li><li>Evaluate and process pricing requests from Camping World team members based on historical data and profit objectives.</li><li>Maintain and update data in Excel, SQL databases, and enterprise systems.</li><li>Perform advanced analysis is Excel and PowerBI applying knowledge of statistics and business processes.</li><li>Develop analytics to quantify willingness to pay, price elasticity, and other pricing related measures using Excel, PowerBI and other analytical tools as necessary.</li><li>Create reports, presentations, and information dashboards to present results of analytics work.</li><li>Work with the pricing team to develop new processes to improve overall efficiency of the pricing process.</li><li>Use Excel, PowerBI, SQL, Python, and/or R to organize and clean data for use in analysis.</li><li>Identify and perform exploratory data analysis on datasets. Join multiple datasets for deeper analysis.</li><li>Take on special projects as needed.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Must Have Qualifications – Education<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Computer Science, Data Science, Business Analytics or closely related degree at bachelor’s or master’s level <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Must Have Qualifications – Experience<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>5 years of relevant pricing or data analysis work experience (3 years with master’s degree)</li><li>Experience in data exploration, statistical analysis, and visualizations.</li><li>Experience with Microsoft Office tools (Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook).</li><li>Experience with common data science programming tools such as SQL, Python, and/or R.<br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Must Have Qualifications – Other<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Strong analytical and problem-solving skills</li><li>Ability to maintain professional demeanor and strict confidentiality.</li><li>Ability to work independently to complete assigned tasks.</li><li>Must be diligent, organized, and extremely detail-oriented</li><li>Strong written and verbal communication skills <br/><br/></li></ul><strong>Preferred Qualifications<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>Direct experience in pricing as an analyst, researcher, manager, within marketing, inventory, finance, or pricing departments.</li><li>Experience developing or contributing to machine learning-based pricing models.<br/><br/></li></ul>Full-time associates are offered a comprehensive benefit package including medical, dental, vision, PTO, 401k and more!
