I look forward to working with smart passionate young engineers, experiencing the journey from idea to product.<br/><br/><strong>What The Team Says<br/><br/></strong>“What I love about working in this team is that I have a rare opportunity to contribute to a cutting-edge product that competes with most famous worldwide companies while being surrounded by great teammates.”<br/><br/><strong>How We Work<br/><br/></strong><ul><li>We work in a hybrid style and all of our team members enjoy coming to the office 2-3 days a week.
